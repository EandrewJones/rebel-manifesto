Chibougamau- The Parliamentary Assistant to the Minister of Natural Resources (Nordic Development component) and MP for Ungava, Mr. Luc Ferland, on behalf of the Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and the Occupation of the Territory and Minister of Transport, sir Sylvain Gaudreault, Announces the award of $ 25,000 Boreal Escapade Financial Assistance for the realization of a specialized guide training and tourist package training project, combining snowmobile and Cree culture. 
Non-profit organization jointly led by Jamesians and Cree, Boreal Escapade has been specializing, since 2010, in the organization and development of adventure tourism in Nord-du-Québec, in harmony with local communities . The aid granted will enable it to strengthen its tourism offer by the launch of new packages incorporating the Cree culture and the snowmobile that will be offered from the winter of 2014. In addition, two courses will be offered to a group of ten participants. The first will focus on first aid in the isolated region and the second, on security during hiking by off-road vehicle. Boreal getaway seeks to create, in collaboration with the Waswanipi community, tourist products that would be proposed by Cree guides on the periphery of the federated snowmobile trails. 
"It is with great pride that our government supports this booming body, which has quickly acquired a beautiful notoriety in Quebec. I have the strong conviction that this structuring project will promote the creation of stimulating jobs for our community, will contribute to the safeguarding of local Cree culture and will position the North-du-Québec as the destination par excellence of the Nordic Expedition. "Said MP Luc Ferland. 
Financial assistance comes from the Cyclical Development Fund of the Ministry of Municipal Affairs, Regions and the Occupation of the Territory.
