Quebec- The Minister of Culture and Communications, Maka Kotto, wishes to warmly congratulate the craftsmen and artisans from Quebec and abroad who have been illustrated on the occasion of the 37th edition of the Montreal World Film Festival. He particularly emphasizes the victory of the filmmaker Mathieu Roy, awarded for his first feature film the other house, as well as the actor Marcel Sabourin, who won the masculine interpretation price for his role in this feature film. 
"The World Film Festival contributes to the promotion of the diversity of cultural expressions through the films of here and those of a multitude of countries around the world. Again this year, an important place has been given to creators and creators from home who have proposed remarkable works to the many cinemaphiles loyal to this festival. I congratulate them, "said Minister Kotto. 
Among the ten featured Quebec fiction films for this edition, two were in world competition, the fisherman's house in Alain Chartrand and the other house of Mathieu Roy.
