Quebec, on February 27, 2017- The spokesperson for the Parti Québécois in the field of culture and communications, Agnès Maltais, said very proud that many Quebec artisans of cinema are now illustrating on the world stage. She obviously mentioned Sylvain Bellemare, who won, yesterday at the Oscars, the statuette rewarding the best sound montage for the Arrival film, the Denis Villeneuve filmmaker. 
"On behalf of the Québécois Party, I warmly congratulate Sylvain Bellemare and the entire Arrival team, including Denis Villeneuve, for this exceptional price. Their names are added to the list of women and men who shine Quebec on the international art scene. And this is a real pride in finding that from year to year, this list is only lying, "said the entry of play, the spokesman. 
The Oscar ceremony remains the most prestigious and the most popular year of the world cinematographic sphere; To shine is synonymous with immense talent, it goes without saying. At this 89th edition, like the previous one, several Quebeckers were in the running, by the way. "For years, in Quebec, we are aware of the excellence and originality of our cinema. And this applies to both actresses and actors and those who are practicing a technical profession, often less known to the general public, less highlighted. Today, through the Academy, it is the whole world that recognizes these qualities to our seventh art. For a nation of our size, it's sensational! "Sought Agnès Maltese. 
"Quebec cinema is now one of the best; He joined the big door, the one bordered with a red carpet. And, given the energy, creativity and knowledge? Doing his craftsmen, there is a lot to bet that this beautiful momentum will continue ... for our greatest pleasure, "she concluded.
