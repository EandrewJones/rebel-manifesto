Quebec - The Government of Quebec takes note of the decision of the Compagnie Montreal, Maine & Atlantic Railway (MMA) to be under the protection of the Companies' Creditors Act. 
The government wants to reassure the population of Quebec and, in the first place, that of Lac-Mégantic, that the application today will not interruption in the course of work in progress. 
"Our priority remains the same, or to take all necessary measures to ensure the continuity of the cleaning, decontamination and restoration of the affected area for the benefit of the citizens of Lac-Mégantic," said the Minister of Health. and social services and minister responsible for the Estrie region, Mr. Réjean Hébert.
