Quebec- The Minister of Public Safety, Mr. Stéphane Bergeron, makes the victims of 3 new municipalities eligible for financial assistance following the railway accident on July 6, 2013, in the city of Lac-Mégantic. In total, it is now the victims of 18 municipalities who can benefit from the specific financial assistance program for this event. 
"The role of the department is to make every effort to support the municipal authorities and help all Quebeckers to ensure their safety and that of their property," said Bergeron. 
This government program is a last resort assistance and covers, in particular, additional expenditure on current expenditure on municipalities for the deployment of intervention and recovery measures. 
"The financial assistance program puts a balm on the unfortunate events experienced by the victims. My priority as Minister of Public Security is to provide all necessary efforts for these people to regain tranquility and return to their normal life, "said Bergeron. 
For more information about this program or to obtain the necessary claim forms, contact the Recovery Branch at 1,888,643-aid (2433) or visit the Department of Public Safety website at www.securitepublique.gouv .qc.ca.
