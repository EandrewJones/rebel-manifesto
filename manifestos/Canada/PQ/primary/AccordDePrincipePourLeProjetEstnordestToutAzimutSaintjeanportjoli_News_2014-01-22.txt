Saint-Jean-Port-Joli- The Minister of Culture and Communications, Maka Kotto, announces an agreement of principle of more than $ 2 million for the immobilization project of the East Northeast, residence of Artists. 
"He reigns to Saint-Jean-Port-Joli a natural atmosphere conducive to creation and cultural innovation. Art and culture lovers and artists come from abroad to draw inspiration and creativity. Ultimately, the entire community of artists in visual arts, art professions and media arts as well as the general population could benefit from the investments made in the East-Northeast project: all azimuth! , "Said Minister Maka Kotto. 
This agreement in principle will allow the East-Northeast Organization, a residence of artists to carry out complementary studies and finalize the definition and planning of the project. It is intended to provide the organization of infrastructure and equipment essential to the full realization of its production mandate, a functional place all year round. 
The amount provided for in the agreement in principle is $ 2,169,000, or 90% of the estimated total cost. It is made possible through the Department of Culture and Communications Capital Assistance Program. The East-Northeast Organization, a residence of artists will contribute to the project through a reserve of $ 200,000 accumulated in particular as part of the Program Patronage Investments Culture of the Arts and Letters of Quebec. The municipality of Saint-Jean-Port-Joli is also a partner for $ 40,000. 
The financial support of the department is part of the 2013-2023 Infrastructure Plan, released last April. Recall that this ten-year plan will eventually result in $ 2 billion investments for the cultural component.
