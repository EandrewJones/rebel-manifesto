"The Prime Minister must commit to publicizing Quebec's conditions as quickly as possible to reintegrate the Constitution, its schedule, and when it intends to consult the population on this important issue. If he wishes to undertake such an approach, he has an obligation of transparency, "said Stéphane Bédard.
