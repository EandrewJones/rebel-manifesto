Quebec- The time when the date of the elections was fixed according to the interests of the party in power is now something of the past: the bill on the elections on date fixed finally was adopted today, unanimously, by the deputies of the National Assembly. The minister responsible for democratic institutions and citizen participation has welcomed his adoption. "It's a beautiful victory for Quebeckers and for our democracy, said the minister. After more than six months of discussions, tractings, negotiations between all parties in the Assembly, we managed to re-unanimously of all parties on a bill to restore to Quebeckers their elections.
