Quebec- The responsible minister of the regions of the Capitale-Nationale and the Chaudière-Appalaches, Minister of Labor, Employment and Social Solidarity and Minister responsible for the Status of Women, Agnès Maltais, announces financial assistance to the Quebec City of $ 150,000 over three years to support Labable Concertation Work from Old Quebec. 
"As a result of the newly announced changes regarding the Mission of the Quebec Hôtel-Dieu Hospital, I made a commitment to financially support the work of the Old Quebec Concertation Table. This work will allow the establishment of carrier economic, social and patrimonial projects that will consolidate the vitality of this unparalleled living environment at the heart of my riding, a cultural jewel of UNESCO World Heritage, "said Madame Maltais. 
Old Quebec's consultation table was created in the wake of Old Quebec States of Old Quebec from 2012. At the beginning of 2013, the table became a permanent structure whose main objective is to promote the maintenance and the Harmonious development of the different uses - residential, commercial, institutional, administrative, tourist and festive - so that this historic district remains a living and inhabited environment. Chaired by Mrs. Julie Lemieux, City Councilor, the table brings together 18 representatives of organizations, institutions, associations of traders and citizens as well as three municipal elected. 
The Secretariat, attached to the Ministry of the Executive Board, advises the Government and the Minister responsible for the Capitale-Nationale, Ms. Agnès Maltais, on any question or measure having a significant impact on the region and its economic, cultural, tourist and social development . The Secretariat acts in consultation with its various governmental and regional partners, while financially supporting the implementation of projects contributing to the growth of the Capitale-Nationale.
