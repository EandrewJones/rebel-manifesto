Quebec- The member of Pointe-aux-Trembles and official opposition spokesperson for primary and secondary education and school perseverance, Nicole Léger, denounces the inconsistency of the Minister of Education, Leisure and Sport, Yves Bolduc, when it comes to massive budget cuts in Quebec schools. 
Today, the minister says, through his spokesman, that the duties imposed cuts in schools are totally unacceptable. However, in a daily article The sun appeared on August 28, it was learned that the minister's office had awarded school boards a list comprising 32 suggestions for savings. Of these, we found, in particular, a reduction in assistance to homework. 
"It's the minister who prompted schools to cut in help with homework. And, now that some followed his suggestion, he undigurs. He can not wash his hands; It will have to understand that its budget cuts affect the services to students. That he assumes his lack of leadership. He is responsible, "said Nicole Léger. 
"By refusing to take responsibility for the cuts in schools, Minister Yves Bolduc demonstrates once again that he is not up to his function. How long will the Prime Minister Philippe Couillard still tolerate this rough minister who improvises day-to-day? ", Concluded the member of the Parti Québécois.
