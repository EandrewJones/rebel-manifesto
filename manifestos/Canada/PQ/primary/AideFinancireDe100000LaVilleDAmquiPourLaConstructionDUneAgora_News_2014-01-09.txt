Amqui- The Minister Delegate to Tourism, Minister responsible for the Bas-St. Lawrence and Matane-Matapédia Region, Mr. Pascal Bérubé, on behalf of the Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and the Occupation of the Territory and Minister of Transport, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, announces the award of $ 100,000 financial assistance to the city of Amqui for the construction of an agora at Pierre-et-Maurice-Gagné Park. 
Thanks to a capacity of 2,000 people, this vast outdoor scene, overlooked by a roof, will become the largest exterior entertainment room of the Matapédia Valley. It will be the host of shows and events of all kinds, especially as part of the festivities surrounding the 125th anniversary of Amqui, which will be celebrated in 2014. The project, the cost estimated at $ 624,000, will therefore allow To complete the offer of services in this park, which already includes playgrounds for children, water games and an asphalt cyclable ring. 
"It is with great pride that I announce this financial assistance from the Government of Quebec, which will enhance the attraction of this magnificent meeting place and will offer an invaluable springboard for cultural activities not only in amqui, but Also for the whole of the Matapédia Valley, "said Minister Pascal Bérubé. 
Financial assistance comes from the cyclical development of the Ministry of Municipal Affairs, Regions and the Occupation of the Territory.
