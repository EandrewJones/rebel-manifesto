Val-d'Or- The member for Abitibi-East and Minister Aboriginal Affairs, Ms. Elizabeth Larouche, on behalf of the Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Occupation of the Territory and Minister of Transport, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, Announces the granting of $ 30,500 financial assistance to the Kitcisakik economic company for the renovation of houses at Grand Lake Victoria. 
More concretely, the project consists in restoring about twenty summer houses located on the peninsula of Grand Lake Victoria. This project will allow the inhabitants of the community of Kitcisakik to continue their tradition of going every year in these places for a period of about six months from May to October. 
"The Government of Quebec is pleased to contribute to the well-being of the members of the Kitcisakik community as well as to the maintenance of their cultural wealth by associating on the realization of this project which will allow them to continue the practice of traditions and Rituals who are dear to them, "said Minister Larouche. 
Financial assistance comes from the Cyclical Development Fund of the Ministry of Municipal Affairs, Regions and the Occupation of the Territory. 
It should also be remembered that the Secretariat for Aboriginal Affairs has recently granted a $ 49,500 grant for the completion of this project.
