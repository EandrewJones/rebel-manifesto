G Aspon - Minister Delegate to the Regions, Minister responsible for the Gaspé-Îles-de-la-Madeleine Region and MP and Deputy of Gaspé, Mr. Gaétan Lelièvre, Announces Funding Assistance of $ 305,657 As part of the Regional Initiatives Fund to support the implementation of seven structuring projects for the Gaspé region, whose complete list is attached. 
"Our government has made the development of Gaspésie a priority, which is why I am proud to support these promising projects, which will promote the socio-economic growth of the region. These various projects from the environment and representing various sectors of the Economy of the Gaspé region including tourism, culture, wood processing, testify to the dynamism of the people of the region. These projects will have positive benefits for a large number of companies and organizations from here, but also on the quality of life of Gaspé citizens, "said Minister Lelièvre. 
The Regional Initiatives Fund is part of the Government Intervention Strategy for the Development of the Gaspé-Islands-de-la-Madeleine Region and constitutes a major lever to promote, in particular, the realization of Projects from development priorities in the region. An annual $ 6 million annual envelope is scheduled for this fund, for a period of five years. The program is in effect until March 31, 2018.
