Quebec, February 14, 2020 - The member for Matane-Matapédia and parliamentary leader of the Parti Québécois, Pascal Bérubé, announced that he will now be the spokesperson for his parliamentary group in Aboriginal business. 
"The Parti Québécois is proud of the determining gestures that it has asked during its history to improve relations with First Nations and Inuit du Québec, including the official recognition of the Aboriginal nations of Quebec, the peace of the brave and the law. instituting the regional government of Eeyou Istchee Baie-James. We therefore deemed it to be appropriate for our parliamentary leader to be responsible for this important issue. I will use it with all the opening and the attention it requires, "he said.
