Trois-Rivières- The Premier and Head of the Parti Québécois, Pauline Marois, is pleased to announce the candidacy of Alexis Deschênes in the riding of Trois-Rivières. "Alexis is a man of heart and conviction. This is an exceptional application for the people of Trois-Rivières, who will recognize in him of the undeniable qualities of integrity and dedication. I ask them to support Alexis to represent them to the National Assembly, "Pauline Marois said. 
Father of three children, Alexis Deschênes is a former journalist and corresponding to the National Assembly. He has been a member of the Bar since 2013 and holds the position of lawyer at the Litigation Branch of the Quebec Ministry of Justice. 
"I have always felt that my heart was anchored in the soil of the regions of Quebec. I want to do politics to serve citizens. I will make a duty to be very present on the ground to meet people and hear their concerns. I am committed to the social and economic development of the regions, especially that of Trois-Rivières, "said Alexis Deschênes. 
"After taking courageous decisions in Mauricie, the government is committed to making the region more prosperous. We delivered the goods: the unemployment rate decreased and investments of more than 500 million were realized, which helped preserve or create more than 1700 jobs. And that's just the beginning. We are committed to promoting the prosperity of the entire region, "added Pauline Marois. 
"Through the economic diversification fund set up for Mauricie and Center-du-Québec, we work with local stakeholders to attract investment and support businesses in their development. We have a plan and the team to succeed. Today, it's time to give us the means to go further, "concluded Mr. Deschênes.
