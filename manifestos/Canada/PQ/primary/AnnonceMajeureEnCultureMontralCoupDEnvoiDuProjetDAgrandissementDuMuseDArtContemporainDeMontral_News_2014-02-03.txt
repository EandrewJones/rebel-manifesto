Montreal- The Prime Minister of Quebec, Pauline Marois, accompanied by the Minister of Culture and Communications, Maka Kotto, and the Minister of International Relations, Francophonie and Foreign Trade and Minister responsible for the Montreal Region, Jean-François Listed, announces a crucial first step in the realization of a major project: the transformation of the Montreal contemporary art. 
"I am very proud to lead a government that deeply believes in the importance of a lively, current and accessible culture. Today, I am pleased to announce that we are crossing an extra step towards access to this culture. It is therefore with pleasure and pride that I kits the expansion project of the Montreal Contemporary Art Museum, "said the Prime Minister. 
"I am very proud to lead a government that deeply believes in the importance of a lively and accessible culture at all," said the Prime Minister // (c) Marc-André de Blois (source)
