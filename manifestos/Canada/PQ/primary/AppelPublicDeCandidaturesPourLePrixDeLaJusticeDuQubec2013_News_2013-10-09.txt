Quebec- The Minister of Justice and Attorney General of Quebec, Mr. Bertrand St-Arnaud, officially launches the public call for applications from the Québec Justice Award, the highest honorary distinction in the legal field in Quebec . The nomination period will take place until 31 December 2013. 
"The price of justice allows us to pay tribute to a person who has been illustrated by his commitment to defending the values of justice that are accessibility, universality and quality, essential values to our rule of law. And who are at the heart of our democratic society, "said Bertrand St-Arnaud. 
Since its inception on 4 June 1990, the price of the Justice of Quebec has been awarded to 9 women and 12 men in recognition of their great contribution to the evolution of Quebec society, thanks to their actions to promote justice within reach. Of all, incorporates, impartial and effective. 
Each year, the recipient of this prestigious award receives a massive silver medal symbolizing the values of justice, creation of the Quebec artist of Polish origin Bozena Happach. This medal is awarded by the Minister of Justice during a ceremony at the hotel of Parliament, in the presence of representatives of the public, the legal environment and the media. 
Last June, the 2012 Justice Award was handed over to the Honorable René Dussault, OC, OQ, AD. E., who, in his unwavering commitment for the well-being of the community, has devoted the 50 years his career to promote the fundamental values of justice. 
For more information, the population as well as organizations wishing to submit a person's candidacy are invited to visit the sitewww.prixdelajustice.gouv.qc.ca. It is also possible to obtain leaflets with the price of justice in the services offices Québec and in courthouses.
