Montreal - The leader of the Parti Québécois, Pauline Marois, wishes to rectify the facts regarding an article in the newspaper the press of this morning that suggests that the Party does not "commit to subject the CEGEPs to Law 101". This offormation conveyed by the press is quite false. 
"In our last Congress, the activists have adopted a proposal to expand the application of the Charter of the French language to CEGEPs. This proposal is part of our program and will be part of our electoral platform. Point at the line, "said Pauline Marois. 
Watch an extract from the Press Conference of the Parliamentary Session Review, while in response to a question, Pauline Marois unequivocally rectifies the words conveyed by the press.
