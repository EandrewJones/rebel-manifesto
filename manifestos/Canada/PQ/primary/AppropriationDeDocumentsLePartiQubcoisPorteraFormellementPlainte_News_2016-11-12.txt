MONTREAL- Following the appropriation of strategic documents by two transferred employees of the Parti Québécois at the Coalition Future Québec, the Directorate General of the Parti Québécois has made the decision to file a complaint with the police officers for the flight. Document. 
Following recovery efforts, it was possible to find that the transferred employees had granted nominative, confidential, strategic and electoral documents. Also, IT statements indicate that one of the employees in question attempted to access the IT system of the Parti Québécois during and after passing at the CAQ. In the coming days, the Parti Québécois will put a complaint with the police service concerned for theft of documents.
