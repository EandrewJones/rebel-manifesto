Quebec- The member for Chicoutimi and Leader of the Official Opposition, Stéphane Bédard, as well as the hon. Member for Hochelaga-Maisonneuve and spokesperson for international relations, Carole Poirier, express their solidarity with relatives of the victims and the Tunisian people , as a result of the attack perpetrated at the National Bardo Museum in Tunis. 
"Horror and violence still struck, this time at the heart of a highly historical and tourist place. On behalf of the whole team of the Parti Québécois, I extend my sincere condolences to the relatives and families of the victims, as well as to all the Tunisian people; We are at your side in this event, "said Stéphane Bédard. 
"No matter what reasons that have been able to motivate these gestures, they remain unacceptable and barbarous, and we must every time denounce them high and strong. Faced with terror, solidarity and democracy must triumph, "said Carole Poirier.
