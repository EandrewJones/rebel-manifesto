Quebec- As a result of the tragic events that took place in Bamako this morning, the leader of the official opposition, Pierre Karl Péladeau, and the spokesman for the Quebecois in international relations, Carole Poirier, held 'Address to the Malian people. 
"On behalf of all the elected officials of the Quebecois, I want to express our deep solidarity towards Mali, while Bamako faces the horror of terrorist attacks. We share the pain and affliction of Malians, particularly those of the families and relatives of the victims, before these barbaric and ignorable gestures that we condemn vigorously. Ending terrorism must be a priority for the entire international community. This insane violence must stop. We offer in Mali our support against this drama, "said Pierre Karl Péladeau. 
"I am deeply shaken by the events that occurred this morning in Bamako. Mali is struck by acts of unspeakable terrorism. My thoughts go to the families of the victims and the Malian people, who I offer my sincere condolences. I also have a particular thought for Quebeckers on the scene of the tragedy, especially for Mr. Maxime Carrier-Legaré, an employee of the National Assembly of Quebec, who, fortunately, escaped safe and except, " Concluded Carole Poirier.
