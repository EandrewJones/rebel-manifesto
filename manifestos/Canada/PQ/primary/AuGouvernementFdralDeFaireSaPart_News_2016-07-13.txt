Quebec, on July 13, 2016- The member for Sanguinet and spokesperson for the Québécois party in the field of Economics, Alain Therrien, deplores the lack of flagrant coherence of Liberal Prime Minister Philippe Couillard, who suggested that Québec taxpayers could New must pay for the center of excellence of the C Series. 
"Prime Minister improvises with hundreds of millions of dollars. After its investment of 1 billion, it boasted that in exchange, there would be a center of excellence. Today, he says it may be we who pay it. Now, while the federal government has changed the law to ensure that aircraft interview is no longer in Montreal, Quebecers should pay for the future center of excellence to day. That's enough, the federal government to do his part, "said Alain Therrien. 
The member once again denounced the abandonment of the Prime Minister for the federal contribution in the Quebec flagship of aeronautics. "The Prime Minister should require and insist that the federal government participates, especially for the creation of the Center of Excellence. After abandoning pressure on the federal government to get financial assistance, he now says that Quebec alone will have to pay, "he concluded.
