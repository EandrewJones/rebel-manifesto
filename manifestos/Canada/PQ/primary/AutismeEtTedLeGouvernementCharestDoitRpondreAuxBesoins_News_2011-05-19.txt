Quebec - Member for Matapédia and official opposition on social services, Danielle Doyer, said infinitely disappointed the answers from the Minister Delegate to Social Services, Dominique Vien, to his questions about families. children and persons autistic or who have invasive development disorders (TED). Families of autistic people were gathered in front of the National Assembly today. 
"It's a true trail from the fighter currently going through these families to succeed in obtaining a diagnosis, specialized services and some respite. They feel literally abandoned by Jean Charest's government who had promised them sea and world in 2003, "said the hon. Member for Matapédia.

These data do not take into account adults and adults who are not integrated into the education network. 
"The Liberal government does not hear the cries of the hearts launched by families. That Minister Vien refuses to meet with his entry into action in August 2010, organizations that offer front-line services, it is deeply despiring, "said Danielle Doyer. 
"The Prime Minister must act now and show the same compassion he had manifested in the opposition when he claimed the deposit of a government action plan, asking which the government of the Parti Québécois had responded in 2003. Now that the number of people who need services has tripled, can he hear the demands and meet the needs? ", Concluded Danielle Doyer.
