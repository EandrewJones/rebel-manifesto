Quebec, March 2, 2018 -The official opposition spokesperson for transport electrification, Marc Bourcier, presented 5 measures to electrify all of Quebec's school buses by 2030. 
In press points, Marc Bourcier detailed the 5 measures: 
· Enhance the financial support program for the deployment of electric school buses. The Liberal government has announced that the unique discount given to the purchase of a fully electric school bus will increase, between 2018-2019 and $ 2020-2021, $ 125,000 to $ 105,000. We will maintain this amount to $ 125,000. 
· Establish a Hydro-Québec's energy credit to reduce the electricity bill for electric school bus operators.
