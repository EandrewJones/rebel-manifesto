Quebec- The Prime Minister of Quebec, Pauline Marois, is pleased to announce the appointment of the member for Johnson and Chief Whip of the Government of Quebec, Yves-François Blanchet, as Head of the Region of the Mauricie and the Region Central-du-Québec replacing Bernard Drainville, Member of Parliament for Marie-Victorin and Minister responsible for Democratic Institutions and Citizen Participation, and Pierre Duchesne, Member of Parliament for Borduas and Minister of Higher Education, Research, Science and technology. 
"I am very pleased to entrust these new responsibilities to Yves-François Blanchet. It is a man of convictions and I am convinced that he will be brilliantly defending the interests of the citizens of Mauricie and Center-du-Québec, "said Quebec Prime Minister Pauline Marois.
