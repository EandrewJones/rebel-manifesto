Quebec- The Minister Delegate to Industrial Policy and the Quebec Economic Development Bank, Ms. Élaine Zakaïb, undertakes a tour that will lead her, in the coming weeks, in several regions of Quebec. His first stop will take place on Thursday, October 4, in Bonaventure, in the Gaspésie region? Islands-de-la-Madeleine. 
"As soon as it arrives as a delegated minister, it was essential for me to meet the main actors of economic development in the field. Our territory is vast and the various realities that entrepreneurs live in each of our regions require special attention. I am convinced that the future of Quebec goes through the sustainable development of the regions. I need to hear and exchange with business people. I want to know their concerns and, above all, present our guidance on economic development. These exchanges will fuel our reflection on the creation of a one-stop shop that will facilitate the life of entrepreneurs. The establishment of the Quebec Economic Development Bank is one of the important mandates entrusted to me and I attack it now, "said Minister Zakaïb. 
During this tour, Minister Zakaïb will meet with the various business communities (local development centers, chambers of commerce, entrepreneurship tables, etc.), will discuss with municipal elected officials and visit some companies. 
"For our government, regions are a great wealth. To enrich us collectively, we must also rely on prosperous regions offering quality jobs. It is up to us to take all necessary measures to provide businesses from here the instruments specific to the establishment of sustainable prosperity and exceeding their possibilities. We have the ideas, we have the team and, above all, we have the desire to put adequate gestures to improve the lives of Quebeckers throughout the territory, "concluded the minister.
