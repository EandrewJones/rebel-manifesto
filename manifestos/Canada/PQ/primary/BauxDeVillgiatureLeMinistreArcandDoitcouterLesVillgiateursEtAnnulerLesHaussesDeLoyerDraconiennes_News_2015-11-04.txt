Québec- With the support of several chalet owners built on the public lands of Quebec, the member for Bonaventure and spokesman for the Quebecois on forest, wildlife and parks, Sylvain Roy, exhorts once again The Minister of Natural Resources, Pierre Arcand, to cancel faeceous rent increases for resort leases. 
"It is a strong message that the government of thousands of citizens who are at the accessibility of Quebec's public lands, without discrimination as to their income or social status. Seven thousand villagers signed the petition to claim, on the one hand, the cancellation of the increases decreed by the Liberal government - which may, in some cases, reach 530% - and, on the other hand, that Minister Arcand 'Ensure to avoid a return to private clubs, abolished in Quebec in the late 1970s by Yves Duhaime and the Parti Québécois. The minister must hear the message sent by these thousands of citizens and to announce that he permanently renounces the increases, "the member insisted. 
Sylvain Roy also calls for the minister to sit down with the various stakeholders in the field of resort to put in place long-term solutions guaranteeing the maintenance of the accessibility of the lands of the state domain to all Quebeckers . 
"The actors of the community have interesting proposals that deserve to be taken into account by the government. Millions of Quebecers appreciate the resort and outdoor activities. These have significant economic benefits in all regions. The minister must take his responsibilities and reassure the citizens of Quebec that they can continue to take advantage of their own territory, "concluded the member.
