Beloeil- On behalf of the Minister of Culture and Communications, Maka Kotto, the Minister of Higher Education, Research, Science and Technology and MP, Pierre Duchesne, announces the signing of an agreement cultural development with the city of Beloeil. This agreement includes financial commitments totaling $ 163,500 and will be able to support various sectors of cultural activity. 
"This first agreement will allow the creators of Beloeil and the surroundings of sharing their passion with their fellow citizens and their fellow citizens and will contribute to better access to culture and the arts. Such an agreement can serve as a vehicle to increase artistic practice but also the development of heritage on the territory, "said Maka Kotto. 
The contribution of the Ministry of Culture and Communications, for the duration of the 2013-2016 agreement, is $ 75,000, while that of the City of Beloeil is $ 88,500. This agreement covers the achievement of 16 actions related to the objectives pursued by the City in the Visual Arts, Media Arts, Performing Arts and Heritage sectors. These objectives aim to mobilize the population through cultural mediation, to promote the participation of cultural resources by opening up to new cultural practices and to highlight heritage with interpretation circuits. 
"Culture has identity and stimulates creativity, while constituting a powerful leverage of socio-economic progress. The city of Beloeil understood it and, for the last few years, its cultural efforts are remarkable. These led to the happy settlement we know today. In addition, the 2013-2015 culture plan for culture today the city is another example, "said Pierre Duchesne.
