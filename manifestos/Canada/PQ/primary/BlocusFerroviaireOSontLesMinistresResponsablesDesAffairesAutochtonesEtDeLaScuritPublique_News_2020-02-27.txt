Quebec, on February 27, 2020- The member for Matane-Matapédia, parliamentary leader and spokesman for the Parti Québécois on Aboriginal business, Pascal Bérubé, as well as the member for Duplessis and Spokesperson for Public Safety, Lorraine Richard, launch a research notice: In this 22nd day of rail blockade, we would need indications from the Quebec Minister responsible for Aboriginal Affairs, Sylvie d'Amours, as well as his alternate colleague to public safety, Andrée Laforest. 
IN SHORT 
The parliamentary leader of the Parti Québécois clearly poses the question: "Where are the ministers responsible for aboriginal affairs and public safety? They are attributable. They have accounts to pay to the population. » 
" It is important; I invite them to hold daily press points for the rail crisis, where they could take stock of the state of the discussions, "proposed Lorraine Richard.
