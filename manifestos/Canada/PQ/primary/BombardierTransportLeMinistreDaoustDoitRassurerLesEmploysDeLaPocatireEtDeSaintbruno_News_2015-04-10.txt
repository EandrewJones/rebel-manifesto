Quebec- As a result of the information unveiled by several media about transaction rumors involving Bombardier Transport, the member for Saint-Jérôme and official opposition spokesperson for economy, entrepreneurship, SMEs And exports, Pierre Karl Péladeau, asks the Minister of Economy, Innovation and Exports, Jacques Daoust, to reassure employees who work for this division in Quebec, the Pocatière and Saint-Bruno. 
"The Minister must contact the Bombardier business and make sure that, regardless of the scenario, which will be retained, the factories and employees of Quebec are not forgotten. The Transport of Bombardier division is a state-of-the-art sector that contributes significantly to Quebec exports. Trains manufactured in Quebec roll all over the world and offer a showcase on our genius. A production facility is located in La Pocatière, while the engineering center is located in Saint-Bruno; The minister must immediately reassure employees, "said Pierre Karl Péladeau.
