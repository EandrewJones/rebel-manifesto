Québec- On this national day watch, the official opposition leader, Jean-François Lisé, wished, on behalf of the members of Parliament and members of the Quebecois party, a day under the sign of pride and camaraderie To all Quebeckers and to all Quebeckers, inviting them to participate in large numbers in the proposed activities. 
"Everywhere in Quebec, in all regions, people will gather to celebrate, in songs and French. Join them. Go to meet them. Meet each other! First mentioned Mr. Lisée. 
"Quebeckers and Quebeckers form a brave and determined people. We struggled very hard for our freedom, our culture, our language. And, in addition to being progressives, Democrats, we are an extremely fertile soil in terms of innovation, creativity, know-how. In short, there are really enough to be proud! , "He continued. 
"Other fights still need to be conducted. We will take them. But always in the spirit of openness, respect and optimism that characterizes us. In the meantime, today, everywhere in Quebec, it is time to celebrate what we share, what we are, and what we aspire to become as people. To each of you, Happy National Day, and good summer! ", Concluded Jean-François Lisée.
