Quebec, August 21, 2016 - The Olympic Games in Rio, the member for Jonquière and Leader of the Official Opposition, Sylvain Gaudreault, as well as the member for Labelle and spokesman for the Parti Québécois. , leisure and healthy lifestyle, Sylvain Pagé, wanted to congratulate these tremendous athletes who watched the world by their remarkable performances and, more particularly, our Quebec athletes. 
"At the top of their art, the athletes who participated in the games made us live intense emotions. All of all deserve our brighter congratulations, if only for having bravely persevered on the road leading to this prestigious competition. Attend the Olympics, it is already an accomplishment in itself, the coronation of long years of work and preparation, mental and physical ", first declared Sylvain Gaudreault. 
"In total, there were 22 medals that were harvested by athletes representing Canada at the Rio Games, and several Quebeckers are at the origin of these successes. This result is fantastic. We are very proud of these women and men who have proved to us that dreams can come true, provided the necessary efforts and conviction. I obviously hope that several young people have found in these Olympians a source of inspiration and motivation that will lead them to adopt healthy lifestyle habits, "says Sylvain Pagé. 
"Whether or not they have collected the great honors, whether they work alone or in a team, our athletes are among our noblest ambassadors; In addition to our congratulations, they deserve our unconditional support. Of course, I continue to see soon athletes wear the colors of Quebec to the Olympics, and distinguish! ", Concluded the leader of the official opposition.
