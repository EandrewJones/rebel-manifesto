Saint-Raymond- The new palliative care unit of the Portneuf Health and Social Services Center, located at the Portneuf Regional Hospital, was inaugurated today by the Minister Delegate to Social Services and the Protection of Youth , Mrs VÃ©ronique Hivon. 
The unit welcomes, for a few weeks, patients at the end of life. Three beds have been added for palliative care, beyond the two already existed at the Portneuf Regional Hospital. In addition to the five private rooms, the new unit has two salons for families and a shared kit. "This new unit makes it possible to offer end-of-life care in a climate of privacy, tranquility and serenity. The places have been appointed optimally and warmly, to ensure the comfort of the patient and his loved ones, "rejoiced Mrs HIVON. 
The Minister has confirmed a $ 75,000 upload of the Department of Health and Social Services for the development of this palliative care unit, which adds to an earlier investment of $ 180,000 from the Department and of the Capitale National Health and Social Services Agency. The Foundation of Portneuf Health and Social Services contributed $ 60,000 for the purchase of the equipment and furniture required. 
"The development of palliative care is a priority for our government and is part of our goal of improving the offer of service with respect to end-of-life care. The addition of three beds in the Portneuf sector will result in better meet the needs of its population in terms of palliative care. The new unit inaugurated today will allow dozens of people every year to cross the last stage of their lives in a peaceful place specially designed for their needs and those of their loved ones, "concluded Mrs Hivon.
