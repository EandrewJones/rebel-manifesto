Quebec, October 30, 2014 -The MP for Chicoutimi and Official Opposition Leader, Stéphane Bédard, announces changes to the Parliamentary Wing of the Parti Québécois. 
The member for Duplessis, Lorraine Richard, will henceforth be spokesperson for the official opposition in northern development. 
The hon. Member for Rimouski, Harold Lebel, becomes spokesperson for seniors, in addition to maintaining his current spokesperson for poverty, solidarity and social economy.
