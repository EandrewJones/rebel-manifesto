East Broughton - The leader of the Quebecois party, Pauline Marois, welcomed the candidacy of Kaven Mathieu in the riding of Lotbinière-Frontenac. 
Sovereignist activist for several years, Kaven Mathieu knows very well the constituency he intends to represent. He is currently mayor of the Municipality of East Broughton. 
"It is with great pride, but also a lot of humility, which I will go to meet the citizens of Lotbinière-Frontenac. I will offer them my experience, my energy and all the strength of my convictions to earn their trust. The whole team of the Parti Québécois and myself are going to work hard so that the wind of change is blowing strong on Lotbinière-Frontenac, "said Kaven Mathieu. 
"The arrival of Kaven Mathieu in our team is a great news for the Parti Québécois. Our candidate combines the energy of his youth with solid political experience. I am sure that the voters of Lotbinière-Frontenac will find in him a candidate who has all that is necessary to represent them in the National Assembly, which already represents and serves the citizens of the municipality of which he is mayor " , added the leader of the Parti Québécois. 
"From now on, it's no longer to the Donors of the Liberal Party to decide. It is to the citizens of Lotbinière-Frontenac, "concluded Pauline Marois.
