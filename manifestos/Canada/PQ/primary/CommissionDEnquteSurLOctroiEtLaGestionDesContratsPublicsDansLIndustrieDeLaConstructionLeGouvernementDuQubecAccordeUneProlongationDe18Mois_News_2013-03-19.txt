Quebec- The Minister of Justice and Attorney General of Quebec, Mr. Bertrand St-Arnaud, announces that the Quebec government responds to the 18-month extension application formulated on March 7 by the Chairperson of the Inquiry Commission on The granting and management of public contracts in the construction industry, France Charbonneau J.. 
"We have long called for this commission of inquiry. She is now in place, she has all our confidence and it seems essential to us that she goes deep down. An extension of 18 months is very long, but it is the duration that the France Charbonneau president considers it necessary to completely fulfill its mandate, "said Bertrand St-Arnaud. 
The Government of Quebec also requires the Commission to file a progress report by 31 January 2014. 
"We want to continue to act in terms of integrity, as soon as possible. Not in 2015 or 2016. That's why, in responsible government, we ask the Commission to produce, by January 31, 2014, a progress report on the progress of its work, the resulting observations, Possible solution tracks, any recommendation it deems appropriate and the work it still plans to make the production of its final report at the latest on April 19, 2015, "concluded the Minister of Justice and Prosecutor general.
