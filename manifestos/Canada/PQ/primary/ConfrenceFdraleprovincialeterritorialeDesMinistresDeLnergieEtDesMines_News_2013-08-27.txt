"Quebec promotes discussions on a bilateral basis with other governments that share a common interest on subjects specific to the areas of energy and mines, with respect for constitutional competencies," said Minister Martine Ouellet. 
Energy efficiency and natural resource development have been discussed at the Yellowknife Conference. Quebec took the opportunity to discuss various initiatives and measures implemented in these areas. 
The Minister of Natural Resources said his department will not participate in the work of the working group on compulsory reporting standards for extractive industries, since it is an issue that is the exclusive jurisdiction of Quebec. In this regard, it emphasized that provisions aimed at increasing transparency in the mining sector are provided for in Bill 43 amending the Mining Act that has been tabled this spring.
