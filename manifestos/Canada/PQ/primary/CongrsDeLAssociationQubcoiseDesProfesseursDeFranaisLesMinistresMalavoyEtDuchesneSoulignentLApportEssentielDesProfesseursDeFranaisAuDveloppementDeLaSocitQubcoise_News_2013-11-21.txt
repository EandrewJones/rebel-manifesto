Quebec- The Minister of Education, Recreation and Sport, Mrs Marie Malavoy, and the Minister of Higher Education, Research, Science and Technology, Mr. Pierre Duchesne, took advantage of the symposium of The Quebec association of French teachers, which takes place from 20 to 22 November, to emphasize the essential contribution of French teachers to the development of Quebec society and the influence of the French language. 
"Mastery of French is an important factor of success and school perseverance. I know that French teachers have considerable efforts to bring the largest number of young people to the success and learning of Quebec culture. These are leading partners in the educational success of young Quebeckers, "said Malavoy Minister. 
The Minister Duchesne has for his part to salute the essential contribution of French teachers to the development of Francophone research. Support for research in French is also one of the actions of actions of the national research and innovation policy it has unveiled last October. 
"Quebec is today a major player in the development and dissemination of knowledge in French. It is in a good time thanks to them if Quebec occupies this enviable place within the Francophonie. They know how to convey the taste of language and culture to their students and I want to salute the work they make in that direction, "said Minister Duchesne. 
The minister took the opportunity to announce that it would soon unveiled the measures it intends to implement to strengthen French learning. "By choosing as the theme of their Congress untie the language, French teachers reveal the mind that animates them, that of freedom, commitment and access to knowledge. There is no doubt that the pride of living in French is learned, lives and develops at school, "concluded the minister.
