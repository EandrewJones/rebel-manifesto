Halifax- At the end of its first participation in the Council of the Federation, the Prime Minister of Quebec, Pauline Marois, said pleased to have had the opportunity to meet his counterparts from other provinces to discuss economy . 
"It was a good work meeting that allowed us to get an up-to-date portrait of the international context and its potential impacts on our respective economies. We had frank exchanges on the additional challenges posed by the global economic downturn for the efforts we make to restore our public finances and to ensure the growth of our economies, "said Pauline Marois.
