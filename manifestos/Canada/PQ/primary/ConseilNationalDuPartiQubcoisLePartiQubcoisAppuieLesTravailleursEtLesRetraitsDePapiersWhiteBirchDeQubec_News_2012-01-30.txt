Montréal-joined in Montreal, the 500 citizen party activists present at their National Council adopted a proposal supporting the approaches of workers aimed at ensuring the recovery of the Québec White Birch papers. The resolution proposed by the districts of Taschereau and Jean-Lesage reads as follows: 
It is proposed that the National Council request that the parliamentary wing requires the Government of Energetic Gestures to ensure the revival of the White Birch Package of Quebec and to protect the Pension Fund of the Workers and Persons Retired from the Factory and That the Parti Québécois announces its support for the workers' cooperative project that could be implemented by White Birch papers to save their factory and employment. 
"It's a clear message that the Parti Québécois. We want the government to act energetically to preserve the jobs and the Pension Fund of the White Birch Papers in Quebec. Thousands of workers have devoted their lives to make this plant a profitable and prosperous business for the region, "said the member for Taschereau, Agnès Maltais. 
"The owners of the plant have been enriched widely thanks to the quality of the workforce of the region and with the natural resources that belong to the Quebec community. The workers of the plant, their families and hundreds of subcontractors live in anxiety since the announcement of the closure of the plant. The government can not let go and must act now with determination, "concluded the Québécois candidate in Jean-Lesage, Pierre Châteauvert. 
In the event of the negotiations, the Parti Québécois also expressed its full support for the workers' cooperative project that could be marked.
