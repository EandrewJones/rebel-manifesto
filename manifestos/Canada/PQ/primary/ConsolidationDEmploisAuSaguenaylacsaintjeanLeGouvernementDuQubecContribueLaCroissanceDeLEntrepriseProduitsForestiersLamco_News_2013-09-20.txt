This support will help the company in the commercialization of its products and contribute to the maintenance or creation of a total of 40 jobs in the Saguenay-Lac-Saint-Jean region. 
"The creation and maintenance of jobs are an obsession for my Government, as evidenced by our support for Lamco Forest Products, a strategic company for the economic growth of the Saguenay-Lac-Saint-Jean region. We work with all Quebec's economic players to support the growth of all our regions. We are therefore present to support companies and structuring projects that create wealth for all Quebeckers, "said Pauline Marois. 
Founded in 2000, Lamco Forest Products is a manufacturing company specializing in the manufacture of structural and appearance category engineering wood. The company has developed a technology to produce lamellar pieces from northern softwood, which gives added value to its engineering products.
