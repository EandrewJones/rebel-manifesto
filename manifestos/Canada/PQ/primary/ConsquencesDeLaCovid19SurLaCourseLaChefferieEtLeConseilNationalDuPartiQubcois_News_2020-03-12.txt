Montreal, on March 12, 2020 - The Parti Québécois has made the decision to make significant changes to its management run to comply with the guidelines of the Public Health Branch on coronavirus (COVID-19). 
The two debates, which had to stand in Montreal and Drummondville, will take place without a public and will be webcast on social media and the Quebec party website. The details of these debates will be communicated later. 
The vote to elect the next or the next leader is maintained at the same dates and according to the same modalities, from 15 to 19 June, by the Internet or by telephone. With regard to the other events of the race and the territorial activities of the Parti Québécois, decisions will be taken later. 
Moreover, until further notice, the National Council to be held on April 18 in Montreal is canceled.
