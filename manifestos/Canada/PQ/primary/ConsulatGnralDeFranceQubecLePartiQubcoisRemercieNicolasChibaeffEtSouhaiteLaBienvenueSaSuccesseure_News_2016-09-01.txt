Quebec- The member for Jonquière and Leader of the Official Opposition, Sylvain Gaudreault, as well as the member for Hochelaga-Maisonneuve and spokesman for the Quebecois in international relations, Carole Poirier, emphasized the handover of powers who has took place at the Consulate General of France in Quebec City. 
"It is with recognition that we salute the outgoing Consul General, Mr. Nicolas Chibaeff. Since its entry into office, in October 2012, its contribution to the harmony and deepening of the relations between France and Quebec has been considerable, in addition to being marked by a sincere attachment to Quebec, to its people and In his culture, "said Sylvain Gaudreault. 
"Mr. Chibaeff greatly facilitated the official visits that Ms. Pauline Marois, as Prime Minister of Quebec, made in France during his mandate. It is also he who worked for the smooth running of the passage to Quebec of Mr. Jean-Marc Ayrault, as part of the alternating meeting of French and Quebec prime ministers, in 2013. On behalf of my colleagues members of the Parti Québécois, I thank him for his great professionalism, his generosity and courtesy every moment, "added Carole Poirier. 
The Leader of the Official Opposition also wanted to Ms. Laurence Haguenauer, who took over by Mr. Chibaeff at the head of the Consulate General of France in Quebec City. "I welcome the arrival of Mrs Haguenauer and ensures the full collaboration of the members of the official opposition. Knowing that she already knows Quebec, I trust she will effectively help defend and promote the direct and privileged relationship between France and Quebec for a long time, "he concluded.
