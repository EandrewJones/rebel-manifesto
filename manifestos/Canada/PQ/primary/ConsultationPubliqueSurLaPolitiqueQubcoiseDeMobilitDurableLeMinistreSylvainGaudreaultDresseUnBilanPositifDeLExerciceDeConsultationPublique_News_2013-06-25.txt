The Minister also thanked the 200 representatives of organizations that participated in consultation meetings on the same subject in Longueuil and Quebec at the end of May. 
"The number of submissions and forms received as well as the quality of the exchanges that took place during the four days of consultation testify to the interest aroused by the issues related to transport. The comments collected will influence the choice of the actions we will undertake by 2020 in order to improve the supply of public transport service throughout Quebec, to achieve our goals of sustainable mobility and energy independence, and to contribute significantly to the targets of reducing greenhouse gas emissions, "said Minister Gaudreault.
