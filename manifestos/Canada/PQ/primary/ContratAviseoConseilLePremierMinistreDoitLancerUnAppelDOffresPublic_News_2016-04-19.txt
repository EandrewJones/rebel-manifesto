Québec- As a company led by relatives of the Liberal Party, Aviseo Council, received a contract of nearly $ 100,000 from the Ministry of Finance, without a call for tenders, to evaluate the impact of the Energy Energy project, the Chief From the official opposition, Pierre Karl Péladeau, demanded the Prime Minister that he launched a public call for tenders for this contract. 
"True to his habits, the Liberal government has a relative vision of independence. The new Aviseo Consulting firm is actually led by Pierre Lefebvre who, under Monique Jerome-Forget, led the Liberal government to the fiasco of university megahospitals in PPP. Worse still, the second shareholder of Aviseo Council is Jean-Pierre Lessard, former colleague of the current Cabinet director of the Minister of Finance, Guillaume Caudron. Can the Prime Minister explain to us how the Minister of Finance has been able to entrust, without a call for tenders, a contract of nearly $ 100,000 to one of his cabinet director, which is also recognized for overvalued Grossely the economic benefits of the North Plan? Asked Pierre Karl Péladeau. 
Indeed, in February 2012, Jean-Pierre Lessard and Guillaume Caudron co-signed and published in the pages of several newspapers an outrageously complacent assessment of the North Plan. The text is falling from 148 billion. 
"The Minister of Finance himself announced the grant of the contract without call for tenders to Aviseo Council, while the experts of the Ministry of Finance also produce such analyzes. We ask the Prime Minister to make public the evaluations and analyzes made by the Ministry of Finance on the economic benefits of the Energy Eastern project. Moreover, in the light of the facts revealed today, does Philippe Couillard undertake to launch a public call for tenders for the granting of this contract? ", Concluded the leader of the Parti Québécois.
