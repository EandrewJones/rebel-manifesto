Quebec- The member of the Islands-de-la-Madeleine, Ms. Jeannine Richard, is pleased to announce, on behalf of the Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Agriculture, Fisheries and Food, Mr. François Gendron, The allocation of $ 30,000 financial assistance to the solidarity cooperative in the animal production of the Madeleine Islands. This financial assistance aims to support the cooperative in the pursuit of its activities, that it has been leading almost 15 years. 
"It is important for me to support Quebec companies, such as the solidarity cooperative in animal production of the Madeleine Islands, form the core of the bioalimentary industry in their region," said the minister. 
"I am pleased today to announce financial support to a company from home. This cooperative is at the heart of the animal production of the Îles-de-la-Madeleine, since fifty different products are treated and manufactured, "said Richard. 
The solidarity cooperative in animal production of the Madeleine Islands is the only A-1 slaughterhouse of all the Madelinot archipelago, making it an essential link in the animal sector in the region. Indeed, nearly 40% of agricultural enterprises in this territory use the slaughter services offered by the cooperative to carry out the marketing of their products. 
"By its activities in the field of transformation, but also because it proposes a mode of sustainable transformation, the solidarity cooperative in animal production of the Madeleine Islands participates in the development of a prosperous bioalimentary sector, this Which fits perfectly with the objectives of the Food Sovereignty Policy of the Government of Quebec, "added Gendron.
