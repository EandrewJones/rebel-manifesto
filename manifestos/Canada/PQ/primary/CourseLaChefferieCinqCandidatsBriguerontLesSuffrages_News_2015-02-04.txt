MONTREAL - As officially opens today the race at the Crederer of the Parti Québécois, the President of Election, Jacques Leonard, confirms that five candidates filed a bulletin in accordance with the regulations. 
"I am happy to confirm the candidates of Pierre Cere, Alexander Cloutier, Bernard Drainville, Martine Ouellet and Pierre Karl Péladeau. The complementarity of their professional luggage, and from the circles of which they come from, reflects the formidable diversity and the attraction capacity of our party. The next few months are very excited to members of the Quebecois party, "said Jacques Leonard. 
A race that everyone can participate 
On the first day of the chemsel race, the Parti Québécois also puts online a website (race.pq.org) dedicated exclusively to the candidate and the upcoming events organized by the party, and who can answer the questions of citizens. 
In addition, the members who wish will be able to challenge the candidate directly by writing their questions to the Parti Québécois through Facebook, Twitter (#vosquestions), YouTube or writing at racing@pq.org. 
"I ask Quebeckers and Quebeckers to join us, to get involved in this race, to engage their willingness to act alongside ours and take their future in hand," concluded the President of election.
