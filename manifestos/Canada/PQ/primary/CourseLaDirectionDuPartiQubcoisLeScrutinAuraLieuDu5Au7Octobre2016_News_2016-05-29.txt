Drummondville- The National Conference of the Presidents and Presidents (CNPP) of the Parti Québécois, gathered in Drummondville, adopted the rules of the race to the Bias of the Parti Québécois. It is October 7 in the evening that the results will be unveiled and that will be declared elected the next or the next leader of the Parti Québécois. 
The election presidency will be assumed by Raymond Archambault, also president of the Parti Québécois. 
"With the adoption of these rules, the race to the leadership of the Parti Québécois actually takes shape. This democratic exercise in our political training will put the members at the heart of this approach. I have no doubt that at the end of this exercise, the Parti Québécois will reinforce new ideas and renewed energy, "said Raymond Archambault, President of the Parti Québécois and election president.
