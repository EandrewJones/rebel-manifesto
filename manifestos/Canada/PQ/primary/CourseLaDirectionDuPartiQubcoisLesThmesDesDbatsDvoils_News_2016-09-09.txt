Montreal- The election president of the Quebec party leadership, Raymond Archambault has made public today the themes of the two national debates. "Our concern is that all the issues that challenge Quebeckers are addressed during these two meetings. The economy, social policies, Quebec's identity or the national issue are at the heart of the concerns of our fellow citizens, "said Archambault, president of election. 
Thus, the candidate and the candidates for the party of the Parti Québécois will discuss four major themes divided into two meetings: 
September 11, 2016: Sherbrooke 
Theme 1- The economy, public finances and sustainable development.Thema 2- The national issue and the Parti Québécois. 
September 25, 2016: Montreal 
Theme 3- Collective life, democracy and society.Thema 4- A fairer Quebec. 
The debates will be animated by the retired journalist Gilles Gougeon. 
"The Parti Québécois will allow the greatest number of Quebeckers to attend these exchanges that are captivating. Whether on the spot, in one of the places of diffusion planned in several regions or in the comfort of its home, via the webcast, all means will be at the rendezvous to ensure that a maximum can attend the Debates "concluded the election president, Raymond Archambault.
