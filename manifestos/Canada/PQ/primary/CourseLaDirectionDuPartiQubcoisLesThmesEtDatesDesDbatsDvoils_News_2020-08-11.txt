Montreal, August 11, 2020 -The President of the Parti Québécois and President of Election of the Management Run, Dieudonné Ella Oyono, made public today the themes and dates of the three debates. "The issues calling Quebeckers and Quebeckers will be at the heart of these meetings, as well as the fundamental values of the Parti Québécois, defined in our statement of principle," he said. 
Thus, candidates for the party of the Parti Québécois will discuss around the following four themes, divided into three meetings: 
August 26 
Theme 1 - Freedom and Parti Québécois 
September 8th 
Theme 2 - Equity and Justice 
September 22 
Theme 3 - Nationalism 
Theme 4 - Protection of the environment and territory 
The debates, virtual, will be animated by the former president of the Parti Québécois, Gabrielle Lemieux. 
"The Parti Québécois will make every effort to ensure that the greatest number of people can attend these exchanges between the candidates, via the webcast, in accordance with the instructions of public health," concluded Dieudonné Ella Oyono.
