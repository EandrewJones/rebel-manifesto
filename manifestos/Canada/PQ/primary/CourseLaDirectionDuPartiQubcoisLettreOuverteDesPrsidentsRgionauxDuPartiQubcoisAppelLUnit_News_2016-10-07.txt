The closing of the vote aimed at designating the next or the next leader of the Parti Québécois party, the 17 regional presidents of the Parti Québécois sign a letter testifying to their commitment to pursue their activism in the Parti Québécois and to rally to the choice of members. 
"It is why even that the result of the race to the chemsefferie, we, presidents and regional presidents of the Parti Québécois, wish to reaffirm very clearly that we will immediately agree with our new leader or to our new Chefe. It is with him, with her, that we will prepare the election of 2018 and that we will draw a new way to make Quebec a country "write the regional presidents. 
This management race has been time to reaffirm the foundations of the Parti Québécois and to recall the essence of its foundation: "The Parti Québécois is a coalition. It is not necessary to always think the same on all the subjects to be able to work together, hand in hand - as long as it is moved by the goal of realizing the independence of Quebec. »
