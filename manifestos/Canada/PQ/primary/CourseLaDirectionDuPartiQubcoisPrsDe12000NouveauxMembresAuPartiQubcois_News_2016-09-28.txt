Montreal- The President of the Parti Québécois, Raymond Archambault released today the evolution of memberships to the Parti Québécois. 
Nearly 12,000 people have joined the party for the first time in their lives since May 2, 2016. "This race will have allowed people from all walks of life to join us and participate in our political project. These new members will therefore be able to vote in the election of the next or the next leader of the Québécois party from 5 to 7 October, "Archambault said. 
The electoral list has 73,236 members-electors. "It's more than in the previous race race and that's another sign of the citality of the Parti Québécois. It is with enthusiasm that we welcome them in our movement, "added Mr. Archambault. 
The Parti Québécois has undertaken the shipment to voters in the voting package containing all the information to participate in the vote from October 5th to 7th.
