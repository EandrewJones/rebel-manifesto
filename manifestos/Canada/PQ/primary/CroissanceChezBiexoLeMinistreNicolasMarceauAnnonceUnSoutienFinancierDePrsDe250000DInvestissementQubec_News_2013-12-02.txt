Saint-Roch-de-L'achigan- Minister of Finance and Economy, Mr. Nicolas Marceau, today announced that Investpazing Québec has granted a $ 242,500 loan to the companyBexopour a project of expansion of more than $ 750,000. Biexo manufactures tanks, retention ponds, established and other parts of steels for industrial customers. 
"Identify a need, create an innovative product and constantly seek to improve its ways of doing things, here is the basis of entrepreneurship, Minister Nicolas Marceau indicated. When Biexo has decided to have new facilities to improve productivity, the government responded to the call by investment funding Québec. These new facilities will enable the company to increase competitiveness and create wealth in the region, with six new jobs. " 
Biexo is currently experiencing strong growth in sales, and its expensive premises limit its growth and production capacity. The company therefore chose to build a new building near its old premises and acquire new equipment. 
"This support has allowed us to complete the financial assembly for the construction of our new building, which will allow us to better meet the needs of our customers," says Guy Limoges, Co-owner of Biexo. 
Investissement Québec's mission is to promote investment growth in Quebec, thus contributing to economic development and job creation, in all regions. The company puts companies with a full range of financial solutions, including loans, loan guarantees and equity capital investment, in order to support them at all stages of their development. In addition, it is responsible for the administration of tax measures and prospecting investments abroad.
