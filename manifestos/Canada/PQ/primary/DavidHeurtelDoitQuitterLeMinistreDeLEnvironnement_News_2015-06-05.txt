Against climate change, David Heurtel, since he confessed, this morning, having missed his ministerial responsibility, as revealed by an internal investigation report made public yesterday.

"The investigation report is clear and serious. On many occasions, the minister should have active and protect the nursery of Belugas, a species threatened with disappearance.
