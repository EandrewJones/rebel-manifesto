Montreal, on January 29, 2019 - Factory following the remarks in the media concerning the employment link between the Parti Québécois and his managing director, Alain Lupien, the latter and the President of the Party, Gabrielle Lemieux, wanted to clarify the situation. : 
"In order to implement the new orientations that are being defined by the National Executive Board and considering the mandates made by Mr. Lupien, we made the joint decision to conclude the employment bond between the Parti Québécois and Mr. Lupien . The latter will remain in office until the end of February to follow up on current records and prepare the transition. The Québécois Party wishes to thank and congratulate Alain Lupien for its important contribution to the development and professionalization of political training, "said Quebec's president, Gabrielle Lemieux. 
"It has been a privilege for me to put my skills and expertise at the service of the Quebec party and the cause of Quebec's independence. I arrive now when passing the torch and I hear it with the greatest collaboration and opening possible in order to succeed in a harmonious transition in which I will be personally involved, "concluded the Director General of the Parti Québécois, Alain Lupien. 
No further comments will be done on this subject.
