Quebec- The Minister Delegate to Industrial Policy and the Quebec Economic Development Bank, Ms. élaine Zakaïb, is pleased with the meeting she held with the Minister of the Minister of Economy and Finance, responsible for The social and solidarity economy and consumption in France, Mr. Benoît Hamon. 
At the end of this interview, the ministers have made it clear that Quebec and France have developed, for more than fifty years, of direct and privileged cooperation links in many areas of activity. They confirmed their intention to continue trade and collaborations in terms of social economy, respectful of the legislation of the two countries. 
"Considering that the development of the social and solidarity economy is essential, we are very pleased with the discussions engaged with France. Even more, Quebec and French governments are convinced that the social and solidarity economy is a way to explore in efficient, fair and sustainable economic development. This meeting is therefore a tangible manifestation of our interest in optimizing and enriching our policies through intergovernmental cooperation, "said Minister Sulaian Zakaub 
Ministers wanted to mark the beginning of a process to encourage and promote trade in social and solidarity economy, in order to: 
"Quebec is a leader in cooperative development. Our cooperatives are bold, creative and prosperous; They represent an important economic force throughout the territory.
