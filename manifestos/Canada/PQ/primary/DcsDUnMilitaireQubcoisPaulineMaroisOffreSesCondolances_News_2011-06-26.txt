Montreal -The Leader of the Official Opposition and MP of Charlevoix, Pauline Marois, and all the members of the Parti Québécois have to offer their most sincere condolences to the family and relatives of Francis Roy, Corporal-Chief, who has At first, an infantry soldier of the Royal 22nd Regiment before joining the Special Operations Regiment of Canada in 2009. 
"On behalf of the parliamentary wing of the Parti Québécois and in my name, I want to offer my most sincere condolences to the family and relatives of Mr. Roy. I wish them to find the peace, strength and comfort needed to cross this terrible test, "said the official opposition. 
"My thoughts also accompany Quebec soldiers who have lost arms companions in recent years. I thank them for the will they show to defend their ideals in Afghanistan, "concluded Pauline Marois.
