The Minister of Culture and Communications, Mr. Maka Kotto, meant his deep regret before Georges Hamel's departure, this great singer whom the Country Gentleman was called. "His texts, who tell the daily lives with emotion, accompanied thousands of admirers of Quebec country music. Georges Hamel has devoted his life to the song and will remain an emblem of country music in the hearts of Quebeckers and Quebeckers, "said Kotto. 
Born on January 20, 1948 in Sainte-Françoise-de-Lotbinière, Georges Hamel started his singer career at the end of his twenties. In 40 years of business, he composed more than 200 songs and recorded 44 albums. He was rewarded with the Adisq, which awarded him four Felix prizes. He also received the medal of the National Assembly in 2009, in recognition of his entire career. He died Wednesday in Drummondville, in the region who saw the singer take off and where his cultural impact is considerable. 
Georges Hamel had launched his last album lately, on February 11th. Title a flower for you, this album offers times of its greatest successes interpreted in duet with other singers. The disease prevented the singer from promoting it.
