Montreal -Now the name of the parliamentary wing of the Parti Québécois, the official Leader of the official and deputy of Charlevoix, Pauline Marois, wishes to express his sadness following the death of Mr. Jack Layton. 
"This morning, it was a shock for me to learn the departure of a man so friendly, so passionate about political action. He had retired for some time in the hope of coming back to fulfill his work as head of the official opposition in Ottawa, "commented Pauline Marois. 
"Public life is demanding. Until the end, we will have seen Jack Layton Combatative, always accompanied by his wife Olivia Chow. Together, they offered to all those who make the policy a brilliant example of resilience and engagement. Similarly, Mr. Layton's smile and determination represent a message of hope with regard to all people who fight cancer and their loved ones, "added the official opposition. 
"Beyond political divergences, it must be recognized that Mr. Layton had succeeded in receiving the hearts of Quebec citizens. He had managed to rebuild a political party who lived great difficulties when he took over. I find it very sad to see that he has not been able to enjoy his victory in the last election. In his life, by his work, he put forward a way of doing positive, inspiring politics, close to people. Beyond his fight against the disease, that's what the story will retain from him, "said Pauline Marois. 
"On behalf of my parliamentary team, I want to express my deepest condolences to his wife Olivia Chow, his children, his loved ones and friends, and all the elected and collaborators of the New Democratic Party," concluded the leader of the Parti Quebecois .
