Quebec- The Minister of Labor, Employment and Social Solidarity, Minister responsible for the Status of Women and Minister responsible for the Regions of the Capitale-National and Chaudière-Appalaches, Ms. Agnès Maltese, learned with sadness Mr. Marc Simoneau. 
"Deeply engaged in his community, Mr. Simoneau has devoted his life to the service of the people of Quebec. Whether as a sports commentator or municipal councilor, we will remember an authentic and passionate man. I greet the memory of a person who has contributed in a personal way to the democratic life of Quebec and who has enthusiastically share his love of sport. It will remain a model for sports journalism in Quebec. 
On behalf of the Quebec government, Minister Maltese offers his deepest condolences to his family and loved ones.
