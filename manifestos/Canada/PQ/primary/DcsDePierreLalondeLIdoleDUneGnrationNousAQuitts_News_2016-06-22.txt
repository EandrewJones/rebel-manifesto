Quebec- The member for Jonquière and Leader of the Official Opposition, Sylvain Gaudreault, as well as the member for Joliette and the Quebec Party Word in Culture and Communications, Véronique Hivon, greet the work and career of M Pierre Lalonde, whose death was announced today. 
"First of all, I want, on behalf of the members of the official opposition, to offer my deepest condolences to the family, relatives and many admirers of Mr. Pierre Lalonde. We all keep an excellent memory of the one who was one of the figures of bow of the Quebec cultural scene of the 1960s and 1970s, "said Sylvain Gaudreault. 
"Pierre Lalonde has had a long and prolific career, both on stage and the small screen. Rightly, it is said of him that he was "the idol of a generation"; He will forever embody the spirit of the Yé-Yé era, the emancipation of youth. His unique gestures and laughter, who resonated up to the United States, are certainly part of our cultural heritage, "Hivon honored. 
"The songs that Pierre Lalonde interpreted are timeless. The inheritance he leaves behind is so doom to live for a long time. I invite all Quebeckers and all Quebeckers to revisit his repertoire; To pay tribute to man, certainly, but also because he illuminates and rejoices the hearts, inevitably, "concluded the head of the official opposition.
