Quebec- The member for Bourget and the official opposition spokeswoman in the field of culture and communications, Maka Kotto, wanted to pay tribute to the deceased Friday's Humorist Serge in Longueuil. 
"Serge Grenier and his three companions, members of the famous group of humorists Cynics, will have marked Quebec humor, or the Quebec society of the 1960s then in full peace revolution. By jostling taboos with a humor of the most bite, Serge Grenier will have left his mark. With Yvon Deschamps, among others, the cynics will draw the path of several generations of humorists who will not hesitate to soak their feather in the sometimes acid ink of social criticism, "said Maka Kotto. 
"Quebec also loses an author and a talented screenwriter who contributed to significant events of our humor and television. He participated in bye bye, at galas just to laugh, of which he was the first facilitator in 1983, as well as several humorous emissions and many finely written chronicles, favoring the intelligence and the quality of The French language ", continued the member for Bourget. 
Maka Kotto, on behalf of the official opposition leader, Pauline Marois, and his colleagues from the parliamentary wing of the Parti Québécois, offers the family and relatives of Serge Grenier his most sincere condolences.
