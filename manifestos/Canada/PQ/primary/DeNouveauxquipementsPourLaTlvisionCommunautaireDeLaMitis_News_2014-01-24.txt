Mont-Joli- On behalf of the Minister of Culture and Communications, Maka Kotto, Minister Delegate to Tourism, Minister Responsible for the Bas-Saint-Laurent Region and Map of Matane-Matapédia, Pascal Bérubé, Announces the Attribution of Financial assistance of $ 122,000 on MITIS Community television (TVM), for the purchase of specialized equipment and equipment necessary for HD turn. 
"Community media play a vital role in developing the sense of belonging to an environment. They are anchored in their community and position themselves in the foreground in the evolution of the regions of Quebec. They offer a space of exchange close to the reality of people by dealing with the concerns of their living environment, "said Minister Kotto. 
The acquisition of computer and technical equipment that is better adapted to technological realities will improve the productions disseminated by this station. The digital turn is essential for this community television to remain competitive. The realization of this project is therefore part of a high-definition catchment and dissemination initiative in the territory of the MRC of Mitis. 
"TVM has been offering television content for thirty-five years that speak for people from cities and served villages. It plays a strategic role in the socio-economic development of the territory. Community television plays an indispensable role in the Quebec media panorama by completing the information broadcast by major television networks, "said Minister Pascal Bérubé. 
The total amount of the project, estimated at $ 154,540, is distributed as follows: $ 122,000 is paid by the Ministry of Culture and Communications (MCC) under the Capital Assistance Program, $ 30,300 comes from the MRC Mitis and $ 2,240 are provided by TVM. Remember that this community media is supported by the MCC operation.
