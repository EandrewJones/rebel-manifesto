Quebec- The Minister of Labor, Employment and Social Solidarity and Minister responsible for the Status of Women, Ms. Agnès Maltais, welcomes recent agreements on important labor relations issues and involving several hundred women workers and workers. 
The Minister emphasizes the agreement reached in recent days between the Quebec Casinos Society and 2000 of its employees. These union members, who work in the casinos of Montreal, Charlevoix and Lac-Leamy in Outaouais, have just ratified the agreement with their employer in the presence of conciliators of the Ministry of Labor. 
"As a holiday approach, this regulation is good news for the regions concerned for whom tourism is a significant economic contribution," said the Minister of Labor. 
The minister also recalls the renewal of collective agreements of the 950 syndicates of the Bécancour alumiller where the parties accepted the report of the conciliator of the Ministry of Labor. "The population of a whole region has followed with interest the course of these negotiations. The conciliation allowed the parties to come to a settlement, thus avoiding the inconveniences, at the economic level, that could have had a labor conflict, "concluded Minister Maltese.
