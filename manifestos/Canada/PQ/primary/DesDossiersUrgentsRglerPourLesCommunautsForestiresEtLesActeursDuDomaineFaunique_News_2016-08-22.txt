Quebec, August 22, 2016 -Bey it offers its full collaboration to the new Minister of Forests, Wildlife and Parks, Luc Blanchette, the member for Bonaventure and Spokesman for the Parti Quebecois in these materials, Sylvain Roy, Recalls that urgent files must be settled, both in the forest sector and in the wildlife sector. 
Softwood Lumber "Quebec Forest Businesses live in uncertainty since the Softwood Lumber Agreement has expired. The minister must ensure that future trade negotiations will not hurt Quebec companies. In addition, it is urgent that our new forest regime is recognized by Canada and the United States, "the spokesper said. 
Spruce buds in private forest "Moreover, while the epidemic progresses at a blazing speed, there is still no help plan for small forest producers who are struggling with budworms. spruce. The new minister must propose three things: a plan, spouse with the federal government, for the watering of young stands, a marketing strategy of the wood cut prematurely, and another for reforestation. And you have to do quickly; As long as the government does not act, many owners will continue to sacrifice an important part of their forest capital, "said Sylvain Roy. 
Increase in the cost of wildlife permits for volunteer organizations "Finally, Luc Blanchet must abolish the bad idea of his predecessor, Laurent Lessard, who has set up a volunteer tax. The rise in permits, up to $ 2477, is simply unacceptable; These volunteers conduct wildlife management in addition to the work of the ministry! ", Concluded the hon. Member for Bonaventure.
