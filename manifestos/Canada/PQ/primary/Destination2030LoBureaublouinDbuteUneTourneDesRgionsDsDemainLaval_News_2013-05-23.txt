Québec- The member for Laval-des-Rapides and Parliamentary Assistant to the Quebec Prime Minister for Youth Records, Leo Bureau-Blouin, undertakes tomorrow a tour of the regions of Quebec to meet Quebec youth. He will be accompanied by deputies of Saint-Jean, Dave Turcotte, and Terrebonne, Mathieu Traversy.
