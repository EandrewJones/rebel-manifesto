Montréal - While many Quebeckers are charged with unreasonable timeliness of requests for employment insurance benefits, the Parti Québécois recalls that it is important for Québec to have its own employment insurance program. To offer a regime better adapted to the Quebec reality. 
For the member for Saint John and official opposition spokesperson for employment and social solidarity, Dave Turcotte, recent federal setbacks tell us once again that the Government of Canada is not in able to offer social programs. 
"It is unacceptable that people with unemployment situations have to wait 45 days to get a first employment insurance check while the usual time is two weeks. This is one more reason for Quebec to have its own employment insurance plan. It is essential that employment insurance becomes again what it would still have to be, or a real assurance that helps workers when they need it, "said Dave Turcotte. 
In November 2011, the Leader of the Official Opposition, Pauline Marois invited the representatives of all political parties, both in Quebec and Ottawa, to unite around this winning idea for Quebec and to claim a same voice The repatriation of the employment insurance plan. 
"While it was lost in Quebec 51,000 jobs in 2011 and that the unemployment rate reached 8.7% last December, it is necessary to develop our own regime that will become a solution to the shortage of man. specialized work. The Parti Québécois wishes a plan that would allow employers and workers to come out winners for the benefit of all Quebec society, "concluded the member for Marie-Victorin and spokesperson for Canadian Intergovernmental Affairs, Bernard Drainville.
