Québec- The Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Occupation of the Territory and Minister of Transport, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, and the Minister of International Relations, Francophonie and Foreign Trade and Minister responsible for the Montreal Region, Mr. Jean-François Lisé consider that Mr. Applebaum took the necessary decision. 
"For my part, I pledge to work with the representatives of the City of Montreal so that the latter finds a stability and I will not hesitate, if necessary, to accompany Montreal to ensure its proper functioning", said Minister Gaudreault.  On November 3rd, the citizens of Montreal will democratically elect a mayor or mayor as well as the members who will form the municipal council. "For the moment, I invite the elected representatives of the City of Montreal to quickly designate the person who will ensure the interim so that the management of the Quebec metropolis can resume its normal course," added the minister. 
Remember that the councilors must, within 30 days of the opinion of the vacancy of the Mayor's position, elect one of them in this position. The election is on secret ballot at a council session. The nomination and voting procedure will be established by the clerk.
