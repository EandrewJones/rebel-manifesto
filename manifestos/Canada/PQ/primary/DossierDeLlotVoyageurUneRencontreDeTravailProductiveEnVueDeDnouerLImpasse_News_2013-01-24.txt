Quebec- The member for Sainte-Marie-Saint-Jacques, Mr. Daniel Breton, participated yesterday at a meeting between the spokespersons of the cooperative useful, accompanied by different groups and representatives gathered around the Solidarity Fund, as well as that the President of the Treasury Board, Mr. Stéphane Bédard. The director of the cabinet of the minister responsible for the metropolis, Mr. Jean-François Lisée, was also present. 
During this meeting, participants wanted to continue the work so that the traveler island file has a positive outcome as soon as possible. Just as much, it was agreed that efforts should also be achieved to improve student housing supply in the Latin Quarter and the revitalization of this important area of Montreal. 
"Having worked on this file since the beginning, I am very satisfied with the listening and opening of Minister Bédard against the lack of affordable student housing that tries to untie the dynamic team of useful. In addition, we must find together the best economic and social solution for the traveler island. The City of Montreal has a central role in this case. It will have to become involved in converting this sad legacy of the Liberal government to the subject of pride worthy of a metropolis in full renaissance, "Breton said.
