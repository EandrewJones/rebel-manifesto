Quebec - the member for Matapédia and the official opposition on social services, Danielle Doyer, has arrested this morning the Minister of Health and Social Services, Yves Bolduc, to set up a commission The principal objective would be to counter family dramas in Quebec. 
The Parti Québécois believes that the holding of such a Commission would make it possible to consult various stakeholders concerned by this question, to evaluate the continuum of prevention services available at this time, particularly in mental health, and to identify consensus that will give Place in setting up different measures to promote adequate support for families and to put forward front-line services in prevention to significantly reduce these family dramas. 
"It is necessary to be devastated by family dramas that shake Quebec right now. The distress of many families is palpable and unfortunately, real human dramas occur, dramas that can not let Quebeckers indifferent, "said the member during the question period. "Whether it's that mother who joined the Prairie River with his two three-month and five-year-old children in each arm; Whether they are famous children burned in a father's car; Whether it's a father who entails his two children in death as a result of marital problems, the government must recognize today that the multiplication of these dramas, highly highly mediated in recent months, requires That a debate is held, one ceases to ignore them and that we are concerned about finding adequate solutions, "she continued. 
"Minister Bolduc has expressed an encouraging opening at our request, but the government's leader seemed more reluctant to hold a parliamentary committee on this subject. I ask the Minister of Health to press his leader and the Prime Minister to entrust this mandate without delay for parliamentarians so that solutions are found. He is our duty of elected officials to act quickly, "continued Danielle Doyer. 
"The Parti Québécois believes that even if these issues are heavy, sensitive and delicate, it is necessary now that the elected officials are intended in order to improve the government actions that act mainly in terms of prevention", Has she concluded.
