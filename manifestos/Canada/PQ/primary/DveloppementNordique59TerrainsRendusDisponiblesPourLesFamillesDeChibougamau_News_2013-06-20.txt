Chapais- The Minister of Natural Resources and Responsible for Nordic Development, Mrs. Martine Ouellet, is pleased to announce the signing of an agreement between the City of Chibougamau and the Ministry of Natural Resources in accordance with the Program on Assignment of Land. Nordic municipalities. 
"It is 59 lands that will be made available for the families of Chibougamau, of which 48 for the construction of single-family residences and 11 for the construction of residential buildings. There will also be the construction of a center of early childhood. The development of these land will meet a growing need for accommodation in the region, "said Martine Ouellet. 
"This land allocation allows the city of Chibougamau to prepare concretely to welcome newcomers, and support the economic development of the region. With the North for all, we have a nice demonstration of our government's vision that is to make Nordic development with people here, "said Luc Ferland, MP. For Ungava. 
The Program on the Assignment of State Lands for Nordic Municipalities will allow the City of Chibougamau to set up new infrastructure to welcome workers. The ceded territory covers an area of 80 698.63 m2. Nearly 92% of the ceded lands are intended for the construction of single-family and multifamily residences. On the remaining surface, ie 6,230.83 m2, an early childhood center will be implanted. 
"This agreement makes it possible to cope with a shortage of housing, while the city had virtually no vacant land in its urban core. These development projects are essential to the demographic and economic vitality of the city of Chibougamau, "concluded Martine Ouellet.
