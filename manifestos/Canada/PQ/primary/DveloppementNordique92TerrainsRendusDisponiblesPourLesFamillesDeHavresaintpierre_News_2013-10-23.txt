Havre-Saint-Pierre - Minister of Natural Resources, Mrs. Martine Ouellet, and the member for Duplessis, Ms. Lorraine Richard, are pleased to announce the signing of an agreement between the municipality of Havre-Saint-Pierre and the Ministry of Natural Resources, in accordance with the Program on Assignment of Land for Nordic Municipalities. 
"These 92 land will be made available to welcome families from Havre-Saint-Pierre. The development of these land will meet a growing need for accommodation in the region, "said Martine Ouellet. 
"The Program on the Assignment of State Domain for Nordic Municipalities will enable our municipality to set up a fleet of mobile homes with a total area of 73,186 m2. This development project is essential to the demographic and economic vitality of the municipality, "added Lorraine Richard 
"This assignment of land, the value of which is estimated at $ 222,000, will allow the municipality to carry out this project of magnification at a lower cost, and it is the citizens of Havre-Saint-Pierre who will benefit", concluded Martine Ouellet.
