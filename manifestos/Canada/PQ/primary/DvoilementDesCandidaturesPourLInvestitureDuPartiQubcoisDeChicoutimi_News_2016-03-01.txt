Saguenay - The Parti Québécois announces that the investment assembly to determine who will be the next or next to wear the colors of the Parti Québécois during the partial election to come in the riding of Chicoutimi will take place on Monday, March 14 at 19h to Hotel La Saguenne, Room Cabaret A, 250 rue des Saguenéens, Saguenay. 
The nomination period in order to be the candidate or the candidate of the Parti Québécois ended on February 28, 2016. Three applications will brigue the votes of the members of the Québécois party of Chicoutimi: 
Mario Gagnon 
Elise Gauthier 
Mireille Jean 
"The Parti Québécois is an open and democratic training. This investiture race will be an opportunity to demonstrate it by having idea debates. We will discuss what concerns the constituency voters, in the region but also how one or one member of the Quebecois party may bring our voices to the National Assembly "concluded Mr. Loïc Chartier, President of the Chicoutimi Party Party .
