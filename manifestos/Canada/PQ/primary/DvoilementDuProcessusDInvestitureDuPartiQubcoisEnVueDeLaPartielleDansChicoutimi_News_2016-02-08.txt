Saguenay-in accordance with its statutes, the Parti Québécois announces that an investiture assembly will be held in the riding of Chicoutimi in order to designate the next candidate or the next candidate of the Parti Québécois during the upcoming partial election. 
Meeting in the National Executive Council, activists and members of the Parti Québécois have responded to the demand of the local Chicoutimi Party Executive to formally switch on the process. The Assembly to determine the next candidacy will be held on March 14th. In the meantime, a nomination period is open from February 5 to 28, 2016. 
"This investiture will be an opportunity to emphasize the democratic fiber of the Parti Québécois and to have exchanges of important ideas about the issues of the constituency, the region as well as how to defend them to the National Assembly. It is with great enthusiasm that we are entering this process, "concluded Mr. Loïc Chartier, President of the Chicoutimi Party. 
The place of the Assembly will be communicated later.
