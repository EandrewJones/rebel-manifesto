Quebec- Minister responsible for the Government Administration and President of the Treasury Board, Stéphane Bédard, released the report of the Secor-KPMG firm on the management of additional expenditure on the services and construction contracts of certain organizations . 
In March 2013, the Government of Quebec mandated the Secor-KPMG firm to shed light on the cost overruns of construction contracts and professional services of three major public bodies, the Ministry of Transport, the Quebec Real Estate Corporation and The Shared Services Center of Quebec. 
The Government will pay particular attention to the findings and recommendations of this report and will follow this as soon as possible. 
"The action of our Government, including the adoption of LALOI No1 on Public Contract Integrity and the Deposit of the Infrastructure Governance Act, goes in the direction of an integral and efficient management of public funds. We intend to continue our steps in this direction to adopt the best contractual practices, "concluded the minister.
