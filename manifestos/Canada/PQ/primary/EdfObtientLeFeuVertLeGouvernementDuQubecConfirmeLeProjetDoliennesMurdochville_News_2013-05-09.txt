Quebec- The Quebec Prime Minister Pauline Marois, accompanied by the Minister Delegate to the Regions and Minister responsible for the Gaspé-Islands-de-la-Madeleine Region, Gaétan Lelièvre, today announced the relocation, on Mount Rothery In Murdochville, a 74 MW power project from EDF new energies. This project represents an investment of $ 175 million, and the fallout of $ 185,000 a year for 20 years, for MRC Côte-de-Gaspé and Haute-Gaspésie. 
"The Government of Quebec is pleased to continue its commitment to the wind industry and thus concretely support the retention of jobs that depend on this sector of activity. This announcement confirms the region of the Gaspé-Îles-de-la-Magdalene as a pillar of the wind industry in Quebec, "said the Prime Minister. 
The site will generate significant regional economic benefits with the hiring of 150 workers during construction, while providing a supply of wind turbine components in the designated area of Gaspésie and the MRC of Matane. 
"The government was committed to supporting the municipality of Murdochville, struggling with significant difficulties. The announcement of this major project is a concrete gesture made by the government, "concluded Gaétan Lelièvre.
