Quebec- The Minister of Natural Resources, Mrs. Martine Ouellet, announces the creation of green heat, financial assistance to promote the replacement of residential oil heating systems or propane by electricity-powered systems or by others renewable energies. 
"Heat green will allow a significant reduction in greenhouse gas emissions, or 329,000 tonnes of CO2 annually, the equivalent of the withdrawal of 97,000 vehicles from the road network. It is nearly 60,000 homes that will be able to benefit from financial support for up to $ 1,525, "said Martine Ouellet. 
Central heating systems and water heaters using oil and propane are eligible. For example, the owners of a single-family home converting their electric oil heating system will have a reduction in their heating bill of $ 1,250 per year, or an annual drop of 40%. Thanks to the financial assistance of $ 1,275 to heat green, the profitability of the investment is 2.5 years.
