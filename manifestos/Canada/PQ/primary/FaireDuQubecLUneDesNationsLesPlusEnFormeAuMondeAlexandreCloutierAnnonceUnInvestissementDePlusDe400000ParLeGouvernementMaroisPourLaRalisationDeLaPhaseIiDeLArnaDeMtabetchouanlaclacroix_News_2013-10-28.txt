Alma - Minister Delegate for Canadian Intergovernmental Affairs, Canadian Francophonie and Sovereign Officer Governance, MP Alexandre Cloutier, on behalf of the Minister of Education, Recreation and Sport, Ms. Marie Malavoy, announces the allocation of financial assistance of more than $ 400,000 for the second phase of the renovation of Metabetchouan-Lac-to-La-Croix arena as part of the sports facilities support program and Recreation - Phase II of the Fund for the Development of Sport and Physical Activity. 
Built in 1975, the Arena requires significant investments to meet the needs of the population and neighboring localities. In 2002, investments in the order of $ 1.2 million made it possible to complete the first phase of the renovation work. The second phase of the project, estimated at more than $ 800,000, proposes a seven-point renovation either the renovation of the roof, the replacement of the rink bands, the relocation of the surface, the upgrade of the sound, the replacement The floor coating of the players 'rooms, the replacement of the lighting of the main enclosure as well as the improvement of the arbitrators' room. In concrete terms, this project will upgrade a sporting infrastructure used at full capacity and make it available over a period of seven months annually. 
"It is with great pleasure that I announce a financial contribution from the Marois Government over $ 400,000 to complete the renovation of Metabetchouan-Lac-to-La-Croix arena as part of the phase. II of the project. The accessibility of this sporting infrastructure, highly solicited by the population, is limited. The work that will be carried out will extend the period of use of the arena and to respond adequately to the needs of users, "said Alexandre Cloutier.
