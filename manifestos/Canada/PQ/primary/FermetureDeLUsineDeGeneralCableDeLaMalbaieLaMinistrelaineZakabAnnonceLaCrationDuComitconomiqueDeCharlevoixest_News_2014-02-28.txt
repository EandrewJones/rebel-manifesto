Malbaie- at the exit of the first work meeting of the Economic Committee of Charlevoix-East, the Minister Delegate to Industrial Policy and the Quebec Economic Development Bank, Ms. Élaine Zakaïb, held to confirm, on behalf of the Prime Minister of Quebec, Ms. Pauline Marois, that the Government of Quebec intends to continue its efforts to ensure the continuity of the activities of the General Cable Factory of Malbaie, whose closure is scheduled for April 2014. 
"The announcement of the closure of this plant is of great concern to us," said Zakaïb Élain. "For our government, it is very important to support the region and workers affected. I want to participate actively in this economic committee which, I am convinced, will explore all possible avenues to keep the activities in place and preserve existing jobs. » 
Leaded by the Local Development Center (CLD) of the MRC de Charlevoix Eastern, this committee is composed of local economic partners and Minister Zakaïb. The participation of the Government of Quebec is ensured by the Ministry of Finance and the Economy, Investissement Québec and Emploi-Québec. 
"For my government, all regions of Quebec are a great wealth and are one of the priorities," said Pauline Marois. "That's why I asked the Minister Zakayb to represent me to this committee to ensure that we bring all our expertise to support the economy of the Charlevoix-East sector, as we do elsewhere when the situation The order. We act promptly to find solutions and we will make every effort to help and accompany the workers as well as the families concerned by this closure. »
