Montreal- The Minister of Transport and Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Occupation of the Territory, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, accompanied by the Mayor of Montreal, Mr. Denis Coderre, and the Mayor of Longueuil, Mrs. Caroline St-Hilaire , met with the members of the Mobility Steering Committee Montreal in order to consult the efforts to mitigate the effects on the circulation of the closure of a path on the Champlain Bridge. 
The meeting of this morning made it possible to rule on the short-term implantation of twelve traffic management and public transport management measures to ensure the fluidity of the trip between the South Shore and Montreal. 
The proposed measures will be re-evaluated on a regular basis for providing solutions to problematic locations.
