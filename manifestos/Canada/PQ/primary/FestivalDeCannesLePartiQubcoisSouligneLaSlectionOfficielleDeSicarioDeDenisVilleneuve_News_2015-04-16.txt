Québec- The member for Joliette and sentence of the official opposition in the field of culture and communications, Véronique Hivon, wishes to congratulate the filmmaker Denis Villeneuve, whose films were selected to be the official competition of the Cannes Film Festival. 
"It's a very nice recognition of the talent of Denis Villeneuve, who will run the Palme d'Or for the first time in his career. This is also filled with many international successes, including filmsIncendieSet. With the filmsicario, Denis Villeneuve turned for a second time in the United States; He now sees the excellence of his work being recognized by the most prestigious movie festival in the world. On behalf of my colleagues from the Parti Québécois, I give him my warmest congratulations, "said Véronique Hivon. 
"More than ever, the talent here is recognized on the international scene, just like the excellence of our cinema. For the second year in a row, the film of a Quebec filmmaker is officially selected in Cannes; It's a pride for all Quebeckers. Thanks to the know-how of our artists, our culture shines all over the world. We wish the best chance in Denis Villeneuve, "she concluded.
