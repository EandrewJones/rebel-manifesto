Rimouski- On behalf of the Minister of Culture and Communications, Maka Kotto, the member for Rimouski, Irvin Pelletier, Announces $ 6,633,900 in principle agreement for a cultural project that brings together 14 organizations from Rimouski-Cooperative of Solidarity Paradise. 
"This new place of creation and dissemination will allow fourteen cultural organizations of Rimouski to improve their practice conditions, while promoting a wider dissemination of their activities to the population. Sharing their resources can allow them to take a step further in the consolidation of their respective mission, "says Maka Kotto. 
This principled agreement announced today will make it possible to move forward in this cultural project to consolidate 14 organisms under one roof in multifunctional equipment. 
"In addition to creating a true synergy in the cultural environment, the realization of this project will revitalize the city center from a sustainable development perspective. It is a carrier project for Rimouski, but also for the entire region and its artistic and cultural community, "said Irvin Pelletier. 
The new cultural equipment will host the organizations that are part of the Cooperative of Solidarity Paradis and currently evolving in various places, the practice of which is in fields of activity as diversified as visual arts, media arts, emerging music, cinema The theater, the written media as well as the organization of shows and festivals. It is paraloeil, caravanserail, arms tower, the exile theater, the editions of the white shepherd, of the film 4, of the Improvisation League of Rimouski, from Festi-Jazz, the international carousel of the film From Rimouski, Roseq, concerts in the BIC Islands, the Art Corporation Art / Bas-Saint-Laurent, Arte Tracto and the Theater of 109.
