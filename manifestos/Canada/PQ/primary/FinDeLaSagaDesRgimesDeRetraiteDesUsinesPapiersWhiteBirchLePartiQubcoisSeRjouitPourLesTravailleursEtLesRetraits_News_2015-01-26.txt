Quebec- The member for Taschereau and spokesman for the official opposition responsible for the Capitale-Nationale Region, Agnès Maltais, and the member for Sanguinet and spokesperson for pension plans, Alain Therrien, rejoice The signing of the texts of the new pension plans of the White Birch papers, which puts an end to a difficult battle of more than five years for workers and retirees. 
"Important concessions have been made by workers and retirees to save their jobs and their pension plan. With the signing of these texts, pensioners of Paper White Birch will finally receive the sums due to them. These are thousands of families, today, a sigh of relief, "said Alain Therrien. 
The member for Taschereau, for his part, held tribute to the men and women who led the battle in this file. "We must emphasize the work, perseverance and efforts made by workers and retirees before this situation become intolerable. On the other hand, those who are fighting to live a dignified and well-deserved retirement must have the support of their governments; Today, the Parti Québécois is proud to have made concrete gestures to defend pensioners of Paper White Birch, "added Agnès Maltais. 
Actions of the Québécois government 
Remember that the government of the Parti Québécois intervened quickly and maintained the pressure on the company to recover the money to which workers and pensioners of Paper White Birch were entitled. In December 2012, he presented and passed the law that allows the existence of the target benefit component of the pension plans established for unionized workers. In addition, the Parti Québécois has enacted the two regulations necessary for the registration of new plans: one establishing the rules relating to the recognition of benefits for the service prior to the date of end of the schemes, and the other establishing the Rules on target benefit pension plans for post-post service.
