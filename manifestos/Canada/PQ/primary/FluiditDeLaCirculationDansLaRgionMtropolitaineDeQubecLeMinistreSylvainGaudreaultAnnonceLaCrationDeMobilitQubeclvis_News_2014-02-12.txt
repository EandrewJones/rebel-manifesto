Quebec- The Minister of Transport and Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Occupation of the Territory, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, is pleased to announce the creation of the Quebec-Lévis mobility committee. This initiative aims to improve traffic in the Quebec City's metropolitan area. 
"We are aware of the problems of traffic fluidity as much on the side of the north shore and the south shore of the national capital. This committee will better coordinate the actions of the various transport stakeholders and to work in consultation, both at the administrative and political level, in order to better plan our interventions on the departmental road network and municipalities, "said Minister Gaudreault. 
The Quebec-Lévis Mobility Committee will consist of the Ministry of Transport, the cities of Quebec and Lévis, the Capital Transportation Network, the Lévis Transportation Corporation and the Quebec Truck Association. 
"Better coordination among stakeholders will mitigate impacts on the road network, both for motorists, public transit users and truckers. The road user is at the heart of our actions, "said Sylvain Gaudreault. 
"Current issues related to the fluidity of the traffic of people and goods are asking for common solutions for everyone; This committee is the essential tool for achieving it, "concluded Sylvain Gaudreault.
