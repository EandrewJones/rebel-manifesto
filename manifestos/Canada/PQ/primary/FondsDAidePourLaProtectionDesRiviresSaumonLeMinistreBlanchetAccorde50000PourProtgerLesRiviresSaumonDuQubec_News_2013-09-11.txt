Quebec- Minister of Sustainable Development, Environment, Wildlife and Parks, Mr. Yves-François Blanchet, in collaboration with the President of the Quebec Federation for Atlantic Salmon, Mr. Jean Boudreault, grants more than 50 $ 000 for the protection of salmon rivers in five regions of Quebec. 
In total, 19 Salmon River Management Organizations will share this support under the Salmon River Protection Fund in Quebec. "This financial contribution, which demonstrates the government's effort in the protection of the wildlife resource, will promote, among other things, the purchase of equipment, the hiring of protection staff or the maintenance of jobs related to the protection. Wildlife in Quebec Salmon Rivières Managers Benefiting from the Aid Fund, "said Minister Blanchet, which thanked the Quebec Federation for Atlantic Salmon for its financial participation in the aid fund. . 
The purpose of this fund is to protect certain salmon rivers in difficulty in five regions of Quebec where salmonids are found: the Bas-Saint-Laurent, the Saguenay-Lac-Saint-Jean, the Capitale-Nationale, La Gaspésie -Is-de-la-Madeleine and the North Shore. 
In addition to being subject to certain conditions, each beneficiary body is invited to consolidate its activities to ensure its sustainability and work in partnership with local communities and salmon fishermen to find sustainable solutions to protection issues. of the resource. "The salmon rivers of Quebec bring significant economic benefits in these regions and is our duty to ensure the sustainability of this resource," says Blanchet.
