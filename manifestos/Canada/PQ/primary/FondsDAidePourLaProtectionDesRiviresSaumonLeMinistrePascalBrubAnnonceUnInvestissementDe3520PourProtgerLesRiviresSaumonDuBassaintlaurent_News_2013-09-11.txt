Quebec- On behalf of the Minister of Sustainable Development, the Environment, Wildlife and Parks, Mr. Yves-François Blanchet, Minister Delegate to Tourism and Minister responsible for the Bas-Saint-Laurent Region, Mr. Pascal Bérubé, in collaboration with the President of the Quebec Federation for Atlantic Salmon, Jean Boudreault, announces an investment of $ 3,520 for the protection of salmon rivers in the Bas-Saint-Laurent region. 
"In the region, two Salmon river management organizations will share this support under the Québec Salmon River Protection Fund. This financial contribution will promote, inter alia, the purchase of equipment, the hiring protection staff or the maintenance of wildlife protection in the Salmon River leaders of Quebec benefiting from the Fund. Help, "said Mr. Bérubé. 
"The salmon rivers of Quebec bring significant economic benefits in these regions and is our duty to ensure the sustainability of this resource," says Blanchet. 
In 2013, in total, it is more than $ 50,000 that are granted to 19 salmon river management organizations in five regions of Quebec. 
In addition to being subject to certain conditions, each organization benefiting from financial assistance from this Fund is invited to consolidate its activities to ensure the sustainability and to work in partnership with local communities and salmon fishermen in order to Finding sustainable solutions to the problems related to the protection of the resource.
