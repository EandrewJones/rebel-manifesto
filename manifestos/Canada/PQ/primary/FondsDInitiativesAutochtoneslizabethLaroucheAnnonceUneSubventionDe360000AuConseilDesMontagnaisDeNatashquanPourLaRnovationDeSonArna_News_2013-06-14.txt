Qu√©bec- The Minister Delegate to Aboriginal Affairs, Ms. Elizabeth Larouche, announced the granting of a financial assistance of $ 360,000, for the repair of the dome of the rink of the municipality of Natashquan. 
"The arena of a municipality is a place of meetings and family exchanges, where young and old are taking recreational and sports activities. It is imperative that this type of building meets safety standards. The roof rehabilitation will reopen this place to its visitors, who will again indulge in their hobbies, "said Minister Larouche. 
This project is structuring for the Innu Community of Natashquan, since it promotes the consolidation of jobs in Aboriginal people in addition to increasing the well-being of the members of the community. 
"I look forward to this announcement for the whole community of Natashquan, and especially for his youth. It is important for young people to benefit from a place to play sports and entertain, "said the member for Duplessis, Ms. Lorraine Richard. 
The financial assistance granted to the Natashquan Montagnais Council is equivalent to 50% of the project costs. It comes from the Aboriginal Initiatives Fund, Community Infrastructure Envelope, the Aboriginal Affairs Secretariat (SAA). The objective of this fund is to support projects that can have significant benefits on Quebec's Aboriginal Nations Economic, Social or Community Development. 
It should be remembered that in 2010, the Natashquan Mountain Council had acquired the building serving as shelter to renovate it in Arena. For this project, the Aboriginal Affairs Secretariat (SAA) had contributed financially to $ 569,967.
