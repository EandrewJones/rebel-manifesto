Terrebonne - Minister Delegate to Tourism, Mr. Pascal Bérubé, the President of La Cré de Lanaudière, Mr. Gaétan Morin, and the President of Tourism Lanaudière, Mrs. Evangeline Richard, are proud to draw up a very positive assessment of the Development Fund. The Lanaudière tourist offer (2008-2013). 
In total, the Fund contributed to the completion of 20 tourist projects for total investments of nearly $ 16.6 million thanks to $ 1.3 million funding. The leverage effect is all the more interesting since every dollar granted, generated $ 12 in investments in the development of regional tourism offer. "I would like to salute the work accomplished Lanaudière, the investment record is extremely positive for regional tourism development, at the four corners of the region. The tourist offer of Lanaudière diversifies, burses and metamorphoses, for the benefit of more and more visitors to discover this beautiful region, "said Minister Bérubé. 
"We are particularly proud of the structuring nature of the selected projects. Their achievement will significantly improve the tourist infrastructures of Lanaudière, "said La Cré de Lanaudière, Mr. Gaétan Morin. 
"Sustained projects will make it possible to diversify the tourist offer and to improve the quality of the infrastructures already in place to make the Lanaudière region even more competitive. Therefore, these projects will undoubtedly have an impact on the increase of tourists and tourist revenues, "concluded the President of Tourisme Lanaudière, Mrs. Evangeline Richard. 
Remember that the Lanaudière Tourism Offering Development Fund aims to focus investment in the tourism industry based on regional priorities and strengthen the role of partners in this process.
