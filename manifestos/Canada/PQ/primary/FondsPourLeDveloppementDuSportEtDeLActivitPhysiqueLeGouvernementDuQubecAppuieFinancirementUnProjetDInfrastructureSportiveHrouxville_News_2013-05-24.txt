Hérouxville- The Minister of Education, Recreation and Sport, Mrs Marie Malavoy, today announced that a financial assistance up to $ 116,447.70 is granted to the Municipality of Hérouxville for the renovation of the center of Leisure Alexandre-Massicotte. 
"Thanks to this project, the municipality may enhance its offer of sports and recreational services and allow its residents and residents to have access to safe facilities adapted to their needs. I firmly believe that it is thanks to the projects like this one that we will give to all and all the taste of moving! "Said Malavoy Minister. 
It should be noted that the planned work consists mainly of building a service building, installing game modules and renovate tennis courts as well as the pool filtration system. 
The project announced today has been selected as part of the support program for sports and recreational facilities - phase Iidufonds for the development of sport and physical activity, which provides maximum financial assistance equivalent to half the costs. eligible. 
"Each of the projects we support financially under this program is rigorously analyzed according to the eligibility criteria established in the rules and standards of the latter. The projects meet the needs of the population concerned and will allow greater accessibility to various clients, thus promoting the adoption of a physically active way of life by the greatest number of people possible, "added the minister. 
By creating the Fund for Sport Development and Physical Activity, the Government of Quebec wanted to ensure that all regions enjoy modern and safe sports and recreation facilities.
