Saint-Mathieu-du-Park- Minister of Education, Recreation and Sport, Mrs Marie Malavoy, and the hon. Member for Saint-Maurice, Mr. Luc Trudel, today announced that a financial assistance $ 699,148.22 is granted to the Energy School Board for the construction of a community and multifunctional gymnasium, which will be annexed to the School of Turtle-des-Bois. 
"On May 7, I received from the very nice visit to the National Assembly of Quebec. About 40 students from the School of Turtle-des-Bois came to meet me to talk to me about this project, which is important to them. I am pleased today to confirm that the government will support it, "said Malavoy Minister. 
This project, the costs of which are estimated at nearly $ 1.4 million, will benefit both school students, who currently do not receive premises adapted to the practice of sports activities, as well as Mathieus population. 
"I am very pleased that the Government helps to allow students of the School of Turtle-des-Bois to have access to spaces adapted to the practice of sports activities, so that they can spend their energy "Trudel said. 
The project announced today has been selected as part of the support program for sports and recreational facilities - phase Iidufonds for the development of sport and physical activity, which provides maximum financial assistance equivalent to half the costs. eligible. 
By creating the Fund for Sport Development and Physical Activity, the Government of Quebec wanted to ensure that all regions enjoy modern and safe sports and recreation facilities. For more information on this fund, visit www.mels.gouv.qc.ca/sections/fondsDevelopment
