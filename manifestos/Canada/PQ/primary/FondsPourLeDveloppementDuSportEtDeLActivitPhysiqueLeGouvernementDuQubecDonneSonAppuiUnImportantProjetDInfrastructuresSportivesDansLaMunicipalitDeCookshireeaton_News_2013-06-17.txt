Cookshire-Eaton- The member for Sherbrooke, Mr. Serge Cardin, today announced, on behalf of the Minister of Education, Recreation and Sport, Mrs Marie Malavoy, a financial assistance up to 138,606, $ 66 is awarded to the Cookshire-Eaton Municipality for the addition of a multifunctional room at Johnville Community Center. 
The work, whose total costs are valued at just over $ 280,000, consist mainly by adding a multifunctional room. The new facilities will include increasing the supply of sports center sports activities. 
"I look forward to this project, which meets an important need for Cookshire-Eaton people. We want to encourage them to move and stay in shape and this multifunctional room is undoubtedly an excellent way to get there, "said Cardin. 
This project, to which the municipality is financially participated, has been selected as part of the Support Program for Sport and Recreation - Phase II of the Fund for Sport Development and Physical Activity, which provides maximum financial assistance equivalent to half of the eligible costs. 
"Each of the projects we support financially under this program is rigorously analyzed according to the eligibility criteria established in the rules and standards of the latter. The projects meet the needs of the population concerned and will allow greater accessibility to various clients, thus promoting the adoption of a physically active way of life by the greatest number of people possible, "said Malavoy Minister. 
By creating the Fund for Sport Development and Physical Activity, the Government of Quebec wanted to ensure that all regions enjoy modern and safe sports and recreation facilities. For more information on this fund, visit www.mels.gouv.qc.ca/sections/fondsdevelopment/.
