Quebec- The member of Pointe-Aux-Trembles and official opposition spokesperson for primary and secondary education and school perseverance, Nicole Léger, asks the Minister of Education, Leisure and Sport, Yves Bolduc, ordering an audit of fees imposed on parents in primary and secondary schools in Quebec. 
This verification is necessary, in the light of the publication, today, of an article in the press showing $ 100 fee imposed by the school board of the Great Lord Seigniors for monitoring on dinner time , even if the child eats at home. 
"Minister Yves Bolduc must be concerned about the invoice sent by the schools and that there may be illegal billing, require parents to be reimbursed as soon as possible. Only an exhaustive verification will determine whether it is a current phenomenon or not in all the school boards, "said Nicole Léger. 
"There are already rules framing the expenses payable. The least thing is that the government make them respect. To do this, he must order an immediate verification, "concluded the member of the Parti Québécois.
