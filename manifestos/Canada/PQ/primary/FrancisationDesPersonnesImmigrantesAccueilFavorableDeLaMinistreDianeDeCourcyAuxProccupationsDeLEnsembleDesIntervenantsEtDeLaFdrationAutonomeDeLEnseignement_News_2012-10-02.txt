Montreal- The Minister of Immigration and Cultural Communities and Minister responsible for the Charter of the French Language, Ms. Diane de Courcy, wishes to let her positively host the concerns expressed by the Autonomous Federation of Education and the Organizations expressed at the press conference today, with regard to the francization of immigrant people and non-Francophones and their integration into Quebec society. "This concern joins my immigration mandate as well as that relating to the language. The vitality and quality of French, which includes the francization of newcomers, is clear for me a priority, "says the minister.
