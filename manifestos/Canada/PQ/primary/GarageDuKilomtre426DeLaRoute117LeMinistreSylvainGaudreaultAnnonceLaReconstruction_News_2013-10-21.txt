Val-d'Or- The Minister of Transport and Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Occupation of the Territory, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, today announced, with the Minister Delegate to Aboriginal Affairs and MP Abitibi East, Mrs. Elizabeth Larouche, and the hon. Member for Rouyn-Noranda-Témiscamingue, Mr. Gilles Chapadeau, the reconstruction of a Garage of the Ministry of Transport located at Kilometer 426 of Route 117 in the Wildlife Reserve La Vérendrye. 
The new garage will have an area of 652 m2 and will be equipped with facilities for the removal and maintenance of the removal vehicles used on the road 117 between Louvicourt and the environment of the Wildlife Reserve La Vérendrye. In addition to the vehicle delivery area, the new building will include a storage room, a mechanical room and a room for the installation of a generator. 
"The safety of road users is the priority at the Ministry of Transport. This is why everything has been implemented to ensure that the reconstruction work of the new Kilometer 426 garage is made as quickly as possible. Although efforts have been made to ensure that the contractor can continue to work through temporary facilities, they will never replace modern permanent facilities, "said Minister Gaudreault.
