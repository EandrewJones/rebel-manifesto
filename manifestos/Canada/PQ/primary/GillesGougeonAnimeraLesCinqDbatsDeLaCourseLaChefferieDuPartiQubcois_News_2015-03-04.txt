Montréal - The Parti Québécois is very pleased to announce that Gilles Gougeon has agreed to animate the debates of the race chieftaincy. 
"Mr. Gougeon is a known and appreciated figure of Quebeckers. His exemplary career as a professional information professional and his long course as an observer of the Quebec political scene are all guarantees he will be an impartial facilitator. The Parti Québécois is very happy to be able to count on someone from this stature, "said Election President Jacques Leonard. 
"The eyes of Quebeckers will be riveted on the five candidates for the coming months, and each of the debates will be a significant showcase on the evolution of the Quebec party's speech, on all the issues affecting our community. It is a pleasure for me to be able to participate in this episode of the democratic life of a political party, "added Gilles Gougeon. 
Journalist and author of an essay on the history of Quebec nationalism and three novels, Gilles Gougeon will not be at his first experience in this field, he who animated a debate of the chiefs during the 1997 Federal Election Campaign .
