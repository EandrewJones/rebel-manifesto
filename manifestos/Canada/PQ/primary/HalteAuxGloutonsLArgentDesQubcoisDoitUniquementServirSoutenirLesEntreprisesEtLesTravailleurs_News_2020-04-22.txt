Quebec, April 22, 2020 - The member for René-Lévesque and spokespersons of the Parti Québécois in finance, Martin Ouellet, request for guarantees: "The financial assistance paid by Quebec to companies must be used to support a maximum of SMEs and workers, and only to that. In other words, it must be avoided that it succeeds in the pockets of senior executives, "he said. 
IN SHORT 
"It's a movie in which Quebeckers have already played, and they hated it: see the public money designed to save businesses from here to serve, finally, to enrich a handful of leaders at their heads. As we get to rain billions, we must absolutely avoid that this scandalous scenario repeats itself, "said the member for René-Lévesque. 
The Parti Québécois, by the voice of its spokesman, therefore proposes that the assistance to companies, in particular that of the Québec Filing and Investment Fund (CDPQ), be granted only if the following two requirements are respected: 
Remember that on March 30, at the announcement of a $ 4 billion envelope to support Québec companies that are temporarily affected by the crisis, the Caisse has among other things, given the current economic context, to freeze the Salaries of all the leaders of the organization and its subsidiaries. 
"More than ever, we need to squeeze the elbows and act consistently. I also encourage a maximum of corporate leaders to deliver voluntarily at the same regime; It would be a beautiful gesture of solidarity, "concluded Martin Ouellet.
