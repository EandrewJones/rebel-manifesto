Quebec, July 6, 2014 -The MP for Chicoutimi and Leader of the Official Opposition, Stéphane Bédard, wishes to reaffirm the solidarity of the entire Parti Québécois team towards the Lake Community. Mégantic. 
"A year ago, a frightful tragedy struck the heart of lake? Mégantic. Our thoughts are now turning to families and relatives of the missing. With courage and dignity, the community gets up. Under the direction of the Mayor, Colette Roy Laroche, and the whole of the municipal council, the citizens rolled up the sleeves and worked hard to take care of their world and their city. Let's pay homage for this year of strength and bravery, "said Stéphane Bédard. 
"From the beginning, the Prime Minister Pauline Marois coordinated the Ministerial and Government team with humanity, solidarity and dynamism. In the months and years to come, work remains to be done. I want to ensure the population of lake? Mégantic that we will continue to accompany it, "concluded the official opposition.
