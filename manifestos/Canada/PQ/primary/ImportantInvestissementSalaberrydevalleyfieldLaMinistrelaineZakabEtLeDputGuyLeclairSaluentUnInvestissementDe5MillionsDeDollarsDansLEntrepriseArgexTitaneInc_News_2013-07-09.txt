SALABERRY-DE-VALLEYFIELD- Minister Delegate to Industrial Policy and the Quebec Economic Development Bank, Ms. Élaine Zakaïb, and the member for Beauharnois and Vice-President of the Committee on Economy and Labor, Mr. Guy Leclair, welcome with great enthusiasm the agreement between Argex Titania inc. and Investissement Québec aimed at ensuring the success of the company's growth plan. 
The investment, carried out through Resources Québec, a subsidiary of Investissement Québec, consists of a total participation of $ 5 million in the share capital of Argex Titanium (TSX-V: RGX). It will allow the company to concretize its project on the establishment of a new research and development center and to develop its first titanium dioxide production plant in Salaberry-de-Valleyfield, Quebec. 
"The Government of Quebec is proud to support this strategic investment project, which will generate significant economic benefits for the Montérégie region. Quebec's prosperity is dependent on projects that combine innovation and economic development, such as that of Argex Titanium, "said Sulaian Deputy Minister Zakaïb. "Argex Titanium will not only be able to carry out its research and development activities in Salaberry-de-Valleyfield, but also to continue its growth. » 
Argex Titanium specializes in the production of titanium dioxide of high purity, an inorganic pigment extract from titaniferous minerals. The main objective of the company is to quickly start production in order to deploy its exclusive process to produce tiO2 of a high purity directly from raw materials. 
"This development project will contribute to the economic growth of Salaberry-de-Valleyfield, in addition to raising the many assets of Beauharnois and the Suroît with a view to encouraging other transformation companies to come there. This investment will also, ultimately, the creation of several quality jobs in the region, which is an excellent news, "said the member Guy Leclair.
