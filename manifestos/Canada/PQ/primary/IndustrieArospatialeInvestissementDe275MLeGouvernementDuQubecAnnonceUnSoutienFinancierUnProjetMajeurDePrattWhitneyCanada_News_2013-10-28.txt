Longueuil- The Prime Minister of Quebec, Pauline Marois, accompanied by the Minister of Finance and the Economy, Nicolas Marceau, today announced the award of $ 19 million in Pratt & Whitney Canada for the Modernization of the facilities of its Longueuil plant, which will allow the creation of 90 jobs. 
Rated $ 275 million, the project includes the development of a global center of excellence in intelligent manufacturing, with the implementation of three automated manufacturing cells for the making of critical parts of the new generation of PurePower® engines. Pratt & Whitney USA. These cells will improve operational efficiency in addition to minimizing the carbon footprint of the company.
