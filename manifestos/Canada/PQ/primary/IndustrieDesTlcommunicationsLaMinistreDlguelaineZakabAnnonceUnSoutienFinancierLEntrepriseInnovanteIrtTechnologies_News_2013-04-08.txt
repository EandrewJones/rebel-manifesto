The Prairie - Minister Delegate to Industrial Policy and the Quebec Economic Development Bank, Ms. Élain Zakaïb, confirms the award of $ 229,500 financial assistance to the IRT Technologies of the Prairie for design and The manufacture of a high-capacity frequency converter for the satellite telecommunication market, an innovative project estimated at more than $ 753,000. 
"The growth of the Quebec telecommunications industry is based on the dynamism of companies and the avant-garde character of their research and development projects," said Zakaïb. "IRT Technologies specializes in a key sector of our economy representing about 43% of revenues and 55% of Quebec's job and telecommunications technology jobs. Today's announcement reflects our government's commitment to innovation and businesses that are distinguished by their creativity and bold, and this, beyond simple start. » 
Founded in 2011, the company IRT Technologies designs and manufactures satellite telecommunication devices. It is still at the start-up stage and is currently working on the development of its first product line. 
"Our frequency converter will provide the relay between satellite operators and residential and commercial customers," said Arkadi Potapov, President of IRT Technologies. "The financial assistance granted by the Government of Quebec will allow us to develop and test our new technology, which is desired, we will quickly open the doors of the North American market. »
