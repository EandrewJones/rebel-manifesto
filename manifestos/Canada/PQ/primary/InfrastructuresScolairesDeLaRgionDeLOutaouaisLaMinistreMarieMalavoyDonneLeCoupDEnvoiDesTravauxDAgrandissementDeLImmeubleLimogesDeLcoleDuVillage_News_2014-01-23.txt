Gatineau - The Minister of Education, Recreation and Sport, Mrs Marie Malavoy, today participated in the ceremony of the first shovel of land marking the start of the expansion work of the school Limoges building From the village, in the Aylmer sector, in Gatineau. 
It should be remembered that in May 2013, the minister announced an investment of $ 44.05 million in Outaouais school infrastructure. In particular, the school board of Portages-de-L'Ataouais obtained $ 5.45 million to add 10 classes and 1 gym to the Limoges building of the village school. 
"I am very pleased with the realization of this project, which will have a tangible effect on the well-being and quality of life for students, parents, school staff and the community. It meets the important needs of premises and space in the region, significantly improves services to the people and will give more taste and pleasure to teach our children, "said Malavoy Minister. 
This announcement is part of the 2013-2023 Infrastructure Plan Quebec, which will renew and improve infrastructure in all sectors. In total, nearly $ 8.1 billion in investments will be intended for Quebec school infrastructure. The projects that will be carried out consist of additions of space, the maintenance of assets or other initiatives. 
"These investments testify to the government's desire to provide quality educational services, in environments conducive to learning and fulfillment. We put strong and bearer gestures, while ensuring a healthy management of public funds, which demonstrates to me a real vision for Quebec, "concluded Malavoy Minister.
