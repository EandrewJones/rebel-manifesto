Quebec- The Minister of Education, Recreation and Sport, Marie Malavoy, warmly congratulates Quebec's Eugenie Bouchard, who, thanks to his care of five victories until the semifinal to the Tennis Internationals of Australia, could Access the top 20 of the world women's ranking. 
"What a spectacular breakthrough of this talented and charismatic young tennis player! It was with feverish that we followed his recent course, which should allow him to pass, in just over a year, from the 144th position to the top 20 of the world ranking. All hopes are allowed for Eugenie Bouchard, who can now target the highest peaks, "Ms. Malavoy said. 
Eugenie Bouchard lost in the semifinal in front of Chinese Li Na. At 19, she became the first Quebecer to reach the final square of one of the four Grand Slam tournaments. It should be noted that the athlete has succeeded this feat, while it was only in his second full season on the female circuit. 
"His performance and inspiring attitude provoked a huge craze, which is very promising for Quebec tennis. She is already an example for several young sports. No doubt it will now be an extraordinary ambassador for this sport. Quebec needs models that give the taste and pleasure of moving and leading a physically active life, "concluded the minister.
