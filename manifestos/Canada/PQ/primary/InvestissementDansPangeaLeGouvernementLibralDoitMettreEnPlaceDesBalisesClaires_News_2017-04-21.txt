Quebec, April 21, 2017- The spokesperson for the Parti Québécois in agriculture, André Villeneuve, asks the Liberal government, as well as to the company Pangea and its partners, to respond transparently to the many concerns of the Farmers, raised in the wake of the announcement, today an investment. In addition, clear tags must be put in place to ensure that farmers will remain farm proprietors. 
IN SHORT 
"The legitimate worries of farmers must be heard by the government. For three years, the uncertainty continues as to this model that is constantly expanding, while tags are not all defined and that several questions remain unanswered. The government should not sign a blank check; He must require transparency in return of public funds that will be invested. Moreover, it is necessary to adopt legislation that will ensure that the Pangea model does not lead to the financialization of our agricultural land or speculation, which penalizes the succession rather than offering them real solutions. To ensure that we ask the Liberal government to get out of its inaction and call Bill 599 to counter the grabbing of the Québécois' agricultural land, "claimed the member for Berthier. 
"The main concern of the Parti Québécois is to obtain the guarantee that farmers operators will remain the owners of Quebec farmland. We have always been along with farmers. Their concerns are very understandable, and the government has the obligation to answer it, "concluded André Villeneuve.
