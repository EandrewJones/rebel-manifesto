Matane - Minister Delegate to Tourism and Minister responsible for the Bas-Saint-Laurent Region, Mr. Pascal Bérubé, is pleased to announce, on behalf of the Minister of Transport and Minister of Municipal Affairs, regions and occupation From the territory, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, studies that will lead to urbanization of Highway 132 in Matane. 
This project expected by the medium consists of the development of Route 132 on a length of about 0.9 km, in urban section, from the entrance of the road 195 to the access is of the street of Matane-sur -Sea. The work will include the installation of a storm sewer system, the addition of borders as well as the refurbishment of the pavement. This work is needed as a result of the development of this sector. 
These works will enhance road drainage and standardize road configuration 132 in this sector. The entire project will allow users of the road to benefit from a safer road network, while promoting the economic development of the city of Matane. 
The Ministry of Transport will undertake a geotechnical study in this summer before starting the project design. The realization of the plans and quotes is planned in 2014, so that the work can begin in the fall of 2014 and end in 2015. "As a member of Parliament, Minister responsible for the Region and Citizen of Matane, I am particularly Happy to make my contribution to improving the quality of life of the people of our region, "mentions Mr. Bérubé. 
This project is one of the 149 projects that will take place in 2013-2015 in the Bas Saint-Laurent region. To develop this programming, the Department of Transport conducted a thorough revaluation of projects by prioritizing road safety and the maintenance of the road network.
