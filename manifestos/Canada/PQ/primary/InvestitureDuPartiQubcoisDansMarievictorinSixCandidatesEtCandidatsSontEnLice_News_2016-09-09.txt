Montreal- At the end of the nomination period, six candidates had filed valid application ballots. Members of Marie-Victorin will have to express their choice among these six people at the November 18 investment assembly. 
Candidates are: Philippe Nautiernicolas Dioncatherine FournierCarl Gilbertmathieu Marcottesophie Stanké "The six candidatures offer rich and diversified profiles. Their number attests to the citality of the Parti Québécois and it is with great enthusiasm that I wish everyone to all a good campaign, "said Quebec's president, Raymond Archambault. 
The 1489 members of the constituency's political training are invited to the Investiture Assembly of September 18, 2016, from 10am, in Square Desaulniers in Longueuil.
