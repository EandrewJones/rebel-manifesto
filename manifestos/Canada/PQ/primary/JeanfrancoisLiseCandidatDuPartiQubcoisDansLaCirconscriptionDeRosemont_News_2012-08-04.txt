Montreal - The leader of the Parti Québécois, Pauline Marois, is pleased to announce the candidacy of Jean-François listed in the riding of Rosemont. " Finally! Jean-François makes the jump, exclaimed Pauline Marois. It is a man who has an extraordinary flair, ideas for Quebec and is made for political commitment. This is an excellent news for our party, for sovereignists and for Quebec. » 
Journalist, Professor, Founder of Cerium, Chronicler, Blogger, Prime Ministers' Political Advisor and Renowned Author, Jean-François Lisée is a recognized personality of the community of communications, especially for his blogs in the news. 
"Quebec must resume its march towards its sovereignty and get back up. We had a government that behaved in the board of directors for too long. With Pauline Marois, we will finally have a major head of state with a project of society that will restore strength in Quebec and pride to Quebecers, "said Jean-François Lisée. 
Pauline Marois has been packed with the team that the Parti Québécois presents and believes that Quebeckers will also be. "We have a dream team, women and men from all walks of life who want to contribute to the improvement of Quebec, we have ideas and a program that will rally the population and we have the most beautiful company projects. » 
The leader of the Parti Québécois also took advantage of the investiture of Jean-François listed to recall the commitments that his party has taken so far. "We have stated our ideas to train an honest government: fixed date elections and public funding of political parties. We shared our ideas for affordable access to education for all, early childhood to university. We have proposed solutions to enrich Quebecers. For the Liberals and the Caquists, it's the companies first. For the Québécois party, Quebeckers first. It's now up to us to choose! "Exclaimed Pauline Marois. 
Remember that the Parti Québécois is the greatest political training in Quebec with its 90,000 members.
