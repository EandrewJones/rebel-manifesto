Quebec, on April 12, 2017 - The Leader of the Official Opposition, Jean-François Lisée, is proud to announce the appointment of Mr. Guy Amyot as Deputy Cabinet Director. 
"We have the responsibility to prepare well to offer Quebeckers, by October 2018, a team ready to govern. To contribute to this preparation, I am pleased to welcome a person of caliber, Guy Amyot, who chose, to join us, to leave his position as Secretary General of the Quebec Press Council, after having Busy for 8 years, "said Jean? François Lisée.
