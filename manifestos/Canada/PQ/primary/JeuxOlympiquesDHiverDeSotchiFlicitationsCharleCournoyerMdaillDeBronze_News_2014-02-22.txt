Quebec- The Quebec Prime Minister Pauline Marois, and the Minister of Education, Recreation and Sport, Marie Malavoy, are happy to congratulate Charle Cournoyer, who won today a bronze medal for the test. 500 meters in short track speed skating, as part of Sochi's Olympic Winter Games in Russia. 
"What a brilliant performance for this young athlete who is in his first Olympic participation! Rising Star of Speed Skating, Charle Cournoyer embodies a beautiful model of will and success for young people and for all of Quebec society. It will undoubtedly be monitored over the next few years! "Said the Prime Minister. 
"By this accomplishment, Charle Cournoyer has shown that he has the assets to climb the highest peaks of excellence: the talent, of course, but especially a force of character and an unshakeable determination! Well done! , "Said Malavoy Minister. 
We invite you to monitor tomorrow snowboarding competitions, long track speed skating and biathlon, to which Quebec athletes will take part. 
For more information on Quebec athletes who participate in the Sochi Olympic Winter Games, appointment ausportcom.qc.ca.
