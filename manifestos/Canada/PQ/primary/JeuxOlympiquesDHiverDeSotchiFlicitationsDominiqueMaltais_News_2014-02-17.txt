Quebec, February 16, 2014 - The Quebec Prime Minister Pauline Marois, and the Minister of Education, Recreation and Sport, Marie Malavoy, are pleased to congratulate Dominique Maltese, who won today a medal of Silver Snow Silver (Snowboard Cross). 
"Dominique Maltese is one of the brightest snow surf stars on the world stage. Its success is fully deserved. She can be proud to have achieved her goals and dreams thousands of Quebeckers who have followed her career with passion and who today rejoice in her performance. This athlete originating in Petite-Rivière-Saint-François, it is not only as a premier, but also a member of Charlevoix-Côte-de-Beaupré that I tell him a sincere bravo! "Said Pauline Marois. 
"This Medal of Dominique Maltese is the result of years of effort, and also support and assiduous support from his parents and coaches. The values that are at the heart of our future national politics of sport, leisure and physical activity, Dominique Maltese embodies them all: the taste and pleasure of moving, overflowing, perseverance, discipline and trust in our abilities. This is an athlete who represents what we want better for Quebec, "said Malavoy Minister.
