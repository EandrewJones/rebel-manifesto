Québec- On the occasion of a ceremony given at the red show today, in honor of the athletes and trainers who participated in the London Olympic and Paralympic Games, the Prime Minister of Quebec, Ms. Pauline Marois, And the Minister of Education, Recreation and Sport, Mrs Marie Malavoy, greeted their exceptional performances. 
"The example of perseverance you gave us this summer is very evocative. You have become ambassadors and representatives of all Quebeckers, their ambitions and expectations, "said Premier Pauline Marois. 
The success of the Quebec delegation at these Games inspired the entire Quebec population. Indeed, the 63 Olympic athletes and the 45 Quebec Paralympic Athletes who participated in the Games harvested 21 of the 49 medals of Canada. 
"I congratulate our athletes, who are sources of inspiration as well as models of courage, determination and success for young people and for the entire society. I also want to salute the admirable work of their coaches, who give them all the support and confidence they need to go faster, earlier, further, and so continue their quest for excellence, "said the minister Malavoy. 
Olympic and Paralympic athletes receive financial support under the Team Québec program. This program is part of various government initiatives aimed at supporting sports excellence. In 2011-2012, Quebec spent nearly $ 65 million in support of sport and high level athletes.
