Quebec- The Quebec Prime Minister Pauline Marois, and the Minister of Education, Recreation and Sport, Marie Malavoy, highlight the exceptional performance of Mac Marcoux and his guide, Quebec Robin Fémy, who won, to Sochi, two bronze medals at the Paralympic Winter Games: one to the descent test in ski para-alpine and the other, at the super-g. 
"These medals, symbols of sports excellence, constitute a tribute to their complicity and their courage. The confidence that unites these two athletes has equal only their daring to the challenge they found together. I transmit them my most sincere congratulations, "said the premier. 
"MM. Marcoux and Fémy offered us an impressive performance. Thanks to their sustained efforts and the sharing of their talents, the dream of these two athletes has become reality. In this sense, these are models of perseverance for our young people, "said Malavoy Minister. 
Robin Femy is one of the athletes who benefit from the Government of Quebec. Several programs and measures of the Ministry of Education, Recreation and Sport, including the Sport Excellence Development Support Program and the High Level Athlete Tax Credit Measure, aim to promote the practice of sport Elite, to develop a sports culture in Quebec and to encourage succession.
