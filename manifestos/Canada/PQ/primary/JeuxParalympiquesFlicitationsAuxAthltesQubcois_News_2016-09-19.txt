Québec- On the occasion of the closure of the Rio Paralympic Games, the member for Jonquière and Official Opposition Leader, Sylvain Gaudreault, as well as the member for Labelle and spokesman for the Quebec of Healthy Lifestyles , Sports and leisure, Sylvain Pagé, wanted to send their lively congratulations to Quebec and Canadian athletes for their very fine performances. 
"Despite some difficulties, and by perseverance, these women and men have accomplished great prowess, and it is a pleasure for me to salute their efforts, their courage and their determination. All Quebeckers and all Quebeckers are proud of their athletes, who are all sources of inspiration, "first declared the official opposition leader. 
"Canada has harvested 29 medals at the Rio Paralympic Games, and many Quebeckers contributed to this remarkable result; What worthy representatives do we have! Let's continue to see them, one day, do the same under the colors of the flowered! Sylvain Gaudreault continued. 
"Beyond the great performances at the games, it is essential to point out all the necessary preparation work to achieve such a degree of competition. Paralympic athletes are important models for our young people and for the entire population because they represent self-succeeding. Congratulations to each and every one of them! , Added Sylvain Pagé. 
"In closing, like all our colleagues, we learned yesterday with great sadness the death of the Bahman Golbarnezhad cyclist athlete, native to Iran. We sympathize with his family, loved ones and sports community, "have also stated to mention members.
