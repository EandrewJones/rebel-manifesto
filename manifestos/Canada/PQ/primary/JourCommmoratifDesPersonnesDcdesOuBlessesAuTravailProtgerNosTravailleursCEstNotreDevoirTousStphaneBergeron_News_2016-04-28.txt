Quebec- On this commemorative day of persons deceased or injured at work, emphasized around the world, the member for Verchères and spokesperson of the Quebecois Party of Work, Stéphane Bergeron, wanted to raise awareness of the government and the whole of the Quebec population to the importance of workplace prevention. 
"In 2015, in Quebec, 196 of our workers lost their lives as a result of an accident or an occupational disease. And 87,000 others were victims of a work accident. This sad assessment, we must, together, work to improve it. From this the importance of awareness and prevention in the workplace, "said Stéphane Bergeron. 
On this commemorative day, everywhere in Quebec, thousands of workers will observe a minute of silence to honor the memory of their deceased colleagues. "Each accident that occurs in the workplace is one too much. These are so many human and family dramas that we need to use to avoid. It is simply inadmissible that a person hurts, or even losing life by walking up, "added the member for Verchères. 
"I invite the entire population, especially the government, to make the health and safety of our workers with absolute priorities. Quebec can not afford to deprive yourself of any of them. Make sure to improve coaching and prevention; We will be all and all winners, "concluded Stéphane Bergeron.
