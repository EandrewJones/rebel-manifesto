Quebec- The Minister of Transport and Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Occupation of the Territory, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, stressed, by a statement to the National Assembly, the National Day of Remembrance of the Victims of the Road, Who stands this year under the theme Credit carefully, your future depends on it. 
"I invite the whole population of Quebec to honor the memory of the victims of the road and to remember all those who have been affected by these dramas. I also call all the users of the road to be of the greatest caution in their travels to further reduce the number of victims, "said the minister. 
Although the 2012 road balance sheet is the best record of the last 65 years, road safety remains an essential issue and an important concern for many citizens. "Thanks to the combined efforts of all the partners and users of the road, we see a clear improvement, but the achievements are fragile. This is why it is important to continue remaining awareness-raising actions and support the adoption of safe driving behaviors, "said President and Chief Executive Officer of the Car Insurance Corporation of Quebec, Ms. Nathalie Tremblay . 
Remember that in October 2005, the United Nations adopted a resolution asking governments to introduce a commemorative day for the victims of the road, which is now observed in many countries.
