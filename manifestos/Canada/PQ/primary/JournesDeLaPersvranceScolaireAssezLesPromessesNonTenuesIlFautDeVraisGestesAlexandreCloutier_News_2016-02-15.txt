Québec- This publishing day, the spokesman for the Parti Party in education, research and educational perseverance, Alexandre Cloutier, was accompanied by several representatives of organizations fighting the school dropout of the region. of the Chaudière-Appalaches to denounce the consequences of government cuts in the field. 
"People from the Montmagny-l'islet region have come to testify and launch a heart cry to the government. The projects they had set up are an example of success. In a particularly disadvantaged environment, marked by a high dropout rate, a community has taken control of reversing the situation. The two formidable initiatives that are Mobile Biblio and the environment are now threatened by Cute Cups, "said Alexandre Cloutier. 
As early as September, the Quebecois party denounced the important cuts that the Liberals made in the network of regional bodies to combat school dropout. The government then committed to restoring all cut sums. 
"Five months later, the sums are always waiting and the consequences continue to be felt on the ground. The days of school perseverance are a unique opportunity to educate our young people about the importance of graduating. Unfortunately, this year, in several regions of Quebec, no activity will be organized, because several organizations have seen their budget decreases alarmingly or are downright closed, "concluded Alexandre Cloutier.
