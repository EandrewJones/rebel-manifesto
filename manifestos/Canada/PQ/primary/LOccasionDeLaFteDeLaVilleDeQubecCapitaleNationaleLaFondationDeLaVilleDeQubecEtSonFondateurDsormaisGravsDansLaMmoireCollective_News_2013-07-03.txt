Quebec- On the occasion of the anniversary day of the National Capital Foundation, the Minister of Culture and Communications, Maka Kotto, made the designation of Samuel de Champlain as a historical character, under the law on Cultural heritage, which entered into force on October 19th. The minister also appointed the Foundation of Quebec City as a historic event. 
"These gestures that we ask today are notable because they make it possible to highlight the first signs of a French presence in America. This highly symbolic exercise aims to raise awareness among our fellow citizens and our fellow citizens in this major aspect of our national history. This recognition is all the more relevant as it reminds an important pan of Quebec's history for today's generations and those of tomorrow, "said Minister Kotto.
