Québec- On the occasion of the 350th anniversary of the Foundation of the Quebec Seminary, the Minister of Culture and Communications, Maka Kotto, appointed this national historic event under the Cultural Heritage Act. 
"The Foundation of the Quebec Seminar is now one of the events enrolled in the Quebec Cultural Heritage Register. Indeed, this institution posed the foundations of the higher education of our nation and contributed to the preservation and dissemination of French culture in America, "said Minister Kotto. 
The seminary of Quebec was founded on March 26, 1663, by Bishop François de Laval, Vicar Apostolic of New France and Future Premier Bishop of Quebec. Its foundation is now designated as historical event because it marked the history of Quebec. Indeed, the seminar is at the origin of the creation of the network of classical colleges which has extended to French Canada and the Foundation of Laval University in 1852. It has crossed the centuries and still persists today in His educational mission.
