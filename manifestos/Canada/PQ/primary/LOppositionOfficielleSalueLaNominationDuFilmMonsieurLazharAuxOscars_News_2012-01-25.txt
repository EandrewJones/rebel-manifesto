Quebec- The hon. Member for Charlevoix and Leader of the Official Opposition, Pauline Marois, and the member for Bourget and spokesman for the official opposition in culture and communications, Maka Kotto, greet the selection of the film Mr. Lazhar of the Quebec director Philippe Falardeau in the category of the Best Foreign Language Film Oscar.

"Thanks to a work all in subtlety and finesse, this film transports us to the world of great cinema and filmmakers, actors and actresses of talent. We send to Philippe Falardeau and to all the film team our most sincere congratulations. We also wish them the best chance for the grand night! , "Said Maka Kotto.
