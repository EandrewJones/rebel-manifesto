Quebec- The Quebec Prime Minister, Pauline Marois, and the Minister of Culture and Communications, Maka Kotto, expressed their deep regret before Georges Hamel's departure, this great singer who was called the Country Gentleman. 
"In 40 years of career, this striking figure of Country in Quebec, but also in North America, has been able to recognize the place and importance of country with us. He paved the way for many artists from his time and paved that of the new generation. It was an example of perseverance for many of us, "said Pauline Marois. 
"His texts, who tell the daily lives with emotion, accompanied thousands of admirers of Quebec country music. The late Georges Hamel devoted his life to the song and will remain an emblem of country music in the hearts of Quebeckers and Quebeckers, "said Maka Kotto. 
In 2009, Georges Hamel obtained the recognition of his peers in the form of the medal of the National Assembly. The recipient of four Felix launched two weeks ago, his 44th album in a career, entitled a flower for you. 
Prime Minister Pauline Marois and Minister Maka Kotto want to express their deepest condolences to Mr. Hamel's relatives.
