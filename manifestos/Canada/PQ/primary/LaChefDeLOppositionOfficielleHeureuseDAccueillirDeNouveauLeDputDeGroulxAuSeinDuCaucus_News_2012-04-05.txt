Quebec- The hon. Member for Charlevoix and Leader of the Official Opposition, Pauline Marois, is happy to welcome the hon. Member for Groulx, René Gauvreau, within the Caucus of the Parti Québécois. 
"I look forward to working with the member for Groulx in the Quebecois Party. His commitment, especially in the issue of youth protection, is an asset for our political formation, "she said. 
The verifications made did not establish any connection between the member for Groulx and the charges against his former political attaché. René Gauvreau will finish his mandate, but will not be a candidate at the next election.
