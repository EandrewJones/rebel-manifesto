Quebec- The member for Charlevoix and Leader of the Official Opposition, Pauline Marois, wanted to pay tribute to Evelyn Dumas, who died yesterday from a long illness. 
"Ancient political attaché of René Lévesque and journalist of great talent, Evelyn Dumas has marked the story by becoming the first woman journalist at the tribune of the press," said the official opposition. 
The member for Charlevoix also stimulated that the exceptional work of Evelyn Dumas, in particular as a day-to-day editor on the day and as a journalist in the newspaper Le Duty and the press, has repeatedly been rewarded . 
"I take this opportunity to offer my deepest condolences to the family and relatives of this Grande Quebecker who has inspired several women in the field of journalism," concluded Pauline Marois.
