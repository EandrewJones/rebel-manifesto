Quebec- The Minister of Labor, Minister of Employment and Social Solidarity, Minister responsible for the Status of Women, Minister responsible for the Capitale-Nationale Regions and the Chaudière-Appalaches and Minister responsible for the Régie des rentes du Québec Ms. Agnès Maltais, introduced the bill this morning to establish target benefit pension plans in some companies in the pulp and paper sector. 
In doing so, Ms. Maltais respects his commitment to proceed quickly in order to enable the establishment of the legal framework that will make possible the creation of new pension plans, particularly in papers White Birch. "This is an essential first step, but there is still work to be done. The union and the employer must always agree on certain provisions of the text of the new regimes and, more importantly, the company must pay the promised money, "Mrs Maltais recalled. 
The Minister relies on the collaboration of the members of the National Assembly to pass the bill before the end of the present session and welcomes commitments already made to that effect.
