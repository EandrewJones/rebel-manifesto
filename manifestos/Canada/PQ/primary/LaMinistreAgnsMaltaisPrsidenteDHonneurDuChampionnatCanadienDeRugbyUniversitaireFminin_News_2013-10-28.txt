Quebec- The Minister responsible for the Capitale-National and Minister responsible for the Status of Women, Agnès Maltais, is proud to act as President of Honor for the Canadian Female University Rugby Championship, which will take place at TELUS- Laval University, October 31 to November 3rd. 
"Sporting events are among the attraction forces of the Capitale-Nationale region. It is therefore a pleasure for me to welcome, for the first time, this major tournament and to be the president of honor. Reaffirming our expertise in the organization of high-level events, the presentation of this competition will demonstrate the modernity and quality of our sports infrastructure, "mentioned Minister Maltese. 
This championship will receive the six best female rugby teams from across Canada, including two Quebec teams. "I therefore invite you to encourage in large numbers these determined players, who will certainly offer us a highest competition. Through their performance, both sporty and school, these young women represent real sources of inspiration, "added Maltese minister. 
The Secretariat at the Capitale-Nationale contributes financially to the 2013 Canadian University Rugby Championship, assigning $ 7,500. 
The Secretariat, attached to the Ministry of the Executive Board, advises the Government and the Minister responsible for the Capitale-Nationale, Ms. Agnès Maltais, on any question or measure having a significant impact on the region and its economic, cultural, tourist and social development . 
The Secretariat acts in consultation with its various governmental and regional partners, while financially supporting the implementation of projects contributing to the growth of the Capitale-Nationale.
