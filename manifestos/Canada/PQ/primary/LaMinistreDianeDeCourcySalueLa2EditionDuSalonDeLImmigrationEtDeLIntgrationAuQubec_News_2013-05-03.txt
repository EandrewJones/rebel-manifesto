"This activity, very well organized, offers immigrants not only a good overview of the services offered to them, but also the opportunity to meet the various stakeholders and employers who are on site. I am pleased to see that among the exhibitors, there are several partners of the department as well as companies open to diversity that welcome competent immigrants to participate in the development of our society, "said Minister Diane de Courcy. . 
Every year, nearly 50,000 immigrants are established in Quebec permanently. Madame de Courcy recalled that in order to intensify the francization and integration of these newcomers and to promote their establishment in the regions, the department has developed a plan of measures. Title for a successful francization and integration of immigrant persons: Living and working in French in Quebec, this plan accompanied Bill 14 aimed at strengthening the status of the French language, tabled in the National Assembly on December 5th. It is also the fruit of the meetings that the minister has made in the regions of Quebec to talk to partners, representatives of the community, as well as immigrants established in each of these regions. 
"With more than 165 exhibitors and many speakers, the Immigration and Integration Fair of Quebec illustrates how much the commitment of all the actors of the company is necessary. In terms of immigration, the responsibility to welcome, francizing and integrating newcomers not only falls within the government, it also concerns the entire Quebec society, "concluded Minister Diane de Courcy.
