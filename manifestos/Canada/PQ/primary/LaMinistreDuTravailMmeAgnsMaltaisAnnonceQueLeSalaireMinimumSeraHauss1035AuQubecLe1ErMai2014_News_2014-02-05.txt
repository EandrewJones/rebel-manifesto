Quebec- The Minister of Labor, Minister of Employment and Social Solidarity and Minister responsible for the Status of Women, Agnès Maltais, announces that the minimum wage will increase by $ 0.20 on May 1st. The minimum wage will therefore increase $ 10.35 per hour in Quebec. 
The minimum wage rate payable to employees will be increased from $ 8.75 to $ 8.90 per hour. With respect to the minimum hourly wage payable to workers in certain sectors of the garment industry, it will be increased from $ 10.15 to $ 10.35. 
"Since his arrival, our government has increased the minimum wage of $ 0.45 an hour, thereby increasing the remuneration of low-income workers," said Minister Maltese. This year, a total of 345,363 people will benefit from the increase in the overall minimum wage rate, including 212,744 women. More than nine ten paid payroll at the minimum wage in Quebec work in the service sector, such as hotel, restaurant or retail trade. 
In addition, Ms. Agnès Maltais confirms that raspberry or strawberry gatherers will continue to be paid for performance after May 1, 2014. "In order to ensure adequate remuneration for employees in this industry and facilitate the access of strawberry producers. And raspberries to a picking time, we decided to maintain the rate of performance for these workers. In addition, we put in place a committee to study the issue of remuneration in this environment, "added Minister Maltese. 
Thus, as of May 1, the minimum wage rate for raspberry gatherers will increase from $ 2.98 to $ 3.04 from KG, and $ 0.79 to $ 0.81 from the KG for pickers. Strawberries.
