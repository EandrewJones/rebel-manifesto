Quebec- The Minister of Education, Recreation and Sport, Mrs Marie Malavoy, today announced, at a press point in the National Assembly, that the work of the Table Qu√©bec-School Boards will be relaunched . 
"Education is a priority for the Quebec government. That's why we are acting first to meet the needs of students. The table will allow us to strengthen the collaboration with our partners and to make sure the student is at the heart of our action, "Ms. Malavoy said. 
The mandate of the table is to maintain a constructive partnership between the Ministry and the school boards to promote, on the one hand, efficient management of the school system and, on the other hand, effective monitoring of the various framework of pre-school education , of primary and secondary education, vocational training and adult education, with a view to informing ministerial decision-making. 
The table meetings will be an opportunity to explore the issues related to the quality of educational services, governance and accountability carried out by school boards. Topics such as the fight against school dropout, partnership agreements, decentralization and autonomy of school boards, school bureaucracy as well as school democracy can be addressed.
