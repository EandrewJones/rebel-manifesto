Quebec- On this International Volunteer Day, the Minister of Education, Recreation and Sport, Mrs Marie Malavoy, wishes to warmly thank the thousands of Quebeckers who contribute, every year, to offer their fellow citizens a vast Range of services and activities in the areas of leisure and sport, but also to support the network of education. 
"Quebec is pampered with the participation of more than half a million volunteers in leisure, sports and education. These people generously offer their expertise and time, allowing a multitude of activities in their community. They contribute to the development of a sense of belonging that sies the links between Quebecers and Quebeckers and promotes solidarity. Volunteering is an essential wealth for our society, "said Malavoy Minister. 
Remember that the Ministry of Education, Recreation and Sport has recently honored 24 laureates and winners as part of the Recreation and Sport Dollard Morin Volunteering Award. These exceptional volunteers have known, by their actions, to radiate their communities. They are a good example of altruism and testify to the training effect that can cause the gift of self. 
In 1985, the United Nations General Assembly decreed the International Volunteer Day (JIB) on December 5 to pay tribute to the commitment of those who, all over the world, contribute to the well-being of their fellow citizens and fellow citizens. . Thus, several organizations and businesses now benefit from this day to highlight the work of their volunteers and the value of their commitment.
