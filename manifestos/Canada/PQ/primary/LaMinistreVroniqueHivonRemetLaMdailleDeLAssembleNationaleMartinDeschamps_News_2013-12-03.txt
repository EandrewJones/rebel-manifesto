Quebec- On the occasion of the International Day of Persons with Disabilities, the Minister Delegate to the Social Services and the Protection of Youth and Minister responsible for the Lanaudière Region, Mrs Véronique Hivon, awarded the Medal of the National Assembly to Author-Composer-Interpreter Martin Deschamps for his contribution to the promotion of the social participation of people with disabilities. 
"Martin Deschamps is a long-standing partner of the Office of Persons with Disabilities of Quebec (OPHQ), with whom he has collaborated for more than ten years as a spokesperson for the Quebec Week of Persons with Disabilities. I wish to salute the immense contribution of this citizen who, by his talent, has been able to stand out as an artist on the Quebec scene and who, by his personality, has become a model of perseverance and commitment, not only For people with disabilities, but for the entire population, "said the minister.
