The Minister Delegated to Industrial Policy and the Quebec Economic Development Bank, Ms. Élaine Zakaïb, is pleased to highlight the inauguration of the new Moldexpert Inc. facilities. The company benefited from a financial contribution of $ 150,000 for its modernization project, which aims to increase its competitiveness. 
Moulexpert designs and manufactures injection molds for plastic and aluminum sectors. The modernization project, which totaled an investment of more than $ 750,000, included the acquisition of a five-axis machining center of very high performance and the complete redevelopment of the factory functional spaces. Moulexpert wishes to improve productivity and gradually increase its market share, particularly with the clientele in the slim wall packaging sector. 
"The competitiveness of our companies requires increased productivity that goes mainly through innovation and modernization of their equipment. The Moldexpert project, a leader in the design and manufacture of injection molds, will ensure the growth of its activities, and that is why we are pleased to provide our support, "said Zakaïb Élain. "This intervention allows the company to consolidate all of the 20 existing jobs, which is an excellent new for the economic development of the Chaudière-Appalaches region. » 
"The financial support of the Government of Quebec, granted through the ESSORT Investment Québec program, will allow us to be more productive and to offer products to our customers in shorter deadlines," said Mr. Steve Nadeau, Managing Director of Moulexpert. "With a more efficient factory, Moulexpert gives themselves the means of its ambitions. Thanks to recent modernization work, we intend to increase the turnover of the company by 25% annually. »
