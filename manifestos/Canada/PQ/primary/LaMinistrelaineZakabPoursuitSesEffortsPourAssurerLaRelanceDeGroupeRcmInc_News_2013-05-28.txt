Quebec- The Minister Delegate to Industrial Policy and the Quebec Economic Development Bank, Ms. Élain Zakaïb, continues his efforts to ensure the safeguarding of RCM Group Inc.. Yamachiche. This social economy enterprise, active in the field of recyclable recovery, is currently experiencing financial difficulties. 
RCM Group is a non-profit organization whose mission is to create jobs for people with physical or intellectual limitations. The company has 210 employees, the vast majority of which could not work in a conventional context. 
"The RCM Group is a strategic company in the social economy that is strongly supported by the Community," said Zakaïb Élain. "The recovery of this company becomes a priority for our government. That's why we have demonstrated a proactive attitude by paying the payment of a $ 750,000 financial contribution to avoid collective dismissal. I also remind you that an adaptation committee of the workforce has been set up to analyze all the stimulus options. A consultant specializing in corporate recovery was mandated to chair this committee of which representatives of the Maskinongé, Employment-Québec's Local Development Center, of the Desjardins Movement, of the Development Assistance Society. The Community and the National Research Council Canada. A report is expected in a few weeks. » 
In addition, in order to validate RCM Group stimulus opportunities, engineers from the Quebec Industrial Research Center (CRIQ) are currently performing a series of technical tests to improve the technological process for a bag processing project. plastic. 
"What motivates me even more to persevere in this case, it is the mobilization of socio-economic environment," added Élain Zakaïb. "This desire to continue looking for solutions will allow us, I am convinced, to revive this company and, above all, to maintain the jobs. »
