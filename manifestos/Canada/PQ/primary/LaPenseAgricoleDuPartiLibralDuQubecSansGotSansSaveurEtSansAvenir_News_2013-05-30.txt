Québec- The Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Agriculture, Fisheries and Food, François Gendron, is stumbled to learn this morning that for the Liberal Party of Quebec (PLQ) of Mr. Philippe Couillard The Quebec agri-food industry is tasteless, tasteless and without a future. 
The hon. Member for Huntington, official opposition spokesperson for agriculture and fisheries and president of the next electoral campaign for the PLQ, Mr. Stéphane Billette, took over, commented and supported a chronicle of Ms. Lysiane Gagnon. Daily La Presse through the Twitter Social Network. This column, published this morning and entitled "Life without olive oil", contains gross and absolutely erroneous affirmations. 
Thus, according to Ms. Gagnon and Mr. Billette, canned tomatoes from Italy will always be more tasty than the tomatoes of Quebec's greenhouse; Supply management requires the consumption of poultry and eggs of Quebec with tasteless taste; The Quebec food industry is nothing distinctive and contributes to the underdevelopment of the Third World and Quebec's craft cheeses are two times more than a good French cheese. 
"It's aberrant and absolutely unacceptable. I ask the Liberal Chief Philippe Couillard to dissociate from his member. The latter must apologize with the 475,000 people who work hard daily to put on the market products of unparalleled quality. We can not let the member salize the work they do with passion, "said Francois Gendron.
