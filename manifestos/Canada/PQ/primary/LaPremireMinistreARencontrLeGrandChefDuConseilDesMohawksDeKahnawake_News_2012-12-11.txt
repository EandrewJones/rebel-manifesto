Quebec- The Quebec Prime Minister, Ms. Pauline Marois, is pleased to announce that his meeting yesterday with the Grand Chief of the Kahnawake Mohawk Council, Mr. Michael A. Delisle, has reconnected the links. 
"We exchanged issues of common interest, including transportation and taxation, and I reiterated the commitment of Quebec to support Kahnawake's economic development," said Pauline Marois. 
The Prime Minister has also committed to ensuring greater efficiency of Kahnawake-Quebec Central Table, responsible for maintaining communication, ensuring good relationships and finding mutually satisfactory solutions. In addition, sectoral tables will have the mandate to treat more in depth of particular topics. 
"By restarting the work of the Central Table, we want to take an opportunity to strengthen our relationship with the Kahnawake Mohawk community. In this respect, the meeting with the Grand Chief Delisle marks a renewal in the establishment of a strong relationship between Quebec and Kahnawake, "concluded the Prime Minister.
