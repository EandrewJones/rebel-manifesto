"Thanks to the quality of his work and especially for his commitment to defending the French language, Richard Garneau was a great ambassador from Quebec around the world. This medal that is awarded to it on the very scene of the next games, and in the presence of our athletes and personalities of sport, is a recognition more for an exceptional Quebec. We will have the opportunity to highlight this event in Quebec at a special ceremony, "said the Prime Minister. 
Richard Garneau has been from all the summer Olympics since those from Rome in 1960 to London in 2012 (with the exception of Atlanta), and winter from Innsbruck in 1964 to Vancouver in 2010 (at The exception of Nagano). He is the journalist who covered the most games of all time. This recognition adds to the many other honors received by the journalist during his long career.
