Quebec- Quebec Premier, Ms. Pauline Marois, starts tomorrow a two-day mission in New York. It will present the economic guidelines of the Government of Quebec with influential American figures from the media of trade and finance. On this occasion, it will be accompanied by the Minister of International Relations, Francophonie and Foreign Trade, Mr. Jean-François Lisée. 
"In the current economic context, it is essential to strengthen our already fruitful relationships with our leading partners. The United States receives nearly 70% of our international exports. It is, by far, our main business partner. The Quebec government is committed to continuing and even deepening its commercial and economic ties with the United States, particularly with New York State, in order to develop mutual prosperity, "said the Prime Minister. For this purpose, several private meetings are planned on the agenda of this economic mission. 
The passage of the Prime Minister will also be an opportunity to open the first Expansion Québec offices, an incubator at the service of Quebec companies seeking to set up on the US market. 
Pauline Marois will also deliver an address at the invitation of Foreign Policy Association to present the economic priorities of the Quebec government. Friday, the Prime Minister will share with experts to discuss the post-election political and economic context in the United States.
