Quebec- The Quebec Prime Minister and MP from Charlevoix-Côte-de-Beaupré, Pauline Marois, welcomes the entry into force of the new MRC Development and Development Scheme of the Côte-de-Beaupré. This scheme received a favorable opinion from the Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and the Occupation of the Territory, Sylvain Gaudreault. 
"The entry into force of the planning scheme will create more dynamic and sustainable living environments that meet the needs of the Côte-de-Beaupré citizens. The main orientations put forward by the MRC will result in optimal use of its resources, while ensuring a harmonious development of its territory, "said Pauline Marois. 
This is the first planning scheme adopted in accordance with the Metropolitan Development and Development Plan (PMAD) of the Quebec City Metropolitan Community (CMQ). Recall that the schemes in force in the CMQ territory must be modified to take into account the provisions of the PMAD. 
"I would like to congratulate the MRC of Côte-de-Beaupré for the quality of the work done. As a member of Parliament for the riding, I am very proud of the efforts you have made to clear the planning of your territory's planning to that of the CMQ. This result shows the willingness of local elected officials to work sustainably for the development and preservation of the riches of this territory, "concluded the Prime Minister.
