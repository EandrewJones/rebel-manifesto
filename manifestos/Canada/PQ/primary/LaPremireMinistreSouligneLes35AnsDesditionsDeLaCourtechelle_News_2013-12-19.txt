Ms. Marois took the opportunity to emphasize the gift, by the short scale, of 500 new-foundation pounds for literacy, as part of his program reading as a gift. This program focuses on giving a new book to children for whom the report to books, within their families, is often non-existent. 
The Premier also welcomed the initiative of the President and Literacy Foundation for Literacy, Ms. Diane Mockle, for the operation to gift reading. "This campaign makes it possible to convey to children the magic of reading and feed their extraordinary curiosity. On their behalf, thank you, "said Pauline Marois.
