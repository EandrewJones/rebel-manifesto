The work done by the Quebec government prior to this $ 50,000 investment found that the majority of employers in the community do not have common resources to reduce the risks and costs associated with work accidents. where the relevance of financing a feasibility study on the creation of a mutual health and safety prevention. 
The coordination of the completion of the feasibility study was entrusted to the collective of Quebec's integration enterprises, a group whose mission is to support the social and professional integration of people seeking a better future. The work must begin in 2014-2015. 
This measure is part of the government guidelines on which the "solidarity: a wealth for Quebec" pillar is based, one of the four pillars of government action, with integrity, prosperity and identity. These guidelines, made public on October 30 by the Prime Minister Ms. Pauline Marois, will guide the government's action in the implementation of measures aimed at improving the well-being of the most vulnerable individuals and families.
