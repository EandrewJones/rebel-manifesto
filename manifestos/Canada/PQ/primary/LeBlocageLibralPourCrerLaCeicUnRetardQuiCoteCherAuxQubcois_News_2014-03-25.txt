Montréal - the candidate of the Parti Québécois in the riding of Chicoutimi and President of the Treasury Board, Stéphane Bédard, and the candidate in Jonquière and Minister of Transport, Municipal Affairs, Regions and the Occupation of the Territory, Sylvain Gaudreault, have Summer to hear Philippe Couillard minimizes the impacts of the Liberal blocking on the holding of the Public Construction Investigation Board in the Construction Industry (CEIC). 
"In just eight months, thanks to new measures, the government of the Parti Québécois saved $ 240 million to Quebecers, solely in road work. How much would we have saved if the Liberal government had listened to us from the outset? Asked Sylvain Gaudreault. 
Indeed, by the journalists at a press briefing on the consequences of the negligence of the Liberal regime to introduce the Charbonneau Commission, Philippe Couillard lamented the end of the lips the delay in the implementation of the Commission, while stating that this delay did not have any consequences. 
"All the sums we have paid too much in infrastructure projects, we will never see them again. The Liberal Party must make a serious conscience examination on the money that was wasted during these two years in which the Quebec population was waiting for its government to listen to it, "added Stéphane Bédard. 
The next government will inherit the CEIC report. "People have to trust the Parti Québécois government, which has shown that it places integrity at the center of its concerns, rather than the Liberal Party, which has done the opposite during its nine years in power," have concluded candidates. 
We have a plan and the team to succeed. Today, it's time to give us the means to go further.
