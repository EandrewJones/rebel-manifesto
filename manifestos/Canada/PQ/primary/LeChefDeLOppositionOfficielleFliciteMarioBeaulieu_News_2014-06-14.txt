Quebec- The member for Chicoutimi and Leader of the Official Opposition, Stéphane Bédard, congratulated Mario Beaulieu for his election as head of the Bloc Québécois. 
"On behalf of the entire parliamentary wing of the Parti Québécois, I extend my deepest congratulations to Mario Beaulieu for his election. When it comes to the defense of Quebec's interests in Ottawa, he knows he can rely on our support. I wish him good success in his mandate, with a view to the 2015 federal elections. 
Congratulations also to the hon. Member for Richmond-Arthabaska, André Bellavance, for the very beautiful countryside he led, "said Stéphane Bédard.
