Quebec, November 4, 2014 - The hon. Member for Chicoutimi and Leader of the Official Opposition, Stéphane Bédard, met at the end of the day the President of the French Republic, François Hollande. This interview was an opportunity to reaffirm the bonds of friendship that unite France and Quebec. 
"I am very happy to have discussed with President Holland. It is important to maintain the exceptional relationship we have with France. In this evolving world, where the challenges get complex, where the benchmarks change without shouting, in a world that continues to redefine, the sustainability of the direct and privileged relationship between France and Quebec takes its value " , said Stéphane Bédard.
