Montreal, March 8, 2020 - The Congress course held on March 7th and 8th in Montreal, the youth delegates of the Parti Québécois adopted ambitious proposals, constituting their claims at the candidates of the CHEUSFRY race. These claims will be brought by the new youth executive, elected on this occasion and led by the new president of the youth of the Parti Québécois, Alec Ordon. 
During the weekend, more than 120 young members exchanged on the proposals, filed by the Executive of Young People of the Parti Québécois, and aimed in particular to establish the foundation of an independent Quebec. Among other things, proposals to carry out a referendum on the independence of Quebec in the first mandate of a government of the Parti Québécois, to create a Quebec currency in the first ten years of a Quebec country, or at Limit to 35 hours the normal work week. 
"I have repeated it since my president: the independence of Quebec is the project of all regions, Quebeckers of all origins and generations. You are a responsible youth, bold, capable of dreaming and able to act. Quebec trusts you. The Québécois party has confidence in you. Let's continue to build together a Green Quebec, open to the world, and free. He said Dieudonné Ella Oyono, President of the Parti Québécois. 
"It's a great pride for me to see as many young people to work a weekend to imagine what the foundations of a sovereign Quebec and work concretely to the realization of this great project that is our national independence . Youth is the future of the Parti Québécois, but it is also the future of Quebec. And we will continue to work to give us the means to build it. Concluded Alec Ordon, President of the National Youth Council of the Parti Québécois.
