Quebec- The member for Sanguinet, Mr. Alain Therrien, is pleased to announce, on behalf of the Minister of Transport and Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Occupation of the Territory, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, that two subsidies totaling near $ 1.5 million are granted to the Intermunicipal Tips of Transport Roussillon and the Richelin for the operation of a collective transportation service. 
A grant of $ 484,373 is paid to the Roussillon Transport Intermunicipal Council. For its part, the Intermunicipal Transport Council The Richelain is granted $ 1,047,950. These grants are paid under the Government Assistance Program for the Public Transport of Persons (PAGTCP). The program aims to develop public transit to make it the main mode of travel. 
"Our Government encourages and supports the development of collective transport, which optimizes the use of the road network while increasing the fluidity of displacements, the decrease in the level of congestion and the reduction of pollution attributable to the consumption of hydrocarbons. . Today's announcement will allow the Montérégie region to continue its efforts in collective transport, "said MP Alain Therien. 
"By participating in the financing of collective transport, we invest in sustainable development and the mobility of people, an at the same time social, environmental and economic challenge that is at the heart of our government's mission," said Minister Sylvain Gaudreault. 
"These grants will maintain an effective transit service that meets the needs of the population served by these two intermunicipal transport councils," concluded the member for Sanguinet. 
The parameters of this program can be viewed on the website of the Ministry of Transport at the address www.mtq.gouv.qc.ca.
