Quebec- The hon. Member for Bonaventure and Parliamentary Assistant of the Minister of Sustainable Development, the Environment, Wildlife and Parks for the Fauna and Parks component, Mr. Sylvain Roy, is pleased to announce, on behalf of the Minister of Transport. and Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Occupation of the Territory, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, that a $ 150,000 grant is granted to the City of Chandler for the Improvement of the Hamilton Road in the Pabos district. 
This grant is paid as part of the Municipal Road Improvement Assistance Program (PARARM). This program helps to finance improvement projects achieved by municipalities on their network. It aims to achieve improvement projects that will achieve objectives such as improving the quality of the road, drainage and security of road users. 
"By participating in the financing of this project, our government supports the city of Chandler, giving it ways to carry out work and contribute to the well-being of its community. Today's announcement will allow the city to carry out asphalt work of the Hamilton road, thus making it possible to ensure the comfort of rolling on this section, "said the deputy Roy. 
"The announcement of this investment is a concrete gesture of our government to support municipalities in maintaining the integrity of their municipal network" concluded Mr. Roy.
