Ascension - The member for Labelle and Head of the Laurentians Region, Mr. Sylvain Pagé, is pleased to announce, on behalf of the Minister of Transport and Minister of Municipal Affairs, regions and occupation of the territory, M Sylvain Gaudreault, a $ 50,000 grant will be paid to the Club Snow Snow of the Ascension, for the construction of the Lanthier Bridge, under the Financial Assistance Program for Trail Infrastructure and Wildlife Protection . 
This program aims to financially support the implementation of training or improvement of trail infrastructures for off-road vehicles (VHR). Its objectives are to promote the durability of trails, reduce, or even eliminate, the neighborhood disadvantages related to the practice of this leisure, to repair damage caused by bad weather, to ensure the safety of VHR followers. or to promote economic benefits in the region. It also aims to finance projects about the protection of wildlife and wildlife habitat, directly related to VHR practice. 
"By this financial assistance, the government contributes directly to economic development by supporting the winter tourism of the Hautes-Laurentides. In a region that has been strongly struck by the forest crisis, it is important for us to maintain quality snowmobile trails that help support jobs in our tourism industry, "said Mr. Pagé. 
For 2012-2013, nearly $ 4 million were forwarded to snowmobile trail promoters in Quebec. In the Laurentians region, $ 140,404 was paid for the realization of 4 projects whose objective was the development or improvement of trails.
