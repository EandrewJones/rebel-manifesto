Quebec- The Quebec Prime Minister Pauline Marois, and the Minister Delegate to Tourism, Pascal Bérubé, warmly congratulate LeFestival International Jazz from Montreal, who was awarded yesterday by the Association of the Tourism Industry of Canada , the price of the event of the year 2013, delivered during the ceremony of the Grand Prix du Tourisme Canada. 
"This well-deserved honor has reflected on Montreal and all Quebec. For 35 years, the Montreal International Jazz Festival has been discontinuing us by exceptional programming, attracting the biggest names of Jazz in the metropolis and thus becoming a must in North America. The event of the event of 2013 in Canada comes to testify again with the importance of this large popular festival in Montreal's cultural, event and economic life, "said the Prime Minister. 
The price of the event of the year, presented by the Metro Toronto Convention Center, is awarded to an event or festival which testifies to the best practices of the industry in all aspects of its operations, and which constitutes an example. of excellence for the entire tourism sector. 
"Quebeckers are proud of the Montreal International Jazz Festival; It gives international visibility to the metropolis, but also to our tourist destination. Among the most popular festivals on the planet, it also contributes to the reputation of Montreal and Quebec as a whole as a festive destination par excellence, "said Minister Bérubé. 
The Canadian tourism prices are awarded annually by the Canada Tourism Industry Association to highlight success, leadership and innovation in the industry. They also reward people, places, organizations and events that have surpassed in order to offer superior tourist experiences.
