Montreal- The Minister of Culture and Communications, Maka Kotto, announces a $ 600,000 agreement agreement for the development of the Vivier in Gesù. At the amount announced today, the Quebec Arts and Letters Council will add $ 100,000 a year for the next three financial exercises thus a supplementary assistance for the operation of the Vivier. 
"Already recognized on the international scene, Quebec creators members of the Vivier Group, in the Gesù, will continue to enrich the musical scene of Montreal and Quebec entirely. This agreement will open the public access to the new musical repertoire as well as the trends of today's composers, "said Maka Kotto. 
The Group Le Vivier is a specialized broadcaster who brings together 33 organisms or emerging music sets. It offers concert programming year-round in connection with its mission to promote the development and development of creation music. The mandate of the Vivier is centered on dissemination, cultural mediation and improving artistic practice. 
The agreement of principle announced today will continue to continue the definition of the development project and finalize the financial assembly. While pursuing its mission of center of creativity, the Gesù will welcome a new cluster of creation and research in new and contemporary music. The implantation of the group the pool in a permanent place, adapted to its mission, will establish a real crossroads of the new music.
