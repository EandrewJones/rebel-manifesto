Quebec- The member for Saint-Jérôme and Leader of the Official Opposition, Pierre Karl Péladeau, and the member for Rousseau and spokesman for the Parti Québécois in finance and income, Nicolas Marceau, require the Prime Minister Advance The deposit of the government's economic and financial update, in order to take stock of the effect of economic deterioration on the Quebec budget. 
"During the summer, he lost nearly 12,000 jobs in Quebec, while he was created in the rest of Canada. Montreal is even considered the capital of unemployment! Since their arrival, the Prime Minister and his team have been strictly offering anything in terms of economic development or job creation. Philippe Couillard must stop saying that everything is fine, take stock and act to get the economy out of the marasm, "said the leader of the official opposition. 
Nicolas Marceau has argued that the budget forecasts do not hold anymore: "In addition to the jobs lost this summer, GDP has further declined by 0.5% in May, after declining 0.3% in April and 0.1% in March. These bad news led Desjardins to reduce its growth forecast for Quebec, setting it at only 1.3% for 2015. The government is based on 2% growth, which does not take absolutely The road, "he worried. 
Even the Ministry of Finance confirmed last week a deficit of a half billion, after only two months of the 2015 financial year, as well as an important delay on the side of the autonomous income, while we should, according to the Liberals , we find in a budget balance. 
"Our economy deteriorates; This is what all these bad news indicates. It is important to know quickly and exactly where we are. It is therefore necessary that the economic and financial update must be filed as quickly as possible, "concluded Pierre Karl Péladeau.
