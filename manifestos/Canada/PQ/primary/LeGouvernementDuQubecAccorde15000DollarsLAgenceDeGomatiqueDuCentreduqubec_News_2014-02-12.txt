Quebec- The Minister of Sustainable Development, Environment, Wildlife and Parks, Minister responsible for the Center-du-Québec Region and MP Johnson, Mr. Yves-François Blanchet, on behalf of the Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Agriculture, Fisheries and Food, Mr. François Gendron, is pleased to announce that $ 15,000 financial assistance is granted to the Center-du-Québec Geomatics Agency for the Prevention of erosion of agricultural land. This assistance comes from the Program Support for Agriculture and Agri-Food Development in the Region, which aims to support regional projects contributing to the adaptation and development of agriculture and agri-food. 
"It's always nice to support organizations that, like the Center-du-Québec Geomatics Agency, carry out projects to improve the working methods of agricultural producers in the region," said François Gendron. 
"Such support from the Government of Quebec to an organization of the Center-du-Québec fills me with pride. This aid will allow agricultural advisers to better prepare their field interventions and go to meet agricultural producers in order to offer them agri-environmental solutions adapted to their soil erosion problems, "said Yves-François Blanket 
"Projects like this make it possible to encourage producers to adopt production modes in harmony with the environment and promote the sustainable development of our agriculture, objectives consistently concordant with the pale of food sovereignty will last May," added the Gendron Minister. 
With investments of $ 2.3 billion and an annual food demand of nearly $ 34 billion generating 475,000 jobs, the bioalimentary industry is a powerful engine of Quebec's economy.
