"This is a compactive activity that greatly contributes to improving the know-how and the growth of knowledge and skills in our microbreweries. Speakers, both in Quebec and abroad, participate in the annual conference this year. They will address different facets of the job and many aspects of the functioning of a brewing company. Everything is implemented to maximize knowledge transfer as well as networking with these guests or with the establishments they represent. In addition, the ambq organizes on this occasion the "supplier trade fair" that takes place of exchange for microbrewers and suppliers of products, services and expertise and which aims to consolidate or develop relations of 'business. 
The ambq was created in 1990. Its mission is to bring together Quebec microbreweries and ensures promoting and defending their common interests. The association includes among its members 33 brewer companies. These give jobs to more than 800 people in Quebec. 
Recall that with investments of $ 2.3 billion and an annual food demand of nearly $ 34 billion supporting 475,000 jobs, the bioalmic industry is a powerful engine of Quebec's economy.
