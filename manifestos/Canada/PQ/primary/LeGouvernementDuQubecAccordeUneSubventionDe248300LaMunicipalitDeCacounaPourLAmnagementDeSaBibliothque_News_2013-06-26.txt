Cacoua- The Minister of Culture and Communications, Maka Kotto, today announced the award of a $ 248,300 grant for the development of the Cacouna Library in the sacristy of the Church of Saint-Georges . The Mayor of the municipality of Cacouna, Ghislaine Daris, also participated in the event. 
"The planning project of the municipal library in the sacristy of the Church of Saint-Georges is a model for the reappropriation of religious buildings. I congratulate those who have had the idea of putting this heritage place at the service of culture and knowledge, for a whole community, "said Minister Kotto. 
"The importance of keeping a classified heritage building and finding a community and cultural vocation was the fundamental idea. With this library project, the entire community can take advantage of this cultural place while preserving its architecture, "added Daris mayor. 
The grant will redevelop the premises, enlarge the sacristy to add an entry and make the building accessible to people with reduced mobility and in accordance with the construction code. The project costs, valued at $ 496,634, will be shared equally between the municipality and the Ministry of Culture and Communications, under the Program Assistance to Capitalization.
