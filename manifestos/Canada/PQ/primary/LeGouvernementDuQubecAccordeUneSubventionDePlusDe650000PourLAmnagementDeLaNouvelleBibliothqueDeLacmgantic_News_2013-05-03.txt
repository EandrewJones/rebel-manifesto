Lac-Mégantic- On behalf of the Minister of Culture and Communications, Maka Kotto, the member for Sherbrooke, Serge Cardin, accompanied by the Mayor of the City of Lac-Mégantic, Colette Roy Laroche, today announced that the government From Québec attributes $ 653,882 for the moving to the Lac-Mégantic Library in the Old Factory Building. 
"The realization of this library development project in Lac-Mégantic will be an advantage for access to culture, information and knowledge by all citizens of the region. It is more than the award of a grant that we announce today is an investment for the future in what constructed, student and promotes our identity: culture and knowledge, "said the Minister Maka Kotto. 
"I want to highlight the commitment of municipal elected officials with respect to this planning project of a municipal library with a college center. By relying on the recycling of an industrial building, the former Canadian factory, the city gives a good example of safeguarding and developing a heritage that belongs to the history of Lac-Mégantic and that contributed to its Economic Development, "said MP Serge Cardin. 
The $ 653,882 grant awarded by the Ministry of Culture and Communications is paid under the capital program. The City of Lac-Mégantic completes the financial assembly of this project estimated at more than $ 1,307,764.
