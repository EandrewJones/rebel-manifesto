London- On the occasion of an economic union in Europe, the Premier of Quebec, Pauline Marois, announced the implantation in Montreal Decinsite Inc., a new company that will be charged with North American activities of the company London Cinese VFX, specializing in the production of visual effects and animations for cinema and television. 
The new Cinesite studio in Montreal will create 200 jobs, and this figure could reach 250 by 2018. The establishment of this studio will result in capital investments, by the company, $ 6.7 million, and the payroll will be $ 42.4 million. Cinesite also plans to carry out R & D activities in production techniques as well as activities for creating animated films. The Government of Quebec, through Investissement Québec, grants a non-interest loan from $ 1.2 million to Cinesite inc. for the realization of his project. 
"I am proud that Cinesite chooses Quebec as a key partner in North America. The creation of sustainable jobs in Quebec is the priority of economic lapolitics of my government. To achieve our goal of creating 43,000 quality jobs by 2017, we support the realization of major economic projects, such as the establishment in Montreal de Cinesite inc., Which evolves in a growing sector, that of visual effects. The metropolis will thus count in its ranks a strategic company for its film and television industry, and its video game industry; A company that could attract in Québec more cinematographic filming and multimedia production, "said Pauline Marois.
