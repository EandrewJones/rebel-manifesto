Already in London and New York, the White Star Capital Fund carries out investments in priming and starting companies in the information and communications technology industry (ICT). The establishment of White Star in Montreal will promote the channeling of foreign capital to ICT companies and will attract in Quebec world-class businesses. This fund is based on an international network that can facilitate the contribution of foreign capital for Quebec companies. Those that will be funded by White Star will therefore be able to benefit from better positioning internationally, which will be profitable for all of our ICT sector. 
"As evidenced by its importance in the 2013-2017 industrial plant, the Quebec ICT industry is a real economic engine for Quebec. It contributes to the influence of our expertise and our ability to innovate, and this, all over the world. The White Star Capital Fund is a privileged opportunity to promote the start of visionary and dynamic companies in this growing industry, "said Pauline Marois.
