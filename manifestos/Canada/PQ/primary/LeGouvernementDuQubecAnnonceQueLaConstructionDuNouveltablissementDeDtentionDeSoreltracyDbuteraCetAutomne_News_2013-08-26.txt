Sorel-Tracy- The Minister of Public Safety, Stéphane Bergeron, with the member for Richelieu and Minister Delegate to Industrial Policy and the Quebec Economic Development Bank, Ms. élain Zakaïb, announced that the construction of the New detention facility in Sorel-Tracy will begin in this fall and, for this purpose, that a 30-year partnership agreement was signed with the Horizon Justice Sorel-Tracy (HJST) consortium. 
"I am proud of the efforts made by the many people who work with the realization of this project, which can finally be realized, thus boosting the economy of the region, to better meet the needs and functional requirements. Security, in addition to providing a better work environment for the shelter staff of Sorel-Tracy, "said Minister Bergeron. 
The new Sorel-Tracy detention facility aims to replace the current establishment that is obsolete by a new building that will accommodate 300 offenders, offering 211 additional places. The financing, management, construction and maintenance of the building will be provided by HJST for a period of 30 years, which is an investment in the order of $ 265.7 million. The Department will remain the sole responsibility of the offending persons entrusted to it. 
"As you know, I followed very closely the evolution of this file which represents a major issue for the city of Sorel-Tracy and for the economic development of the entire region. The 300 jobs generated by this project during the work will create wealth not only in the short term, but also long-term with more than 155 new ongoing jobs, "said Minister Zakaïb. 
The contribution of the Ministry of Public Safety to this project is part of the 2013-2023 which will eventually lead to the $ 92.3 billion investment for the government of the Government of which more than 1 billion $ for public safety institutions. This plan will have a major economic impact throughout Québec, with nearly 60,000 jobs created or maintained on average per year.
