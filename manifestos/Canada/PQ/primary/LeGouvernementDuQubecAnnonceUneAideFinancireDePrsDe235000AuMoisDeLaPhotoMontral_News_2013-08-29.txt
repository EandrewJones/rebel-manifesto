Montréal- The Minister of Culture and Communications, Maka Kotto, and the Minister of International Relations, Francophonie and Foreign Trade and Minister responsible for the Montreal Region, Jean-François Lisé, announce the assignment of aid $ 234,600 in support of the photo in Montreal 2013, which will take place from September 5th to October 5th, under the theme Drone: the automated image. 
"The biennial The month of the photo offers the public a bold program composed of original works by artists from here and elsewhere. Thanks to the free exhibitions presented in 14 mailing places in Montreal, the event is a beautiful showcase for the discovery of this contemporary art that has adapted, marrying traditional techniques to new technologies, "said Minister Kotto. 
"The month of the photo in Montreal offers the Montreal population and visitors an ideal opportunity to enjoy exclusive photographic works or which are presented for the first time in the country. This exhibition comes across several Montreal places, making the metropolis a real territory of exploration. I am proud to support this eminently creative and innovative cultural event, "added the minister. 
The contribution of the Government of Quebec is distributed as follows: $ 160,000 comes from the Support for Operation - National and International Events for Digital Arts, Visual Arts, Cinema and Video, Crafts and Architectural Research of the Council of Arts and Letters of Quebec, $ 60,000 from the Metropolitan Initiative and Radiation Fund, administered by the Secretariat to the Metropolitan Area, and $ 14,600 of the Ministry of Culture Projects Program and communications.
