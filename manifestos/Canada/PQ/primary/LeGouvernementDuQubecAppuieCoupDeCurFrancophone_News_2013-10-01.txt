From one year to the next, the Francophone Coup de Coeur Festival and its tour create incredible effervescence in Montreal and dozens of Canadian cities. With its 350 Quebec artists and musicians, Canadians who occur on Montreal scenes, then on tour, it's a real wave of Francophone songs that sweeps, for the delight of an audience always and still conquered, after 26 years. 
"Quebec is recognized as a talent nursery of all kinds. The quality and diversity of our song artisans impress the most critical audiences around the world. Francophone coup de cœur has understood it well and put on this creativity to feed our spirits. He even became an inspiring model as to the development of alliances between Quebec broadcasters and elsewhere, "said Minister Kotto.
