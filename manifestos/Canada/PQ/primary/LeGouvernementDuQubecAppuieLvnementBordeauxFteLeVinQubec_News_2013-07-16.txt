Quebec- The Minister of Labor, Employment and Social Solidarity, Minister responsible for the Status of Women and Minister responsible for the Capitale-Nationale and Chaudière-Appalaches, Ms. Agnès Maltais, and the Minister Delegate to Tourism , Mr. Pascal Bérubé, are pleased to announce the award of $ 80,000 financial assistance to support the Bordeaux celebrates the wine in Quebec City will take place from 29 August to the next 1 weeks. 
For its second year, Bordeaux celebrates the wine in Quebec City offers a variety of activities that will allow all lovers of wine and gastronomy to discover the fineness of Bordeaux wines and to taste regional flavor products. Participants will therefore be able to browse the various pavilions erected for this occasion, enjoy gourmet kiosks, participate in training workshops, and finally, attend a wide variety of shows. In short, there will be for all tastes. 
"This year again, the population and visitors of the City of Quebec will have the pleasure of discovering this event that highlights the wealth of Quebec soil as well as the dynamism of our producers. It is with great pride that the Quebec government therefore associates with this popular appointment which, for many, constitutes a must of the summer season, "mentioned Minister Maltese. 
"Bordeaux celebrates the wine in Quebec City is an exceptional event that has diversified and enrich the tourist offer of the region. This event mobilizes important crowds come to revel on Bordeaux products and our terroir, while enjoying Quebec City's many tourist attractions, "said Minister Bérubé. 
Government assistance is as follows: $ 60,000 stem from the tourism industry development program to the festivals and tourist events, Tourisme Québec, and the Secretariat at the Capitale-Nationale contributes for an amount of $ 20,000.
