Sherbrooke- The Minister of Health and Social Services, Minister responsible for Elders and Minister responsible for the Estrie Region, Mr. Réjean Hébert, on behalf of the Minister of Transport and Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and the Occupation of the territory, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, accompanied by the member for Sherbrooke, Mr. Serge Cardin, confirmed this morning the government's willingness to complete the extension of Highway 410 south of Sherbrooke. 
"This project remains a priority for my government. Thus, I am pleased to inform you that the project component 2 was included in the Infrastructure Plan, "said Mr. Hébert. The department can therefore prepare the project, which provides for the construction of a section between Highway 108/143 in Lennoxville to the East Sector, facing the Federal Experimental Farm of Agriculture and Agri-Food Canada, in Respect for the Framework Policy on Governance of Major Public Infrastructure Projects ".
