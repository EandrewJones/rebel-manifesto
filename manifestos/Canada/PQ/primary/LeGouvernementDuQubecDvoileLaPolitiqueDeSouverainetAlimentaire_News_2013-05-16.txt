Baie-Saint-Paul- It is in the presence of several partners in the bioalimentary sector that the Prime Minister of Quebec, Pauline Marois, and the Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Agriculture, Fisheries and Food, Fran√ßois Gendron , unveiled the first food sovereignty of Quebec. 
"Today are gathered the government, the agricultural production sector and the food sector. I hope that together, we work for at least 50% of Quebeckers' products to be products from home. It is the whole economy of Quebec who will win, "said the premier. 
For Mrs. Marois, the concept of food sovereignty is well adapted to Quebec reality. "We gave it a color reflecting the aspirations and expectations of artisans in our bioalimentary sector. For us, for Quebec, food sovereignty, it is first of all the capacity of the Quebec state to define its own agricultural and food policy according to the interests of its population. Food sovereignty favors local production as well as access to land and resources to achieve this without excluding trade and exports, "she added.
