Lac-Mégantic- Following the judgment accepting the Company Application Montreal, Maine & Atlantique Canada Cie and Montreal, Maine & Atlantic Railway Ltd to be under the protection of the Companies 'Creditors' Arrangements Act, the Government of the Government Quebec and the City of Lac-Mégantic have decided to entrust the coordination of cleaning operations to the Pomerleau firm. The announcement was made in the presence of the Minister of Health and Social Services, and Minister responsible for the Estrie Region, Mr. Réjean Hébert and the Mayor of Lac-Mégantic, Ms. Colette Roy-Laroche. 
The firm Pomerleau will be responsible, among other things, to coordinate all cleaning and characterization operations as well as the removal of existing railways. 
"The Government of Quebec and the City of Lac-Mégantic thus take the appropriate measures, so that the work continues without interruption in Lac-Mégantic and the Chaudière River. As it has been done since the beginning, all the necessary workforce and equipment will be deployed to ensure effective and concerted action of the different partners in the field by providing the population of Lac Mégantic the best possible support, "supported the minister.

The Government, as well as the City of Lac-Mégantic will provide sustained surveillance of the construction realized. Similarly, throughout the process, the consultation will be the motto between the designated firm and the ministries as well as the municipalities concerned. Thus, the various components of the work will be harmonized and scheduled for the ultimate stage of the reconstruction of the city center.
