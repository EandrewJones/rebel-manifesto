Quebec- Québec Minister of Quebec, Pauline Marois, and the Minister of Education, Recreation and Sport, Marie Malavoy, are keen to congratulate Alex Harvey for his performance at the World Cross-Country Ski Championships in Italy . The Quebec founder won the bronze medal during the classic Sprint race. 
"Over the years, Alex Harvey has shown courage and determination to overcome the challenges that are inevitably on the course of each high-level athlete. He demonstrated an impressive force of character. Despite an injury to the shoulder shortly before the competition, he got up and gave everything he had. His efforts have been rewarded today. Quebeckers can be proud of this young inspiring athlete from Saint-Ferréol-les-Neiges, which is proof that assiduous work and self-succeeding are the key to success. Congratulations! "Said Pauline Marois. 
"I would like to convey my most sincere congratulations to Alex Harvey. His performance of today testifies to a tremendous talent, but also an incredible determination. It is a true model of perseverance for young people who aspire one day to follow his traces. The Government of Quebec is proud to support it in its quest for the highest peaks! ", Concluded Minister Malavoy.
