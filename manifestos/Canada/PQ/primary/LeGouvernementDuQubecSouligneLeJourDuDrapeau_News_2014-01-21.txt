Quebec- The Minister of Justice and Minister responsible for the application of the Flag and Emblems of Quebec, Mr. Bertrand St-Arnaud, participated today at the ceremony highlighting the day of the flag, in the presence of several Dignitaries and parliamentarians. 
"There is no stronger symbol for a nation than its flag. This flag, it is up to all Quebeckers and all Quebeckers, regardless of our origins, our beliefs, our political choices. He gathers us, "said Minister Bertrand St-Arnaud. 
It was at 15 hours at the hotel of Parliament that the minister paid tribute to the Quebec flag, as the tradition is. Across Quebec, municipalities have also been invited to emphasize the day of the flag. 
"Since 1948, this flag has accompanied us in all stages of our national development, whether social, cultural, economic and democratic. He testifies to our story, but he also evokes the present and he celebrates the future. Let's be proud of our flag and all it represents, "added Minister Bertrand St-Arnaud. 
On January 21, 1948, at 3 pm, the flowered flag was hoisted for the first time at the top of the Central Tower of the Hotel du Parliament, replacing the British flag, the Union Jack. That day, by decree, Quebec granted the status of official flag. For more information, the population is invited to consult the flag website at www.drapeau.gouv.qc.ca.
