Lac-Mégantic- The Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Occupation of the Territory and Minister of Transport, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, is pleased to announce that the Government of Quebec will support the City of Lac-Mégantic in the acquisition of Buildings and disused lands of the disaster area. 
"Since the tragic events that shook the City of Lac-Mégantic, the Quebec government has always been present to support the community. The commitment made today is one step further towards the reconstruction of the city and will help the owners who have been experienced by tragedy, "said Sylvain Gaudreault. 
The parameters and modalities of this government assistance will be established in collaboration with the City of Lac-Mégantic. Preliminary assessments indicate that the cost of acquisition by the municipality could reach about twenty million dollars.
