Quebec- The Minister of International Relations, Francophonie and Foreign Trade and Minister responsible for the Montreal Region, Jean-François Lisée, and the Minister Delegate to Tourism, Pascal Bérubé, are pleased to announce the allocation of a $ 184,500 Financial Assistance to support the presentation of the festivalHeavy MTL. 
From 9 to 11 August 2013, a series of concerts, featuring the greatest names of rock and Heavy Metal, will be presented at Jean-Drapeau Park on the island of St. Hélène, as well as the Montreal Metropolis. 
"Festival growing, Heavy MTL attracts crowds and enjoys good media coverage. Our financial support aims to encourage the development of the festival, since it helps to promote the reputation of Montreal as a festive city, "said the minister listed. 
"I welcome the audacity of the members of the organization, who have made this gathering a must-see appointment for all those who love the Heavy Metal. This event testifies well from the diversity and authenticity that animate Montreal and make it a desired destination, "said Minister Bérubé. 
The contribution of the Government of Quebec is distributed as follows: $ 94,500 comes from the initiative and influence of the metropolis, administered by the secretariat to the metropolitan area, and $ 90,000 of the tourism industry development program Financial at the festivals and tourist events, Tourisme Québec.
