Quebec- With the support of the various police officers of Quebec, the Minister of Public Safety, Mr. Stéphane Bergeron, calls for the adoption of a unanimous motion of the National Assembly so that the funding of the Fund for the Recruitment of Police prolonged permanently, beyond March 31, 2013 
This program has so far provided crucial financial support to police organizations for the establishment of Squads specialized in the fight against crime as well as the maintenance of more than 100 police officers assigned to these squads. This is an important issue for public safety in Quebec. 
"The cessation of this funding program would be serious of consequences. The federal government can not booster to fight organized crime and, of the same breath, threaten the sustainability. As a result, we ask the Federal Minister of Public Safety, Mr. Vic Toews, to re-evaluate his position and to ensure full, sustainable and long-term funding from this fund, "said Minister Bergeron.
