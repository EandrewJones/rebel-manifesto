Québec- quickly following one of the commitments set out in the opening speech, the Government announces the creation of the Nordic Development Secretariat, whose mandate will be to coordinate the actions of the entire government apparatus to respond to to the needs of Nordic communities and to ensure coherently northern development. 
In addition, the Ministerial Action Group, chaired by the Prime Minister, and supported by the Ministry of Finance and the Economy, the Ministry of Natural Resources and the Future Bank for Economic Development, will closely follow the major economic projects on The territory of Northern Quebec. 
Located administratively within the Ministry of the Executive Board, the Nordic Development Secretariat will be under the responsibility of the Minister of Natural Resources, Martine Ouellet, who will be seconded by the Minister responsible for the North Coast and Nord-du-Québec Regions. , Alexandre Cloutier, the member for Duplessis, Lorraine Richard, the member for René-Lévesque, Marjolan dujour, and the member for Ungava, Luc Ferland. The Secretariat will carry out the necessary links with departments and agencies to provide appropriate monitoring on requests that will be made. 
"Northern development will enable Québec to register among nations that have taken the turn of sustainable development, environmentally friendly and communities," said Pauline Marois during his opening speech. However, to ensure the coherence of the development of such a vast territory as Northern Quebec, the coordination of the shares of the entire governmental apparatus is essential. The government puts in place the appropriate tools for Quebec to move forward with northern development, while ensuring that this development is beneficial for all.
