Quebec- Passage to Rimouski, the Minister of Sustainable Development, Environment, Wildlife and Parks, Mr. Yves-François Blanchet, announced the projects that were selected for the Bas-Saint-Laurent region in the Framework for the responsibility component and enhancement - regional envelope, reinvestment in the field of wildlife. 
"By this reinvestment of public funds, the government intends to strengthen the implementation of sustainable funds and wildlife activities to make one of the engine dynamism engines," said Minister Blanchet.
