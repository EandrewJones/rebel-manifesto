Témiscouata-sur-Le-Lac- The Minister Delegate to Tourism and Minister responsible for the Bas-Saint-Laurent Region, Mr. Pascal Bérubé, announces the award of $ 10,000 financial assistance to the CartonFoliestai event will hold June 21-23. 
For one thirteenth year, the organizers have concocted a variety of programming, including activities for the family, musical performances and a village for children. Based on a unique concept, cartonfolies include boat racing and cardboard tat, cartonlympiades and contest of cardboard works. 
"The cardboard industry is an integral part of the history of the Témiscouata region. This festive event helps to pay tribute to the population that mobilized in the 1970s to maintain this economic activity of jobs. Cartonfolies offer an unforgettable experience, where the spirit of local culture and the reception of Témiscouatins will charm all visitors. I also invite visitors to enjoy this holiday to discover the region and its many attractions, "said Minister Bérubé. 
The financial contribution of Tourisme Québec comes from the program of support for the development and promotion of tourism - financial voletaid at festivals and tourist events. 
Through concrete actions such as the financing of festivals and events, the Government of Quebec supports the completion of initiatives that contribute to the economic and tourist development of the Bas-Saint-Laurent region.
