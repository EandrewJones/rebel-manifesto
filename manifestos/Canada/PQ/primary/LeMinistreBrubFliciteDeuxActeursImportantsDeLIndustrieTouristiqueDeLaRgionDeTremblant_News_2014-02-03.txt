Québec - Minister Delegate to Tourism, Mr. Pascal Bérubé, congratulates two important actors in the Tremblant area of the Tremblant region for the honors they received lately. 
As a first step, in its "Top 10 Most Ski Resorts" ranking, USA Today has awarded the sixth place of its world ranking at Mont-Tremblant station, which celebrates this year its 75th anniversary. Modern infrastructures, the immensity of the wooded territory and the unique charm of the tourist village of Mont-Tremblant are some of the reasons that justify the position of the Lanaudish ski resort in the ranking. 
"I am extremely proud to see a Quebec ski resort listed in this ranking. The Government intends to make Quebec one of the world's leading tourist destinations, and this honor that is conferred from us by USA Today has reinforced the reputation of Quebec in this respect, "said Minister Bérubé. 
In a second step, the Ironman Mont-Tremblant team received the "Best Wide Triathlon Race" award, awarded by the Triathlon Business International, at the Annual Conference of the Organization held on January 26-28 Last, California. 
In this sense, Minister Bérubé emphasized the significant intake of major sporting events in Quebec's tourism development. "Sport tourism is a segment of the industry that is full effervescence and on which Quebec has to structure its event offering and to develop a new clientele. This international recognition, awarded to an event from here, will help to shine our destination abroad and strengthen the credibility of Quebec as a sports destination par excellence. " 
Located north of Montreal, Laurentians are one of the first resorts in Quebec. Renowned for its ski resorts, its vast cross-country ski network and high-level golf courses, Laurentians offer a range of activities, attracting visitors from here and elsewhere, summer and winter.
