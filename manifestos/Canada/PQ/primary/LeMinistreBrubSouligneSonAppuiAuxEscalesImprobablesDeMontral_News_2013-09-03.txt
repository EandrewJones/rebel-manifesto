Québec - Minister Delegate to Tourism, Pascal Bérubé, is pleased to announce the award of $ 7,500 unlikely MontréalQui $ 7,500 will take place from 3 to 14 September 2013. 
For this 10th edition, the unlikely stops of Montreal propose a creative and rewarding program that features the talents of many artists from here and elsewhere. Festival-goers will be able to attend visual arts exhibitions, different dance and music shows of all kinds, plays and more. 
"This gathering in the metropolis is an excellent opportunity for the public to interact with artists. I invite people in the region and everywhere to participate in large numbers about this unique event. This artistic event contributes to positioning Montreal as an important cultural and artistic destination. I would like to thank the organizing committee, artists and volunteers for their initiative in the realization of this unmissable event, "said Minister Bérubé. 
The financial assistance granted by Tourisme Québec comes from the tourism industry development program - financial assistance to festivals and tourist events.
