Quebec- The Minister of Sustainable Development, Environment, Wildlife and Parks, Mr. Daniel Breton, will participate in the meeting of the Canadian Council of Ministers of the Environment (CCME) to be held on October 11 in Lake Louise , in Alberta. 
At this meeting, the ministers of the environment plan to discuss the implementation of a pan-Canadian air quality management system. 
They also intend to decide on the pan-Canadian approach to the management of biosolids from the purification of wastewater and on industry commitments to reduce the ecological footprint of packaging. 
"I go to Lake Louise in a spirit of collaboration with a view to advancing important environmental files for Quebec, while aiming at the coherence of the interventions of the two levels of government in the field of the environment and the respect of the competences of the Quebec in this area ", concluded Minister Breton.
