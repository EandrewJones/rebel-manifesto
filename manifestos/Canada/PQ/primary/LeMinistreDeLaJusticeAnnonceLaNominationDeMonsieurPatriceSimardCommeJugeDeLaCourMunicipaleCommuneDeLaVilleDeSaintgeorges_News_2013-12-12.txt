Quebec- The Minister of Justice of Quebec, Mr. Bertrand St-Arnaud, announces the appointment of Mr. Patrice Simard as a judge of the municipal court of the city of Saint-Georges. 
Mr Patrice Simard is holding a bachelor's degree in Laval University. Since admission to the bar in 1994, he has practiced privately in various areas including municipal law and criminal law. Since October 10, 2013, he is also a judge of the Municipal Court of the MRC de la Côte-de-Beaupré.
