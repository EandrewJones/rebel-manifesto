Quebec- The Minister of Justice and Attorney General of Quebec, Mr. Bertrand St-Arnaud, today tabled the National Assembly the report of the Judges Compensation Committee. 
The Committee on the Remuneration of Judges, established under Laloi on the courts, was set up on April 1, 2013. Chaired by Mr. Michel Clair, he is formed of the Honorable Claire L'Heureux-Dubé, of the Hon. Pierre-A. Michaud, the Honorable André Forget and Madame Julie Gosselin. 
The mandate of the Committee was to assess whether the treatment, the pension plan and the other social benefits of the Judges of the Court of Quebec and the judges of the peace magistrates are adequate. It had to do the same for treatment, other benefits and, where appropriate, the pension plan for municipal judges. 
LALOI ON JUDICIAL TribunalsPrequit that the National Assembly may, by reasoned resolution, approve, modify or reject, in whole or in part, the recommendations made by the Committee in its report. This resolution must be adopted no later than the 30th day of the meeting following the filing of the report. 
The report of the Judges Compensation Committee can be consulted on the website of the Ministry of Justice: www.justice.gouv.qc.ca.
