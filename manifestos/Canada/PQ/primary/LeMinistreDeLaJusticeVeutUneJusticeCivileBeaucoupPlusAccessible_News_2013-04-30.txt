Changes to current rules are proposed to make civil justice much more accessible, while preserving the right of all parties to assert their rights to the Tribunal. Thus, in order to reduce justice time, the bill emphasizes the amicable modes of conflict resolution, such as mediation or conciliation, more user-friendly, accessible and rapid modes. 
In addition, for the parties opting for the traditional judicial means, the draft new Code of Civil Procedure provides that the applications, the procedural acts and the evidence must be proportionate to the nature and complexity of the case.
