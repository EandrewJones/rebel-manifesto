Quebec- The Minister of Public Security, Mr. Stéphane Bergeron, proceeded today, with the member for Bertrand and Vice-President of the National Assembly, Claude Cousineau, as well as the Director General of the Security of Quebec, Mr Mario Laprise, at the inauguration of the new position of Quebec Security of the MRC des Laurentides. 
Located at 501 Léonard Street, in Sainte-Agathe-des-Monts, the new position consolidates Quebec's security position in the MRC and provides police with a state-of-the-art work environment. 
"By fighting day after day, both to prevent that to circumscribe and reduce crime in the territory, the police and police contribute, with the expertise and dedication that they are known to them, to ensure a safe environment for the whole Population of the MRC des Laurentides. I am convinced that this new position of Québec's security has already positive effects on police interventions, for the benefit of the population of the region, "said Minister Bergeron. 
Built at a cost of $ 6.1 million and with respect for the environment, the position of the MRC des Laurentides, is on track to obtain LEED certification (Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design). "This new building represents the commitment of Québec's security to offer high quality services to the people it serves, but also to contribute to the improvement of the quality of life of citizens, as well as its employees. , through sustainable development, "said Mario Laprise. 
The new building houses 53 police officers and 6 civilian members, uniting their expertise to provide police coverage adapted to the needs of the population. It will gather in one roof the administration, the gendarmerie, the criminal investigations as well as premises of detention and storage.
