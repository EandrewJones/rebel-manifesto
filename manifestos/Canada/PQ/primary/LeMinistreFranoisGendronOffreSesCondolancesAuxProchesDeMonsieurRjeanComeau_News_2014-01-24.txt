Quebec- The Deputy Prime Minister, Minister of Agriculture, Fisheries and Food and Minister responsible for the Abitibi-Témiscamingue Region, Mr. François Gendron, wants to present his condolences to the family and relatives From Mr. Réjean Comeau, a pillar of the agricultural sector of Abitibi-Témiscamingue, who died on January 22nd. 
"Réjean Comeau has undoubtedly marked the agricultural world of our region by its dynamism and by its great ability to rally the sector's strength around the major issues we faced. I know it was greatly appreciated by all those who had the privilege of rubbing it over 30 years of engagement in agricultural unionism, "said the minister. 
President of the Abitibi-Témiscamingue UPA Federation from 2008 to 2011, Mr. Réjean Comeau also assured the Chair of the Macamic UPA Basic Union from 1999 to 2008. 
"To his family and his loved ones, I offer my most sincere condolences," said Francois Gendron.
