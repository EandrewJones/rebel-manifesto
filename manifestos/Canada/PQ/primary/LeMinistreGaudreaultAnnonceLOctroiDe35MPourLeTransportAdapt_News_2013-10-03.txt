Quebec- The Minister of Transport and Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Occupation of the Territory, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, is pleased to grant $ 3.5 million from the Transportation Government Assistance Program Suitable for people with disabilities. He announced the announcement at this afternoon, in Gatineau, on the occasion of the annual symposium of the Quebec Urban Transport Association. This amount will allow the hundred transportation agencies to deal with the continued growth of demand. 
"Nearly 95,000 people with disabilities have access to a transportation service adapted to Quebec, and this number continues to grow over the years. For our government, it is essential to enable the organizations to better meet the needs of these people to ensure a degree of mobility and autonomy comparable to that of the general population, "says Gaudreault.
