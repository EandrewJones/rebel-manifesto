Quebec- Minister of Health and Social Services, Minister Responsible for Elders and Minister responsible for the Estrie Region, Dr. Réjean Hébert, today announced the addition of additional professional resources for five medicine groups. Family (GMF) of the Estrie region. These GMF will share a total of 16 full-time equivalents (FTEs) to complement their professional workforce. This represents for Estrie an annual investment of $ 1,194,496. 
"I am pleased that GMFs in the region can benefit from the addition of health professionals such as nutritionists, social workers or kinesiologists. In addition to supporting GMF medical practice, this will improve the accessibility and continuity of medical care and services to patients, particularly those with chronic diseases, "said Minister Réjean Hébert. 
"The consolidation of the GMF network is one of the priority actions of our government in order to have a strong and accessible line. We are committed to achieving a series of measures to this effect, and the allocation of new resources for the most successful GMFs, "he added. 
Note that this addition of professionals is determined based on the performance of GMF, according to various criteria, including the respect and renewal of their contractual agreement, the achievement of their clientele enrollment objectives, as well as their offer of service. unfavorable hours (evenings and weekends) relative to the number of patients enrolled. 
At the national level, additional 181.5 FTEs will be distributed between 60 GMF, for an average of 3 FTEs each. This addition represents an annual investment of $ 13.6 million.
