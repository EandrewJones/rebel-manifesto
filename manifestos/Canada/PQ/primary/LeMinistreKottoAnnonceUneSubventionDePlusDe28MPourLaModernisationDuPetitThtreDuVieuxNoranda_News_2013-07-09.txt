Rouyn-Noranda- Tour in the region of Abitibi-Témiscamingue, the Minister of Culture and Communications, Maka Kotto, in the presence of the member for Rouyn-Noranda-Témiscamingue, Gilles Chapadeau, announced a grant of 2,864,500 $ for the modernization of the small theater of old Noranda. The Mayor of Rouyn-Noranda, Mario Provencher, also participated in the event. 
"New modern and user-friendly facilities will delight spectators and artists. This building has a soul and will soon have all the assets to shine the theater and the creations of the alternative scene throughout the region as well as to offer training to the performing arts. Culture plays a predominant role in the quality of life and the development of communities; This is the vital breath of our cities, villages and our regions, "said Minister Kotto. 
"With a better adapted building, the small Theater of Old Noranda will be able to fully assure its mission, to provide an alternative location for creating, production, disseminating and training to artists in the region. Thanks to this renovation project, the building will contribute to the image of this sector of the city which is being moured in the arts district of Old Noranda, "said Chapadeau. 
Built in 1948, the small theater of old Noranda is used, since its acquisition in 2001 by the theater troupe the Zybrides, as a place of distribution of the performing arts and for the holding of various cultural activities. Occupied approximately 200 days a year, this entertainment room is a privileged place of access to culture for citizens of Rouyn-Noranda. 
The current project aims for a general upgrade, including the redevelopment of the interior premises and the renewal of specialized equipment, in order to comply with the standards affecting the performing arts as well as the comfort and safety of users.
