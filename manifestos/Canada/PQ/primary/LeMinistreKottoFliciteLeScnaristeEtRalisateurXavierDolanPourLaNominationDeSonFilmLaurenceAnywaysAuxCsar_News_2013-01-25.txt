Quebec- The Minister of Culture and Communications, Mr. Maka Kotto, wishes to congratulate the Scriptwriter and director Xavier Dolan for the appointment of his feature film Laurence Anyways at the 38th César ceremony, in the category of the best foreign film. This is the third nomination for this young Quebec filmmaker who was a finalist for the same price in 2011, for his movie the immerses imaginary, and in 2010, for I killed my mother. 
"It's once again a tangible French soil of Xavier Dolan 's talent. The latter, now recognized by the cinematographic and artistic circles internationally as a high-level filmmaker, perfectly embodies the new breath of Quebec cinema. I warmly congratulate him for this appointment, "said Minister Kotto. 
The feature film, realized and written by Xavier Dolan, tells the story of Laurence who announces to his girlfriend that he wants to become a woman. The third film of Xavier Dolan was presented in world premiere at the Cannes Film Festival in May 2012. The main actress of the film, Suzanne Clément, was given the female interpretation award in the section a certain look. Laurence Anyways has also been described as best Canadian film at Toronto's International Film Festival. 
Laurence Anyways is one of the seven titles in the running that were selected by the César César Arts and Techniques Academy. The 38th César ceremony, the equivalent of the Oscars in France, will take place on February 22, 2013, at the Théâtre du Châtelet in Paris.
