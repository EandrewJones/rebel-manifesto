Qu√©bec- The Minister of Finance and the Economy, Mr. Nicolas Marceau, today tabled the National Assembly on the application of the Insurance Act and the Trust Society Act and the Companies savings.  These two laws provide that the Minister must report their application to the National Assembly and make recommendations on the desirability of maintaining their provisions or modifying them. The reported report today proposes various improvements that could be made to these two laws to modernize them and adapt them to the new realities of the financial sector. 
"We have developed this report in collaboration with industry representatives. The report contains 52 proposals, which aimed to make more coherent and simplify the legislative framework by giving the industry the room for maneuver necessary to continue to develop, while ensuring that Quebeckers remain well served and Well protected, "said the minister. 
He indicated that these proposals will be submitted to community stakeholders for comments. The proposals and the comments that will be collected will serve as a basis for a complete revision of the two laws. "We continue to collaborate with the industry to propose legislative changes to this important area of our economy," the minister concluded.
