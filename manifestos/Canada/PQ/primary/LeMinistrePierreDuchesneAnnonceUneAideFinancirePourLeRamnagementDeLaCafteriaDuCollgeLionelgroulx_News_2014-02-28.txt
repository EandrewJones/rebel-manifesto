Quebec- Minister of Higher Education, Research, Science and Technology, Mr. Pierre Duchesne, Announces Funding Assistance from $ 700,586 at Lionel-Groulx College for renting Modular units for food service spaces. 
"The financial assistance I announce today will allow the College to maintain adequate food services. It is important to emphasize that CEGEPs are also a living environment for the student population and the staff attending them. Quality facilities are therefore essential to ensure a stimulating and valuing environment, "said Minister Duchesne. 
In recent years, major expansion works have been carried out in college to enable it to host a growing student population. This work required investments of more than $ 46 million, funded by both levels of government and college. 
In 2007, the ministry allowed the increase in the school estimate of Lionel-Groulx College, allowing it to increase the capacity of 3,800 to 4,800 places on the basis of the workforce forecasts. 
The College and the Department thus have the time needed to set up an appropriate real estate solution, this grant of $ 700,586 is spread over the next three years. 
"The investment announced today by the minister comes to eliminate a major irritant for our students who attend the college cafeteria and, from now on, they will benefit from better access conditions," mention Mr. Michel Louis Beauchamp, Director General of College Lionel-Groulx.
