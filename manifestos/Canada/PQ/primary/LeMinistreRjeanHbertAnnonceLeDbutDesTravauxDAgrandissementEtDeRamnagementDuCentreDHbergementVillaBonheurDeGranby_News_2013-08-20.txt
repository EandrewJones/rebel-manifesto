Granby- The Minister of Health and Social Services and Minister Responsible for Elders, Dr. Réjean Hébert, today gave the kickoff to the expansion and redevelopment work of the Help Happiness Accommodation Center, an installation From the health and social services center (CSSS) of Haute-Yamaska. 
"By supporting this infrastructure project, our government ensures that the health and well-being of residents still remain at the heart of our concerns. Improvements that will be made to this hosting and long-term care center will provide more comfortable and safe premises. Their relatives and their visitors will also benefit from the increased privacy and space, "said Minister Hébert. 
Most of the work to be made consists of an expansion of the building and a redevelopment of existing premises. In particular, the addition of two additional wings is expected to the 4th floor and an increase in the available space in the common areas of each floor, either the living rooms and the dining rooms. In addition, this work will allow the elimination of triple rooms. Finally, an implementation of the seismic requirements was carried out. 
As confirmed in 2012, the Ministry of Health and Social Services will assume the financing of these works up to $ 3.12 million. The CSSS of Haute-Yamaska and the Montérégie Health and Social Services Agency will invest with respect to $ 2.61 million and $ 0.95 million for the completion of this estimated project at $ 6.68 million .
