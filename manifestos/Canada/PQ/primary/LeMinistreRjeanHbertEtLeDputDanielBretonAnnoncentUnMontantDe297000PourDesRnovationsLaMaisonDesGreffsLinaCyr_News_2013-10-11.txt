Montreal- The Minister of Health and Social Services and Minister responsible for Elders, Dr. Réjean Hébert, and the hon. Member for Sainte-Marie-Saint-Jacques and Parliamentary Assistant to the Prime Minister (Electrification of Transport), Mr. Daniel Breton , today announced that $ 297,000 will be granted to the Maison des Grafts Lina Cyr to carry out renovation of its building. 
The amount allocated will be devoted to the complete refurbishment of the building facade, the repair of joints and caulking windows, to roof maintenance work, as well as the standards of the elevator. 
"The Maison des Grafts Lina Cyr does a remarkable job and assistance that it offers people grafted or about to be made of this organization a very important partner of our health network. Moreover, we have awarded him the 2012 Coup de Excellence Award for this reason. It is therefore crucial to continue to concretely bring our support, "said Minister Hébert. 
For 20 years, the Maison des Grafts LINA CYR has been aware of, at an affordable price, accommodation in a safe environment and a personalized setting for people waiting for a transplant, remission or evaluation process. The organization welcomes people from all regions of Quebec as well as the loved ones who accompany them. Since its inception, more than 9,700 patients have benefited from its services. 
"As soon as the government has been aware of the urgency of doing this, the answer has not been long. I believe that the speed with which everything took place shows how much we hold the work of community organizations in high esteem, "said Daniel Breton.
