Quebec- The Minister of Health and Social Services and Minister Responsible for Seniors, Réjean Hébert, provided $ 10,000 to the Alzheimer Society of the Gaspé-Îles-de-la-Madeleine, an amount from his Discretionary budget. 
"Alzheimer's disease upsets the lives of whole families. People with and their loved ones have a great need for support and support, and this is exactly what the Alzheimer Society of Gaspé-Îles-de-la-Madeleine offers. I am very pleased to encourage its activities, especially since its role is called upon to gain in importance in the context of aging of the population, "said Minister Hébert. 
The hon. Member for Bonaventure, Sylvain Roy, is very happy with Minister Réjean Hébert's contribution that aims to support the activities surrounding the 11 marches of the memory held this year in the riding: "the funds raised on the occasion of The march of the memory will allow the Alzheimer Gaspé-Îles-de-la-Madeleine society to continue to offer quality services to people with illness as well as their loved ones, "he said. 
Recall that the Alzheimer Society of Gaspésie-Îles-de-la-Madeleine offers a very wide range of services whose hot tuition, animation of support groups and various other information and awareness activities across the region , thanks to its partnership with nine volunteer action centers. 
"The activities of the Alzheimer society are also part of a great complementarity with all our actions to support home support, to facilitate access to adapted services and improve the supply of services for loved ones caregivers, "concluded the Minister Hébert.
