Quebec- The Minister of Health and Social Services and Minister Responsible for Seniors, Dr. Réjean Hébert, wanted to emphasize today the launch of the Week for the Rights of Users of the Health and Social Services Network, who This year took place from 28 September to 5 October, under the theme the right to be represented. 
Users' Week is an awareness activity that aims in particular to inform network users about their rights and to highlight the work done in Quebec health and social services institutions. 
"Users are at the heart of the provision of health care and services in Quebec, and the week of users' rights certainly contributes to reaffirm it. As Minister of Health and Social Services, I encourage users 'committees as well as all organizations interested in respecting users' rights and improved health services to achieve this week awareness-raising activities " , said Dr. Hébert. 
Remember that the users' committees ensure that those who use network services at one time or another are handled in accordance with their dignity and recognition of their rights and freedoms. The very role of these committees actually important spokespersons from institutional bodies. 
"I would also like to take advantage of the user's rights week to welcome the commitment of the many volunteers in the users and resident committees. Their dedication contributes not only to respect for the rights of users and their loved ones, but also to the general improvement of the quality of the services offered by the network. This contribution is valuable because our network needs people who are at heart the rights of users and who ensure the improvement of the living conditions of households, "concluded the Minister Hébert.
