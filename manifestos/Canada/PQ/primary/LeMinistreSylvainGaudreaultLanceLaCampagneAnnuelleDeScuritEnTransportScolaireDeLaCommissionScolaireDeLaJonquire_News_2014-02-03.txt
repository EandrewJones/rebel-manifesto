Saguenay- The Minister of Transport, Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and the Occupation of the Territory and MP from Jonquière, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, launched this morning, as President of Honorary, the annual security campaign in School transport of the School Board of La Jonquière. 
On the occasion of a luncheon held in the borough of Jonquière, in Saguenay, Mr. Gaudreault went to a group of stakeholders composed of elected officials and staff members of the school board, coordinators in academic transport and territory bus drivers. "We must unite our voices to remember that the safety of schoolchildren is everyone's business. In this sense, I assured myself that the Ministry of Transport disseminates security reminders everywhere in Saguenay-Lac-Saint-Jean, "said Gaudreault, indicating that they are about 5,200 students who use the services daily. The School Board of La Jonquière to go to twenty primary and secondary schools. 
The launch was done on the occasion of the 26th School Transportation Campaign of the Quebec School Transport Association (ATEQ). On the theme "did you see me? This campaign aims to raise awareness of the users of the Route de liability for them with regard to the safety of schoolchildren who use the bus. This year, the ATEQ posted the new micrositewww.mastutivu.info, which allows all, both to the drivers and students, to review the safe behaviors to adopt in the presence of school buses, in school zone and in school lessons.
