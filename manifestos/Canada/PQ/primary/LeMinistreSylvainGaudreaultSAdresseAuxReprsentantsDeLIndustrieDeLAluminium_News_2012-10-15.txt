Quebec is the third largest exporter of aluminum on the planet with 7% of world production. More than 10,000 direct jobs and a network of 4,500 suppliers and 1,800 transformers are dependent on this industry that contributes to the economic development of several regions of Quebec. 
By using hydropower, the Quebec aluminum industry uses green energy, which gives it an environmentally comparative advantage. Aluminum, by its lightness, contributes to the improvement of the energy efficiency of the vehicles. In the perspective of government willingness to reduce GHG emissions, it is necessary as a material of the future. 
"I will take advantage of my visit to this AAC meeting to reiterate Quebec's commitment to energy independence and transport electrification. I will also be part of the representatives of this industry of my optimism with regard to the contribution of aluminum to the development of transport in the 21st century, "concluded Mr. Gaudreault.
