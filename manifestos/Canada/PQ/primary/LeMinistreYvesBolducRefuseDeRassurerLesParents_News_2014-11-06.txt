Quebec, November 6, 2014 - Member of Pointe-Aux-Trembles and sentence of the official opposition in terms of primary and secondary education and school perseverance, Nicole Léger, denounces the Minister of Education, the leisure and sports, Yves Bolduc, who refuses to deny the scenario increase of childcare rates in schools, while those of other child care will be modulated according to the parents' income.

"The Prime Minister has confirmed yesterday that his government would further tax the families of the middle class. The parents of the 180,000 students who attend school care services want to know if the fees will increase. I asked the minister to reassure these parents; He refused to deny a potential rise in the tariff, "Nicole Léger said.

The member for Pointe-Aux-Trembles deplores the government to further tax the families of the middle class. "The Liberals, while they have promised, in the election campaign, to limit the rise in inflation child care rates, totally make the other reverse by establishing a new family tax. By doing so, they violate Quebec's family policy, which is the pride of Quebeckers and who is mentioned around the world, "concluded Nicole Léger.
