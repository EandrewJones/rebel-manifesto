Quebec- The official chief opposition and hon. Member for Chicoutimi, Mr. Stéphane Bédard, today proceeded to the composition of the Quebec party spokesperson for the next parliamentary legislature. 
The leader of the official opposition has stressed the quality of the team that will make up the official opposition. "The 30 men and women who form the Parti Québécois team will be dedicated to improving the fate of our world. Asking us to constitute the official opposition, Quebeckers entrust us with the responsibility of ensuring that an integrity government ensures employment growth and preserves fiscal rigor. These values that animate us are the foundation of our political training, "concluded Mr. Bédard.
