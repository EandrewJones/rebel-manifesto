Quebec- The hon. Member for Hochelaga-Maisonneuve and spokesman for the official opposition for seniors, Carole Poirier, is proud to have participated yesterday in the march for quality care organized by a little beneficiary attendants everywhere in Quebec. 
This march, in connection with the petition 'care provided to seniors' deposited at the National Assembly on 1 December 2011 by the member Carole Poirier, aimed at promoting the improvement of the entire health system in Quebec by increasing Its accessibility, quantity and quality of care with seniors and all those in need. 
"As a spokesperson for the official opposition for seniors, I had the honor of presenting this petition to the National Assembly and I continue to support the citizen approaches undertaken by these women and men who Beat for the elderly to have better access to health care, "said Carole Poirier.
