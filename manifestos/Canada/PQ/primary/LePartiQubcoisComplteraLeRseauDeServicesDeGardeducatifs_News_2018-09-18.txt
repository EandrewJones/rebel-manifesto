The leader and vice-cheft of the Parti Québécois, Jean-François Lisée and Véronique Hivon, as well as candidates Nicolas Marceau (Rousseau), Mathieu Traversy (Terrebonne), André Villeneuve (Berthier), Eric Tremblay (Repentigny), Sylvie Langlois ( The Assumption) and Diane Gadoury-Hamelin (Masson), are committed to completing the network of educational custody services by creating 26,800 new CPE places in a first mandate, which will eliminate the waiting list.
