Quebec, October 21, 2014 -The MP for Chicoutimi and Leader of the Official Opposition, Stéphane Bédard, and the member for Saint-Jean, Dave Turcotte, offer their support to the families of the victims and the entire community of Saint-Jean- Sur-Richelieu. 
"On behalf of my colleagues from the Parti Québécois, I offer my deepest condolences to the family of Warrant Officer Patrice Vincent. Our thoughts go to his loved ones as well as those of the injured member during these sad events, "said Stéphane Bédard. 
"I offer my fellow citizens all my support; I am the situation closely by staying in contact with the police forces of the city of Saint-Jean-sur-Richelieu and those of the security of Quebec, as well as with the military authorities of the garrison of Saint John. Our entire community is tightening elbows in these difficult times, "added Dave Turcotte. 
"I also want to emphasize the exceptional intervention work of the police officers of Saint-Jean-sur-Richelieu and congratulate them. It is reassuring to know that we have a police service that is able to respond promptly in all situations to ensure the safety of the public, "concluded the member for Saint John.
