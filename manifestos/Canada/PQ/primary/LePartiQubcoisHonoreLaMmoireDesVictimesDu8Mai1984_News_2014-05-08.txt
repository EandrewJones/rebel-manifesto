Quebec- On behalf of the elected officials of the Parti Québécois parliamentary wing, the official Leader, Stéphane Bédard, wishes to honor the memory of the victims of the shooting that took place at the Hotel du Parliament on May 8, 1984 . He also welcomes the decision of the President of the National Assembly, Jacques Chagnon, who announced the production of a plate in memory of this tragic event. 
"In this 30th anniversary of this sad day, our thoughts go to the three people killed in the performance of their duties, Camille Lepage, Georges Boyer and Roger Lefrançois, as well as their loved ones. On May 8, 1984 will remain engraved in the history of Quebec and that of our democracy, "said Stéphane Bédard. 
The member for Abitibo West, François Gendron, was a member of the National Assembly at that time. Witness of this unfortunate event in our history, he particularly holds to honor the memory of the victims and the courage of the staff of the National Assembly present on 8 May 1984. 
"All those present and all members remember this day. Also have a thought for them, and for those who have completed heroic gestures. I am thinking in particular about the sergeant of arms, René Jalbert, whose extraordinary courage marked the memoirs, "said François Gendron.
