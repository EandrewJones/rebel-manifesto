Montréal - The Parti Québécois has proceeded today to launch, on its social media, its new training school. This school will be a new platform where militants and sympathizers of sovereign political training can interact to consult a media library related to the independence of Quebec and to register for training dealing with the organization and the organization. political communication. 
"We will be ready for the next electoral appointment. The Quebec Party Training School is part of the range of new tools available to citizen citizens and activists. We will have all that is necessary to demonstrate to the electors and the only real option to allow Quebec to fully succeed, it is the Parti Quebecois, "said the Director General of the Parti Québécois, Alain Lupien. 
The training school will propose and improve a public page where conferences and independence documentation will be presented to all persons interested in the project to make Quebec a country. It is also possible to register to consult the offer of training.
