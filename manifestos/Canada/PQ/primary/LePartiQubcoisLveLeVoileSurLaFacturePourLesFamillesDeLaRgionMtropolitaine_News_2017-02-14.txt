Quebec, on February 14, 2017- The Leader of the Official Opposition, Jean-François Lisée, and the spokesperson of the Parti Québécois on transport, Alain Therrien, today unveiled how families of more than 50 municipalities of The metropolitan area will have to pay for the metropolitan electricity grid (REM), in addition to the costs caused by the reform of the regional metropolitan transport authority. 
"The government had promised that the Government of Quebec and the municipalities would not subsidize the REM. However, not only there are subsidies paid directly from taxpayers' pockets but, in addition, they will be added to the new tax 450 imposed on families in the Montreal area. What is the Liberal government's strategy? Do not  talk about additional costs or financial assembly before contracts are given and that the  Rem construction starts? , "Said Jean-François Lisée. 
"We are in favor of this great collective transport project, has also recalled the head of  the official opposition. We simply ask for transparency for citizens. We  Claims from the Government that it undertakes that the REM does not result in any increase in taxes  For the families of Montreal and 450. "
