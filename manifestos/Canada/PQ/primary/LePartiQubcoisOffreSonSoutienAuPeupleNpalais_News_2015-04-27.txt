Québec- Following the terrible earthquake that affected Nepal and makes thousands of victims, the member for Chicoutimi and Leader of the Official Opposition, Stéphane Bédard, and the member for Hochelaga-Maisonneuve and spokesperson International relations, Carole Poirier, address their deepest condolences to the Nepalese people, to Quebecers of Nepalese origin and their loved ones. 
"In my name and that of my colleagues deputies of the official opposition, I wish to express my deep sympathy to all those affected, near or far, by these tragic events; We are in solidarity with the Nepalese people and share the sentence of its 6,000 citizens established in Quebec, "said Stéphane Bédard. 
"Such a disaster is an immense shock. The life of a whole nation is upset, transformed forever. To get up, the Nepalese people will have to show a lot of courage and will need international cooperation. We hope that help, on the ground, is organized as quickly as possible, "said Carole Poirier.
