Quebec, October 28, 2019 - This Monday was invested Sylvain Barrette, the Québécois candidate for the partial election of December 2nd in the riding of Jean-Talon. In addition to some 200 activists in the region who moved for the occasion, the parliamentary leader of the Parti Québécois, Pascal Bérubé, MEPs Méganne Perry Melançon, Sylvain Gaudreault and Harold Lebel and the two members of the Bloc Québécois of the Region. From Quebec City, Caroline Desbians and Julie Vignola, came to give him their support. 
A candidate engaged in his cultural community, Sylvain Barrette has a career as a teacher at the primary and musician level. While supervising class classes, it has occurred internationally, in several countries. Very engaged professionally and in his community, the candidate of the Parti Québécois in Jean-Talon has, among other things, contributed to initiate many young people to music and to help newcomers better integrate. 
"We are very proud to present to the citizens of Jean-Talon a candidate with many qualities; In addition to working for a long time in the field of culture, it is enthusiastically involved to help newcomers better integrate into Quebec City. It also wears a refreshing look at politics, "mentioned the parliamentary leader of the Parti Québécois, Pascal Bérubé. 
For independence for these partial elections, the Parti Québécois puts its project of independence in the foreground. The slogan of the campaign will be: "For independence. » 
"The independence of Quebec is to have all the levers necessary to fight against the climate crisis and to protect our culture. That's why I present myself in politics. These are the themes that will bring my campaign, "says Sylvain Barrette.
