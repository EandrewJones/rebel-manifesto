Québec- Following the announcement of his death, the member for Chicoutimi and Leader of the Official Opposition, Stéphane Bédard, wished to pay tribute to the Archbishop Emeritus of the Diocese of Montreal, Cardinal Jean-Claude Turcotte. 
"Quebeckers and Montrealers will keep the memory of a dedicated man who has created lasting links with people and organizations in his community. Concerned about the fate of the poorest, he first exercised his ministry in the labor force, from which he was derived and from which he has always remained very close. I offer my deepest condolences to his family, his friends, as well as his colleagues from the Archbishop of Montreal, "said Stéphane Bédard. 
Among the achievements of Cardinal Turcotte, hear his active participation in the organization of Pope John? Paul II in Quebec in 1984. He also held many manager positions in the Catholic Church, where he was respected And appreciated by his peers. 
Born in Montreal, Jean-Claude Turcotte was ordained a priest in 1959. In 1990, he was appointed Archbishop of the Diocese of Montreal; He became Cardinal in 1994.
