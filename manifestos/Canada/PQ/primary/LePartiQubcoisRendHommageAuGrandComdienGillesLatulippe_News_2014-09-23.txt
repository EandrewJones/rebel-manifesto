Québec- It is with sadness that the member for Chicoutimi and Leader of the Official Opposition, Stéphane Bédard, and the member for Joliette and Spokesperson in the field of culture and communications, Véronique Hivon, have learned the death of Mr. Gilles Latulippe. This one turned out today at the Montreal General Hospital. He was 77 years old. 
"Gilles Latulippe is a real monument of the theater, comedy and culture from here. King Quebec Burlesque and Varieties, he left his mark from the beginning of the Cabarets era. With his comparses, including Olivier Guimond, Claude Blanchard, Suzanne Lapointe and Fernand Gignac, to appoint only those, he begins with immense heritage to Quebec culture. On behalf of the parliamentary wing of the Parti Québécois, I offer my deepest condolences to the family and relatives of this great Quebecer, "said Stéphane Bédard. 
Véronique Hivon recalled, for his part, the large number of television broadcasts he participated. Symphorien, pepper and salt, the brilliance, the demons of the South, among others, have touched the hearts of Quebecers at various times of our television. Mr. Latulippe was also the father of the illustrious theater of Montreal varieties; In 2004, the room was renamed in his honor. 
"There is no doubt that Gilles Latulippe will remain forever in the memory of thousands of Quebeckers and Quebeckers, for whom he was so familiar. By him invariably had joy and gaiety; His memory will always be comforting. Obviously, he won an unconditional love to his audience and his gaming partners, who made him well; Shorts at his long successful career, "said the member for Joliette.
