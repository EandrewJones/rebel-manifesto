Quebec- Following the announcement on Saturday of the death of the author Georges-Hébert Germain, the spokesman for the Parti Québécois in the field of culture, Véronique Hivon, stressed his important contribution to the literature and The culture of Quebec. 
"On behalf of all the elected officials of the Parti Québécois, I extend my deepest condolences to the family and relatives of Georges-Hébert Germain, including his spouse and his daughter. Prolific author, he leaves inheritance a considerable work, whose guideline was without a doubt the desire to raise awareness of Quebeckers some of the most significant characters in their history, past and contemporary. He has played a big role in preserving and promoting our collective memory. We are very grateful to him, "said the member for Joliette. 
"Interested in a multitude of subjects, Georges-Hébert Germain will have touched, during his long career, to several literary styles, going from journalism to the biography, passing through the historical novel, the test, even the song . Over the years, he has received many awards that came to emphasize the quality of his work, his research. We will remember him among others for his love of words, which he also transmitted to his daughter Rafaële, as well as to many authors of the new generation he has inspired, "added Véronique Hivon.
