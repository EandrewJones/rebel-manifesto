Québec- The member for Joliette and spokesperson for the official opposition in the field of culture and communications, Véronique Hivon, would pay tribute to the facilitator of Radio Jacques Bertrand, died on November 10. 
"On behalf of my colleagues from the Parti Québécois, I extend my sincere condolences to the family, relatives and former colleagues of Jacques Bertrand. He was an exceptional facilitator and communicator, who has changed many ways the world of radio. He has been at the head of unforgettable emissions on the airlines of the Canadian Radio-Canada, especially the Macadam Tribes, from 1997 to 2009. In addition to its great professionalism, audacity, humor and love. He had for his audience marked his auditors of all ages, during the three decades he spent Radio-Canada, "said Véronique Hivon.
