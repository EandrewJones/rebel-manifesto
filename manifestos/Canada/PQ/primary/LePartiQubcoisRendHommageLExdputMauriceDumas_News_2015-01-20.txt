Quebec- The hon. Member for Chicoutimi and Leader of the Official Opposition, Stéphane Bédard, pays tribute to the former member of the Bloc Québécois, Maurice Dumas, died on Sunday at the age of 87. 
"On behalf of my colleagues deputies, I extend my sincere condolences to the family and relatives of Maurice Dumas. We lost a remarkable, passionate man, and engaged for Quebec's sovereignty. Very attached to the French language and the roots of Quebec culture, we will remember from him as someone to proud, who had keen to celebrate the Quebec difference, "said Stéphane Bédard. 
Maurice Dumas was among the first cohort of Bloc Québécois deputies, in the House of Commons. He represented the citizens of the federal riding of Argenteuil-Papineau from 1993 to 2000.
