Quebec- The leader of the Party Party, Pierre Karl Péladeau, wanted to pay tribute to the economist Pierre Harvey, former director of HEC Montréal and Economic Advisor of Prime Minister René Lévesque, who recently died. 
"On behalf of the entire Quebec party team, I offer my sincere condolences to the spouse and children of Pierre Harvey, as well as his relatives and former colleagues of the Parti Québécois. Mr. Harvey advised our political training and his leader, Prime Minister René Lévesque, for several years. He has invested a lot for the sovereignty of our nation and its economic independence. Professor then Director of HEC Montréal from 1982 to 1987, he left the mark of a generous man, loyal and great humility. Thank you, Mr. Harvey, "said Pierre Karl Péladeau.
