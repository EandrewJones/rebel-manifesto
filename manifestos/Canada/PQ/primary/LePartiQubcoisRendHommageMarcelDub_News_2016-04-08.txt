Québec - It is with sadness that the members of the Parti Québécois have learned today the death of one of the most illustrious Quebec playwrights, Mr. Marcel Dubé. On behalf of the official opposition and the spokesperson of the Parti Québécois in the field of culture and communications, Pierre Karl Péladeau and Véronique Hivon, expressed their strong sympathy to those and those he leaves in the mourning. 
"I extend my deepest condolences to the family and the many friends of Mr. Marcel Dubé. Although his absence creates a great emptiness, the work that he leaves us in heritage is immensely rich. Beyond any doubt, she will survive and will continue to marvel for future generations, "said the official opposition. 
Véronique Hivon emphasized the fact that Marcel Dubé's career was particularly flourishing, as much on stage as on television or on the radio, and that his work testifies well of the Quebec reality. "Marcel Dubé has shot the best of every media that can spread his ideas. From the area to the series The world of Marcel Dubé, through the time of the lilacs and a simple soldier, he also knew how to describe the internal conflicts which all the human beings live with acutely, without any form of complacency . Marcel Dubé has always maintained a parallel between his writings and the evolution of the Quebec nation, which makes his work an eloquent and relevant historical reference. Quebec students will study it a long time, as I did myself, "she mentioned. 
"Marcel Dubé was a pillar of our culture, one of the most talented and prolifical writers of its generation. We are very grateful for the generosity he had to us, "concluded Pierre Karl Péladeau.
