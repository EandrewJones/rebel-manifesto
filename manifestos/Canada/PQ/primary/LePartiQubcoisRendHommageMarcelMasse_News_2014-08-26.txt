Quebec- It is with sadness that the member for Chicoutimi and Leader of the Official Opposition, Stéphane Bédard, learned of the death of Mr. Marcel Masse, on August 25th. He was 78 years old. 
"On behalf of my members' colleagues from the Parti Québécois, I offer my condolences to the family, relatives and many friends of Marcel Masse. He has, throughout his public life, a great defender of Quebec society, culture and French language, here and around the world, particularly in France, with which he maintained particular links ", Affirmed Stéphane Bédard. 
After having held many ministerial positions in Quebec, from 1966 to 1970, Marcel Masse did the Federally from 1984 to 1993. He then fulfills several political and public functions, including the Presidency of the Regional Commission of Montreal and the Vice. -Pressency of the National Commission on the Future of Quebec in 1995. He also presided over the French Language Council and the Quebec Cultural Property Commission, and was General Delegate of Quebec in Paris from 1 January 1996 to 2 June 1997.
