Quebec- The member for Chicoutimi and Leader of the Official Opposition, Stéphane Bédard, as well as the hon. Member for Taschereau and Parliamentary Leader, Agnès Maltese, pay tribute to the former member of the Bloc Québécois and former Party candidate, Michel Guimond, dead suddenly today. 
"On behalf of my colleagues deputies of the official opposition and the big family of the Parti Québécois, I extend my sincere condolences to the family and relatives of Michel Guimond. Throughout his political career, this passionate has defended with conviction the place of the regions of Quebec and the importance of being near his world. Michel was a particularly committed man, especially for the advancement of Quebec and its independence, "said Stéphane Bédard. 
"The great Quebec region loses a great ambassador. Michel Guimond has always been very dedicated to the citizens he represented. He was committed to defending them and working for the development of the major regions of Quebec City, Charlevoix and the North Shore. We will regret it all, "added Agnès Maltese.
