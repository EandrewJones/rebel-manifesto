Montreal, May 29, 2020 - The Parti Québécois coordination conference decided to end the break marked in the management race, and resume the nomination period from Friday, June 5th. She also made several changes to the race schedule, in order to adapt to the context of the pandemic. 
In addition, the Coordination Conference has appointed the President of the Parti Québécois, Dieudonné Ella Oyono, President of Election. 
Here are the new dates to remember for this race at the chieftaincy: 
▪ June 5th: recovery of the nomination period 
▪ 26 June: End of the nomination period and official start of the race 
▪ Between August 15 and October 2: Keeping Virtual Debates 
▪ September 9: deadline to become a member or sympathizer in order to vote 
▪ October 5 to 9: telephone and Internet voting 
▪ 9 October: unveiling the new or new leader of the Parti Québécois 
"The gradual deconformation of Quebec is initiated; It is therefore with caution and a great sense of the responsibilities we will resume on June 5, our running race. Everything will be implemented to ensure the safety of our members, our supporters, as well as our activists so that we can, together, take this step in the history of our political formation, "said the president of the Parti Québécois and President of election, Dieudonné Ella Oyono. 
The member for Matane-Matapédia, Pascal Bérubé, will continue to provide interim as a parliamentary leader of the Quebecois party, until October 9th.
