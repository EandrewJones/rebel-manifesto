Quebec- following the announcement of the death of Quebec producer and television director Jean Bissonnette, the member for Saint-Jérôme and Leader of the official opposition, Pierre Karl Péladeau, and the member for Joliette and spokesman of the party Québécois in culture, Véronique Hivon, wanted to salute the memory of this emblematic figure of Quebec culture. 
"Quebec loses a patriarch of its cultural scene, a monument of entertainment. On behalf of the elected officials and party activists, I want to express my deepest sympathy as well as my condolences to family, friends and relatives of Jean Bissonnette. Respected of all, passionate about his job, he was a great source of inspiration for those who have surrounded him or followed it, "said the official opposition. 
"We owe to Jean Bissonnette some of the most striking emissions in the history of the small screen in Quebec: me and the other, call me Lise, Mondays Ha! Ha!, A guy, a girl, as well as several bye bye, to name only those. It leaves a considerable heritage, especially at Radio-Canada, where it worked for many years. We are very grateful for his contribution to our cultural life; He accompanied Quebec for decades, and in his emancipation.The television and his craftsmen owe him a lot, "added Véronique Hivon.
