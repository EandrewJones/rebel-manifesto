Quebec, on May 7, 2017- The Leader of the Official Opposition, Jean-François Lisée, and the Word of the Parti Quebec's International Relations, Stéphane Bergeron, are committed to commending the victory of Emmanuel Macron and his accession to The Presidency of the French Republic. 
"The direct and privileged relationship that unites Quebec and France is an invaluable asset. With the election of Mr. Macron, I trust that we will maintain it, and I wish we can, even, strengthen it. Certainly, the new President of the Republic will always be welcome in Quebec! "Affirmed Jean-François Lisée. 
"In France and Quebec, for several months - and especially during the primary - we multiply meetings with the actors of French politics. We obviously intend to continue and enrich these exchanges, especially with the team of the new President of the French Republic, "added Stéphane Bergeron.
