Quebec- The member for the Assumption and Spokesperson for the Official Opposition on Sustainable Development, Environment and Parks, Scott McKay, will finally join the Quebec delegation that must go to the United Nations Conference on the Sustainable development in Rio, June 20-22. "It's a climate change of the Minister of the Environment, Pierre Arcand," said the member. 
The member of the Québécois party intends to take advantage of his presence at this summit to assert the best choices that Quebec must do if it wants to fight climate change effectively. "Quebec has all the assets to further electrify its transport. There is only a lack of political will; The Liberal Plan is clearly insufficient in this regard, "said Scott McKay. 
"The Auditor General has strongly criticized the first government plan that has been insufficient and ineffective despite the investments made by taxpayers. The new 2013-2020 plan of the Liberal regime may know the same fate because it is based on measures whose effectiveness and efficiency have never been demonstrated, "concluded Scott McKay, reiterating his request for a consultation. Public on the last plan presented on June 3 and will serve as a green mask for Jean Charest at the Rio + 20 summit.
