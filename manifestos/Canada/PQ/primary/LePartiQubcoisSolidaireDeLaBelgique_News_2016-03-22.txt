Québec- As a result of terrorist attacks that hit Brussels, today, the leader of the official opposition, Pierre Karl Péladeau, and the spokesman for the Quebecois Particular on International Relations, Carole Poirier, have expressed their solidarity with Belgium and their friendship to the citizens of the capital of the European institutions. 
"Hate and violence struck Brussels this morning. We offer our sincere condolences to families and relatives of the victims of these barbaric attacks. These attacks on freedom and democracy are unacceptable, and we condemn them strongly, "said Pierre Karl Péladeau. 
"We share the sadness and emotion of all Belgians. In these difficult times, we want to reaffirm our friendship and support, "said Carole Poirier.
