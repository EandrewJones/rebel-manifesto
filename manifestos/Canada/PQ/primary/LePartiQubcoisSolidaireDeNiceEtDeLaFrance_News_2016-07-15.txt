Quebec, on July 15, 2016- The hon. Member for Jonquière and Leader of the Official Opposition, Sylvain Gaudreault, as well as the member for Hochelaga-Maisonneuve and spokesman for the Quebecois Party for International Relations, Carole Poirier, want to condemn The night's attack on July 14 in Nice. They express all their solidarity to the people of Nice and France. 
"Horror and barbarism have again struck. French and tourists come to celebrate the National Day were mowed. We can not remain silent in front of the terror. These acts attack our societies and democracy. We firmly condemn this terrorist act. On behalf of the Québécois Party, I extend my sincere condolences to the relatives and families of the victims, as well as to the entire Niçoise and French population, "said Sylvain Gaudreault. 
"The National Day is an opportunity for the French to celebrate what brings together this great nation and its place in the world. It will rise, strong and confident in the future. She can count on Quebec and the Quebec party to support it, with the same solidarity that has always marked our relationships. Beyond the great sadness that lives today, we express our deep solidarity towards the families of the victims and France, "concluded Carole Poirier.
