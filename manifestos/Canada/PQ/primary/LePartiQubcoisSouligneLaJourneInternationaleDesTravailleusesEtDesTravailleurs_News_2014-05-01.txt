Quebec- The member for Sanguinet and official opposition spokesperson for the work and pension plans, Alain Therrien, joined his voice to that of all workers on May 1. 
"The International Day of Workers and Workers is an opportunity to recall the essential contribution of women and men for the advent of a more prosperous and just society. It should be emphasized the collective need that we have to offer a safe environment for all and, above all, good working conditions. The government must take the place to promote and guarantee the defense of the rights of workers. Especially, we stop the Couillard Prime Minister to continue the implementation of economic priority employment priority, which was promising in particular for the thousands of Employees in Quebec manufacturing, "said Mr. Therrien.
