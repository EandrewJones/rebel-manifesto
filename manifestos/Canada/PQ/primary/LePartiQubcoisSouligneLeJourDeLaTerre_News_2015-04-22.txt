Quebec- The member for Jonquiere and the official opposition spokeswoman in sustainable development and environment, Sylvain Gaudreault, invites Quebeckers to emphasize Earth Day and to participate in the global transition movement to a world more eco-friendly. 
"Quebeckers are increasingly mobilized to make Quebec a green society. They are interested in participating in the collective effort and move towards a world that lives more in harmony with its environment, "said Sylvain Gaudreault. 
The choice of Québec's climate, energy, transportation and spatial planning society will be critical for the future viability of our planet. "It is by informed decisions in these areas that we will ensure the generations that will follow us a long-term environment," added the member for Jonquière. 
The Parti Québécois recalls that in order to fulfill its environmental protection obligations, Quebec has every interest in becoming a country; He will be able to vote all his laws and sign all his treaties. "Canada is at the trailer of other countries. It is as a sovereign state that Quebec will ensure the viability and future of its environment. In the meantime, the Parti Québécois filed Bill 390, aimed at ensuring the primacy of Quebec's environmental competence. We ask the Liberal government to call our bill, "concluded Sylvain Gaudreault.
