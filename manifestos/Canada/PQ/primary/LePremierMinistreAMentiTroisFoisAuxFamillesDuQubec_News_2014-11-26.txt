Quebec- The member for Chicoutimi and Leader of the Official Opposition, Stéphane Bédard, and the member for Terrebonne and Family Spokesperson, Mathieu Traversy, denounce the new surprise that the Liberal government reserves Quebec's families, while The development of new custody places is totally braked. 
"All Quebeckers keep in mind that the Prime Minister has been elected by promising to cancel the $ 2 increase in the child care rate, which had been announced before the elections. However, he knew the real situation of public finances at the time he entrusted the Minister with the need to develop the network and prevent a tariff shock. In fact, when the Minister of the Family has been sworn on April 23, the Prime Minister told him, and I quote: "You will see to continue the development of the daycare places and [to] avoid a tariff shock to families From the middle class by opting for price indexation ". So he denied his word twice, "said Stéphane Bédard. 
Third retreat 
In addition, on 20 November, by a small line of a press release, the Prime Minister announced the cessation of the development of places in custody of reduced contribution, except those already under construction or made official by a contract. sign. In particular, it means that the development of the 15,000 places is arrested, but, even moreover, than that of three quarters of the 13,000 places announced in 2011 too. 
"Since yesterday, the councilors of the Ministry of the Family call for project managers to tell them that everything is now on the ice. In Montreal, 2692 places were granted. As only 205 places are already built or in the process of being, it is 2487 children to whom we will now have to find a new place. In Laval, all projects are blocked. In short, Quebec families were betrayed three times by the Prime Minister and his government, "concluded Mathieu Traversy.
