Quebec- This morning, one hour before the start of the work, the member for Taschereau and the parliamentary leader of the official opposition, Agnès Maltese, sent to the President of the National Assembly a notice of violation of the rights and privileges of parliamentarians , concerning the disclosure of the program content 3 before that members are seized. 
Yesterday, June 11, an article published by pressing the state of nine elements of the pension plan bill. 
"The rules of the National Assembly contains no ambiguity; Parliamentarians must be the first informed of the content of a bill. The parliamentary procedure allowed the President of the National Assembly to rule on the concerns of the official opposition because he has the privilege of receiving the bill one hour before his deposit and, as well, to compare his content with the article from Journal. It is for this reason only that we opposed the filing of the bill before the chairman's decision, "said Agnès Maltais. 
As for the bottom of the bill, the parliamentary leader recalled that the Parti Québécois has tabled a draft to deal with this important problem. The official opposition will collaborate in solving the difficulties of pension plans.
