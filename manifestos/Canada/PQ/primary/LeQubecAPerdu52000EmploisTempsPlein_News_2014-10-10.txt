The member for Rousseau and sentence of the official opposition in the field of finance, Nicolas Marceau, the member for Saint-Jérôme and spokesperson for economy, entrepreneurship, SMEs and exports, Pierre Karl Péladeau, and the member for Saint-Jean and Porter in employment, vocational training, literacy and youth, Dave Turcotte, require Prime Minister Philippe Couillard that he comes out of his torpor and act concretely To revive job creation in Quebec. 
The stagnation of the labor market continues in Quebec, just like full-time employment tumble. Since the arrival of the Liberal government, 51,900 full-time jobs have been lost. 
"While our neighbors create jobs, Quebec is lagging behind. This may continue since all that the government proposes is a northern derive plan, as well as a maritime strategy and a free trade agreement with Europe that will not create jobs before several years "Said Nicolas Marceau.

"The Liberal government has abandoned the economic policies launched by the Parti Québécois government. Since that time, employment goes wrong in Quebec.
