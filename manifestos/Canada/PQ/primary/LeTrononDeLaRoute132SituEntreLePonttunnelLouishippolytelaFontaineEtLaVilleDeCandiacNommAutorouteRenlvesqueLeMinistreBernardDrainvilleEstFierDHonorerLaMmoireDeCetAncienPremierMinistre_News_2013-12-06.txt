It should be remembered that until it was created in 1980 the current constituency of Marie-Victorin, which Mr. Bernard Drainville represents, was partly included in the ridal riding. 
Mr. René Lévesque founded the Quebecois Party in 1968. He was a member of Parliament for Taillon and Prime Minister of Quebec from 1976 to 1985. He died in 1987.
