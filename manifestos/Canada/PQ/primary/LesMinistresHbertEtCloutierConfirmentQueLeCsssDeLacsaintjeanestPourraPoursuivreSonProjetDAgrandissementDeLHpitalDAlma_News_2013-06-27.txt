Alma- The Minister of Health and Social Services and Minister Responsible for Elders, Réjean Hébert, Treasury Board Chairman, Minister responsible for the Saguenay-Lac-Saint-Jean Region and MP for Chicoutimi, Stéphane Bédard, as well as Minister Delegate for Canadian Intergovernmental Affairs, Canadian Francophonie and Sovereignist Governance and MPs of Lac-Saint-Jean, Alexandre Cloutier, confirmed today that the Lac-Saint-Jean- Health Health and Social Services Center. Is will be allowed to begin its expansion and emergency redevelopment project and external clinics from Alma Hospital. Thus, the work will be able to start from next spring. 
This project, which is estimated at about $ 40 million, or $ 24.5 million for fixed assets, has been duly registered by Quebec infrastructure (PQI), allowing the Ministry of Health and Social Services to provide its financing if any.
