Quebec, June 30, 2014 - The member for Marie-Victorin and the official opposition on energy and natural resources, Bernard Drainville, asked the President? Managing Director of Hydro-Québec, Thierry Vandal , and the Minister of Energy and Natural Resources, Pierre Arcand, to make public as soon as possible the internal investigation and audit reports on the granting of contracts at Hydro-Québec. 
In the study of budget appropriations, Bernard Drainville questioned Thierry Vandal and Minister Arcand on the testimony of the Charbonneau Commission indicating that engineering firms contributing to political parties would have been awarded hydro contracts. Quebec. . 
"In a spirit of transparency, we have the right to know if there has been collusion, corruption or any other form of malpractice in the granting of contracts at Hydro-Québec, which, I recall, belongs to all Quebecer, "said Bernard Drainville. 
Asked about this, Thierry Vandal has confirmed that cases of malversing had led to dismissal and disciplinary action within the Crown corporation, without being able to give an example. 
"We are concerned about the refusal of the CEO of Hydro-Québec and Minister Arcand commit to publicize the content of internal investigation and verification reports. They also refuse to provide us with a schedule. All we are told is that cases of malpractice have already occurred and that the Crown corporation collaborates with the UPAC and the Charbonneau Commission, "concluded the member for Marie-Victorin.
