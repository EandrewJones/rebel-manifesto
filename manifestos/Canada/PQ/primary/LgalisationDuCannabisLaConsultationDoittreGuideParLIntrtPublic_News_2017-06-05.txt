Quebec, on June 5, 2017 -The Liberal government finally heard the official opposition arguments about a consultation on the next legislation of cannabis, the Leader of the Official Opposition, Jean-François Lisée, and the Speech on public health, Sylvain Pagé, nevertheless find that the Liberals have lost valuable time. They also deplore their refusal to consult a transparent exercise. However, the official opposition offers its collaboration and will participate in good faith, so that public health and prevention issues are at the heart of the approach. 
IN SHORT 
- The government having gone to its arguments, the official opposition welcomes the announcement of a consultation.
