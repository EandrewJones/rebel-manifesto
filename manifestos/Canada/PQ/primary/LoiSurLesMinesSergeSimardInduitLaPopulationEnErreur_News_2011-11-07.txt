Quebec - "It is aberrant that the Minister Delegate to Natural Resources and Wildlife can rise in the National Assembly for the sole purpose of inducing the mistakes," said the member for Vachon and spokesperson. Official opposition in mining and shale gas, Martine Ouellet, after the question period this afternoon. "The Parti Québécois has always been in favor of responsible and effective mining development. To this end, we have submitted several amendments to improve Bill 14 and they have mostly been postponed from the reverse of the hand by the Liberal Minister, "continued the member. 
"Obviously, the Liberal government is against processing in Quebec, against the royalties and enrichment of Quebec, against the transparency and dissemination of information since the Liberal members refused amendments to introduce all these notions to the bill. , "Added Martine Ouellet. 
"The Parti Québécois is in favor of the protection of the territory, both for the mines and for shale gas. The Parti Québécois wants more than anything that Quebeckers benefit from income from Quebec's natural resources and we will never give out the blackmail that the Liberal government is doing in the regions currently, "said the member.
