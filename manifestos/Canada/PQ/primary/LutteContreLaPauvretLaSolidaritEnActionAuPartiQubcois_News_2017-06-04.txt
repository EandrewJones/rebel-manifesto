Quebec, June 4, 2017 -The Leader of the Official Opposition, Jean-François Lisée, introduced today the members and the mandate of the solidarity team in action: MPs Catherine Fournier (Marie? Victorin), Harold Lebel (Rimouski) and Dave Turcotte (Saint John). 
IN SHORT 
- The solidarity team in action has the mandate to go on the ground, meet organizations and groups, to develop a poverty control policy.
