Québec- The member for Jonquière and spokesman of the Parti Québécois on the environment and the fight against climate change, Sylvain Gaudreault, is pleased to have been invited by the Canadian Section Declimate Reality Project at his next training of ambassadors of the climate, to be held in Denver, Colorado, from 2 to 4 March 2017. 
The Declimate Reality Training Program Projects an initiative of the former US Vice President, Al Gore. Coming from the four corners of the world, hundreds of participants will benefit from Mr. Gore's teachings about climate change. These training courses aim to provide tools to ambassadors to enable them to do education about the climate crisis and becoming effective change players. 
"I am very proud to have been invited to this prestigious event. The Parti Québécois takes it as a sign of encouragement with regard to its firm determination to combat efficiently for the reduction of greenhouse gases and against climate change, "mentioned the member. 
"Our chef has been clear: voting blue is vote green! In this year of Congress for the Parti Québécois, at the end of which the members will adopt a new program, I intend to take advantage of my achievements so that our political training continues to be fully engaged in the fight against Climate change, "continued the official opposition spokesperson. 
Sylvain Gaudreault said that on the environmental level, among the major political parties in Quebec, it is the Quebecois party that already has the most concrete, most realistic and feasible plan. "I plan to inspire the training offered by Leclimate Reality ProjectAfin to improve the already ambitious greenhouse gas emissions reduction plan of the Parti Québécois. I also want to enrich me with the experience of Al Gore to involve the Quebec population in the development and implementation of a concrete climate action plan, "he concluded .
