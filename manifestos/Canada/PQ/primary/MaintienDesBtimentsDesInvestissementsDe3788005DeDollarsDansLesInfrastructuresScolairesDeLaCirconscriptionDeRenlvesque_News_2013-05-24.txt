Baie-Comeau- The member for René-Lévesque, Chief Whip of the Government, Head of the North Shore, Marjolain Dufour, today announced, on behalf of the Minister of Education, Recreation and Sport, Ms. Marie Malavoy, that government investments of $ 3,788,005 would be granted this year to ensure the maintenance of the buildings of the primary and secondary education institutions in the riding of René-Lévesque. This amount will allow the Estuary School Board to carry out the following projects: 
"I am proud today to share important investments in the riding of René-Lévesque, who aim to keep our school infrastructure in good condition. This is an excellent news for our children. It is almost $ 3.8 million that will be invested in our schools, mainly at Boisvert School that must be rebuilt in part, "said Marjolain Dufour. 
For the year 2012-2013, some $ 386.6 million will be invested in carrying out infrastructure projects in all the Quebec school boards. These projects, which will be implemented as part of the Maintaining Buildings measure, aim to carry out components repairing or repairing buildings used for educational or administrative purposes, such as roofing and external facing, Replacing doors and windows, the improvement of heating and ventilation systems or the renovation of sanitary facilities. 
Remember that investments made to improve school infrastructure are part of the Quebec Infrastructure Duplan. In the School Board Network only, this plan provides investments to preserve the school heritage and resort the maintenance deficit of the real estate park. 
"The investments announced today demonstrate the importance of the Government of Quebec by acting to preserve our real estate heritage and to ensure that the school continues to be a healthy living environment, conducive to learning, welcoming and safe. Students who attend our schools and the staff who work there merit, "Malavoy said.
