Quebec- The Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Occupation of the Territory and Minister of Transport, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, and the Minister of International Relations, Francophonie and Foreign Trade, and Minister responsible for the Montreal Region, Mr. Jean-François Lisée, congratulate the new mayor and welcome the speed with which the elected officials of the City of Montreal have the election of a new mayor. 
"I would first like to congratulate Mr. Laurent Blanchard for his election and to ensure my entire collaboration. I also want to thank all the elected officials of the City of Montreal for exercising their right to vote with rigor and commitment despite a difficult context, "said Minister Gaudreault. 
"This rapid election demonstrates that Montréal institutions work. Our government remains available to accompany, if necessary, the elected officials of the City of Montreal so that the projects follow their course and that services to citizens are insured by the next municipal elections, "added the minister. 
Remember that the election law and referendums in the municipalities enact only when the vacancy of the Mayor position is noted within 12 months of the day fixed for the election of the next general election and that the Council did not decree It must be filled by a partial election, the councilors must, within 30 days of the opinion of the vacancy, elect one of them in this position.
