Montreal- The member for Bourget and the official opposition for the culture and communications, Maka Kotto, wanted to congratulate warmly Jean-Marc Couture who won last night the grand finale of Star Academy. He also greeted the thirteen singers and singers who, through their determination and dynamism, held Quebec in breath in recent weeks. 
"After Wilfred Le Bouthillier, Stéphanie Lapointe, Marc-André Fortin and Maxime Landry, the young musician and singer Jean-Marc Couture makes a very remarked entrance in the world of Quebec song. I wish him, on behalf of all my colleagues from the Parti Québécois, a beautiful and long artistic career, "said the official opposition spokesman. 
The member for Bourget also wanted to congratulate Julie Snyder, the faculty and all the craftsmen of the shadow who contributed to the success of this fifth edition which sprayed the Dominical benefit of Star Academy at the top of the listening ratings of the Quebec television.
