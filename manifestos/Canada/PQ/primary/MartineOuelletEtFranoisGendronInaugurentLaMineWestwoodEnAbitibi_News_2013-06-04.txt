Quebec- The Minister of Natural Resources, Martine Ouellet, and the Deputy Prime Minister and Minister responsible for the Abitibi-Témiscamingue Region, François Gendron, are proud to participate in the official opening of the Westwood Mine, an auriferous project From the Canadian Mining Company IAMGOLD, located between Val-d'Or and Rouyn-Noranda. 
"The opening of the Westwood Mine confirms that mining development continues in Quebec. The announcement of 700 jobs for the next 19 years is an excellent news in a region where the mining industry is at the center of economic activities, "said Martine Ouellet, Minister of Natural Resources. 
The investment of IAMGOLD reaches the $ 700 million, which includes the renovation of the treatment plant, located two kilometers from the mine. The tonnage of the mine will be 800,000 tonnes of ore for expected production of 180,000 ounces of gold per year.
