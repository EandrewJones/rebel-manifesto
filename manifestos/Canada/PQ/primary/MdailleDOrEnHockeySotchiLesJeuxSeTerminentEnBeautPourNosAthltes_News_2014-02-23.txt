Quebec- Quebec's Premier, Pauline Marois, and the Minister of Education, Recreation and Sport, Marie Malavoy, are pleased to congratulate the male hockey team for this gold medal obtained today. 
"I extend my most sincere congratulations to the male hockey team, who harvested the gold medal at the Sochi Olympics. Marc-Edouard Vlasic, Patrice Bergeron, Roberto Luongo and Martin St-Louis, in particular, can be proud of their performance. This medal is the joy of their many supporters! "Said the Prime Minister. 
"High-level athletes play an important role in the transmission of values such as the importance of active life. The admiration they arouse leads to many young people in their wake. So we have more than one reason to applaud them and pay homage, "said Malavoy Minister. 
For more information on Quebec athletes who participated in the Sochi Olympic Games, see ausportcom.qc.ca.
