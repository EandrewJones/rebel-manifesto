Economic and commercial MexicoMission in Mexico Prime Minister of Quebec, Pauline Marois, has been a great success. In three particularly intensive three days, it has allowed more than 100 participants representing 73 SMEs, associations and research institutions to hold more than 400 business meetings with Mexican partners. 
During a conference-lunch organized by Quebec, jointly with three major Mexican business associations, the Prime Minister delivered a speech in front of a parterre composed of 340 business figures, including 250 Mexican. "This activity allowed me to see the strength of the relationship between Quebec and Mexico. On this occasion, all understood the need to strengthen and increase economic exchange between partners under NAFTA, "Ms. Marois said.
