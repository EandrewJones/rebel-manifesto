Quebec, January 16, 2020 -The Member of Matane-Matapédia and parliamentary leader of the Parti Québécois, Pascal Bérubé, brought some adjustments to the distribution of spokesperson files in the parliamentary wing of the Parti Québécois. 
IN SHORT 
Fundamental file for the Parti Québécois, the French language becomes the responsibility of the parliamentary chief. "I will continue the excellent work done by Joël Arseneau in order to highlight our proposals to promote French and strengthen Law 101; They are numerous, relevant and can be applied quickly, "said Pascal Bérubé. 
For his part, Joël Arseneau is entrusted with the important responsibility of the health record. "The Calculator government is waiting at this time to fulfill its commitments in this matter, and we are convinced that Joël will know effectively and eloquently defend the interests of Quebeckers, who demand better access to health care. At the same time, the parliamentary responsibilities of Sylvain Gaudreault has thus been alleviated during the CHEFFEE RACE, "continued the parliamentary manager. 
Finally, Harold Lebel became a spokesperson for social services. "Harold is a person of heart; His commitment to vulnerable people is constant and sincere. This new responsibility has just been complementary for its mandates in terms of social solidarity and for seniors, in particular, and that it already fulfills brilliantly. The important workload for Lorraine Richard his role as Commissioner for the Laurent Commission, where it works useful for the protection of childhood, made this adjustment necessary, "said Pascal Bérubé.
