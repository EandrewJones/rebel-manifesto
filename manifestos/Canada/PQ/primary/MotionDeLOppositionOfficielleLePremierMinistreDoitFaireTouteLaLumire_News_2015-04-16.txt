Quebec- The member for Verchères and official opposition spokesperson for ethics and integrity, Stéphane Bergeron, will submit today to the National Assembly a motion requesting the Commission of Institutions to make all the light Of all the professional activities of the Prime Minister within the Companies Amorfix Life Sciences and Canadian Royalties, from his departure from the National Assembly until today. 
"Recently published information in the media, concerning the company Amorfix and the loan consented to Canadian Royalties, asks questions about the professional life of the Prime Minister within these two companies. In all transparency, this one must come to answer the questions of parliamentarians, "said Stéphane Bergeron. 
The motion reads as follows: 
"That the Committee on Institutions makes light on all the professional activities of the Prime Minister within the companies Amorfix Life Sciences and Canadian Royalties, from his departure from the National Assembly in June 2008 to today; 
Whereas the Prime Minister's presentation is for a maximum of 15 minutes and that the exchanges with the members of the Commission are for a maximum of 3 hours; 
Whereas the Commission reports to the National Assembly no later than May 18, 2015, unless the Commission decides otherwise; 
Finally, that this motion becomes an order of the assembly. »
