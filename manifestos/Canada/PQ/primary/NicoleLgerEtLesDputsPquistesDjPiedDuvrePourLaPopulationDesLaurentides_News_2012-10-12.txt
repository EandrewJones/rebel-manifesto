Saint-Jerome - The Minister of the Family and Minister responsible for the regions of Laval and the Laurentians and MP from Pointe-aux-Trembles, Mrs. Nicole Léger, came to meet the members of the caucus of the Laurentides today. On this occasion, she was able to take stock of the stakes and regional concerns: "I am very happy with this first meeting with my colleagues from the Laurentian caucus. They can be assured of my entire collaboration and that I will assume my responsibility as minister responsible for the Laurentians region with seriousness and enthusiasm. I am sure it will be the beginning of a beautiful and good team work. " 
In the afternoon, the deputies and the minister will encounter some of the interveners in the region. "I am very pleased that my colleague Nicole Léger can participate in our Caucus of the members of the Laurentides and may also meet with us this afternoon some representatives of the community. This demonstrates the great interest of the minister with regard to the stakes of our region, "said the member for Labelle and President of the Caucus of the MPs of the Laurentides, Mr. Sylvain Pagé. 
Madame Léger wishes to express his desire to work with all the actors of the region and that the population can count on his determination. "I want to make sure of my greatest collaboration. This is my first visit to the region as responsible minister and it will not be the last one, "concluded Nicole Léger
