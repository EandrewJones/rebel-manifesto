Québec- Québec Prime Minister Pauline Marois announces the appointment of two deputies as parliamentary deputies within the government team. 
Alain Therrien, Member of Parliament for Sanguinet, is appointed Parliamentary Assistant to the Minister of Finance and the Economy. 
Daniel Breton, Member of Parliament for Sainte-Marie-Saint-Jacques, is appointed Parliamentary Assistant to the Prime Minister, for the Electrification of Transport component. 
"These members will play a leading role in our team thanks to their expertise and experience," said the premier.
