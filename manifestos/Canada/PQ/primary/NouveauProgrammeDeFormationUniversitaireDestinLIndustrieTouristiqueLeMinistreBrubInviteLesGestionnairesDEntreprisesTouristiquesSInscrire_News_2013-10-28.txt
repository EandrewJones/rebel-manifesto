Quebec- The Minister Delegate to Tourism, Mr. Pascal Bérubé, invites the tourism industry managers to register for a training program designed for them. The program is offered by the Development Science Development Center (ESG) from the University of Quebec in Montreal (UQAM). 
This training program aims to develop skills for traveler traveler managers taking into account the particularities and issues of this industry. Seven days from November 2013 to February 2014, training activities will focus on diversified topics such as tourism industry issues, tourism marketing, innovation and new tourism models, management in context of change and more. 
"The training offered by the GSP represents an interesting offer for all the tourism industry managers to have access to the development that concerns them directly. The program has been tailor-made to meet the needs and concrete issues of the tourism industry. I strongly encourage the network's stakeholders to take advantage of this opportunity by as soon as possible to the training program whose first activity is scheduled for on November 20, "said Minister Bérubé. 
The establishment of the training program for tourism managers is made possible thanks to the partnership between Tourisme Québec, the Tourism Watch Network of the Transat Tourism Chair and the Center for the Development of the School of Science. UQAM management. Tourisme Québec has provided financial support from $ 42,680 for the implementation of the program. The amount granted came from the development support program and tourism promotion.
