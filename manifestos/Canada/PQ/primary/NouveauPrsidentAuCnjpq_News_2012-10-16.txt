Montreal- The National Committee of Young People of the Parti Québécois (CNJPQ) is proud to announce the appointment of Frédéric St-Jean as Acting President. 
Mr. St-Jean is a long-standing activist for the Parti Québécois. Included since 2002, he has been part of the electoral teams of Maka Kotto and Emilien Pelletier. He sat at the National Youth Executive from August 2009 to May 2011, before returning to last January. He is also president of the Parti Québécois Association of Saint-Hyacinthe since December 2010. Holding a degree in architectural technology, he is currently employed in the same sector of activity. 
The new president and the executive of the National Youth Committee wish to thank Alexandre Banville for his mandate to the presidency and, above all, wish him the best of success in his new challenges.
