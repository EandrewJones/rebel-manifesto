Montreal, August 6, 2018 - The President of the Parti Québécois, Gabrielle Lemieux, and the member for Matane-Matapédia and spokesperson for the initiative, Pascal Bérubé, unveiled the advertising offensive of the Parti Québécois. Fun and irreverent, these advertisements will be broadcast in several regions of Quebec, as well as on the digital platforms of the Parti Québécois. 
"While many are still on vacation, we wanted to challenge Quebeckers and Quebeckers on a light and summer tone. I insist, however: through the jokes, there is a serious message. Quebecers' priorities, it's serious! We have the best proposals to advance Quebec; That too, it's serious! ", Made Pascal Bérubé. 
Particular Party advertisements will be broadcast in the coming days on the airwaves of several radios, on the Internet as well as in the form of posters near highways. This serar'ocasion to send a strong message to Quebecers. "We know we have challenges to take up, but we are attacking and ready for the campaign. Our lucidity and combativity will give us the momentum we need. Do you find our surprising countryside? Expect what you are surprised again on next October 1st, "concluded the president of the Quebecois Party, Gabrielle Lemieux.
