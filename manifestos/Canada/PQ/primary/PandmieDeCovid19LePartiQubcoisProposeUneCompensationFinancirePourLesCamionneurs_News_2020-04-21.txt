Quebec, April 21, 2020 - The member for the Madeleine Islands and spokesperson for the Quebec's transport, Joël Arseneau, wants the Government of Quebec to quickly offer compensation to another category of essential workers: Truckers. 
IN SHORT 
"The truckers already have several heavy financial charges on the shoulders: insurance that skyrockets, registration, maintenance of the vehicle, and I pass. In addition, not only do they have to go to destination - with all the risks that it entails, since they come into contact with many people - but they also have to handle charges, to care for the paperwork , to assume the additional costs associated with their essential needs, including the safety equipment they must now obtain. They live with anxiety, fearing to put their loved ones at risk or lack cash. The time has come to offer them a real support, "said the member of the Madeleine Islands.
