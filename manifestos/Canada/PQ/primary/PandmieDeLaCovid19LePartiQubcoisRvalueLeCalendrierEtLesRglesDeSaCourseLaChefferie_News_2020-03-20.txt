Montreal, on March 20, 2020 - The Parti Québécois Coordination Conference has made more modifications to the operation of the management race, with the aim of collecting the instructions of the Public Health Branch. 
Here are the new dates to remember for this race at La CHEFFERIE: ▪ April 30: End of the nomination period and official start of the race▪ between the 1st of the 1st and the 20th of August: holding debates▪ 1staug: deadline to become Member or sympathizer in order to vote from 24 to 28 August: voting by telephone and by Internet▪ August 28: election of the new or new leader of the Parti Québécois 
In addition, several changes have been made to the settlement of the ladirection race, in order to adapt its functioning to the context of the pandemic. It should also be noted that Tuesday, March 17 last, the President of Election, Ms. Agnès Maltais, took the decision to authorize the transmission by email of the handwritten signatures. 
"We are sensitive to what the population lives in the current context. For the good, health and safety of all, we had to comply with the public health guidelines a priority. Of course, we will remain attentive to the development of Lapandemia and will act accordingly, "said Agnès Maltais. 
The hon. Member for Matane-Matapédia, Pascal Bérubé, will continue to provide the interim in so much parliamentary leader of the Parti Québécois, until August 28th.
