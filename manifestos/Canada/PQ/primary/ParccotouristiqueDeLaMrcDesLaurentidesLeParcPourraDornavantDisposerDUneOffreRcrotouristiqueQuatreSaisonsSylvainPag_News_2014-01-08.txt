SINT-FAUSTIN-SQUARE- The member for Labelle, Government Manager of the Laurentides Region and President of the Government Caucus, Mr. Sylvain Pagé, on behalf of the Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and the Occupation of the Territory and Minister of Transport, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, announces the award of financial assistance from $ 49,668 to the ecotourism park of the Laurentides Regional County Municipality (MRC) for the renovation of his interpretation pavilion. 
More concretely, the project aims to improve the infrastructure of the interpretation pavilion which serves as a place of reception and rallying of the park's visitors. Its renovation is made necessary to avoid irreversible deterioration of infrastructure, but also for the development of a new alternative hosting service offer type "Chalets perched in trees". Indeed, the private "live perch" private company of Mont-Tremblant is ready to invest, from the spring of 2014, in the construction of about twenty safe rustic chalets, arranged in height, and this, without causing wrongs. Trees. This partnership, entirely funded by the company, will generate daily rentals that will report royalties and entrance fees to the ECItouristic Park of the MRC des Laurentides. 
"It is with great pride that the Government of Quebec contributes to the realization of this project that will improve the service offer and to better meet the needs of the tourist clientele of the Laurentians region. By improving infrastructure, the park will now have a four-season recreational offer, "said Mr. Pagé. 
Financial assistance comes from the cyclical development of the Ministry of Municipal Affairs, Regions and the Occupation of the Territory.
