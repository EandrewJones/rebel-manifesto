These requests for access as well as the documents obtained under the Access to Information Law will be accessible to the entire population, by their dissemination on the sitejeveuxSavory.org. 
Under the coordination of the Secretariat to democratic institutions and citizen participation (SIDPC), the pilot project jewelsavoir.org will apply to five public bodies, from October 2013 to March 2014. The ministries which Will participate are: the Ministry of Transport, the Ministry of Health and Social Services, the Ministry of Higher Education, Research, Science and Technology, the Ministry of Culture and Communications as well as the Ministry of Sustainable Development, Environment, Wildlife and Parks.
