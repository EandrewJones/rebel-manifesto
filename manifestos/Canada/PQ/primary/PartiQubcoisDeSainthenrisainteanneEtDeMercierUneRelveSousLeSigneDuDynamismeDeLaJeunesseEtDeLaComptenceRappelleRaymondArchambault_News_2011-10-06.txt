Montreal -The President of the National Executive Board of the Québécois Party, Raymond Archambault, wanted to emphasize today the arrival of two new party constituency presidents in the Montreal area. It is Gabrielle Lemieux, president of the Partijécois Party Association of Saint-Henri-Sainte-Anne, and Marc Desnoyers, President of the Mercier Association. "The Capacity of the Parti Québécois to renew itself by a dynamic, young and competent succession is perfectly illustrated by the election of Gabrielle and Marc. I am very pleased to be able to count them in the president team and party presidents. I welcome them, "said Raymond Archambault. 
Gabrielle Lemieux holds a bachelor's degree in communications and politics from the Université de Montréal and a master's degree in public administration of the National Public Administration School. It is advisor to evaluation in the Quebec public service. 
For its part, Marc Desnoyers holds a bachelor's degree in international relationship and international law and a master's degree in political science from UQÀM. He has been a researcher at the US Observatory of UQÀM. He teaches the history of American politics and is a web communication consultant.
