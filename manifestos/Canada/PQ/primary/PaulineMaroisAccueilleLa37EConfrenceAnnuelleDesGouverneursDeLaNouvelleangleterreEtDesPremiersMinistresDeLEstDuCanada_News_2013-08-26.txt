Quebec- The Prime Minister of Quebec, Pauline Marois, will host his counterparts from the Atlantic Provinces and the New England States as part of the 37th Annual Conference of the Governors of New England and the Prime Ministers of the East of Canada, on September 8th and 9th in Malbaie. It will co-decide the deliberations of this meeting with the governor of Rhode Island, Lincoln D. Chafee. 
The conference will be an opportunity for the heads of government to address common issues such as energy future, regional initiatives promoting the use of energy-efficient vehicles and adaptation to climate change, both in terms of protection. of the environment only under that of mutual mutual aid in case of emergency. The meeting will also help to obtain consensus aimed at increased integration of the actions of different governments into these subjects.
