Quebec- The Quebec Prime Minister and Head of the Parti Québécois, Pauline Marois, announced that general elections will be held on April 7. 
"Since the election of our government 18 months ago, we have accomplished a lot. We acted against corruption and resumed the control of government spending for the first time in 10 years. We have taken care of our world, while very actively supporting the Quebec economy. Our policies are ambitious for Quebec. Today, it's time to give us the means to go further, "said Pauline Marois. 
"In recent years, I have gathered wonderful women and men. With the new candidates that I will present you in the coming days, our already very strong team will be one of the strongest we have seen for a long time. We have proven itself. We were not afraid of making difficult decisions, but needed. We have a plan and the team to succeed, "added the Prime Minister. 
"We find ourselves facing two parties who want to prevent us from moving forward, she continued. We filed a responsible budget, but they had decided to oppose it even before reading it. By doing so, it's not just the government, but all Quebec that opposition parties hinder. » 
"So I gathered the Council of Ministers and we made the provisions to dissolve the National Assembly and trigger general elections. It will now be up to you, Quebeckers to decide. You know my team, which is very solid. And you know me: I'm not afraid to fully assume my responsibilities of head of government. Everything is in place. All we need is to have the means to act. What I ask you is to give us these means. We have the plan. We have the team. We have the determination. My team is determined. I am determined, "concluded the Prime Minister.
