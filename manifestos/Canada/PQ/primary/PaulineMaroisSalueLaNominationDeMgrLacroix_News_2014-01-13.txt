Montreal- The Quebec Prime Minister Pauline Marois, wishes to express his strong congratulations to Bishop Gérald Cyprien Lacroix, Archbishop of Quebec and Primate of the Canadian Church, for his appointment, by Pope Francis, to the rank of Cardinal. 
"This important appointment crowns the deep commitment of Bishop Lacroix to serve the Catholic Church and his faithful, since his ordination until his arrival at the head of the Archbishopé of Quebec. On behalf of the government and all Quebeckers, I would like to congratulate Bishop Lacroix and to extend his best wishes in the exercise of his new responsibilities, "said the Prime Minister.
