Quebec- As today in London the Games of the Thirtieth Olympiad, the official opposition and deputy officer of Charlevoix, Pauline Marois, held to offer his encouragement to the 66 Quebec athletes who will participate in the competitions during the course. for the next two weeks. 
"For years, you have been prepared. At the price of innumerable sacrifices, you targeted excellence. With the audacity of your youth, you are an example for all Quebeckers. For us, you are already champions, "said Pauline Marois. 
The London Olympic Games will take place from July 27 to August 12, 2012, while Quebec is likely to be plunged into election campaign. "I will definitely have a thought for athletes. In politics, as in Olympism, the work of a lifetime and the preparation of four years will be judged during a very short moment. I am in solidarity with these athletes who will lead in the coming days an important fight, "she continued. 
"One day, when the lights of the stadium have turned off and that the crowd will be killed, will only remain the memory of these great moments. I wish they will provide you with a motivation that will last a lifetime. We, in any case, you already inspire us. Take advantage of these games, live it fully! ", Concluded the official opposition leader.
