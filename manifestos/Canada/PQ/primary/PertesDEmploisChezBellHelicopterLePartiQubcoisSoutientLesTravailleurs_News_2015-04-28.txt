Quebec- The member for Saint-Jérôme and sentence of the official opposition in the area of economy, entrepreneurship, SMEs and exports, Pierre Karl Péladeau, deplores the 300 layoffs made by the company Bell Helicopter , in Mirabel. 
"We are in solidarity with the workers and their families, who must deal with this bad news. These layoffs bring worry, especially since the employees did not expect them. The government must accompany them and do more than just promise them a phone call-Québec tomorrow morning, "said Pierre Karl Péladeau. 
"Cups touching the research and development tax credit, as well as the government's refusal to restore the amounts in the last budget, hurt the aeronautics sector. In economic matters, the Liberals are sailing, without a concrete development policy; The result, predictable, is a bad job assessment, "concluded the hon. Member for Saint-Jérôme.
