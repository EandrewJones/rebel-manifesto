Quebec- The hon. Member for Saint-Jérôme and Leader of the Official Opposition, Pierre Karl Péladeau, announces changes to the Quebec party spokesperson. 
The member for Joliette, Véronique Hivon, will hencever speak for justice and professional laws. It retains its responsibilities in terms of culture, communications and end-of-life care. 
The hon. Member for Sanguinet, Alain Therrien, became spokesperson for energy and natural resources, while maintaining his responsibilities for economy and exports. 
The member for Beauharnois, Guy Leclair, will be spokesperson for pension plans and information resources, while maintaining his aboriginal responsibilities. 
Finally, the member for Gaspé, Gaétan Lelièvre, will be spokesperson for the Treasury Board, in addition to maintaining his responsibilities for the development of regions and fisheries.
