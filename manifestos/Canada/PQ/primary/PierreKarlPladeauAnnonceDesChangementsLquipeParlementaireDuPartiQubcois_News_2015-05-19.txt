Quebec- The head of the official opposition and hon. Member for Saint-Jérôme, Pierre Karl Péladeau, announces changes to the parliamentary wing team of the Parti Québécois. 
The hon. Member for Chicoutimi, Stéphane Bédard, becomes a parliamentary leader of the official opposition and spokesperson in the field of Economics, Entrepreneurship, SMEs and exports. The member for Taschereau, Agnès Maltais, is designated the deputy leader, while maintaining the spokesperson's responsibilities for the Capitale-Nationale Region as well as in the area of secularism, reform of democratic institutions and electoral law. . 
"These two deputies are seasoned parliamentarians, well by the debate at the National Assembly. They represent an asset and a force for the Parti Québécois team. I also thank Stéphane Bédard for the excellent work he did as Chief of the Official Opposition. He directed our tremendous team of MPs and MPs with Brio. My thanks also go to Agnès Maltese, for her beautiful work as a parliamentary leader. She will continue to play an important role in this team, "said Pierre Karl Péladeau. 
The Leader of the Official Opposition also wanted to emphasize the work of the hon. Member for Verchères, Stéphane Bergeron. "As an assistant leader, Stéphane was a centerpiece of parliamentary work done by our team. I thank him very warmly. I am also very happy since it will continue its effective spokesperson with the government, particularly in the field of Canadian intergovernmental cases, "he concluded.
