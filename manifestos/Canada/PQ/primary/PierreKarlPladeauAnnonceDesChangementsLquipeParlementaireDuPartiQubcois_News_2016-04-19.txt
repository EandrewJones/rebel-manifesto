Quebec- The head of the official opposition and hon. Member for Saint-Jérôme, Pierre Karl Péladeau, announces changes to the parliamentary wing team of the Parti Québécois. 
The newly elected MP in the riding of Chicoutimi, Mireille Jean, became the spokesperson for the Quebecois Party in innovation and digital economy. The member for René-Lévesque, Martin Ouellet, for his part, gets the records of the social economy and SMEs. He retains his title of spokesperson for entrepreneurship and exports. 
"Mireille is a seasoned businesswoman. She knows that a nation that wants to create wealth must be bold. For 25 years, it has been actively involved in ensuring the prosperity of the Saguenay-Lac-Saint-Jean region. As a spokesperson in innovation and digital economy, his in-depth knowledge of the high technology field will be used, "said Pierre Karl Péladeau. 
"Martin is also very sensitive to the issue of regional economic development. He knows the reality on the ground and has always supported local initiatives, which are often adapted, often, to the context of a given region. I am pleased to give him new records in direct link with his field of expertise, "added the leader of the official opposition.
