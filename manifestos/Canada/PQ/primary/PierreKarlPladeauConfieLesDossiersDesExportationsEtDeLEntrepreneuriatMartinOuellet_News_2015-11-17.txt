Quebec- The Leader of the Official Opposition, Pierre Karl Péladeau, announces that he entrusts the records of exports and entrepreneurship to the new member for René-Lévesque, Martin Ouellet. 
"We welcome a new colleague today, a dynamic man engaged in his community. Martin has led a very nice election campaign, remaining listening to his fellow citizens and at the service of the development of his region. He will do the same as a member of Parliament. He holds a very beautiful economic experience, which is why I told him files that are related to this area. It adds to our economic team, dedicated to supporting job creation and the development of our regions, totally abandoned by the Liberal government, "said the official opposition. 
"I am very proud to make my official entry today to the National Assembly in the Parti Québécois team. I thank Pierre Karl Péladeau for the confidence he shows me by trusting the files of exports and entrepreneurship. The citizens of René-Lévesque can already count on me; My team is in place to support and accompany people in the riding and region, "added Martin Ouellet. 
Sylvain Gaudreault becomes responsible for the region of Saguenay-Lac-Saint-Jean 
By the way, Pierre Karl Péladeau announced that the member for Jonquière, Sylvain Gaudreault, now has the responsibility of the Saguenay-Lac-Saint-Jean region.
