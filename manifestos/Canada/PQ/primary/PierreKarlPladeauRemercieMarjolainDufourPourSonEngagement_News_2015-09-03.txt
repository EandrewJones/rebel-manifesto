Quebec- It is with emotion that the leader of the official opposition, Pierre Karl Péladeau, warmly thanks the member for René-Lévesque and whip of the official opposition, Marjolan Dufour, who is forced to withdraw from the political life for health reasons. 
"In my name and the Quebec party, I want to salute the exceptional work that Marjolan Dufour has done over the last twelve years. It is obvious that I would have preferred to keep it among us, but I understand and respects its decision. When the body tells us to slow down the pace, you have to know listen to it and review its priorities, "said Pierre Karl Péladeau. 
Dedicated deputy and colleague respected, Marjolan Dufour has held various functions within the parliamentary wing of the Parti Québécois in the National Assembly, since its election as a member for René-Lévesque, on the North Shore, April 14, 2003 . He has been a spokesperson in terms of land, mines, forest and work; He also held the duties as the deputy whip of the official opposition, president of the Government Caucus, Chief Whip of the Government and, until today, from the Chief Opposition of the official opposition. 
"Marjolain's commitment to his colleagues, the Parti Québécois, his fellow citizens in the riding of René-Lévesque and Quebec entirely was flawless. I wish him a deserved rest and enjoy the presence of his family and grandchildren, "concluded Pierre Karl Péladeau.
