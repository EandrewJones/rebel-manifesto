Québec- As it has always been committed to doing so, the member for Saint-Jérôme, Pierre Karl Péladeau, held fully respect the provisions of the Code of Ethics and Ethics of the members of the National Assembly. 
For this purpose, as set out in Article 25 of the Code, before the debate on the Calm motion began, the member has declared publicly and without delay a personal interest separate from that of all members of Parliament . He was withdrawn from the session without exercising his right to vote or participate in the debate on the issue. 
The member for Saint-Jérôme had notified the Secretary General of the National Assembly and the Ethics Commissioner and Ethics.
