Quebec- The member for Saint-Jérôme and Leader of the Official Opposition, Pierre Karl Péladeau, would like to warmly thank Stéphane Bédard for all the years he has spent at the service of the population of Chicoutimi and the sovereignty of Quebec. 
"Stéphane Bédard served the citizens of the riding of Chicoutimi with dedication and determination. Since 1998, he has held several important parliamentary and government functions. In particular, it has been the parliamentary leader of the government and the official opposition, President of the Treasury Board and Leader of the Official Opposition. Stéphane is a colleague appreciated by his peers and respected by all members, regardless of their political allegiance. I would like to thank him very sincerely for the very precious help he brought me during my return to parliamentary, on May 19, following the choice of activists after the running race ", Store Pierre Karl Péladeau. 
"I would also like to pay tribute to Stéphane Bédard, the independentist activist. He is first and foremost a Québécois party activist. Since an early age, inspired by his father's action, he has worked for the full freedom of the Quebec nation. Near her world, he is also an indefatigable advocate from the regions of Quebec. On behalf of the whole party of the Parti Québécois, I thank Stéphane and wish him good success for the future, "concluded the official opposition.
