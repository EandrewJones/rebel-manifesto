Quebec- The Minister of Public Safety, Stéphane Bergeron and Mr. Jean-François Lisée, Minister responsible for the Montreal Region, announce the grant of $ 527,650, at eight organizations in the Montreal Area , in the Duplan of Quebec intervention on street gangs: 
"I am pleased to financially support these projects aimed at providing young people from the community different services tailored to their needs, thus preventing crime and their membership of street gangs. The mission of these organizations is a good example of an initiative that the Government of Quebec wants to encourage, so that they can continue their essential work, "said Bergeron. 
"By their commitment and their targeted actions, the interveners and stakeholders of these bodies help prevent and counter the phenomenon of street gangs, and it is with pride that we support them concretely in their efforts," said Mr. Lisée. . 
This program has been maintaining pressure on street gangs and offers young perspectives other than the community of delinquency and crime. It allows to continue actions to reduce the crime associated with street gangs, deteriorate young people at risk of joining and helping street gang members to leave this universe.
