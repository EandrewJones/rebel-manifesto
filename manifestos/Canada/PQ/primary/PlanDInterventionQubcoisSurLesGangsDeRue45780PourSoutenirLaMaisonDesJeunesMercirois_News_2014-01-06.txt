Quebec- The Minister of Public Safety, Mr. Stéphane Bergeron and Mrs Marie Malavoy, Minister responsible for the Montérégie Region, announce the granting of $ 45,780 at the House of Young Mercals, in the Duplan. Quebec intervention on street gangs. 
"I am pleased to financially support the project of this organization aimed at providing young people from the community different services adapted to their needs thus preventing crime and their support for street gangs. The mission of the House of Young Merciaries is a good example of an initiative that the Government of Quebec wishes to encourage, so that the organization can continue this essential work, "said Mr. Bergeron. 
"By their commitment and targeted actions, the interveners and stakeholders of the House of Young Merciaries contribute to preventing and countering the phenomenon of street gangs, and it is with pride that we support them concretely in their approaches," said Ms. Malavoy. 
This program has been maintaining pressure on street gangs and offers young perspectives other than the community of delinquency and crime. It allows to continue actions to reduce the crime associated with street gangs, deteriorate young people at risk of joining and helping street gang members to leave this universe.
