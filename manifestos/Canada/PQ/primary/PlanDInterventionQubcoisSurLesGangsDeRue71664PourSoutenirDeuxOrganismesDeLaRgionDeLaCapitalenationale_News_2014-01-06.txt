Quebec- Minister of Public Safety, Stéphane Bergeron and Ms. Agnès Maltais, Minister responsible for the Capitale-Nationale Region, announce the granting of $ 71,664 to two organizations in the Capital Region -National, in the Duplan of Quebec intervention on street gangs: 
"I am pleased to financially support these projects aimed at providing young people from the community different services tailored to their needs, thus preventing crime and their membership of street gangs. The mission of these organizations is a good example of an initiative that the Government of Quebec wishes to encourage, so that they can pursue this essential work, "said Bergeron. 
"By their commitment and targeted actions, stakeholders and stakeholders of these organizations help prevent and counter the phenomenon of street gangs, and it is proud that we support them concretely in their efforts," said Maltais. 
This program has been maintaining pressure on street gangs and offers young perspectives other than the community of delinquency and crime. It allows to continue actions to reduce the crime associated with street gangs, deteriorate young people at risk of joining and helping street gang members to leave this universe.
