Québec- Minister Delegate to Tourism and Minister responsible for the Bas-Saint-Laurent Region, Mr. Pascal Bérubé, today announced, on behalf of the Minister of Education, Leisure and Sport, Mrs Marie Malavoy Investments of $ 628,461 to improve the vocational training supply in the Bas-Saint-Laurent region. 
Government assistance will enable the Lighthouse School Board to implement the Agricultural Review Program (5335). The school board will be able to acquire the equipment required by the new curriculum. New investments will be carried out at the Mont-Joli-Mitis vocational training center. 
"The labor market is constantly evolving. If we want young people to go successfully, we need to provide them with training that meets their needs and requirements today. That's what these investments will allow and I am very happy, "said Minister Bérubé. 
"If there is a stimulating path that gives young people the taste to learn, it is good vocational training. The choice is vast and the job prospects are often very good, not to say excellent. For many, choosing vocational training is the opportunity to live his passion. Is it not the best way to approach the labor market? Minister Malavoy said.
