Montreal- The Electoral Platform of the Parti Québécois comes in three parts: assert itself, enrich themselves and help each other. "To assert itself, it is the first gesture that proud people make, explained the leader of the Parti Québécois, Pauline Marois. And we have reasons to be. After 250 years of British and Canadian regimes we are still here. We thought we were condemned, we clung to. We were told without literature and today, our artists shine on the scenes of the planet. To protect our language, we had to adopt a law that has been decried. Today, it represents a model of envied integration of the world that allows small Tremblay, Nguyen and Kharim to play together with the same accent. If we listened to the Liberals, it would be necessary to apologize for wanting to live in French in Quebec. We do not have to apologize to speak French, on the contrary, you have to be proud of our language, you have to say it! »
