Montreal- The Electoral Platform of the Parti Québécois comes in three parts: assert itself, enrich themselves and help each other. "We are proud to be Quebec and pride is also used to enrich themselves. Pride is the driving force. We were told that we could not handle a hydroelectric company. We were told that we could not carry North electricity to the south. It was impossible, so we did it. We were treated with water carriers. Today we carry people in our trains and in our planes. The North Plan is already an open bar. We have no control over Hydro-Québec, of which we are all shareholders. The property of our agricultural land is subject to speculation. How could we get there? I answer you that it can change. It's up to us to choose, "said Pauline Marois.
