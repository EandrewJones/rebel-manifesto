Sainte-Marie- The Minister of Labor, Employment and Social Solidarity and Minister responsible for the Capitale-Nationale and Chaudière-Appalaches, Ms. Agnès Maltais, on behalf of the Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and From the occupation of the territory and Minister of Transport, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, announces the award of financial assistance from 201,000 to the corporation of the Domaine du Lord Taschereau for the construction of an aerial gateway. 
Located in the heart of the city of Sainte-Marie, the estate of Lord Taschereau is a vast public space of more than 50 hectares dedicated to the observation and interpretation of a natural wetland clean to the Beauce: the plain Flood of the Chaudière River and its wet ecosystems. Spreading over more than 270 meters in length and 4.5 meters high, the aerial gateway will flourish, successively, a swamp, shrub swamp, a wooded and a wet meadow. Visitors will be able to appreciate the extent of floods which, otherwise, leave no trace in summer season. 
"It is with great pride that our government supports this tourist and ecological structuring project, which will develop the outdoor supply in the region and ensure the conservation and enhancement of the ecological diversity of the site for profit. Current and future generations, "said Minister Agnès Maltese. 
Financial assistance comes from the Cyclical Development Fund of the Ministry of Municipal Affairs, Regions and the Occupation of the Territory.
