Sainte-Agathe-des-Monts- The Vice-President of the National Assembly and Deputy of Bertrand, Mr. Claude Cousineau, on behalf of the Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and the Occupation of the Territory and Minister of Transport, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, announces the allocation of $ 20,520 financial assistance to the Country-d'Edrival pantry for the purchase of a refrigerated truck for delivery of commodities. 
Established in 1995, the Country-d'En-Top Country Country is a non-profit organization that offers food and clothing services to disadvantaged people from the Country Regional County Municipality (MRC) -High. Each week, there are some 500 households that benefit from its services in the food countertops of the seven municipalities and the four community organizations of the territory where it delivers commodities. However, because of the obsolete of his current truck, bought 10 years ago, the organization is obliged to acquire a new refrigerated truck to continue its mission. 
"Providing support services to the most vulnerable people in our society, this is an issue of all the moments that particularly affects our government. That's why we are proud to support this project that will strengthen solidarity in the territory of the MRC of the Upperland, "said the member Cousineau. 
Financial assistance comes from the Cyclical Development Fund of the Ministry of Municipal Affairs, Regions and the Occupation of the Territory.
