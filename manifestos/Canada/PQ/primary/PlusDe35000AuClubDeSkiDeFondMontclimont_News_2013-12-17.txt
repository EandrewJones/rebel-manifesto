Lac-au-Salmon - The member for Matane-Matapédia, Minister Delegate to Tourism and Minister responsible for the Bas-Saint-Laurent Region, Mr. Pascal Bérubé, on behalf of the Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and the Occupation of the Territory and Minister of Transport, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, Announces the award of $ 35,450 Financial Assistance to the Mont-Air Climate Current Ski Club of Lac-Au-Salmon for the acquisition or adaptation of technical equipment , Improvement of cross-country ski trails and renovation of the main building. 
Since its founding, the Mont-Air Clutter ski club plays an important role in the Matapédia community by being the only body to offer cross-country ski lessons given by qualified instructors, in addition to Offer the possibility of renting cross-country ski equipment for the whole family. The population can learn about the practice of cross-country skiing and take part in snowshoeing and snowshoeing activities in the trails. During the summer season, this ski center also serves as a day camp welcoming young people from the region. Thus, the acquisition and renovation of the main building are necessary for the organization to maintain its offer of service while promoting the growth of its customers. 
"Our government is committed to providing adequate services to Quebecers and Quebeckers. It is therefore with great pride that we attribute this financial assistance that will, in particular, make the main and attractive main cottage, "said Minister Pascal Bérubé. 
Financial assistance comes from the Cyclical Development Fund and the Fund of Support to the Flights of the Ministry of Municipal Affairs, Regions and the Occupation of the Territory.
