Sainte-Agathe-des-Monts- The Vice-President of the National Assembly and Deputy of Bertrand, Mr. Claude Cousineau, on behalf of the Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and the Occupation of the Territory and Minister of Transport, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, Announces the award of $ 35,600 to shareholding aid for starting an operation and distribution center of food. 
Sharing objective is a group of non-profit organizations that provide food support to more than 500 disadvantaged people from the Laurentian Regional County Municipality (MRC). To continue its mission and respond to an ever increasing demand, the organization must upgrade its equipment and to have a center of operation, collection and distribution to the six food countertops and the seven collective kitchens of The MRC. This center would be equipped with cold rooms, unloading equipment and a refrigerated truck to collect large suppliers and redistribute them as needed. It can thus meet the needs of the citizens of seven municipalities from the southern MRC. 
"Solidarity is a paramount value for our government, and that is why it is at the heart of our concerns and action. It is therefore proud that we support the realization of this promising project, which will ensure the well-being of the most vulnerable people and families of the MRC des Laurentides, "said the member Cousineau. 
Financial assistance comes from the Cyclical Development Fund of the Ministry of Municipal Affairs, Regions and the Occupation of the Territory.
