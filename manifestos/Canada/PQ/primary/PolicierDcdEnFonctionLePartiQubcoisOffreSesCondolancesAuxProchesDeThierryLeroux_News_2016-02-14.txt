Quebec- The Leader of the Official Opposition, Pierre Karl Péladeau, and the spokesman for the Parti Québécois in terms of public security, Pascal Bérubé, have to offer their condolences to the family, relatives and colleagues of the Police officer Thierry Leroux. Deceased this night during an operation in Lac-Simon, Abitibi-Témiscamingue. 
"All our thoughts are with the family and relatives of Agent Thierry Leroux, who died in the performance of his duties. It reminds us that members of the police forces, everywhere in Quebec, risk their lives every day, putting it at the service of their fellow citizens. The loss of one of theirs is always tragic, and we express them all our solidarity, "said Pierre Karl Péladeau. 
"Thierry Leroux was appreciated and engaged in his community. I want to express our gratitude to this young police officer and to his colleague present on the scene of the drama, just as we thank all the women and all the men who ensure every day to the safety of Quebecers in all regions Quebec, "added Pascal Bérubé.
