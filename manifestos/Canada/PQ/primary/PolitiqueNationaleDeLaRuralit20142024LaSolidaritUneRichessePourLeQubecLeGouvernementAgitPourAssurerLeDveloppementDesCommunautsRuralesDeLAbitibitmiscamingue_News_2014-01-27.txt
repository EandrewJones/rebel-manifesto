Senneterre- As part of a tour of Quebec that stops today in Abitibi-Témiscamingue, the Minister Delegate to the regions and minister responsible for the Gaspé-Îles-de-la-Madeleine region, Mr. Gaétan Lelièvre, Accompanied by the Deputy Prime Minister, Minister of Agriculture, Fisheries and Food, Minister responsible for the Abitibi-Témiscamingue Region and Member of the district of Abitibo-West, Mr. François Gendron, and the Minister of Aboriginal Affairs and MP, Ms. Elizabeth Larouche, confirmed the granting of nearly $ 6 million to rural RCMs in the Abitibi-Témiscamingue region and at The city of Rouyn-Noranda for the implementation of the national ruralicity of rurality. Following a commitment from the Prime Minister of Quebec, Ms. Pauline Marois, to improve rural pacts, the national rurality policy has been renewed for a period of ten years with an envelope of $ 470 million. Created in 2001 by the Parti Québécois, the national rural policy provides unprecedented support to rural and regions of Quebec.
