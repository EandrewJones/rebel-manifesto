SINT-HONORÉ- As part of a tour of Quebec that stops today in Saguenay-Lac-Saint-Jean, the Minister Delegate to the Regions and Minister responsible for the Gaspé-Islands Region of the Islands -Madeleine, Mr. Gaétan Lelièvre, accompanied by the Minister responsible for the Government Administration, President of the Treasury Board, Minister responsible for the Saguenay-Lac-Saint-Jean Region and MP for Chicoutimi, Mr. Stéphane Bédard, confirmed the granting. A sum of nearly $ 5 million to rural RCMs in the Saguenay-Lac-Saint-Jean region for the implementation of the national rurality policy. Following a commitment from the Quebec Prime Minister, Ms. Pauline Marois, to improve the rural pacts, the national rurality of rurality was renewed for a period of 10 years with an envelope of $ 470 million. Created in 2001 by the Parti Québécois, the national rural policy provides unprecedented support to rural and regions of Quebec. 
"The aid announced today will allow those who care about the development of their community to give a new impetus to the most inspiring and promising projects in their environment. Long mature, this policy is based on the capacity of rural areas to mobilize and work on an intersectoral basis: an innovative and decisive approach to occupation of territory and rurality in Quebec. This new national rural policy will allow the establishment of major projects in the regions of Quebec, "said Minister Lelièvre.
