"The Green Paper I present to you today will support a significant democratic exercise: the holding of national and regional consultations as well as a public consultation," said the minister. "The national politics of sport, leisure and physical activity concerns all citizens of Quebec, regardless of their age. This green book focuses on four issues. First, on access to physical, recreational and sports activities: whether it is access to facilities, available time or financial constraints of various orders. Second, on the quality of the experiments, that is to say on the supervision necessary to meet the needs of the people and the establishment of a safe framework. Third, on the valuation of the practice of physical, recreational and sporting activities. To change behaviors, it is necessary to sensitize, inform and motivate citizens and citizens. And finally, on consultation. A clear and shared vision of the roles and responsibilities of the various partners involved is an essential condition for providing the appropriate services.
