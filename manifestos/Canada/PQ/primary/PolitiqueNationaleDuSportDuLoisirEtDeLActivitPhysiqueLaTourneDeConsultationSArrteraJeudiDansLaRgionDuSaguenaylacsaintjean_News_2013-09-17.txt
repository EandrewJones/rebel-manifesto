Quebec- The Minister of Education, Recreation and Sport, Mrs Marie Malavoy, and his Parliamentary Assistant and President of the Government Caucus, Mr. Sylvain Pagé, announce that the Regional Tour of Consultation, led by the latter. will stop in the region of Saguenay-Lac-Saint-Jean on Thursday, September 19th. 
"These consultations allow us to meet many partners in the areas of sport, leisure and physical activity throughout Quebec. I am very happy to take advantage of their expertise so that together we can realize our willingness to improve the physical condition of Quebeckers, to promote the practice of physical, recreational and sports activities as well as to ensure Our sports elite the conditions necessary for its development, "said Mr. Pagé. 
Regional consultations follow the launch, June 10, from the Green Paper Taste and the pleasure of moving: towards a national sport, leisure and physical activity. The Green Paper is essentially a consultation tool that proposes a vision, stakes and guidance on which the discussions rely. 
"The views that will be shared during these consultations will bring valuable lighting for the development of our future national sport, leisure and physical activity. Let us together give the objective that the policy is a reflection of this society that we want to build, a society in action, which takes on hand and which goes at the end of its potential, "Malavoy said.
