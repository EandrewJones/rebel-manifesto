Chibougamau- Minister of Finance and Economy, Mr. Nicolas Marceau, and the member for Ungava and Parliamentary Assistant to the Minister of Natural Resources (Northern Affairs Component), Mr. Luc Ferland, are pleased to announce today HUI, on behalf of the Minister of Education, Recreation and Sport, Mrs Marie Malavoy, an investment of $ 7.6 million to enable expansion and redevelopment work at the Vocational Training Center. Baie-James as well as acquire and develop a modular and mobile school plant in the field of ore treatment in Chibougamau. 
This announcement is part of the Employment Priority Employment Policy, made public on October 7 and aimed at creating jobs in all regions of Quebec, in the short and medium term. This government policy aims in particular to make improvements to vocational training institutions and invest in the development of Northern Quebec. 
"Communities are at the heart of Nordic development. Thanks to this investment in vocational training in Chibougamau, we support innovative projects and meet the needs and aspirations of the population. Our local partner, the James Bay School Board, will be able, as part of its curricula, to diversify the know-how and the available skills and ensure a skilled workforce in the industry. Investing in Northern Public Infrastructure is an essential gesture to prepare for the future, "said Minister Marceau.
