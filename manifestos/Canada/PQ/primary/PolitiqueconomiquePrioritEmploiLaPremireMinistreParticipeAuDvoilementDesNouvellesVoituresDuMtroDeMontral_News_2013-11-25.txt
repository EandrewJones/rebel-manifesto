The Pocatire- The Prime Minister of Quebec, Pauline Marois, accompanied by the Minister Delegate to Tourism and Minister responsible for the Bas-Saint-Laurent Region, Pascal Bérubé, participated this virgin at the unveiling of the new cars of the Montreal metro, On the occasion of an event held at the Bombardier plant, the Pocatière, in the presence of more than 600 employees. 
"We are celebrating Quebec's know-how and the excellence of its workers. These new metro wagons, on the cutting edge of technology, are impressive. These cars will offer exceptional comfort, superior capacity, optimal security, greater performance and increased reliability, "said the premier. 
Presented by the Bombardier-Alstom Consortium, these nine first cars, of the 468 which will be produced in total, represent an important milestone in the establishment of a high priority: the electrification of transport. "The Montreal Transportation Corporation is a key partner to electrify transport. His contribution is valuable and will help us reach, collectively, the ambitious target of energy independence, "added Pauline Marois. 
The production of the new Cars of the Montreal metro creates directly and indirectly creates hundreds of jobs, not only to the Pocatière, but also everywhere else in Quebec. 
"I am proud to note that the Challenge launched at Consortium Bombardier-Alstom is successfully raised by Bas-Saint-Laurent workers. Even more concretely, this challenge represents quality jobs and stimulates the entire regional economy. The slots of the future like those of collective and electric transport are refreshing for the Bas-Saint-Laurent, "concluded the Minister Delegate to Tourism and Minister responsible for the Bas-Saint-Laurent region. 
New cars will gradually be integrated until the end of 2018.
