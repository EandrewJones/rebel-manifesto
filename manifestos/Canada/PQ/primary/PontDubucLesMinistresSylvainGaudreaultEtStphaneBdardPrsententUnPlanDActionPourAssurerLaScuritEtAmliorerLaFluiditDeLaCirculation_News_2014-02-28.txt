"Our plan clearly demonstrates the government's commitment to the population of the region. Scope, estimated at $ 45 million, will provide considerable improvements to the Dubuc Bridge, a strong and high quality infrastructure that is at the heart of our daily activities, "said Minister Gaudreault. 
"Beyond the major work that will be carried out by 2019, we give ourselves through this plan all the elements to meet the needs of the population for several decades," said Minister Bédard. 
The work, which will begin next spring, will bring this year on the repair of foundations, the repair of slabs and support devices. The population will be informed of the content and planning of work to minimize their impact on traffic. The ministry will also work closely with the city of Saguenay. 
"The action plan is a tangible example of the government's desire to offer a safe road network and in line with the needs of the population. Investments in the Dubuc Bridge meet our commitments and at the same time contribute to the prosperity of our region, "concluded Minister Sylvain Gaudreault.
