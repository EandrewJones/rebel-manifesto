Quebec - The member for Gouin and official opposition spokesman, Nicolas Girard, strongly denounces the statements of the Minister of Transport, Sam Hamad, who told the daily edition The Gazette from Thursday, June 30th He refused to make public inspection reports on the Mercier Bridge State because Quebeckers would not understand them. 
"The traffic between the island of Montreal and the South Shore has become a real hell for thousands of motorists and truckers. Everything indicates that the problems will continue for months if not years and that it will have considerable economic impacts. The minister should show transparency and not paternalism and contempt, "said Nicolas Girard. 
"Faced with this attitude of the Liberal government, Quebeckers are entitled to ask questions. Does the Minister of Transport have things to hide? If this is not the case, why play the hiding place? , Asked the member of the Quebecois Party. 
"Mr. Hamad should come back as soon as possible, assume his responsibilities and systematically make public all inspection reports that lead to emergency road session closures. The stuffing has lasted enough. Quebeckers have the right to know, "said the member for Gouin. 
It's not only in the Mercier Bridge's file that the Minister of Transport lacks transparency. For months, Minister Hamad has been refuses to make public a pre-feasibility study on the construction of a new Champlain bridge, the cost of which was at 40% by the Quebec government, despite repeated demands of the opposition. Official and Liberal MP Fatima Houda-Pepin.
