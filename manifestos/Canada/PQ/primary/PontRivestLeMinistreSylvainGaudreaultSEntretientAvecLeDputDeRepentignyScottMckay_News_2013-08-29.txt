Carleton-sur-Mer-on the margin of the Caucus of the Government of the Government of 28 and 29 August 29, the Minister of Transport and Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and the Occupation of the Territory, Sylvain Gaudreault, discussed the file Rivest Bridge with the member for Repentigny, Scott McKay. 
"I assured my colleague from all my collaboration. In addition, I told him about the immediate completion of an inspection that will better know the state of the Rivest bridge and the interventions that might be necessary, "mentioned Minister Gaudreault. 
"This meeting allowed me to clarify the context in which the demand recently formulated by the city of Repentigny is part of, including the strong population growth that has been seen for more than 20 years. I also reminded the minister the next opening of Repentigny Station, "concluded the member for Repentigny. 
The Rivest bridge spans the river the Assumption on the municipal road network of the city of Repentigny. Built in 1966, it is a total length of 168.4 meters. The data from the Ministry of Transport reveal that approximately 15,000 vehicles take the bridge daily.
