Québec- The member for Duplessis, Ms. Lorraine Richard, is proud to give, on behalf of the Minister of Public Safety, Mr. Stéphane Bergeron, the payment of $ 29,818.50 to the city of Port Cartier. This amount represents the last payment to which the City is entitled under the Memorandum of Understanding with the Ministry of Public Safety (MSP) for the realization of an analysis of solutions aimed at preventing and mitigating the risk of erosion. . 
"The realization of this analysis allows the municipal elected officials to study the different avenues in order to make informed choices with respect to the risks in the territory, for the benefit of citizens and citizens. I want to emphasize the excellent collaboration of the City of Port Cartier, which has made it possible to consider such a project and to help to act proactively in civil security, "said Minister Bergeron. 
"The city of Port Cartier has to deal with certain natural risks, including the erosion of the banks. This is why the planning and implementation of sustainable and efficient solutions are paramount to ensure a safer environment at the community, "said the member for Duplessis. 
According to the Memorandum of Understanding, the MSP undertakes to pay financial assistance equal to the eligible costs of the activities referred to in this Protocol, less a financial contribution set according to the demographic assessment. A first amount of $ 29,818.50 was paid to the City of Port Cartier on February 15, 2010 for the realization of the analysis of solutions. 
It should be remembered that this Memorandum of Understanding is part of the work of the prevention of the prevention of the main natural risks, which has been designed to guide government and municipal actions in the erosion of the coast, landslides and floods. This framework is aimed at implementing integrated risk management and is based on a risk analysis, solutions analysis and implementation.
