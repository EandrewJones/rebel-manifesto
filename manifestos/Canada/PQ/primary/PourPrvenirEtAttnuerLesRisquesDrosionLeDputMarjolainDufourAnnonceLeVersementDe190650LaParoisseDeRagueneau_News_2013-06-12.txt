Quebec- The member for René-Lévesque, Mr. Marjolain Dufour, is proud to give, on behalf of the Minister of Public Safety, Mr. Stéphane Bergeron, the payment of $ 190,650 to the parish of Ragueneau. This amount represents the last payment to which the parish is entitled under the Memorandum of Understanding with the Ministry of Public Safety (MSP) for the production of coastal protection. 
"I want to emphasize the excellent collaboration of the Parish of Ragueneau, which made it possible to carry out this work and to help to act proactively in civil security," said Minister Bergeron. 
"The parish of Ragueneau has to compose with certain natural risks, including the erosion of the banks. This is why the planning and implementation of sustainable and efficient solutions are paramount to ensure a safer environment at the community, "said the member for René-Lévesque. 
According to the Memorandum of Understanding, the MSP undertakes to pay financial assistance equal to the eligible costs of the activities referred to in this Protocol, less a financial contribution set according to the demographic assessment. Four payments totaling $ 1,609,350 had been submitted in 2008 and 2011 to the parish for the implementation of the coastal protection work. 
It should be remembered that this Memorandum of Understanding is part of the work of the prevention of the prevention of the main natural risks, which has been designed to guide government and municipal actions in the erosion of the coast, landslides and floods. This framework is aimed at implementing integrated risk management and is based on a risk analysis, solutions analysis and implementation.
