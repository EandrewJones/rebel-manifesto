Quebec- The member for Labelle and Government Manager of the Laurentian Region, Mr. Sylvain Pagé, on behalf of the Minister Delegate to the Regions, Mr. Gaétan Lelièvre, announced the payment of financial assistance from $ 22,430 to the company Gourmet Sauvage Inc. For the production and marketing of wild salty herbs. 
Financial assistance comes from the measure to stimulate the development of specialty products, an innovative measure from the National Rural Policy 2007-2014. 
Thanks to this financial support, the company will develop a new gastronomy product that will satisfy the palaces in search of new and unique flavors. Wild plants will be harvested in a natural environment on Quebec terroir by experienced gatherers. 
"By this measure, promoters in our community have additional financial resources to invest in the development of specialty products so as to diversify the local economy while actively participating in the development of our rural areas," said the member. Pagé. 
"We are very pleased with this innovative measure contained in the national rural policy that helps our craftsmen and our businesses to encourage the development of new products and promote the vitality of our territory", added Minister Lelièvre. 
The measure to stimulate the development of specialty products, the grant of which can reach $ 25,000 per project aims to support the creation of new activities in rural territory by focusing on projects related to resources, knowledge, knowledge. -Faire and the innovation capabilities of rural promoters. It supports microprojects in the bioalimentary sector as well as in the areas of non-timber forest products and forest, culture, heritage and crafts products.
