Chibougamau- The member for Ungava and Parliamentary Assistant to the Minister of Natural Resources (Nordic Affairs Component), Mr. Luc Ferland, on behalf of the Minister Delegate to the Regions, the Gaspé-Islands-de-la-Mapleine Region and MP From the riding of Gaspé, Mr. Gaétan Lelièvre, announced the payment of $ 50,000 financial assistance to Betula Enr Strategy. 
Financial assistance comes from the measure to stimulate the development of specialty products, an innovative measure from the National Rural Policy 2007-2014. 
From this sum, a first amount of $ 25,000 will allow the company to develop a natural birch sap natural health drink. The cost of this project is estimated at $ 111,306. The other portion of $ 25,000 will be devoted to the production and marketing of frozen, marinated and dried wild mushrooms. The cost of this second project is estimated at $ 110,370. 
"By this measure, promoters in our community have additional financial resources to invest in the development of specialty products so as to diversify the local economy while actively participating in the development of our rural circles," said the member Ferland. 
"We are very pleased with this innovative measure contained in the national rural policy that helps our craftsmen and our businesses to encourage the development of new products and promote the vitality of our territory", added Minister Lelièvre. 
The measure to stimulate the development of specialty products, the grant of which can reach $ 25,000 per project aims to support the creation of new activities in rural territory by focusing on projects related to resources, knowledge, knowledge. -Faire and the innovation capabilities of rural promoters. It supports microprojects in the bioalimentary sector as well as in the areas of non-timber forest products and forest, culture, heritage and crafts products.
