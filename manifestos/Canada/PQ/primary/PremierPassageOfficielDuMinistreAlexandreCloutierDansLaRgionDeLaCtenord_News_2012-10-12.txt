Seven-Islands - Sept-Îles Today, the Minister responsible for the Côte-Nord region, Mr. Alexandre Cloutier, accompanied by the member for Duplessis, Ms. Lorraine Richard, will meet several political actors and the world of business. These meetings aim to build links with the people of the North Shore and to exchange with them on the priorities and stakes of this region of Quebec, which is currently experiencing unprecedented economic development. 
"The North Shore offers several promising opportunities for development. North-Coastal people want to take an active part, and seize job opportunities that promise stimulating challenges and competitive remuneration. Our government will encourage this development and promote the investments necessary for the continuation of projects on the North Shore, "says Minister Cloutier. 
The minister will meet with the leaders of the economic development corporations as well as those of the Chambers of Commerce of Sept-Îles and Port Cartier. It will also have the opportunity to discuss with representatives of the Havre-St-Pierre business community and other regions. Economic development, infrastructure development, the exploitation of natural resources and job creation are on the agenda. 
"This first direct contact with the North-Coastal will allow me to print their realities and listening to their needs," said Minister Cloutier who wants his team and maintain continuous and privileged relationships with The member for Duplessis and the various players in the region. 
Mr. Cloutier confirms that he will visit René-Lévesque very soon in the company of his colleague Marjolan Dufour. "The people of the North Shore have expectations towards our government and I can assure you that we will be available and attentive to their needs," he concludes.
