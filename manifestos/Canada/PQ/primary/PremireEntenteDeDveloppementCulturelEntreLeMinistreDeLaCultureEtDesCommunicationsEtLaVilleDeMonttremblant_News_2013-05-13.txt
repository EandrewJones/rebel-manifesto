The Biennial Agreement aims at five main objectives, the dissemination of local cultural information, support for emerging cultivation initiatives, the development of tools for promoting public art course and places of creation, the promotion of young people Regional talents and the development of trembling historical and immaterial heritage. 
The financial participation of the Ministry of Culture and Communications, granted under the Partnership Initiatives Program, amounts to $ 20,000 for two years. The contribution of the city of Mont-Tremblant is the same amount.
