Quebec- The member for Rouyn-Noranda-Témiscamingue, Mr. Gilles Chapadeau, on behalf of the Minister of Public Safety, Stéphane Bergeron, a financial assistance of $ 12,215 to the City of Rouyn-Noranda under the Joint Civil Protection Program (CPPC) 2011-2012. 
"The city of Rouyn-Noranda enjoys this financial assistance for the project to provide training in civil security, and thus facing possible claims more efficient," said Chapadeau.  This project will allow targeted staff to better understand its role in the context of emergency measures and be better equipped for the future. 
"We are proud to support municipalities that put in place measures to prevent a claim from or, where appropriate, to facilitate the return to the normal life of the victims affected by such circumstances. I want to highlight the efforts made by the City of Rouyn-Noranda to ensure the safety of citizens of the region, "said Minister Bergeron. 
Recall that CPPC is a federal program for municipalities, regional county municipalities, metropolitan communities, and government departments and agencies. This program, under the responsibility of Public Security Canada, is administered in Quebec by the Ministry of Public Safety. It aims to encourage efforts to improve and increase emergency response capacity through civil security projects.
