Quebec- The member for Rouyn-Noranda-Témiscamingue, Mr. Gilles Chapadeau, on behalf of the Minister of Public Safety, Stéphane Bergeron, a financial assistance of $ 15,161.93 to the MRC de Témiscamingue under the Joint Civil Protection Program (CPPC) 2011-2012. 
"The Témiscamingue MRC benefits from this financial assistance for the project to install a generator for the emergency coordination center, and thus dealing with possible claims more efficient," said M . Chapadeau. 
This project will allow the Emergency Coordination Center to be functional at any time and MRC municipalities to benefit from the facilities in case of emergency. 
"We are proud to support municipalities that put in place measures to prevent a claim from or, where appropriate, to facilitate the return to the normal life of the victims affected by such circumstances. I want to emphasize the efforts made by the MRC of Témiscamingue to ensure the safety of the citizens of the region, "said Minister Bergeron. 
Recall that CPPC is a federal program for municipalities, regional county municipalities, metropolitan communities, and government departments and agencies. This program, under the responsibility of Public Security Canada, is administered in Quebec by the Ministry of Public Safety. It aims to encourage efforts to improve and increase emergency response capacity through civil security projects.
