Quebec- The Minister of Public Safety, Stéphane Bergeron, in conjunction with the Minister responsible for the Estrie Region, Mr. Réjean Hébert, announces financial assistance from $ 10,314.33 to the City of Valcourt under the City. Joint Civil Protection Program (CPPC) 2011-2012. 
"The City of Valcourt benefits from this financial assistance for the project to develop a training program, and thus facing possible claims more efficient," said Mr. Hébert. 
This training will help improve knowledge in civil security and response capacity to an emergency. 
"We are proud to support municipalities that put in place measures to prevent a claim from or, where appropriate, to facilitate the return to the normal life of the victims affected by such circumstances. I want to emphasize the efforts made by the City of Valcourt to ensure the safety of citizens of the region, "said Minister Bergeron. 
Recall that CPPC is a federal program for municipalities, regional county municipalities, metropolitan communities, and government departments and agencies. This program, under the responsibility of Public Security Canada, is administered in Quebec by the Ministry of Public Safety. It aims to encourage efforts to improve and increase emergency response capacity through civil security projects.
