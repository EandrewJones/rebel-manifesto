Quebec- The Minister of Public Safety, Mr. Stéphane Bergeron, joins the Minister responsible for the Capitale-Nationale Region and the Chaudière-Appalaches Region, Ms. Agnès Maltais, to announce financial assistance of 25,000 $ to the Municipality of Scott under the Joint Civil Protection Program (CPPC) 2011-2012. 
"The Municipality of Scott enjoys this financial assistance for the project to acquire a vehicle, a boat and equipment for the water rescue, and to provide training in this area, in order to deal with Possible claims more efficient, "said Maltais. 
This project will cover the entire territory for staff safety, to intervene rapidly in fast-waters' fast flood situations, and also to intervene in the case of accidents or sports incidents . 
"We are proud to support municipalities that put in place measures to prevent a claim from or, where appropriate, to facilitate the return to the normal life of the victims affected by such circumstances. I want to emphasize the efforts made by the Municipality of Scott to ensure the safety of citizens of the region, "said Minister Bergeron. 
Recall that CPPC is a federal program for municipalities, regional county municipalities, metropolitan communities, and government departments and agencies. This program, under the responsibility of Public Security Canada, is administered in Quebec by the Ministry of Public Safety. It aims to encourage efforts to improve and increase emergency response capacity through civil security projects.
