Quebec- The Minister of Public Safety, Stéphane Bergeron, joins the Minister responsible for the Capitale-Nationale Region and the Chaudière-Appalaches Region, Ms. Agnès Maltais, to announce financial assistance of 3,317 $ 72 at the Kinnear's Mills Municipality under the 2011-2012 Joint Civil Protection Program (CPPC). 
"The Kinnear's Mills municipality benefits from this financial assistance for the project to develop a municipal coordination center, and thus cope with potential claims more efficient," Ms Maltais said. 
This project will improve the intervention capacity of the municipality in civil security. 
"We are proud to support municipalities that put in place measures to prevent a claim from or, where appropriate, to facilitate the return to the normal life of the victims affected by such circumstances. I want to emphasize the efforts made by the municipality of Kinnear's Mills to ensure the safety of citizens of the region, "said Minister Bergeron. 
Recall that CPPC is a federal program for municipalities, regional county municipalities, metropolitan communities, and government departments and agencies. This program, under the responsibility of Public Security Canada, is administered in Quebec by the Ministry of Public Safety. It aims to encourage efforts to improve and increase emergency response capacity through civil security projects.
