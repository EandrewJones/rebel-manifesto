Quebec- The Minister of Public Safety, Stéphane Bergeron, in conjunction with the Minister responsible for the Estrie Region, Réjean Hébert, announces financial assistance from $ 6,127.02 to the municipality of Hatley under the Joint Civil Protection Program (CPPC) 2011-2012. 
"The municipality of Hatley benefits from this financial assistance for the project to update the Civil Security Plan, and thus facing possible claims more efficient," said Mr. Hébert. 
This project will allow a significant upgrade of the state of preparation in civil security. 
"We are proud to support municipalities that put in place measures to prevent a claim from or, where appropriate, to facilitate the return to the normal life of the victims affected by such circumstances. I want to emphasize the efforts made by the municipality of Hatley to ensure the safety of citizens of the region, "said Minister Bergeron. 
Recall that CPPC is a federal program for municipalities, regional county municipalities, metropolitan communities, and government departments and agencies. This program, under the responsibility of Public Security Canada, is administered in Quebec by the Ministry of Public Safety. It aims to encourage efforts to improve and increase emergency response capacity through civil security projects.
