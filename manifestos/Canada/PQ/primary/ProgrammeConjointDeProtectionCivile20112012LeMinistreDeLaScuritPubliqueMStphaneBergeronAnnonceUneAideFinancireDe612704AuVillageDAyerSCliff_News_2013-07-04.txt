Quebec- The Minister of Public Safety, Stéphane Bergeron, in conjunction with the Minister responsible for the Estrie Region, Mr. Réjean Hébert, announces financial assistance from $ 6,127.04 at the village of Ayer's Cliff under From the Joint Civil Protection Program (CPPC) 2011-2012. 
"The village of Ayer's Cliff benefits from this financial assistance for the project allowing him to update the emergency plan and the civil security plan, and thus to deal with possible claims with More efficiency, "said Hébert. 
This project will allow a significant upgrade of the state of readiness and the improvement of civil security in the territory. 
"We are proud to support municipalities that put in place measures to prevent a claim from or, where appropriate, to facilitate the return to the normal life of the victims affected by such circumstances. I want to emphasize the efforts made by the village of Ayer's Cliff to ensure the safety of citizens of the region, "said Minister Bergeron. 
Recall that CPPC is a federal program for municipalities, regional county municipalities, metropolitan communities, and government departments and agencies. This program, under the responsibility of Public Security Canada, is administered in Quebec by the Ministry of Public Safety. It aims to encourage efforts to improve and increase emergency response capacity through civil security projects.
