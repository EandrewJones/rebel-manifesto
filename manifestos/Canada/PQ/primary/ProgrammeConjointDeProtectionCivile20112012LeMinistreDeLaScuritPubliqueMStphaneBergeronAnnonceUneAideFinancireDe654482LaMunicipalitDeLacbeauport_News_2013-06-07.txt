Quebec- The Minister of Public Safety, Stéphane Bergeron, joins the Minister responsible for the Capitale-Nationale Region and the Chaudière-Appalaches Region, Ms. Agnès Maltais, to announce financial assistance from 6,544 $ 82 at the Municipality of Lac-Beauport under the Joint Civil Protection Program (CPPC) 2011-2012. 
"The Municipality of Lac-Beauport enjoys this financial assistance for the project to acquire nautical rescue equipment, and thus facing possible claims more efficient," said Mrs Maltais . 
This project will greatly improve the quality of the majority of interventions related to civil security, while ensuring the safety of stakeholders. 
"We are proud to support municipalities that put in place measures to prevent a claim from or, where appropriate, to facilitate the return to the normal life of the victims affected by such circumstances. I want to emphasize the efforts made by the Municipality of Lac-Beauport to ensure the safety of citizens of the region, "said Minister Bergeron. 
Recall that CPPC is a federal program for municipalities, regional county municipalities, metropolitan communities, and government departments and agencies. This program, under the responsibility of Public Security Canada, is administered in Quebec by the Ministry of Public Safety. It aims to encourage efforts to improve and increase emergency response capacity through civil security projects.
