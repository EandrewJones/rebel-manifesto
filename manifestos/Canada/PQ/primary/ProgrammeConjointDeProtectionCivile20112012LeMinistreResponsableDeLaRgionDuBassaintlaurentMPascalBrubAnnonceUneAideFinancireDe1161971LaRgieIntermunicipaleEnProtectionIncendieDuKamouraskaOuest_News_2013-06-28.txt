Québec- Minister responsible for the Bas-Saint-Laurent Region, Mr. Pascal Bérubé, on behalf of the Minister of Public Safety, Stéphane Bergeron, was a financial assistance of $ 11,619.71 to the Intermunicipal Régie In Fire Protection of West Kamouraska under the Joint Civil Protection Program (CPPC) 2011-2012. 
"The Intermunicipal Régie in Fire Protection of Kamouraska West benefits from this financial assistance for the project to acquire rescue equipment, and thus facing possible claims more efficient," said Mr. Bérubé. 
This project will include rescue in the forest or other places hardly accessible. 
"We are proud to support municipalities that put in place measures to prevent a claim from or, where appropriate, to facilitate the return to the normal life of the victims affected by such circumstances. I want to emphasize the efforts made by the Intermunicipal Régie in the Fire Protection of West Kamouraska to ensure the safety of the citizens of the region, "said Minister Bergeron. 
Recall that CPPC is a federal program for municipalities, regional county municipalities, metropolitan communities, and government departments and agencies. This program, under the responsibility of Public Security Canada, is administered in Quebec by the Ministry of Public Safety. It aims to encourage efforts to improve and increase emergency response capacity through civil security projects.
