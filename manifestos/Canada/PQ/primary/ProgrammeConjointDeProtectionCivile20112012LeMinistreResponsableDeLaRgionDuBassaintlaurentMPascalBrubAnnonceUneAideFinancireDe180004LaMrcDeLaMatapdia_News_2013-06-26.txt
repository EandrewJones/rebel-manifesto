Québec- Minister responsible for the Bas-Saint-Laurent Region, Mr. Pascal Bérubé, on behalf of the Minister of Public Safety, Mr. Stéphane Bergeron, a financial assistance of $ 1,800.04 to the MRC Matapédia under the Joint Civil Protection Program (CPPC) 2011-2012. 
"The Matapédia MRC benefits from this financial assistance for the project to acquire a deployment kit, and thus facing possible claims more efficiently," Bérubé said. 
This kit will serve as the regional coordinator in civil security to intervene in all municipalities of the MRC de la Matapédia during emergency situations. 
"We are proud to support municipalities that put in place measures to prevent a claim from or, where appropriate, to facilitate the return to the normal life of the victims affected by such circumstances. I want to emphasize the efforts made by the Matapédia MRC to ensure the safety of citizens of the region, "said Minister Bergeron. 
Recall that CPPC is a federal program for municipalities, regional county municipalities, metropolitan communities, and government departments and agencies. This program, under the responsibility of Public Security Canada, is administered in Quebec by the Ministry of Public Safety. It aims to encourage efforts to improve and increase emergency response capacity through civil security projects.
