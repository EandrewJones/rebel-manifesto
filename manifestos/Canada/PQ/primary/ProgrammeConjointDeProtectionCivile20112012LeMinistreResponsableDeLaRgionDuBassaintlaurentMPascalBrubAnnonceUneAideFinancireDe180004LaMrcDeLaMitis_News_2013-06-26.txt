Québec- Minister responsible for the Bas-Saint-Laurent Region, Mr. Pascal Bérubé, on behalf of the Minister of Public Safety, Mr. Stéphane Bergeron, a financial assistance of $ 1,800.04 to the MRC MITIS under the Joint Civil Protection Program (CPPC) 2011-2012. 
"The MRC of the Mitis benefits from this financial assistance for the project to acquire a deployment kit, and thus deal with possible claims more efficiently," said Mr. Bérubé. 
This kit will effectively intervene on the entire territory of the MRC of Mitis. 
"We are proud to support municipalities that put in place measures to prevent a claim from or, where appropriate, to facilitate the return to the normal life of the victims affected by such circumstances. I want to highlight the efforts made by the MRC of Mitis to ensure the safety of citizens of the region, "said Minister Bergeron. 
Recall that CPPC is a federal program for municipalities, regional county municipalities, metropolitan communities, and government departments and agencies. This program, under the responsibility of Public Security Canada, is administered in Quebec by the Ministry of Public Safety. It aims to encourage efforts to improve and increase emergency response capacity through civil security projects.
