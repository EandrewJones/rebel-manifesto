Québec- Minister responsible for the Bas-Saint-Laurent Region, Mr. Pascal Bérubé, on behalf of the Minister of Public Safety, Mr. Stéphane Bergeron, a financial assistance of $ 25,566.36 to the MRC Rivière-du-Loup under the Joint Civil Protection Program (CPPC) 2011-2012. 
"The Rivière-du-Loup RCM benefits from this financial assistance for the project to implement a regional telecommunications network for emergency responders, and thus facing possible claims with more efficiency "Said Mr. Bérubé. 
This project will enable the improvement of communications between stakeholders in mutual assistance as well as the assurance of an emergency complementary link.  "We are proud to support municipalities that put in place measures to prevent a claim from or, where appropriate, to facilitate the return to the normal life of the victims affected by such circumstances. I want to emphasize the efforts made by the RCM of Rivière-du-Loup to ensure the safety of citizens of the region, "said Minister Bergeron. 
Recall that CPPC is a federal program for municipalities, regional county municipalities, metropolitan communities, and government departments and agencies. This program, under the responsibility of Public Security Canada, is administered in Quebec by the Ministry of Public Safety. It aims to encourage efforts to improve and increase emergency response capacity through civil security projects.
