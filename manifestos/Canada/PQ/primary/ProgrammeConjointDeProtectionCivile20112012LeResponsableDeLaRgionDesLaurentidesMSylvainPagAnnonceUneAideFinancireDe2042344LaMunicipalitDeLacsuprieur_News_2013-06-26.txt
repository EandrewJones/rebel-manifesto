Québec- The Government Manager of the Laurentians and Labelle Member Region, Mr. Sylvain Pagé, said, on behalf of the Minister of Public Safety, Mr. Stéphane Bergeron, a financial assistance of $ 20,423.44 to the municipality of Lac-Superior under the Joint Civil Protection Program (CPPC) 2011-2012. 
"The Municipality of Lac-Superior benefits from this financial assistance for the project to acquire a generator for the emergency operations center and the accommodation center, and thus facing any disasters with more efficiency, "said Mr. Pagé. 
This project will enable the municipality to implement all measures established in the emergency plan, including communications and computer systems. 
"We are proud to support municipalities that put in place measures to prevent a claim from or, where appropriate, to facilitate the return to the normal life of the victims affected by such circumstances. I want to emphasize the efforts made by the Municipality of Lac-Superior to ensure the safety of citizens of the region, "said Minister Bergeron. 
Recall that CPPC is a federal program for municipalities, regional county municipalities, metropolitan communities, and government departments and agencies. This program, under the responsibility of Public Security Canada, is administered in Quebec by the Ministry of Public Safety. It aims to encourage efforts to improve and increase emergency response capacity through civil security projects.
