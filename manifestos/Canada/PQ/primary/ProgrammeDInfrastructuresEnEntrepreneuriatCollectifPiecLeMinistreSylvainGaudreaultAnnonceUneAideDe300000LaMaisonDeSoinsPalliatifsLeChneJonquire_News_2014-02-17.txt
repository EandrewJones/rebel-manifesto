Jonquière - Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Occupation of the Territory, Minister of Transport and MP Officer of Jonquière, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, is pleased to announce the granting of $ 300,000 government assistance at home Oak for the construction of a residence specialized in palliative care in the territory of the Jonquière Health and Social Services Center (CSSS). This assistance comes from the program of infrastructures in collective entrepreneurship (PIEC). 
Social economy enterprise committed to people requiring palliative care, the mission of the oak is to offer beneficiaries at the end of life and their loved ones an environment and a set of diversified services allowing them to live this step in the Respect, integrity and serenity. 
"I am very proud of the help granted by our government for the House project Oak, which is a way of recognizing the important intake of this organization to the community. Thus, the construction of a new residence specialized in palliative care will help provide care and support services to beneficiaries until the end of their lives, in dignity. The realization of this project will allow this establishment to have adequate spaces for the greatest benefit of the people and their loved ones who will have access to a set of diversified services in a warm living environment, "said Minister Gaudreault.
