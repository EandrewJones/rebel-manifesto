Lac-Mégantic- The Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Occupation of the Territory and Minister of Transport, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, is pleased to confirm the $ 4.5 million financial assistance granted by the Government of Quebec for The completion of the construction of the new Lac-Mégantic bridge. 
"Confirmed financial assistance today demonstrates that our government is continuing its actions towards the City of Lac-Mégantic, so that its citizens quickly resume a normal life. The construction of this bridge will also contribute to unify, symbolically and economically, the founding districts of the city, "said Minister Gaudreault. 
More concretely, this future infrastructure will facilitate travel between the west and the east of the municipal territory by connecting the Fatima sector to the city center. This major project stems from the particular urban planning program that the Municipal Council of Lac-Mégantic adopted on September 9 to define a vision for its city center. The contribution of the Government of Quebec comes from the Infrastructures Québec-Municipalities Program (PIQM) included in the 2013-2023 Infrastructure Plan. 
In addition, remember that the Council of Ministers proceeded, on January 15, to the appointment of Mr. Martin Arsenault as Assistant Deputy Minister at the Ministry of Municipal Affairs, Regions and the Occupation of the Territory. Mr. Arsenault is responsible for coordinating government interventions as part of the reconstruction of the city center and the Lac-Mégantic infrastructure.
