Matane - Minister Delegate to Tourism, Minister responsible for the Bas-Saint-Laurent Region and MP Pascal Bérubé, is pleased to announce, on behalf of the Minister of Municipal Affairs, regions and the Occupation of the territory, Minister of Transport and MP for Jonquière, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, that the City of Matane will benefit from maximum government financial assistance of $ 733,740 for the replacement of several water pipes in the city. This help comes from the Infrastructure Québec-Municipal Infrastructures (PIQM). 
More specifically, the project aims to replace drinking water pipes, wastewater and rainwater under several segments of Avenue Saint-Jérôme. There will also be a sewer separation in some of these segments. At the end of these works, water pipes over almost half a kilometer from streets will be renewed in Matan. 
"I am very pleased with financial support today in the city of Matane. Our government recognizes the need to renew and replace drinking water, wastewater and storm sewer in communities across Quebec. That's why, in partnership with municipalities, we want to make sure that citizens benefit from sustainable and quality public services for current and future generations, "said Minister Pascal Bérubé. 
The realization of this project requires maximum government financial assistance of $ 733,740, under 1.5 of the PIQM. With a total envelope of $ 462 million, this new component of the PIQM program allows not only to ensure the sustainability of infrastructure, but also to enhance the quality of life of the population and to create many jobs overall.
