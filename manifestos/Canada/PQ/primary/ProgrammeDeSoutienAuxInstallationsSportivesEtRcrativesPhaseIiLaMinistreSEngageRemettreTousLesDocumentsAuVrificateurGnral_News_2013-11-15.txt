Quebec- The Minister of Education, Recreation and Sport, Mrs Marie Malavoy, is committed today to deliver to the Auditor General of Quebec all the documents used to the selection of projects announced on October 24 Last under the sports and recreational facilities support program - Phase II (PSISR). 
"The 84 announced projects are all eligible, complete and correspond to real needs. If the Auditor General wants to see the technical sheets prepared by the departmental officials, I will give him without hesitation all the documents relating to these choices because they were made on the basis of the value of the projects, regardless of The political color of the constituencies concerned. We have nothing to hide, unlike the Liberals, "said the minister. 
Of the 315 projects submitted to the appeal that ended in May 2012, 84 projects were selected from the projects deemed eligible by the department team responsible for the file.
