Témiscamingue- The Minister of Finance and the Economy, Mr Nicolas Marceau, with the Deputy Prime Minister, Minister of Agriculture, Fisheries and Food and Minister responsible for the Abitibi-Témiscamingue Region , François Gendron, and the member for Rouyn-Noranda-Témiscamingue, Mr. Gilles Chapadeau, today announced the award of a $ 17.8 million loan to TEMBEC for the continuation of the work modernization of the Témiscaming plant. 
This project, estimated at $ 355 million, requires modernization of plant facilities to increase the production capacity and quality of cellulose specialties produced and thus increase the profitability of the business. The plant's modernization project involves the installation of highly efficient equipment dedicated to the manufacture of cellulose.
