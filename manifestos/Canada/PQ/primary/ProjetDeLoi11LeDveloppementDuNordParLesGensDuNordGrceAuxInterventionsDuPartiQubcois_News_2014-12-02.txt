Quebec- The member for Duplessis and the official opposition to the Nordic development opposition, Lorraine Richard, is pleased that the amendments made by the Quebecois party to draft law 11 have ensured that the company from the North Plan should Being more present north of the 49th parallel, more transparent and more attentive to the populations of the territory. 
"The best placed to implement a sustainable and responsible North development, and thus avoid the mistakes of the past, are Quebeckers and Quebeckers who live there. After several hours of parliamentary committee debate, we managed to convince the government that it is essential for the Northern Plan society to be more symbolic with local populations. The amendments we brought to the bill creating society are reflecting this concern, "said Lorraine Richard.
