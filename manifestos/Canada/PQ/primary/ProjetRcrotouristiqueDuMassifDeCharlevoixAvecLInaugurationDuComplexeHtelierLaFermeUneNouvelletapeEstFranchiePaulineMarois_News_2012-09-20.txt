Baie-Saint-Paul- The Prime Minister of Quebec, Ms. Pauline Marois, is pleased to participate in the official inauguration of the Hotel La Farm, which constitutes the third pole of the recreational tourism project of the Charlevoix massif. 
The development of the mountain, the revitalization of the train and the construction of the new hotel complex The Farm will allow tourists and visitors to experience a unique experience, over the four seasons. In addition, these initiatives will help to highlight the riches of regional producers as well as the beauty of the majestic landscapes of the region. 
"Aimed at positioning the Charlevoix massif as a must-see destination, this major project, in addition to being beneficial for the tourism industry, will make it possible to radiate our capital internationally," said the first Minister of Quebec. 
"As a member of Charlevoix-Côte-de-Beaupré, I feel a sense of pride with respect to the people of our visionary and their audacity, mobilized to make us discover all the Potential of this innovative and structuring concept for the entire region, "concluded Pauline Marois.
