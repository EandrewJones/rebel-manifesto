Quebec- Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Agriculture, Fisheries and Food, Mr. François Gendron, participates today, at the launch of the first generic promotion campaign of Quebec's aquatic products on the market Interior, an initiative of the Quebec Association of the Fisheries Industry (AQIP). 
The activity will bring together in Boucherville from representatives of the fishing, aquaculture, food processing, marketing and distribution of Quebec aquatic products. From the outset, the minister welcomes this new campaign. "I am particularly proud to be here today to highlight the official launch of this first generic campaign that will feature aquatic species fished or high in Quebec," said Gendron. 
AQIP, which received financial assistance from $ 109,600 from the Government of Quebec to carry out this advertising transaction, provides, among other things, to create a particular certificate to enhance the goods of the sea from here with restaurateurs and Hoteliers du Québec. 
"We are proud to support the initiative of AQIP that will help highlight our fish and seafood and to show Quebec's know-how. Its realization is consistent with the priorities of the 2013-2018 NewPlan. Develop our business and commercial aquaculture industry, which I recently announced. It also frames with the objectives of the food sovereignty policy that will be launched soon by encouraging local production and the sustainable exploitation of our aquatic resources, "said Gendron.
