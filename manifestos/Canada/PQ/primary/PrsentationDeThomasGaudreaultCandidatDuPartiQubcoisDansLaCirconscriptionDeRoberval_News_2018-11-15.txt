The parliamentarian part of the Parti Québécois and MPA of Matane-Matapédia, Pascal Bérubé, as well as the member for Jonquière, Sylvain Gaudreault presented today in press conference the Quebec candidate in the riding of Roberval, Thomas Gaudreault, already candidate In the general election of October 1st for the Parti Québécois, with a view to the partial election of December 10, 2018. 
The local executive is preparing for the upcoming campaign alongside teams from other counties in the region. "I invite members and citizen activists of the coming region in Roberval. We will work to win, "said Sylvain Gaudreault. 
"I have next to me a young man who will be the 10th of December the youngest member of the National Assembly, and a heir from Bernard Landry. A young man of Albanel, competent, combative and courageous. We are here to win, and we will give every effort. Pursued Pascal Bérubé. 
"The important thing is to make sure to keep our hospitals in place and functional. Same for schools, each municipality must have its own to make education more accessible. Similarly in agriculture: we have to keep it local, with people from here for people from here. The people of Roberval want a strong voice that will represent them at the National Assembly and who will defend the project of independence, it is with pride that I will do it. Thomas Gaudreault concluded.
