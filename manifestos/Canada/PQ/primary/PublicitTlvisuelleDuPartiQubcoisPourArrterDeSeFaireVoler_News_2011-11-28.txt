Montreal -The Parti Québécois launched yesterday its television advertising, second phase of the advertising campaign 5 ideas for Quebec. It will be in the weeks for the next two weeks on the following channels: Life Canal, Series +, Historia and Canal D. You can also view it by following this link: http: //go.pq.org/54p. The listeners of the Double Occupation show could see him last night. 
By this advertising message, which recalls the main scandals associated with the Liberal Party and Jean Charest, the Parti Québécois desires right now establish the issues of the next election campaign. "The Liberal Party has allowed the political climate to rot and is at the origin of the cynicism of the population towards its political class. François Legault's CAQ offers nothing to stop corruption and renovate our democracy. The only party that proposes a real change to regain the confidence of Quebeckers, it is the Parti Québécois. The stake of the next election campaign is clear. The Liberals will have to be judged for having allowed to have stolen Quebecers, "said the President of the National Executive Board of the Quebecois, Raymond Archambault. 
The President of the Parti Québécois also invites Quebeckers to consult the party program by following this link: pq.org/programme and to see the advertising campaign launched last week by following this link: pq.org/5idees. "Seeing what we are proposing in terms of ideas and seeing the Change team representing the members and the leader of the Parti Québécois, I'm sure it's up to us that Quebeckers will trust the next election" , concluded Raymond Archambault.
