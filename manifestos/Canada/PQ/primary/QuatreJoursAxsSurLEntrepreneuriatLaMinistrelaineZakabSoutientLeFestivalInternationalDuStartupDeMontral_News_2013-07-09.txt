Montreal- The Minister Delegate to Industrial Policy and the Quebec Economic Development Bank, Ms. Élaine Zakaïb, announced the award of $ 10,000 financial assistance for the holding of the Montreal's third International Startup Festival, which will take place from 10 to 13 July. 
"The Montreal International Startup Festival is an ideal opportunity to increase Quebec entrepreneurship by promoting networking and expertise. For our entrepreneurs, it is a privileged moment to create fruitful partnerships with investors and establish an effective mesh. This festival will bring together more than 1,000 entrepreneurs and investors from both North America and Europe, "said Élaine Zakaïb. "Ensuring succession in entrepreneurship is one of the major concerns of our government and that's why we are proud to associate with this event. » 
The Montreal International Startup Festival is an annual event that brings together entrepreneurs, investors and key players in the technology sector. Exhibitions, conferences and workshops devoted to entrepreneurship and start-ups in information and communications technologies take place there. Start-ups are start-up companies that have a high potential for growth. 
"We are pleased with the support of the Government of Quebec, which will provide our companies with significant investment opportunities," said the founder of the Montreal Startup International Festival, Mr. Philippe Telio. "Thanks to this festival, we promote the growth of dynamic and promising companies, in Quebec and abroad. Our entrepreneurs must be in an international nature. They should be inspired by other communities around the world and form a network with them to ensure their prosperity. »
