Quebec- The Minister of Transport and Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Occupation of the Territory, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, announces the extension until December 31, 2013, a grant to the adaptation of taxis and coaches Longbrain for transporting people traveling in wheelchair, extension with a total amount of $ 1 million from the Green Fund. 
"Adapted transportation contributes to the academic, professional and social integration of persons with disabilities and it aims to ensure their degree of mobility and autonomy comparable to that of the general population. By continuing government assistance to the adaptation of taxis and intercity coaches, we clearly demonstrate our desire to continue the development of the accessibility of these means of transport and we reaffirm the importance of the social participation of people with disabilities and in Loss of autonomy, "says Gaudreault. 
Recall that the subsidy program for the adaptation of taxis and intercity coaches for the transport of people moving in a wheelchair ended on March 31st. Since its creation in 2007, the program has adapted 527 taxis and 62 coaches. 
Remember which Ministry of Transportation is currently working on the development of Quebec's sustainable mobility policy, which will take over from the current Quebec collective transport policy: "The new policy will have, among other things, to ensure greater mobility of People with disabilities and people with reduced mobility, regardless of their territory of residence. We want to achieve this goal by an organization of services that meets the needs of users and greater adaptation of public transit services, taxi transportation services and bus transportation services. The extension of the grant program for the adaptation of the taxis and intercity coaches for the transport of people moving in a wheelchair is part of this will, "concludes Minister Gaudreault.
