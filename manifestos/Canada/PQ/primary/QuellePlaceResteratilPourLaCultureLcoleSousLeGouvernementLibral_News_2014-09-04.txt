Quebec- The member for Joliette and sentence of the official opposition in the field of culture and communications, Véronique Hivon, asks the Minister of Culture and Communications, Hélène David, to reassure the circles of culture and the Education As for the place occupied by culture in our schools in this era of austerity. 
"While Quebec has not yet returned from the unacceptable statements by Minister Bolduc with respect to the importance of buying books in our school libraries, voices raise fears about the development of the school culture program. . Will Minister David add this program to advocacy that she has to do with her colleague for education? Requested Véronique Hivon. 
The member for Joliette wanted to remember that our schools are privileged places to put young people in contact with all forms of artistic expression. It is by attending the cultural institutions, and exchanging with the artists and craftsmen who animate them, that young people can themselves take all the extent of the importance and relevance of art and culture in their life, as well as in our society. 
In addition, schools are important links of the cultural chain. School trips, as well as educational and extracurricular activities, contribute to the fragile financial balance of the cultural environment. 
"Minister David has to quickly reassure the cultural and education community so that they can pursue their many partnerships and continue to feed the symbiosis that characterizes their reports and which is mutually beneficial. This is the quality of education, of course, but also the awakening to culture as well as the influence of our artists and our cultural institutions, "concluded Véronique Hivon.
