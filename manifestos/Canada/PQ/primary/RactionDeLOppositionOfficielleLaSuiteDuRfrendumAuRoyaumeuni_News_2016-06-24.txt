Quebec- The Leader of the Official Opposition and MP, Sylvain Gaudreault, and the member for Hochelaga-Maisonneuve and spokesman for the Quebecois in international relations, Carole Poirier, reacted to the result of the referendum which is held in the United Kingdom on Thursday. 
"Yesterday, as a sovereign state, the United Kingdom has voted on the future of its relationship with the European Union. We welcome this democratic exercise in which the British people has committed. The Parti Québécois takes note of the result of the vote of the population, which made the choice to leave the European Union. It is obvious that we will continue to carefully follow the debates that will take place and the decisions that will be taken soon in the United Kingdom and Europe, following this referendum, "said the head of the official opposition and the member. of Hochelaga-Maisonneuve.
