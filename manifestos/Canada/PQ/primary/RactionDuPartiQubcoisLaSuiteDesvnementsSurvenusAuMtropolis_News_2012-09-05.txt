Montreal - It is with sadness and consternation that the Parti Québécois has learned of the death of a man and a seriously injured person at the election rally who took place last night at Métropolis in Montreal. 
"My thoughts go first to the family and relatives of the deceased victim. Following this tragedy, all Quebeckers are in mourning today in front of such an act of free violence, "said the member for Charlevoix-Côte-de-Beaupré and prime minister elected, Pauline Marois. 
"Never has a society like ours will not leave the violence dictating its collective choices. It is by the democratic voice that must be expressed, as Quebeckers have been able to do it yesterday, "she concluded, wishing that the injured person knows unscathed.
