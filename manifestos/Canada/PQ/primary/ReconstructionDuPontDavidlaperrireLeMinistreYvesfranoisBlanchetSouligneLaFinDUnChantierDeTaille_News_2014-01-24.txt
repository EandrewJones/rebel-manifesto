Trois-Rivières- The Minister of Sustainable Development, Environment, Wildlife and Parks and Minister responsible for the Regions of Mauricie and Center-du-Québec, Mr. Yves-François Blanchet, on behalf of the Minister of Transport and Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Occupation of the Territory, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, wishes to salute the success of a major project, the reconstruction of the David-Laperrière Bridge, spanning the Saint-François River, on Route 132. 
This major project, which required investments in the order of $ 60 million, posed a major challenge: building a structure adapted to the needs while preserving the road link during the work. 
"The challenge was raised in accordance with deadlines and costs, and with the constant support of the environment that worked with the Department. This collaboration has been achieved by the active participation of representatives of Odanak, Saint-François-du-Lac and Pierreville to a traffic management committee, through the production of a documentary under the Governor of the Historical Society of The Pierreville area, as well as the preparation of an exhibition on the history of the bridge at the Abenakis Museum, "Blanchet said. 
Beginned in April 2011, the reconstruction work of the David-Laperrière Bridge were completed in November 2013. In particular, the project was to restore road users 132 the functionality of the road link, mainly by eliminating the limit of loads on the bridge. 
"This prestigious bridge by its size and history represents the link uniting three proud and dynamic communities, which now ensure the development of the history of the former bridge, dating back to 1932, to conclude the Minister Blanchet.
