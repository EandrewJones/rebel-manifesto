Québec- Ambulance services will be enhanced in Repentigny, with the addition of an ambulance and an additional 168 hours of service per week. This is what the Minister of Health and Social Services today announced and responsible for seniors, Réjean Hébert, and the member for Repentigny, Scott McKay. 
"Prehospital emergency services are a crucial element of the care chain and that is why we would like to take the necessary steps to improve this service offer. We are therefore happy to support the partners of this territory with the addition of this ambulance resource, "said Dr. Hébert. 
This territory is served by the company Ambulance Repentigny. This development is made possible by uploading $ 1,184,900 on an annual basis by the Ministry of Health and Social Services. 
"This addition of resources responds very fairly to an important need of the region. By improving the ambulance coverage in Repentigny, our government demonstrates that it understands the needs of our population in full population explosion and that it is truly listening, "said the member for Repentigny, Scott McKay.
