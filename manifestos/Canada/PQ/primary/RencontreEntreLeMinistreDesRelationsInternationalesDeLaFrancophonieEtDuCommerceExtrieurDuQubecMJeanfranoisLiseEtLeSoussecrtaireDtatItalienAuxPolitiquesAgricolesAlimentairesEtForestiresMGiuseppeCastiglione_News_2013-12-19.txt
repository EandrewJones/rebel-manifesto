Quebec- The Italian Assistant Secretary of Agricultural, Food and Forestry Policy Mr. Giuseppe Castiglione has hosted the Minister of International Relations, Francophonie and Foreign Trade from Quebec M. Jean-François Lisé. 
The meeting took place in an atmosphere of Cordial Collaboration, which highlighted the willingness of a new collaboration in the agri-food field between Quebec and Italy. 
Considering the expertise recognized on both sides, the cheese and organic sectors offer interesting potential for collaboration, as well as food security and sustainable development. 
They also welcomed the bilateral business prospects that will result from the principle agreement recently concluded between the European Union and Canada for the signing of a global economic and commercial agreement. 
Mr. Jean-François Lisée also served the Quebec's intention to participate in Milan Expo 2015 by a program of activities, despite the lack of Canada's accession.
