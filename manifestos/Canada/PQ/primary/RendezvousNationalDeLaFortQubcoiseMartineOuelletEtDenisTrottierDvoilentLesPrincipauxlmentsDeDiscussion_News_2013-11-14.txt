Quebec- In anticipation of the national rendezvous of the Quebec forest to be held on November 21 and 22, in the presence of the Prime Minister, Ms. Pauline Marois, the Minister of Natural Resources, Mrs. Martine Ouellet, and the member for Roberval and Parliamentary Assistant to Forest Files, Mr. Denis Trottier, reveal the main elements of discussion and make public the participant's notebook. 
"Conduct a national reflection on the timber sector as a driver of a new green economy and agree on a three-year forest plan of action, this is the exercise we invite the partners of the wood industry. The national rendezvous of the Quebec Forest will be a unique opportunity to redefine this sector of the future for Quebec and its regions, "said Martine Ouellet. 
The Government wants to open the discussion at the National Forest Rendez-vous on three main themes: adapterotre forestry in order to optimize the potential of our forests, transform the industry and develop high potential sectors and forces to ensure the future of The wood sector. 
"In recent weeks, I met the national players in the wood sector. The three discussion blocks we propose reflect the concerns expressed during these exchanges as well as the content of the documents we received. In light of these reflections, our government will propose solutions and partners will be invited to express themselves on them, "Trottier explained. 
The national rendez-vous de la Forest Québécoise will be broadcast live on the website of the event. The general public will be able to follow the exchanges between the participants and to take note of the different government proposals.
