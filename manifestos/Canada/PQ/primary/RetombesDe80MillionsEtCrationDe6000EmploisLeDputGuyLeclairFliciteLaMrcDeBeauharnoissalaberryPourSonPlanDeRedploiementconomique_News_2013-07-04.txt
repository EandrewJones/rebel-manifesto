SALABERRY-DE-VALLEYFIELD- The member for Beauharnois and Vice-President of the Committee on Economy and Labor, Mr. Guy Leclair, on behalf of the Minister Delegated to Industrial Policy and the Quebec Economic Development Bank, Ms. Élaine Zakaïb, wanted to congratulate the actors of the Beauharnois-Salaberry Regional County Municipality (MRC) for the results of the redeployment plan. 
Thanks to this redeployment plan, launched in 2008, several structuring projects have been made, including the establishment of new industrial parks, the revitalization of buildings or unoccupied lands and the expansion of the port of Valleyfield. This regional synergy has also increased the economic benefits related to the construction of the Highway 30 and the creation of more than 6,000 manufacturing jobs, while generating fallout greater than $ 80 million. 
"I am very proud of the work done by the middle actors to promote the economic growth of the RCM by Beauharnois-Salaberry," said the member Guy Leclair. "Their important mobilization has led to the success of the MRC's economic redeployment plan and its 15 structuring projects. Their efforts have allowed thousands of people to continue working and fully participating in Beauharnois-Salaberry's economic growth. » 
"The recovery of the manufacturing industry is paramount to diversify our economy," said Sulaian Deputy Minister Zakaïb. "The intervention of the various partners as to the completion of the MRC de Beauharnois-Salaberry projects has allowed many workers to actively participate in the economic growth of their region. These commitments contribute to stimulating the economy and promoting the growth of Quebec. »
