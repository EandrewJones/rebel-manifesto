Quebec- The Minister of Health and Social Services and Minister Responsible for Elders, Dr. Réjean Hébert, today announced a bonus of the coverage of reimbursable dental services under the health insurance plan, especially those. 'Adgression to children. 
From now on, the permanent tooth pulpotomy under general anesthesia will be covered for children under ten years and for any person eligible for the last recourse program and holding a claims book issued by the Ministry of Employment and Social Solidarity. Pulpotomy is an intervention to remove part of the pulp from a tooth. 
In addition, young cancer patients can see a dentist for a second examination in less than twelve months at Montreal Children's Hospital and the Sainte-Justine University Hospital Center. These two pediatric hospitals are added to the list of establishments where this possibility has been offered since 2010. This bonus, which derives from a negotiated agreement with the Association of Dental Surgeons of Quebec, required an amendment to the Regulations. The Act respecting health insurance. 
"I am proud to announce this significant improvement in the accessibility of certain specialized dental services, for the benefit of children and the most vulnerable people in our society. Our government once again confirms its firm willingness to ensure optimal availability of professional services in the field of health, including dental health, "the Minister of Health and Social Services concluded.
