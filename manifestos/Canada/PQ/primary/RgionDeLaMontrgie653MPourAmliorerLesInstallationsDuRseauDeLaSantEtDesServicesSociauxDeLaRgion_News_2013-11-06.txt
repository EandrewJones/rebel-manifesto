Quebec- The Minister of Health and Social Services, Minister responsible for Elders and Minister responsible for the Estrie Region, Dr. Réjean Hébert, as well as the Minister of Education, Recreation and Sport and Minister responsible for the Region of Montérégie, Mrs Marie Malavoy, announce investments of $ 65.3 million in health and social services institutions in the Montérégie region they will use this budget envelope to maintain and renovate their real estate assets, as well as To replace or enhance their furniture and equipment. 
"Our government wishes to invest in all regions, with the greatest rigor in infrastructure spending. Whether it is major development projects or expenses related to existing assets, our government is committed to improving the quality, accessibility and safety of the care and services offered to the Quebec population, what will contribute the regional envelopes announced today, "said Minister Réjean Hébert. 
"This allowance will allow institutions in the Montérégie region to achieve several projects that will provide a quality service offer to the population. In addition to supporting institutions and healthcare and social services professionals in their mission, these outies are a powerful economic leverage, including employment perspective, "said Minister Marie Malavoy. 
This regional envelope stems from total investments of $ 698.2 million across Quebec, which fall within the framework of the Quebec Infrastructure Plan to ensure the sustainability of our infrastructure.
