Montreal- The Minister of Health and Social Services and Minister Responsible for Elders, Réjean Hébert, and Jean-François Lisée, Minister of International Relations, Francophonie and Foreign Trade and Minister responsible for the Montreal and MP Region of Rosemont , announce the granting of additional budgets of $ 15,803,800 to improve home care and services offered to the elderly of the region. 
"Improving senior services in loss of autonomy is one of our government's high priorities, as will also confirm our approach to autonomy insurance, which will be unveiled shortly. Immediate improvement in home support will serve more seniors, even more wisely help those who are already receiving services and better support families, "said Minister Hébert.
