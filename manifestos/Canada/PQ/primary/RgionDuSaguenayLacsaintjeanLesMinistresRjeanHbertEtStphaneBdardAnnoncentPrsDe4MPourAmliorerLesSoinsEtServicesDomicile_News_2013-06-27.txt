Saguenay- The Minister of Health and Social Services and Minister responsible for Elders, Réjean Hébert, and the Minister responsible for the Saguenay-Lac-Saint-Jean Region, Stéphane Bédard, are pleased to announce new annual budgets of 3 $ 879,800 to improve home care and services offered to the elderly of the region. In addition to this amount, an injection of additional appropriations of $ 2,181,400, granted for the development of services in the territory and thus better meet the needs of the population. 
"Additional funding announced today is one step closer to improving home care. Our elders want to stay at home as long as possible, and we take the necessary steps to realize this wish, as confirmed by our autonomy insurance project unveiled lately. These sums will therefore help to serve more seniors, to help even more wisely those who already receive services and to better support families, "said Minister Hébert.
