Montreal - The leader of the Parti Québécois, Pauline Marois, is pleased to announce the candidacy of Robert Carrier in the riding of Mille-Isles. "Robert Carrier is a man's political experience that defended the interests of Quebec for many years in Ottawa. His knowledge of federal politics will be very useful to us. This is another quality candidacy for Laval people, "said Pauline Marois. 
Civil Training Engineer, Robert Carrier was a member of the Bloc Québécois in the district of Alfred-Pellan from 2004 to 2011. In particular, he was a speaker for income from 2008 to 2011. 
"My experience in Ottawa has convinced me that Quebec has nothing to hope for federalism. Only the Parti Québécois has the will and the ability to get up to restore a balance of power before the federal government. We need a government dedicated to Quebec's interests and a woman like Pauline Marois to lead him. I am proud to introduce myself at his side, "said Robert Carrier. 
Pauline Marois has been packed with the team that the Parti Québécois presents and believes that Quebeckers will also be. "We have a great team, women and men from all walks of life who want to contribute to the advancement of Quebec. We have ideas and a program that will rally the population and we have the most beautiful company projects. This campaign is the perfect opportunity for Quebec to restore. »
