Québec- The leader of the Parti Québécois, Pauline Marois, is pleased to announce the candidacy of Rosette side in the riding of Louis-Hébert. "Chairperson of the Pay Equity Commission, member of the Bélanger-Campeau Commission and First Vice-President of the CEQ, the political, professional and trade union course of Rosette is out of the ordinary. It's an exceptional, progressive and engaged woman, "said Pauline Marois. 
During his career, Rosette side actively participated in the creation of the Pay Equity Act and the settlement of his application for the Quebec public service. She has also worked on social assistance reform and the establishment of the child care network. 
"The Parti Québécois presents an exceptional team of women and men who have an absolutely admirable public service experience. I am honored to be part of this outstanding team that testifies to Pauline Marois's attraction and leadership, the most competent person to lead Quebec, "said Rosette side. 
"Now it's our task of making our program known and giving Quebeckers to choose ourselves," concluded Rosette side.
