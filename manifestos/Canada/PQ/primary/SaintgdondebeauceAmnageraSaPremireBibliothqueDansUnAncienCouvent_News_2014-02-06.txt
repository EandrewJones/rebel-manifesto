Quebec- The Minister of Culture and Communications, Maka Kotto announces the award of $ 179,000 financial assistance to the municipality of Saint-Gédéon-de-Beauce, for the development of a library in the Former convent of the Sisters of the Charity of Saint-Louis. 
"I congratulate the leaders of the municipality for having the audacity and the creativity to convert this magnificent building, in the heart of Saint-Gédéon-de-Beauce, a multifunctional center that will welcome a library, a place of diffusion of Culture and communications. This initiative will bring to the entire population an additional attachment to its community and its environment, "said Maka Kotto. 
The former convent of the Sisters of Charity of Saint-Louis, acquired by the municipality during the summer of 2013, will be recycled into a multifunctional center which will house a CLSC, municipal offices and a new library. The latter, affiliated with the Biblio network, will offer the population of Saint-Gédéon-de-Beauce and the surrounding area and equipment allowing them access to books and other cultural documents, animation activities and services. affecting information technologies. 
The total cost of the project is estimated at $ 199,000 and is divided between the department ($ 179,000, or 90% of the total amount), the development corporation of Saint-Gédéon-de-Beauce ($ 5,000) and the municipality Saint-Gédéon-de-Beauce ($ 15,000). 
The department's contribution comes from the Capital Assistance Program and is part of the 2013-2023 Infrastructure Plan.
