Québec - Minister Delegate to Tourism, Mr. Pascal Bérubé, announces that the registration period for the Festivals and Tourist Festivals Program for the summer / fall 2014 is now open. From today and until November 29, the promoters whose events will be held between May 1 and October 31, 2014 will be able to apply via the Queite Webde Tourisme Québec. 
The Québec Tourism Festivals and Tourist Festivals and Tourism Events program supports events that demonstrate significant tourism performance. These events generate significant spin-offs throughout Quebec and allow us to enrich and diversify our tourist offer. 
Corporate or agency representatives who wish to apply must consult the eligibility criteria, register for Tourisme Québec WebInstuff and provide the documents required by November 29, 2013.
