Quebec- The Minister of Education, Recreation and Sport, Mrs Marie Malavoy, wanted to take advantage of the week of school settings to recall the key role played by Quebec's directors and school directors in the fulfillment and educational success of young people. 
"By their leadership and know-how, the guidelines for educational institution perform an essential task, or to ensure that schools allow our students to develop their full potential and succeed in their schooling. Leading a school is not always easy and requires a lot of determination. On this week dedicated to them, I want to thank them for their excellent work and to express our gratitude, "said Malavoy Minister. 
The School Settlement Week was created in 2011 at the initiative of the Montreal Association of School Settlement (Amdes) and the Quebec Association of School Management Personnel (AQPDE). It aims to recognize and enhance school settings. The week is going again this year on the themes you knew everything that the direction does for education.
