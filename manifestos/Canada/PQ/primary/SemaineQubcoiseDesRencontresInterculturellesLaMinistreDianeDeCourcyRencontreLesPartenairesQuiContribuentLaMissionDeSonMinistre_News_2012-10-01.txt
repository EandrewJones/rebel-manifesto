This year, the Quebec week of intercultural encounters is taking place on the theme together emphasize the contribution of diversity in the cities and regions of Quebec. The integration and sense of belonging to Quebeckers of all origins are based on consultation, which requires a firm commitment of all socio-economic actors. This week recalls the importance of encouraging diversity and openness to diversity and breaking prejudices and discrimination. 
From 1 to 7 October, Quebec will be the scene of activities aimed at rapprocating, the exchange and discovery of different cultures: dance nights, tasting dinners, cafes and theater, tales recitals, projections movies and documentaries, exhibitions and shows of all kinds.
