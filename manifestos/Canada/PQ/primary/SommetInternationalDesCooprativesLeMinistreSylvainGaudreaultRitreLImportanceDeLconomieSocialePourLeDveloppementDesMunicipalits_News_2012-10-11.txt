Quebec- The Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Occupation of the Territory and Minister of Transport, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, took advantage of his participation in the International Summit of Cooperatives to reiterate the importance of the social economy for development Quebec communities. 
"Social economy companies contribute to the well-being of all regions of Quebec, including offering goods and services that meet the specific needs of the communities they serve. That's why our government has made the choice to support communities that choose the social economy as a development tool. This will will incarnate in a framework law that will be lodged at the National Assembly, "said Minister Gaudreault. 
"Cooperatives are important engines for creating jobs in all municipalities. In addition to contributing significantly to the economic development of Quebec, they promote the occupation of all territories, including to maintain local services in remote areas, "said Gaudreault. 
Remember that Quebec has more than 7,000 collective enterprises, 3,300 cooperatives and mutuals and 3,700 NPOs, which includes 1,000 early childhood centers (CPE). These companies employ more than 125,000 people, accounting for 8% of gross domestic product (GDP) and turnover exceeding $ 17 billion. They offer products and services in innovative sectors and as varied as those of traditional businesses.
