Quebec - The member for Johnson and the official opposition for the leisure and sports opposition, Etienne-Alexis Boucher, welcomes the announcement made this morning regarding the holding of a hockey summit jointly by hockey Quebec, the Major Junior Hockey League of Quebec (LHJMQ) and the Montreal Canadiens. 
For several years, our national sport has frequently defrayed the negative cuff. "The events surrounding the Roy-Nadeau and Paciooretty-Chara business; the many discussions surrounding braincomions; As well as fights and violence in general in this sport have generated more reactions than the practice of sport itself. A reflection is needed on hockey violence. After the Roy-Nadeau incident, some actions were undertaken following the deposit in August 2008 of the report of the Advisory Committee set up by the LHJMQ, "said Etienne-Alexis Boucher. 
Since 2009, the Director of the Montreal Canadian Hockey Club, Mr. Pierre Boivin, expressed his wish for the holding of such a vertex. "With this summit, all organizations and stakeholders dedicated to the development of hockey in Quebec will have the opportunity to reflect to ensure that regulation allows a safe practice of hockey, youngest professionals. For example, we will see a change in culture with regard to our national sport, "added the member for Johnson. 
"Several other aspects of Quebec hockey will have to be addressed, whether it is the structure of Quebec minor hockey, doping, pursuing the schooling of players, accessibility to practice and under-representation of Francophone players in the NHL. That is why the Parti Québécois made it a matter of importance during its last national congress and hopes that this summit is an important moment in the development of Quebec hockey, "concluded the official opposition spokesman.
