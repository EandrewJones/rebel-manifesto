Quebec- The member for Chicoutimi and Leader of the Official Opposition, Stéphane Bédard, announces changes to the spokesperson for the parliamentary wing of the Parti Québécois. 
The hon. Member for Bonaventure, Sylvain Roy, becomes spokesperson for forests, wildlife and parks. 
The member for Beauharnois, Guy Leclair, becomes a spokesperson for work; It retains its spokesperson on Aboriginal business. 
The member for Sanguinet, Alain Therrien, becomes spokesperson for information resources; It retains its spokesperson for Treasury Board.
