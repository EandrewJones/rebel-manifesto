Québec- On the occasion of the parliamentary wing caucus of the Parti Québécois, the new acting leader of the official and deputy opposition of Chicoutimi, Stéphane Bédard, proceeded to the announcement of the team of the parliamentary officers of the official opposition. 
"The parliamentary team of the Parti Québécois will meet the task of conducting constructive and rigorous work, in the interest of Quebecers. By his experience and strength, she will be able to act and work to ensure her essential and democratic role in the National Assembly, "said Mr. Bédard.
