Quebec- The hon. Member for Chicoutimi and Leader of the Official Opposition, Stéphane Bédard, and the member for Saint-Jean and spokesperson for employment, vocational training, literacy and youth, Dave Turcotte, congratulate the New President of the National Committee of Quebec's Young People (CNJPQ), Leo Bureau-Blouin, as well as the entirely elected team in the National Executive. 
"The mandate waiting for the CNJPQ is important. I am convinced that Leo and all his team will work to create the presence and participation of young people who militate with us, but also to encourage young people from all over Quebec to join the Quebec party, "said Stéphane Bédard. 
The elected persons within the national executive of the CNJPQ are: 
Leo Bureau-Blouin, President; 
Stefanie Tougas, Vice President of Political Affairs and Program; 
Alexandre Banville, Vice President of Communications; 
Vincent-Gabriel Langlois, Vice President of the Organization; 
Mathieu Morin, Secretary-Treasurer; 
"Congratulations to the new CNJPQ team; She can count on me to make the voice of young people heard on the issues that affect them more particularly. Its members, from different backgrounds and possessing various experiences, will have the support of the members of the Parti Québécois in order to advance their ideals and go to meet young people from all regions, "has assured Dave Turcotte.
