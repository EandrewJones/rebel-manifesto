Quebec- The hon. Member for Chicoutimi and Leader of the Official Opposition, Stéphane Bédard, congratulated warmly Pierre Lavoie for the edition of the 1000 km of the Grand Challenge Pierre Lavoie, as well as all the cyclists who took part in it. 
"I congratulate Pierre Lavoie and I thank him for his exceptional contribution to the health of our young people. It is proof that it is possible for a citizen to change the world when he shows leadership and tenacity. May the movement he started harvests even more success and keeps us moving! , "Said the leader of the official opposition. 
Stéphane Bédard also wanted to congratulate the deputies, all parties combined, who pedaled 1000 km in 60 hours. The team formed by Sylvain Pagé (Labelle), of Sylvain Gaudreault (Jonquière), François Bonnardel (Granby), Sébastien Schneberger (Drummond-Bois-Francs) and Stéphane Billette (Huntingdon) noted the challenge with Brio, and This, despite the hard weather of the last days. 
The two members of the Quebecois Party participating in the Challenge for the National Assembly team of Quebec, Sylvain Gaudreault and Sylvain Pagé, have raised more than $ 5,000 from their colleagues from the Parti Québécois. This amount will be given to two primary schools, the International School of Mosaic, located in Jonquière, and the School of Friendship, located in Notre-Dame-du-Laus, in the constituency of Labelle. 
"This money will be used to finance projects promoting healthy lifestyle in these schools, what sport and physical activity contribute greatly. We are pleased to participate in this great initiative that encourages our young people to move, "concluded Stéphane Bédard.
