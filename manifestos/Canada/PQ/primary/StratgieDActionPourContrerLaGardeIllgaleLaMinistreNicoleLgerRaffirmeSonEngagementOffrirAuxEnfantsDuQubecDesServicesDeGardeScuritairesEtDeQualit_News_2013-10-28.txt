Quebec- The Minister of the Family, Ms. Nicole Léger, wants to clarify the actions posed by the Ministry of the Family to offer the children of Quebec safe and quality care services. Several actions are currently being implemented to counter illegal custody. 
"The safety and quality of our child care is a priority. We act on several plans to offer our child care children who meet all the requirements and who offer them a living environment first and foremost safe, but also stimulating and positive for their development. The recognized custody service logo posted in child care is a sign of quality and compliance, "said Minister Nicole Light. 
Lastrategie action to counter the illegal guard was adopted in May 2013. It comes in four axes: the creation of places at $ 7, awareness of illegal guard, the detection of illegal childcare services and the application of sanctions if applicable. 
In addition, the creation of the 28,000 places at $ 7 announced last November by Minister Nicole Léger is an important action to support interventions against illegal custody. The consolidation of the $ 7 child care network allows parents to benefit from quality care services. 
"I invite Quebec's parents to vigilance and not hesitate to consult the Ministry of the Family for any questions on this subject. It is important for parents to be aware of this risk and do not hesitate to report problem situations so that we take appropriate action. It should be known that the majority of custody environments comply with the Child Care Act. The goal is to accompany the forum guard services to conform, "said Minister Nicole Léger. 
Lastrategia of action to counter illegal guard can be consulted on the website of the Ministry of the Family at the address www.mfa.gouv.qc.ca.
