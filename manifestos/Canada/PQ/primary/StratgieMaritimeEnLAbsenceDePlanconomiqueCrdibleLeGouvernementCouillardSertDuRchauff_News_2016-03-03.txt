Quebec- The Quebec Party Spokesperson for Transport, Maritime Strategy, Electrification of Transport and Digital Strategy, Martine Ouellet, finds that the Couillard Government, down to all that relates to the economy , he hastens to serve as heated to Quebeckers, who remain waiting for the deposit of a real government plan for economic development. 
"The sums announced this morning have already been announced in great pomp during the unveiling of the Maritime Strategy last June. Failure to have raised interest at that time, the government is trying to give its announcement a second wheel tower, repeating it this morning! "Estimate the member. 
The Parti Québécois recalls that the local committees that the minister wants to train already exist in several places, as in Sorel-Tracy, Port Cartier and Bécancour. Fortunately, these regions have not waited for the signal of the Minister of Love to mobilize! 
"Relight the same investments, announces after announcement, does not replace a real economic development strategy for our regions and for the St. Lawrence Valley. It is flaunting that after two years of Liberal government, the Minister of Love has just noticed that there are at least 16 locations in Quebec with industrial-port investment needs, "concluded Martine Ouellet. .
