Québec- Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Agriculture, Fisheries and Food, Mr. François Gendron, stressed the importance of the work done by the partners of the Quebec Health and Welcome Strategy to be animals that are gathered in Quebec City on the occasion of their second annual meeting. 
"I am impressed by the extent of the work done by our partners in all media to improve the well-being and health of animals. I am of the opinion, just like you, that it is by doing thoughtful gestures and leading to concerted actions that we will achieve it, "said Gendron to the participants. 
This Assembly aims to take stock of the actions taken by more than 60 organizations from the bioalimentary industry, the public health sector and the health and well-being of animals. These partners voluntarily took part in this initiative which consists of ensuring the health and well-being of pets and production.
