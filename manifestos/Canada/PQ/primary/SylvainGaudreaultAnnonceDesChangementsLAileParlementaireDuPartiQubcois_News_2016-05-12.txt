Quebec- The member for Jonquière and Leader of the Official Opposition, Sylvain Gaudreault, announces changes in the parliamentary team of the Parti Québécois. 
"The members of the Parti Québécois will continue their work, the National Assembly and on the ground, to defend the interests and carry the aspirations of Quebeckers. Our team is diversified and competent; It will with rigor work to continue to reflect the population's concerns to this government, in all areas and in all regions of Quebec, "said Sylvain Gaudreault.
