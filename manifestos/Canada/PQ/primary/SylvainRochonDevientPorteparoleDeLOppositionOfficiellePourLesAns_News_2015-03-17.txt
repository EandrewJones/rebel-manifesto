Quebec- The member for Chicoutimi and Leader of the Official Opposition, Stéphane Bédard, announces that the hon. Member for Richelieu, Sylvain Rochon, will occupy the seniors' spokesperson. 
"We welcome a new member today, who will proudly represent the population of Richelieu. The people in this riding have trusted a man deeply engaged in his community, in his community, for several years. He joins a tremendous team of deputies, in which he will be spokesperson for seniors' records. It is an important mandate, linked to several aspects of government actions. Sylvain has all my confidence to hold this function, "said Stéphane Bédard. 
"It's an honor of making my entry into the National Assembly today to represent my fellow citizens, who trusted me. I assure them of my full commitment. In addition, the important case entrusted to me, the elders, has a special resonance in my riding where, as in several regions of Quebec, the population is aging. The challenges are great and I put myself with the task with heart in order to contribute to improving the living conditions of seniors, "said Sylvain Rochon.
