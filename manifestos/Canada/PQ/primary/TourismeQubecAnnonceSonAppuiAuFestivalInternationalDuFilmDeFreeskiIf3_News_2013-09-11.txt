Québec - Minister Delegate to Tourism, Mr. Pascal Bérubé, is pleased to announce the granting of $ 10,000 AUFESTIVAL International of the Freeski-IF3 film, which will take place from 11 to 15 September 2013. 
For a 7th year, the festival brings together independent film producers, professional athletes and festival-goers from more than 20 countries. For five days, the event offers exceptional programming including first ski films, concerts, remarkable photographs, documentaries, webisodes and several other interactive activities. 
The Freeski International Film Festival - IF3 promotes all aspects of this global sport, while producing several editions of its festival in various countries. He honors and celebrates producers, photographers, international athletes and all those who contribute to the constant progression of the ski and export industry from its culture at different levels. The festival has earned this year this year the Montreal Tourism Award for Best Event with a budget of less than $ 300,000. 
"Over the years, this sports celebration has become unavoidable for strong sensations and franteski enthusiasts. This event is a tourist attraction that distinguishes Montreal from the international as a cultural and creative city. Thanks to this appointment with the seventh art, Quebec is positioned as a vanguard winter destination, "said Minister Bérubé. 
The financial assistance granted by Tourisme Québec comes from the tourism industry development program - financial assistance to festivals and tourist events.
