Quebec- The Minister Delegate to Tourism, Mr. Pascal Bérubé, is pleased to announce the award of $ 700,000 financial assistance in support of the 34Ediment of the Rogers Cup presented by National Bank, which will take place in Montreal. 2 to 11 August 2013. 
Every summer, amateurs meet to admire the game of the best tennis players around the world. This year, the metropolis greets the return of male competitors, while women will compete with honors in Toronto. 
Eventful sports event and world class tournament, the Rogers Cup is one of the oldest tennis tournaments in the world after Wimbledon and the United States International. This event also includes the prestigious Masters 1000 circuit of ATP World Tour. 
"The holding of a sporting competition of this magnitude certainly contributes to making Montreal a tourist destination of the most attractive. The Quebec government is pleased to participate in the success of this event that shows our tourism destination on the international scene, "said Minister Bérubé.  The financial assistance granted by Tourisme Québec comes from the tourism industry development program - financial assistance to festivals and tourist events.
