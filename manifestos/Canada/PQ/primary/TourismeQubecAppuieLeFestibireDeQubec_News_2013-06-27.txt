Québec- The Minister Delegate to Tourism, Mr. Pascal Bérubé, is pleased to announce the award of $ 17,500 financial assistance in support of the 4th / Edition Dufestibière de Québec that will take place from 15 to 18 August 2013. 
Great party in the heart of the Old Port of Quebec, the Festibière is the unique opportunity for beer lovers and artisanal drinks to discover a multitude of Quebec products, in addition to meeting craftsmen and luminaries of the brewing world. 
Since its creation in 2010, this event has been growing and attracting more and more festival-goers on the 400th space website. Tastings, conferences and musical services give this festive event a unique and distinctive flavor. 
"I am very pleased to support the Quebec City Festiber and welcome the determination of the organizers to make this event one of the most important brewing festivals in the country. Their efforts to diversify the tourist offer contribute greatly to the influence of the capital-national, "said Minister Bérubé. 
The financial assistance granted by Tourisme Québec comes from the development of the tourism industry - financial assistance to festivals and tourist events.
