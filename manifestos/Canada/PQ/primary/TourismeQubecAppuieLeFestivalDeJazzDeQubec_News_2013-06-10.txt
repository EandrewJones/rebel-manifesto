Quebec- Minister Delegate to Tourism, Mr. Pascal Bérubé, is pleased to announce the allocation of $ 17,500 financial assistance to support the holding of the Jazz of Quebec Festival, which will take place from October 15-27 . 
This year, the Quebec Jazz Festival celebrates its 7th Issue with a rich programming that has great musicians from Quebec and Canada, but also abroad. The festival helps to appreciate the jazz and musicians of great talents that adonte it. It aims to create a must-see event that will do Quebec a recognized Jazz destination in North America. 
"The Quebec Jazz Festival will undoubtedly please appeal to music lovers, whether they are from the region or visiting. I am happy to support this musical event that helps to renown Quebec here and abroad. I want to emphasize the work of the organizing committee, artists and volunteers who, through their efforts, ensure the success of this beautiful event, "said Minister Bérubé. 
The financial assistance granted by Tourisme Québec comes from the tourism industry development program - financial assistance to festivals and tourist events.
