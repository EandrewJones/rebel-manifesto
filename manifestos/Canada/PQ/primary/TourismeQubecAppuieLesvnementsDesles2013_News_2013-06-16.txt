Québec- Minister Delegate to Tourism, Mr. Pascal Bérubé, is pleased to announce the award of $ 40,000 financial assistance to the Island events' consultation table to support the holding of the third edition of the events of the islands , which will spread from June 16th to November 2nd. 
The events of the islands, is a group of five Madelinot events that have joined forces so, among other things, to animate the tourist season of the archipelago and to highlight it. Spread throughout the tourist season, these events will be able to please families as well as outdoors, culture, regional products or relaxation. 
The season will begin with the seafood party (June 16th), then will continue with the figurative painting symposium of the North (from July 22nd to 28th), the construction of p'tits construction competition Boats (August 3rd), The Country Festival (August 29th to 1st Template) and finally with the Short Film Festival Images in views (November 8th and 9th) that will close the 2013 season. 
"This festival cluster allows visitors to discover the diversity of the products of the sea offered by producers and culinary leaders, to take advantage of the typical activities presented by the artisans and artists of Madelinot territory. I wish to warmly thank the table of consultation of the events of the islands which, thanks to its efforts, made it possible to build a cohesion and a common vision of event for the archipelago, "said Minister Bérubé. 
The financial assistance granted by Tourisme Québec comes from the tourism industry development program - Film Festivals and Events Fund.
