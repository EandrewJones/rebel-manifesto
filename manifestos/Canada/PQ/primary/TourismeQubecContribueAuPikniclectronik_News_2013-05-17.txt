Quebec - Minister Delegate to Tourism, Mr. Pascal Bérubé, is pleased to announce the award of $ 35,000 financial assistance to support the 11Ediiknic electronikqui will take place every Sunday from May 19 to September 22 at the park. Jean-Flag. 
The Piknic Electronik is an innovative, friendly and family-friendly musical event that stands outdoors, in an enchanting gathering place. Its dynamic programming will be able to appeal both followers and the profanes of electronic music. Local and international talents are turning on two scenes each offering a different atmosphere in a separate decor. 
"I am delighted to support festivals and events such as the Piknic Electronik who provide remarkable radiation at the Quebec destination. The event industry is an important economic engine for all regions of Quebec. It is through the diversification of its tourist offer and its originality that Quebec shines nationally and internationally, "said Minister Bérubé. 
The financial contribution comes from the tourist development and promotion support program -Volet financial assistance to festivals and tourist events. 
Through the financing of festivals and events, Tourisme Québec supports the completion of initiatives that contribute to the economic and tourism development of the regions of Quebec. It thus promotes renewal of tourism offer in a continuous and sustainable improvement perspective.
