Québec - Minister Delegate to Tourism, Mr. Pascal Bérubé, is pleased to announce the award of $ 5,000 financial assistance in support of the 9th Issue of La Fafe Rainbow of Quebec. 
From August 29 to September 2, the Saint-Jean-Baptiste district will be the scene of several artistic and community events to emphasize the significant contribution of gay, lesbians, bisexual, and transgender (GLBT) to the culture and influence of Quebec. 
"In addition to contributing to the recognition and acceptance of diversity, the rainbow festival also contributes to Quebec City's tourism development. I congratulate the organizers who are determined to make this event an appointment that attracts more and more participants each year, "said Minister Bérubé. 
The financial assistance granted by Tourisme Québec comes from the tourism industry development program - aid financial industry and tourist events. 
Through the financing of festivals and events, the Government of Quebec supports the completion of initiatives that contribute to the economic and cultural development of the regions of Quebec. It thus promotes the renewal of the tourist offer of the capital-national in a perspective of continuous and sustainable improvement.
