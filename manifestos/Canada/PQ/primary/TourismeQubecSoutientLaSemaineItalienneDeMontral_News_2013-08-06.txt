Québec - Minister Delegate to Tourism, Pascal Bérubé, is pleased to announce the award of $ 20,000 in support of Montréal's Italian Laseine, 9 to 18 August 2013. 
A real immersion in the world of the Italian community of Montreal, the 20th Issue of this rendezvous rendered again reflects the multicultural character and welcoming Montrealers and underlines the know-how of the artisans of the metropolis. 
Outdoor concerts, humorous shows and folk shows, exhibitions, fashion show and opera are only some of the activities of a rich and varied program that should please people of all ages. 
"On the occasion of the 20Eniversary of the Italian Week of Montreal, I congratulate the organizers who have highlighted the cultural richness of the Italian Community of Montreal in addition to contributing to the recognition of the metropolis as a first choice tourist destination" , Minister Bérubé said. 
The financial assistance granted by Tourisme Québec comes from the tourism industry development program - aid financial industry and tourist events. 
Through the financing of festivals and events, the Government of Quebec supports the completion of initiatives that contribute to the economic and cultural development of the regions of Quebec. It promotes the renewal of the Montreal tourist offer in a continuous and sustainable improvement perspective.
