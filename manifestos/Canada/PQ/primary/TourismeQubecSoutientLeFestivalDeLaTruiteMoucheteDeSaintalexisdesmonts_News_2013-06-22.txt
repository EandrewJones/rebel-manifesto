Saint-Alexis-des-Monts- Minister Delegate to Tourism, Mr. Pascal Bérubé, announces the award of financial assistance of $ 10,000 to the expense of Saint-Alexis-des-Monts, which stands from the June 22 at 1 July. 
For this new edition, the pleasures of fishing are again honored with this unique festival. The organizers offer a variety of activities, including a fishing tournament, popular artists' shows, animation and fireworks, all in a family party atmosphere. 
"It is with pride that I associate myself at the Festival of Speckled Trout, one of the largest fishing tournaments in Quebec. The exceptional work of organizers and volunteers makes this event a real success. I am convinced that the festival-goers will be at the rendezvous to enjoy the many festivities as well as the attractions of the beautiful region of La Mauricie, "said Minister Bérubé. 
The financial assistance granted by Tourisme Québec comes from the tourism industry development program - financial assistance to festivals and tourist events. 
Through the financing of festivals and events, the Government of Quebec supports the completion of initiatives that contribute to the economic and cultural development of the regions of Quebec. It promotes the renewal of the Mauricie tourist offer in a perspective of continuous and sustainable improvement.
