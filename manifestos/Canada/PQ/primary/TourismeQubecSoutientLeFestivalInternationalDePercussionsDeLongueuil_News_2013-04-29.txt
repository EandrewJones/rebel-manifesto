Quebec - Minister Delegate to Tourism, Mr. Pascal Bérubé, is pleased to announce the award of $ 50,000 Government Financial Assistance to support the 12th International Percussion of Longueuil, which will take place from 9 to 14 July next July. 
This cultural event that occupies a separate music niche in the Festivals and Events circuit again this year to discover its audiences in the world. For the first time in the history of the event, a whole continent will be in the spotlight for the 2013 edition, that of Oceania. Thus, participants will have the leisure to discover Australia, New Zealand, Bali and many other countries since artists from the international and also Quebec will be programming. 
"It's a pride in having such a beautiful tourist offer in Quebec. With a variety of festivals and events more original, such as the Longueuil International Percussion Festival, the population and visitors can live a unique experience that is accessible to all, "said Minister Bérubé. 
The financial assistance granted by Tourisme Québec comes from the tourist development and promotion support program - Fiscontrol Festivals and tourist events. 
Through the financing of festivals and events, the Government of Quebec supports the completion of initiatives that contribute to the economic development of the Montérégie region. It thus promotes renewal of tourism offer in a continuous and sustainable improvement perspective.
