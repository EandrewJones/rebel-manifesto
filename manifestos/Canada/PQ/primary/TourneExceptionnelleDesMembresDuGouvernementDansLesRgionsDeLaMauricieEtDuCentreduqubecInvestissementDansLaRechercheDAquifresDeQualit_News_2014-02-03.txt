Nicolet- On the occasion of an outstanding tour of the members of the Parti Québécois government in the regions of Mauricie and the Center-du-Québec, the Minister of Natural Resources, Mrs. Martine Ouellet, announced the realization of a project $ 130,000 to locate and map the main groundwater tanks in the region, as part of the Ministry of Sustainable Development's Groundwater Knowledge Program, the Environment, Wildlife and Parks . 
"Groundwater is a very precious natural wealth. It is an important source of drinking water for Quebecers. The expertise in geology of the Ministry of Natural Resources is contiguous to draw up a clear portrait of groundwater resources in the region. This portrait will better protect these drinking water sources and manage them in a responsible manner, so that they take long to citizens of the region, "said Martine Ouellet.

The Nicolet-Saint-François project will locate and characterize the aquifers in the region. The maps produced will indicate in particular the geological formations of the territory, the main aquifers of the region as well as their refill areas and high vulnerability to contamination. 
The purpose of the Groundwater Knowledge Acquisition Program aims to draw up a portrait of groundwater resources from the municipalized southern municipal territories for the ultimate goal of protecting them and ensuring sustainability. 
The members of the Parti Québécois and the government ministers will meet on February 5 and 6 in Shawinigan, on the occasion of the Caucus of MPs and the Council of Ministers. They will benefit from this meeting to tour nine constituencies in the Mauricie region and the Center-du-Québec region. In less than a week, they will do more than 80 advertisements of different orders. These ads will contribute to the development and growth of the regions of Mauricie and Center-du-Québec.
