Quebec- On the occasion of an unprecedented tour of members of the Quebec government in the Bas-Saint-Laurent region and the Gaspésie, the Minister Delegate to the Regions and Minister responsible for the Gaspé-Islands Region. Of the Islands -The Madeleine, Mr. Gaétan Lelièvre, is pleased to announce, on behalf of the Minister of Transport and Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Occupation of the Territory, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, that investments of the Order $ 3.3 million will be devoted to the completion of two projects on the road network in Sainte-Anne-des-Monts. 
A first project consists of the refurbishment of the bridge spanning the river Sainte-Anne on Route 132, between the intersections of the rue du Remembrance and the 11th West Street. This work, in the range of $ 1.7 million, will allow a refurbishment of the bridge. They started at the end of July 2013 and will end next December. 
The second project, of the order of $ 1.6 million, desired by the environment, aims for a safer redevelopment of commercial access and an improvement of the visual corridor to the city center. The work is carried out in two separate sectors on Route 132: east and west of the Sainte-Anne River. This project has been under way since last May and will end in early September 2013. These investments will significantly optimize the safety of road users 132 and residents of these sectors. 
"These interventions go in the sense of the announcement that I made last spring; Our government's investments must ensure the renewal and maintenance of the infrastructure in place. The focus here is on road safety and maintenance of assets, "said Minister Gaudreault. 
"This work will greatly improve the flow of traffic and the safety of road users. In addition, these investments are an additional contribution to the economy of the Haute-Gaspésie region, "said Minister Lelièvre.
