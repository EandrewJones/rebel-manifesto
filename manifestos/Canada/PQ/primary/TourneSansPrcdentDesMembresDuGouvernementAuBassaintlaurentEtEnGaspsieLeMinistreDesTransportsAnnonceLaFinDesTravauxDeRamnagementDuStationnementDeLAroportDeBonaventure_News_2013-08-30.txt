Québec- On the occasion of an unprecedented tour of the members of the Government of Quebec in the Bas-Saint-Laurent region and the Gaspésie, the Minister of Transport and Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and the Occupation of the Territory, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, is pleased to announce that the expansion of parking at Bonaventure airport are now complete. 
The work carried out over the last two years represent an overall investment of $ 317,000. The significant increase in traffic at Bonaventure airport, including the numerous trips on the North Shore, required to make a long-term parking area available. Indeed, the number of trips between 2011 and 2012 more than doubled. The new parking has increased from 29 to 82 spaces. 
"Bonaventure Airport is an important link for the economic and social development of the region. These modern airport infrastructure works make it possible to respond more to the needs of travelers, "said Gaudreault. 
For its part, the hon. Member for Bonaventure, Mr. Sylvain Roy, is pleased with the fulfillment of this work, including improvements in lighting and parking marking for motor vehicles. These will ensure increased displacement on the airport site. 
"These investments have made it possible to optimize the major asset of the airport for the region," concluded the Minister Delegate to the Regions and Minister responsible for the Gaspé-Îles-de-la-Madeleine Region, Mr. Gaétan Lelièvre.
