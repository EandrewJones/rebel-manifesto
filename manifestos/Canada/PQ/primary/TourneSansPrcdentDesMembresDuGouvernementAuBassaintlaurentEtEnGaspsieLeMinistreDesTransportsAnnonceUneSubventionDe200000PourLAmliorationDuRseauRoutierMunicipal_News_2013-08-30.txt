NEW- On the occasion of an unprecedented tour of the members of the Government of Quebec in the Bas-Saint-Laurent region and the Gaspésie, the Minister of Transport and Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and the occupation of the territory, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, and the hon. Member for Bonaventure and Parliamentary Assistant of the Minister of Sustainable Development, the Environment, Wildlife and Parks for the Fauna and Parks component, Mr. Sylvain Roy, are pleased to announce that A $ 200,000 grant is granted to the new municipality for the redevelopment of Maguire Street and Route 132 giving access to the industrial park. 
This grant is paid under the Municipal Road Improvement Assistance Program. This program helps to finance improvement projects achieved by municipalities on their network. It aims to carry out work to improve the quality of the local road network and the safety of users. 
"By participating as a partner in financing this project, our government supports the municipality of news for the improvement of its road network, giving it ways to carry out work and contribute to the well-being of its community. Today's announcement will allow the municipality to ensure safe access and fluidity of traffic to heavy vehicles accessing the industrial park, "said Minister Gaudreault. 
The projects targeted by this program concern the improvement of the safety and quality of the roadway by pavement work or pavement reconstruction, the correction of dangerous curves or the installation of safety slides. 
For its part, the hon. Member for Bonaventure is happy with the completion of this work. "This new access will improve both travel security, taking a road that is better adapted to heavy vehicles, and the quality of life of the population in this sector," said Sylvain Roy.
