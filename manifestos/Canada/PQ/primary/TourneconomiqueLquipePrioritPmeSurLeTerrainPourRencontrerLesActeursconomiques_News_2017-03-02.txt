Quebec, March 2, 2017- Team members of the Quebec's SME priority, formed on 24 January, MPs Alain Therrien, Mireille Jean and Martin Ouellet, are proud to begin their tour of the Quebec regions which will take place until June. They start, in Montreal and Quebec City, by meetings with a dozen national players, who are at the heart of economic activity. 
"These organizations with seating across Quebec hold recognized expertise. They are familiar with the challenges facing business people. We are three entrepreneurs, we are therefore aware of the pitfalls that they cross, especially in terms of administrative burden. We will meet economic players to identify irritants, propose solutions and identify best paperwork reduction practices in cities and government agencies. The intention is clear: the government of the Parti Québécois in 2018 will be the Government of SMEs! "Said Alain Therrien. 
"Prosperity, innovation, support for young entrepreneurs, export aid are all aspects we want to address with entrepreneurs across Quebec. We can subsequently offer a concrete plan to globally improve their business environment, the business priorities forkly to support them to create sustainable jobs, for the benefit of all Quebec. From mid-March, we will go to all regions to meet SMEs and organizations that support them, "added Mireille Jean. 
"The actions of the Liberal government in consultation and business support have hurt several regions. We must resume the right course, so that the priorities of the circles be taken into account, the entrepreneurs relieved of their administrative burden and the entrepreneurial succession accompanied and supported. This will fuel our discussions with people on the ground, "concluded Martin Ouellet.
