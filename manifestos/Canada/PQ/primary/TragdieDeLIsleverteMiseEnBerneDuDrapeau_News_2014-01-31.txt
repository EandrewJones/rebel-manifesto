Quebec- In memory of the victims of the fire of the Havre Residence in Isle-Verte, occurred on January 23, 2014, the Prime Minister of Quebec, Pauline Marois, asked for the Bern of the Flag of Quebec, The dawn at dusk on February 1, 2014, on the central tour of the Hotel du Parliament, as well as all the public buildings of the Government of Quebec, municipalities, the health and education networks of the administrative region From Bas-Saint-Laurent, with the aim of expressing all the sympathy of the Government and the Quebec nation at the place of victims and families affected.
