Quebec- The Minister of Transport and Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Occupation of the Territory, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, today announced the imminent end of the major redevelopment works of Charest and Robert-Bourassa, as well as The commissioning of the new routes reserved for collective transport. 
The Minister pointed out that this project of redevelopment, the largest in the Capitale-Nationale Region of the last 25 years, has been implemented to improve the fluidity and safety of this strategic axis in Quebec, in addition to have been completed in the respect of the schedule and the budget.

As for Robert-Bourassa's reserved routes, they will be able to accommodate 500 buses daily including 325 in peak periods. This will allow residents to consider collective transport as an effective option to get to Sainte-Foy, there are many commercial and office buildings as well as educational institutions, and which is growing incredible in recent years. .
