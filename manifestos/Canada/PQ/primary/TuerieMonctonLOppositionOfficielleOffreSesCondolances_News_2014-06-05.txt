Quebec- The Leader of the Official Opposition, Stéphane Bédard, and the member for Matane-Matapédia and Public Security Spokesperson, Pascal Bérubé, offer, on behalf of the members of the Parliamentary Wing of the Parti Québécois, their more Sincere condolences to families and relatives of the three policemen killed Wednesday night in the performance of their duties. 
"We are wholeheartedly with the people of Moncton and the Police forces of the RCMP, who face the tragic loss of three of their colleagues. This sad event reminds us that police officers are daily risking their lives to ensure the safety and well-being of the population, "said Stéphane Bédard. 
"I pay tribute to the three deceased police officers and the two wounded. Our thoughts are with the people of Moncton and New Brunswick. Of course, we all want a denouement of the situation as quickly as possible, "added, for his part, Pascal Bérubé.
