Quebec- The Minister Delegate to Industrial Policy and the Quebec Economic Development Bank, Ms. Élain Zakaïb, announced the award of a non-refundable financial contribution of $ 246,350 in Quattriuum, a company specialized in the analysis of Human gestures, for the development of a technology used to evaluate the started throwing of hockey players. 
The company's investment project, totaling nearly $ 630,000, consists of designing and marketing a product designed to measure performance relating to the lancers of expert and novice hockey. The product includes a sensor and a mobile software application. It is connected to a social web platform where players can consult and compare their statistics. 
"By putting technological innovation at the preferred sport of Quebeckers, hockey, the Quattriuum company demonstrates that creativity has its place in all areas. This is an eloquent demonstration of our inventive potential, "said Élain Zakaïb. "It is with great pride that our government supports this project that will contribute to our reputation for excellence in an avant-garde industry and will allow amateur and professional athletes to push their limits and to surpass themselves. »
