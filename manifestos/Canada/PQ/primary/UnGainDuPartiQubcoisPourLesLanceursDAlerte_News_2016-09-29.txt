Québec, 29 September 2016 - Today, during the question period at the National Assembly, the member of the leader to the Trembles and spokesman for the Treasury Board, Nicole Léger, finally obtained from the part Government assurance that municipal sector warning launchers will be protected by Bill 87. 
"In my view, it was essential to include municipalities in a bill that concerns warning launchers. On many occasions, I made this request to Minister Carlos Leitão. Today, he has finally heard, "said Nicole Léger. 
It should be remembered that this request had been made in the report of the Charbonneau Commission and reiterated by the majority of the groups heard in parliamentary committee. 
In addition, emphasis added that by another bill, 108, the Minister creates the authority of public procurement, which must in particular monitor municipalities. However, until today, this authority would have been unable to have access to information from the municipal world warning launchers, they do not enjoy any protection. 
"We are pleased that the minister finally responds to this part of the report of the Charbonneau Commission, which asked for a general protection of alert launcher protection. This requires consistency between the various bills, and the minister finally understood it, "concluded the hon. Member.
