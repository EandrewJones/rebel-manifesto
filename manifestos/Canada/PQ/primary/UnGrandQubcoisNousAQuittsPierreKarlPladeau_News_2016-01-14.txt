Quebec- The hon. Member for Saint-Jérôme and Leader of the Official Opposition, Pierre Karl Péladeau, and the spokesman for the Parti Québécois in the field of culture, Véronique Hivon, paid tribute to Mr. René Angelil, great Quebecer, man of Culture and song. 
"It is with sadness that I am learning the death of my friend René Angelil. On behalf of the entire Quebec party team, I want to express my sincere condolences to Celine as well as children, Patrick, Jean-Pierre, Anne-Marie, René-Charles, Eddy and Nelson. As a personal capacity, our family today loses a friend. The links we have woven for several years will remain forever engraved in our memories. Thank you for everything, René, "said Pierre Karl Péladeau. 
"All Quebec will remember René Angelil with recognition and emotion. Throughout his career as a singer and impresario, he brought his favorite touch, with humanity and respect. The culture of Quebec and the song here owes him a lot, "added Véronique Hivon. 
"René was not only a businessman and a talented impresario, he was also a man deeply attached to Quebec and his culture. He has done a lot for artists from here and elsewhere, from France, in particular. My thoughts are with Céline, children and relatives of René, "concluded the official opposition.
