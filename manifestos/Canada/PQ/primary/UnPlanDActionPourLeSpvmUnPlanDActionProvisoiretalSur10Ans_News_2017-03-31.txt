Quebec, March 31, 2017- The spokesperson for the Parti Québécois public safety, Pascal Bérubé, reiterates that it is essential to trigger an independent survey on the SPVM in order to shed light on events more than unquiet. revealed by the media in recent weeks. 
In short, a 10-year plan is announced today that a temporary plan that will have to be revised in the light of the administrative investigation report on the SPVM.- It is essential to do any The light on the events revealed in recent weeks, independently. The action plan must be presented in parliamentary committee to all elected representatives of Quebec. ** 
"The action plan provides that 5 to 10 years will be needed to restore confidence. Yet there is an administrative investigation on the current SPVM. When we have the conclusions of this investigation, will the action plan be changed according to the recommendations? The sequence is rather surprising; It must be concluded that this action plan is provisional. It would have been more logical to put the service under guardianship, the time the administrative investigation makes all the light and that it presents its recommendations, "said Pascal Bérubé. 
"As far as the plan itself is concerned, we find particular that the SPVM puts in place measures to inform the managers, whereas they know they are possibly themselves involved in the events. In addition, I am very surprised to see that the first important municipal police force in Quebec promises results to the population in just 10 years, "added the member. 
"The Minister of Public Safety has become aware of the action plan at the beginning of the week, which surprised everyone. Can we have the same explanations as those he has received? The Parti Québécois requests that the action plan be presented to the elected representatives of the National Assembly at a parliamentary committee, "concluded Pascal Bérubé.
