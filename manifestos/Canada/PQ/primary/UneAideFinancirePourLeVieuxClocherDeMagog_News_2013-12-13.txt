Magog- The member for Sherbrooke, Serge Cardin, on behalf of the Minister of Culture and Communications, Maka Kotto, announced the award of $ 230,000 financial assistance to support a project to purchase equipment. Old Magog bell tower. The acquisition of these equipment will provide optimal conditions for the distribution of performing arts, both for producers and spectators. This financial assistance is awarded by the Cultural Business Development Corporation (SODEC). 
"Our government is delighted to support this room, rich in four decades to move and amaze the public, thanks to this investment. The old Magog bell tower is a launching ramp for several Quebec artists who choose to kick off their tour. I can not insist over the brilliant history of this dissemination place, which has seen a number of renowned artists in it, "said Minister Maka Kotto. 
"I have the privilege of announcing an investment for a place that is considered an institution in our region. I am more than happy that our government affirms its commitment to the influence of our national culture in Estrie, and more particularly in the Magog region, "said the member Serge Cardin. 
This financial support will allow the Old Magog Bell Tower to upgrade the sound and lighting equipment, to redevelop the lodges, to obtain a new scene trim and new seats for the spectators. This help thus meets the imperative needs of the Old Magog's bell tower to adequately satisfy the technical requirements of artists and producers, to ensure improved public experience and to help promote better accessibility of citizens of the Estrie region. to culture. This help will help preserve the reputation of excellence in this favorite location for watch lovers that hosts nearly 130 performances annually. The regional conference of Estrie elected representatives and the old Magog bell tower are the other financial partners of this initiative.
