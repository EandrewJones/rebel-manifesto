Quebec- The Official Opposition Leader and MP, Sylvain Gaudreault, as well as the member for Joliette and spokesperson for the Crop and Communications Party, Véronique Hivon, warmly congratulated Xavier Dolan for obtaining Grand Prix and the Ecumenical Jury Award at Cannes Film Festival. 
"On behalf of the entire Quebec party team, I extend my sincere congratulations to Xavier Dolan. He has crossed a new step in his career, already fulfilled. His sixth length film, just the end of the world, won the prize of the second best movie at the festival, a first for a Quebec director. The recognized talent of this exceptional artist and his accomplishments are a pride for all Quebeckers and all Quebeckers. Bravo, Xavier! , "Said the leader of the official opposition. 
"The work of Xavier Dolan is a source of inspiration. He has dreams and hope for Quebec. The Grand Prix is a remarkable recognition for this creator here, and this is indisputably one of the best directors in the world. See as much talent, audacity and ingenuity in this Quebecker is in itself prodigious; Seeing its international qualities abroad is another proof that Quebec makes great things and has nothing to envy in the world in terms of creativity. This is additional evidence that Quebec embellishes the world, "said Véronique Hivon.
