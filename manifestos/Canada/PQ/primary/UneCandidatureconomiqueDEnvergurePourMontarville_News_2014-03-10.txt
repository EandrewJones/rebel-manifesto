Montreal - The Quebec Prime Minister and Head of the Parti Québécois, Pauline Marois, is proud to announce the candidacy of Simon Prévost in the riding of Montarville. "Renewed economist, until recently President of Quebec Manufacturers and Exporters (MEQ), Simon Prévost is a key figure in the Quebec business world. It will undeniably be an asset to work to develop Quebec's economy, manufacturing and job creation in all regions. He joins a strong economic team, determined to enrich Quebec, "said Pauline Marois. 
Before wearing his position at MEQ, Simon Prévost was Vice President for Quebec of the Canadian Federation of Independent Business. Previously, Mr. Prévost worked as Chief Economist at the Silver Canal, after hearing the position of Director, the first vice-presidency capital markets, at Caisse centrale Desjardins. He has also been vice-president, economic and financial research, and chief economist for the Laurentian Bank Financial Group. 
"It's an honor to commit me to Pauline Marois' sides and the Quebec party team. Citizens and citizens of Montarville can count on me to represent them with dynamism in the National Assembly. The Quebecois Party has the best plan to support businesses and the manufacturing sector. He proved it with the 2013-2017 Quebec industrial policy, already implemented, "said Simon Prévost. 
As part of the Industrial Policy, the government will devote more than $ 1 billion to support the manufacturing sector by 2017, for a total of more than $ 2 billion. Policy deploys measures, particularly for the acceleration of business modernization and increased exports. 
"With the arrival of Simon Prévost among his candidates, the Parti Québécois puts the final touch to an exceptional economic team. We are determined to enrich Quebec and make it more prosperous, "concluded Pauline Marois. 
We have a plan and the team to succeed. Today, it's time to give us the means to go further.
