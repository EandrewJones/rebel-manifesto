Sainte-Anne-des-Monts- The Minister Delegate to the Regions, Minister responsible for the Gaspésie-Îles-de-la-Magdalene region and hon. Member of Gaspé, Mr. Gaétan Lelièvre, announces a two-year extension of the Financial Assistance at the Avalanche Center of Haute-Gaspésie to allow it to find a longer-term financing solution. This is $ 70,000 a year for the next two years that will be paid to the organization as part of the Regional Initiatives Fund to maintain expertise in avalanche prevention in chic-shocks. This sum is added to the amount of $ 70,000 already authorized for the current year. 
Founded in 1999, the non-profit organization has as a mission to improve safety in the face of avalanche risks by providing specialized services and activities aimed at raising awareness, informing and educating the general public. 
"Today's financial assistance will help ensure the survival of the Haute-Gaspésie Avalanche Center and maintain a key expertise in the development of winter tourism in the region. This confirmation of financial assistance for the next two years from the Ministry of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Territory Occupation will allow the Avalanche Center to rely on foreseeable support from the Government of Quebec for the next two years. In addition to the financial assistance granted, I gave the mandate to the Regional Directorate of the Ministry to sit on the same table different partners so that the whole financial assembly necessary to the functioning of the organization can be insured in the medium term, "said Minister Lelièvre. 
The Regional Initiatives Fund is part of the Government Intervention Strategy for the Development of the Gaspé-Islands-de-la-Madeleine Region and constitutes a major lever to promote, in particular, the realization of Projects from development priorities in the region. An annual $ 6 million annual envelope is scheduled for this fund, for a period of five years. The program is in effect until March 31, 2018.
