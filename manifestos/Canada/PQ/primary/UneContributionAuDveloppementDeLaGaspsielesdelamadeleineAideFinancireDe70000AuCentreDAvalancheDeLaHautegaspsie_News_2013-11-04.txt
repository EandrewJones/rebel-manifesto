Sainte-Anne-des-Monts- Minister Delegate to the Regions, Minister responsible for the Gaspésie-de-la-Madeleine Region and MP and Deputy of Gaspé, Mr. Gaétan Lelièvre, Announces Assistance Assistance $ 70,000 in the Regional Initiatives Fund to support the Avalanche Center of Haute-Gaspésie. 
"The government intervention strategy for the development of the Gaspé-Îles-de-la-Madeleine region provides for support for the development of winter tourism in Gaspésie. The Avalanche Center supports the development of winter recreational activities, particularly in Gaspésie Park. The development of a pointed expertise in recent years also allows the Avalanche Center to train professionals throughout the province. I am pleased to support this organization that is an important element for the safe development of winter tourism activities in Gaspésie, "said Minister Lelièvre. 
The Regional Initiatives Fund is supported by the Government Intervention Strategy for the Development of the Gaspésie-de-la-Madeleine Region and constitutes a major lever in carrying out projects from priorities. development of the region. An annual $ 6 million annual envelope is scheduled for this fund, for a period of five years. The program is in effect until March 31, 2018.
