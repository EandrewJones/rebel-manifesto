Sainte-Anne-des-Monts- Minister Delegate to the Regions, Minister responsible for the Gaspésie-de-la-Madeleine Region and MP and Deputy of Gaspé, Mr. Gaétan Lelièvre, Announces Assistance Assistance $ 740,000 under the Regional Initiatives Fund to support the municipalities of all MRCs of Gaspésie and the Agglomeration of the Madeleine Islands in the implementation of improvement projects their recreational touring, cultural and leisure infrastructures. The details of the financial assistance is attached. 
"Our government has made the development of Gaspésie one of its priorities. That's why I am proud to support these municipal projects that will promote the socio-economic growth of the region. I have the strong belief that these projects will have positive impacts for municipalities in the region, "said Minister Lelièvre. 
The Regional Initiatives Fund is supported by the Government Intervention Strategy for the Development of the Gaspésie-de-la-Madeleine Region and constitutes a major lever in carrying out projects from priorities. development of the region. An annual $ 6 million annual envelope is scheduled for this fund, for a period of five years. The program is in effect until March 31, 2018.
