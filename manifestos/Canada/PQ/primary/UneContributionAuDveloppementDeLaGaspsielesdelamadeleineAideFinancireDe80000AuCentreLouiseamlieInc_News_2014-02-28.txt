Sainte-Anne-des-Monts- The Minister Delegate to the Regions, Minister responsible for the Gaspé-Islands-de-la-Magdalene region and hon. Member of the District of Gaspé, Mr. Gaétan Lelièvre, announces, as part of the Fund Assistance to regional initiatives, $ 80,000 financial assistance at Louise-Amélie Inc. Center. For the realization of renovations to improve the quality of services offered to the people he hosts. 
This help and accommodation for women who are victims of violence and their children is no longer sufficient for demand because it has been a total of 100% occupancy rate for more than 10 years. The project aims to carry out renovation work that will better welcome customers, which is in vulnerability. Thus, three rooms, including one for people with reduced mobility, will be added, the common room reserved for adolescents will be enlarged, functional spaces for the workers will be developed and a room for the storage of the personal effects of the accommodation will be furnished. 
"Our government is committed to providing adequate services to all Quebecers and Quebeckers. It is therefore proud that we support this structuring project that will meet the basic needs of women and children in the region who find themselves in distress while offering them the support and comfort needed to pass through this Strong period of their lives, "said Minister Lelièvre. 
The Regional Initiatives Fund is part of the Government Intervention Strategy for the Development of the Gaspé-Islands-de-la-Madeleine Region and constitutes a major lever to promote, in particular, the realization of Projects from development priorities in the region. An annual $ 6 million annual envelope is scheduled for this fund, for a period of five years. The program is in effect until March 31, 2018.
