Carleton-sur-Mer- Minister Delegate to the Regions, Minister responsible for the Gaspé-Islands-de-la-Madeleine Region and MP and Deputy Gaspé, Mr. Gaétan Lelièvre, accompanied by the hon. Member for Bonaventure and Parliamentary Assistant to the Minister Sustainable development, the environment, wildlife and parks (wildlife and parks component), Mr. Sylvain Roy, announce the award of $ 177,760 financial assistance to support the implementation of six structuring projects for The region of Gaspésie. Financial assistance comes from the Regional Initiatives Assistance Fund (FAIR) and the full list of projects is attached. 
"Our government has made the development of Gaspésie one of its priorities. That's why I am proud to support these promising projects that will promote the growth of the region. I have the strong belief that these projects will have significant impacts for a large number of companies and organizations here, "said Minister Lelièvre. 
The Regional Initiatives Fund is part of the Government Intervention Strategy for the Development of the Gaspé-Islands-de-la-Madeleine Region and constitutes a major lever to promote, in particular, the realization of Projects from development priorities in the region. An annual $ 6 million annual envelope is scheduled for this fund, for a period of five years. The program is in effect until March 31, 2018.
