G Aspon - Minister Delegate to the Regions, Minister responsible for the Gaspé-Islands-Îles-de-la-Madeleine Region and deputy of the Gaspé constituency, Mr. Gaétan Lelièvre, announces the granting of a financial assistance of $ 187,785 As part of the Regional Initiatives Fund to support the implementation of structuring projects in the Regional County Municipality of Haute-Gaspésie, whose complete list is attached. 
"It is with pride that I announce this contribution that supports the financing of salary measures and promising projects arising from the government intervention strategy of our beautiful region. These initiatives will ensure the economic and tourist development of the Gaspé-Îles-de-la-Madeleine region with the aim of promoting tourism and job creation, "said the minister. 
The Regional Initiatives Fund aims to support the Government Intervention Strategy for the Development of the Gaspé-Islands-de-la-Madeleine Region and constitutes a major lever to promote, in particular, the implementation of projects from the development priorities of the region. An annual $ 6 million annual envelope is scheduled for this fund, for a period of five years. The program is in effect until March 31, 2018.
