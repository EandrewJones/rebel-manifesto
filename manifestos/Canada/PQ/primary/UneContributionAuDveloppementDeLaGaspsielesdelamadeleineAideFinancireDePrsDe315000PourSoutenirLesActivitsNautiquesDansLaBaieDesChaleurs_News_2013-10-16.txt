Bonaventure- The member for Bonaventure, Mr. Sylvain Roy, on behalf of the Minister Delegate to the Regions and Minister responsible for the Gaspésie-de-la-Madeleine Region, Mr. Gaétan Lelièvre, and the Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and of the occupation of the territory and Minister of Transport, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, announces the award of financial assistance of $ 313,633 to support the realization of three structuring projects for the development and promotion of nautical activities in the Bay of Chaleurs , whose list is attached. 
"There is no doubt that the development of nautical activities in the marinas of Chaleur Bay will play a major role in the region's recreational growth. It is therefore with great pride that our government supports these three projects that will contribute to the development of the Bay of Chaleurs, one of the most beautiful berries in the world, and will encourage water sports enthusiasts to visit our region. " , said the deputy Roy. 
Financial assistance is provided by two sources. A first sum of $ 168,559 comes from the Regional Initiatives Fund that supports the Government Intervention Strategy for the Development of the Gaspé-Islands-de-la-Madeleine Region. This fund constitutes a major lever for the realization of projects that meet the development priorities of the region. An annual $ 6 million annual envelope is scheduled for a period of five years. The program is in effect until March 31, 2018. 
A second sum of $ 145,074 comes from the collective entrepreneurship infrastructure program. This program is used to support social economy companies in the implementation of projects of refurbishment, expansion, construction or acquisition of commercial or industrial infrastructure with a view to promoting both the socio-economic vitality of the territories. than the preservation and enhancement of the environment. Recall that this program has a $ 10 million envelope.
