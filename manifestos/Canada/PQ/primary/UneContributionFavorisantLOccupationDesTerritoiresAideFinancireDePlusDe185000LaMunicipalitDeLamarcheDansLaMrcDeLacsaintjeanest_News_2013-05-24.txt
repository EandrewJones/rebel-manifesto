Lamarche- Minister Delegate for Canadian Intergovernmental Affairs, Canadian Francophonie and Sovereignist Governance, Minister responsible for the North-du-Qu√©bec Region and MP Alexandre Cloutier, on behalf of the Minister of Business municipal, regions and occupation of the territory and Minister of Transport, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, announces the granting of a financial assistance of $ 185,250 for the municipality of Lamarche to ensure the protection of the population in terms of Fire safety. 
Thanks to the financial assistance granted, the municipality of Lamarche will have a new autopompe fire truck to ensure its fire safety department. The useful life of the former truck being reached, the municipality of Lamarche can thus ensure adequate protection in its territory. This project, which requires investments of $ 195,000, is consistent with the RCMF fire safety hedge scheme. 
"I am very happy to make the announcement of this acquisition for the municipality of Lamarche. The contribution of the Government of Quebec will allow citizens to benefit from a quality fire protection service in accordance with the RCM risk coverage scheme of Lac-Saint-Jean-Est, "said Minister Cloutier. 
The Quebec government is close to the future of the regions and people who live there, and that is why it has made the dynamic occupation of the territories a priority. By ensuring the safety of the population of the municipality of Lamarche, it reiterates its willingness to support rural municipalities and provide essential services to all Quebeckers. 
Financial assistance comes from the [difficulty-based territorial support fund (http://www.mamrot.gouv.qc.ca/development-regional-et-rual/programs/fonds-se-sout---territoires- In-Difficulty-FSTD /) which is part of LePlan of government action for municipalities. This fund includes five components, aimed at funding the purchase of equipment for municipalities to comply with government standards, including environmental and fire safety.
