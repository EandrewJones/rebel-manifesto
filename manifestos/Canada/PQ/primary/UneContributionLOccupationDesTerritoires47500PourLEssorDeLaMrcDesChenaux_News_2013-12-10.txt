Trois-Rivières- The member for Champlain, Ms. NoëlThe Champagne, on behalf of the Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and the Occupation of the Territory and Minister of Transport, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, announces the award of $ 47,500 for the realization Two structuring projects for the MRC of channels. 
Financial assistance of $ 33,000 will be granted to the municipality of Saint-Prosper for the installation of an elevator at the parish hall to facilitate access to people with reduced mobility. In addition, $ 14,500 financial assistance will be given to Community Channels editions for the modernization of channels' communication tools, including the establishment of a complete web platform for providing continuous information, more Accessible and updated. 
"It is with great pride that the Government of Quebec supports the realization of these two unique and carrier projects for the CNR of the channels that will better meet the needs of citizens by and to strengthen their sense of belonging", has Indicated MP ChristmasLa Champagne. 
Financial assistance comes from the Cyclical Development Fund of the Ministry of Municipal Affairs, Regions and the Occupation of the Territory.
