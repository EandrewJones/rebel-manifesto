New Richmond- The hon. Member for Bonaventure and Parliamentary Assistant to the Minister of Sustainable Development, Environment, Wildlife and Parks (Wildlife and Parks), Mr. Sylvain Roy, on behalf of the Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and The occupation of the territory and Minister of Transport, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, announces the granting of $ 150,000 financial assistance to the Petite-Cascapedia Regional Park to upgrade the sports facilities of the Rouge Pin tourist resort. 
More concretely, the submitted project consists, among other things, to allow the regional park to acquire a groomer, to install a new line of snow, to add lighting on the tube slide track and install an air conditioning system in the station's home pavilion. 
"It is with great pride that the Government of Quebec contributes to the realization of this project that will improve the offer of service and to better meet the needs of the tourist clientele of the Gaspé-Îles-de-Îles region. La-Madeleine, "said Roy. 
Financial assistance comes from the Cyclical Development Fund of the Ministry of Municipal Affairs, Regions and the Occupation of the Territory.
