Beloeil- The Minister of Higher Education, Research, Science and Technology and Deputy of Borduas, Mr. Pierre Duchesne, on behalf of the Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and the Occupation of the Territory and Minister of Transportation, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, announces the award of $ 35,000 financial assistance to the Valley-du-Richelieu Community Development Corporation to support the establishment of a multisectoral and multi-sectoral stowage structure in terms of social development. 
More concretely, this amount will be used to finance two human resources allocated to the creation of the lashing structure which is part of the aims of the R.E.V.E.. (Reflection, evaluation, vision, evolution of consultation). Launched in 2011, this is aimed at establishing a synergy between the various social development stakeholders in the MRC territory to avoid duplication in interventions, ensuring coherence in actions and optimize available resources. In the long term, this project should allow the environment to have a common vision of social and community development. 
"By contributing to the pursuit of this important approach to social and community mobilization, our government makes the choice to support the creation of a strong community that has the means to act according to its own needs. I am convinced that the relevance and quality of this innovative project will inspire similar initiatives in Quebec, for the benefit of all Quebeckers, "said Duchesne. 
Financial assistance comes from the Cyclical Development Fund of the Ministry of Municipal Affairs, Regions and the Occupation of the Territory.
