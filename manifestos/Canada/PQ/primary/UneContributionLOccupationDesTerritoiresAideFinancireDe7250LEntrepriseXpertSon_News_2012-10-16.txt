Sainte-Anne-des-Monts- The Minister Delegate to the Regions, Minister responsible for the Gaspé-Îles-de-la-Madeleine Region and MP and Deputy of Gaspé, Mr. Gaétan Lelièvre, is pleased to announce the granting $ 7,250 financial assistance to X.PerT'Son for the acquisition of a LED wall-screen. 
Specialist in sound, lighting and multimedia, X.Pert'on offers event production and conference services in the Gaspésie and Northern New Brunswick region since 1996. Financial assistance granted will allow x.pert'son to diversify its market while providing the region with a new service currently offered by outside companies. This acquisition will consolidate the six current jobs in addition to contributing to the creation of a new use of technician specializing in video. 
"Our government is proud to support this business that wants to diversify its activities and, at the same time, contribute to the development of the event sector. This support testifies to our desire to promote the economic activity of the region, "said Lelièvre. 
Announced financial assistance comes from the Regional Strategic Response Fund.
