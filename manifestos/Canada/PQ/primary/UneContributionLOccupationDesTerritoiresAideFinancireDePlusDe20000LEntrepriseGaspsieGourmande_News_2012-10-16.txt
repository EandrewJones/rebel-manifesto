New Richmond - Minister Delegate to the Regions, Minister responsible for the Gaspé-Islands-de-la-Magdalen Region and MP for the Gaspé riding, Mr. Gaétan Lelièvre, is pleased to announce the granting of financial assistance From $ 20,881 to Gourmande Gaspésie to improve the quality of the tourist offer of its organization and to establish the basics to set up a regional collective marketing network of Gaspé bioalmimentary products. 
As a first step, the company will receive $ 5,000 to improve tourism offer. To do this, Gaspésie Gourmande will provide personalized follow-up and individual and collective support for the implementation and follow-up of a specifications with its members. The organization will also support certain investments from member companies to comply with the requirements of this notebook. 
Thanks to additional aid of $ 15,881, Gaspésie Gourmande will evaluate the feasibility and profitability of a regional marketing network of Gaspésian bioalimentary products. The creation of such a network would make these products accessible to the maximum of people, both in the region and in large centers. By promoting the consumption of local products, the project could have positive benefits on the Gaspésian economy as well as the sustainable and dynamic occupation of the territory, two factors of importance to maintain and improve the social cohesion of communities. 
"Our government is proud to support this organization participating in the development, marketing and promotion of Gaspé products, both on our territory and outside. This support testifies to our willingness to help businesses promote the economic activity of the region, "said Lelièvre. 
Announced financial assistance comes from the Regional Strategic Response Fund.
