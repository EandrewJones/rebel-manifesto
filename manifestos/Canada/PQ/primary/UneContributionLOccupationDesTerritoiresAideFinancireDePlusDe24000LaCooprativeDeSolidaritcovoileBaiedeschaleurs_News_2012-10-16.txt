Carleton-sur-Mer- Minister Delegate to the Regions, Minister responsible for the Gaspé-Islands-de-la-Madeleine Region and Member of the Gaspé Constituency, Mr. Gaétan Lelièvre, is pleased to announce the granting of Financial assistance from $ 24,260 to the Ecovoile Solidarity Cooperative Baie-des-Chaleurs to support its business development project. 
To do this, the solidarity cooperative will develop a marketing plan to reach the major centers of Quebec and the Maritimes at the same time as it will enhance its offer by creating new partnerships. In addition, the organization plans to buy new equipment to diversify the activities offered to the various clienteles of the school, to integrate the cruise component into its service offer and to consolidate its fleet of equipment to respond more adequately to the request. The realization of this project will not only contribute to the consolidation of seven seasonal jobs, but also to the creation of two new jobs. 
"It is with pride that our government contributes to the development of a young solidarity cooperative that has stand out with its unique offer and promote the recreational tourism growth of the region," said Mr. Lelièvre. 
Announced financial assistance comes from the Regional Strategic Response Fund.
