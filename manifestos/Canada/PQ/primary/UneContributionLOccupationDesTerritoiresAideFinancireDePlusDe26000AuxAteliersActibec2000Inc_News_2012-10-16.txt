Grande-Rivière - Minister Delegate to the Regions, Minister responsible for the Gaspé-Îles-de-la-Madeleine Region and MP and Deputy Gaspé, Mr. Gaétan Lelièvre, is pleased to announce the granting of aid. $ 26,254 in ACTIBEC workshops 2000 inc. For the development of a global flexible and efficient production system that will reduce delays and increase its manufacturing capacity. 
To do this, the company will proceed with interior redevelopment work and the outside of the building, including the leveling of the ground for the transport and the shipment of raw materials and the expansion of the warehouse for Paper Balls. In addition to improving the company's productivity, this work will consolidate 70 jobs and create an additional 8. 
"Our government is proud to support this social economy company that, while significantly contributing to the economic development of the region, promotes the development and integration into the labor market of people with physical or intellectual limitations," Indicated Mr. Lelièvre. 
Announced financial assistance comes from the Regional Strategic Response Fund.
