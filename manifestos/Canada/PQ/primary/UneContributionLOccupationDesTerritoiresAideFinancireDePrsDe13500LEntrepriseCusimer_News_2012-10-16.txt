MONT-LOUIS - Minister Delegate to the Regions, Minister responsible for the Gaspé-Îles-de-la-Madeleine Region and Member of the Gaspé Constituency, Mr. Gaétan Lelièvre, is pleased to announce a financial assistance of $ 13,333 At the company CUSIMER for the realization of a project to add the mackerel to its range of products. 
For more than twenties, Cusimer has been offering a wide selection of high quality bottom fish by mainly transforming the turbot as well as other species from accidental catches such as folds, sebast and ailfin. The addition of the mackerel is therefore a logical sequel to the diversification and consolidation efforts made by the company over the years, in response to the economic situation of the groundfish sector. 
"Our government is proud to support this organization that wants to diversify its activities and, at the same time, develop the fisheries and aquaculture sector. This support testifies to our willingness to help businesses promote the economic activity of the region, "said Lelièvre. 
Announced financial assistance comes from the Regional Strategic Response Fund.
