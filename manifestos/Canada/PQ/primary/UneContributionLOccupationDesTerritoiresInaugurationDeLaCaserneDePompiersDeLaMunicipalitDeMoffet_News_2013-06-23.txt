Moffet- On the occasion of the inauguration of the fire station of the municipality of Moffet, the member for Rouyn-Noranda-Témiscamingue and Deputy Parliamentary Assistant to the Minister of Labor, Mr. Gilles Chapadeau, on behalf of the Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and occupation of the territory and Minister of Transport, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, wishes to recall the financial assistance of $ 113,713 granted to the municipality of Moffet to ensure the protection of the population in terms of fire safety . 
More concretely, a financial assistance of $ 93,190 allowed the municipality to acquire a used emergency autopump and the material required to comply with the new fire safety standards as well as the risk coverage scheme the MRC de Témiscamingue. Until then the municipality owned only one tanker dating back to 1987, which no longer met the fire safety standards in force. Thanks to additional financial assistance of $ 20,523, the municipality could also enlarge and renovate its fire station and rely on a unique space to house fire trucks, firewoods and equipment. 
"The Quebec government is close to the future of the regions and people who live there and reiterates its willingness to support rural municipalities and provide essential services to all Quebeckers. The realization of these two projects, supported by our Government, will allow the municipality of Moffet to provide a better fire service and to offer citizens increased security by significantly reducing the response time in case of fire ", has indicated the Deputy Chapadeau. 
FINANCIAL AID COMES DURABILITY OF FONDS FOR SUPPORT TO PERSONAL TERRITORIES, FOLDERS I and V, which is part of LePlan of Governmental Action for Municipalities. This fund includes five components, aimed at funding the purchase of equipment for municipalities to comply with government standards, including environmental and fire safety.
