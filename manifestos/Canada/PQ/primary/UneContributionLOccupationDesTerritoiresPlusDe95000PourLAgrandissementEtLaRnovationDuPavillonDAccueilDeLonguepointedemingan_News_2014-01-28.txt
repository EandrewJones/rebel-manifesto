Quebec- The Parliamentary Assistant to the Minister of Transport (maritime transport component) and MP from Duplessis, Ms. Lorraine Richard, on behalf of the Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and the Occupation of the Territory, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, announces financial assistance $ 96,160 for the enlargement and renovation of the Long-Pointe-de-Mingan home pavilion. 
The project meets a need for increased traffic for cultural, leisure and tourist activities. Its realization will help to consolidate the tourist vocation of the municipality and to improve the regional offer, in conjunction with the Research Station of the Islands of Mingan, the Center for Reception and Interpretation of Parks Canada and the Island -Is-parrots with his lighthouse and interpretation activities. 
"This is a project that registers from the outset in government guidelines to ensure the occupation and vitality of the territories. Enlargement work will promote the integration of the host pavilion to other recreational infrastructures and creating an entrance gate to Ile-aux Parrots, "said MP Lorraine Richard. 
Financial assistance comes from the Cyclical Development Fund of the Ministry of Municipal Affairs, Regions and the Occupation of the Territory.
