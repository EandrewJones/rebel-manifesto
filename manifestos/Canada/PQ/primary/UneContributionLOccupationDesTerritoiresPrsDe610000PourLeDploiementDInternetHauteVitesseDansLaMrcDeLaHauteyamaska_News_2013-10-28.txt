Granby- The Minister Delegate to the Regions, Minister responsible for the Gaspé-Îles-de-la-Madeleine Region and Member of the Gaspé Constituency, Mr. Gaétan Lelièvre, announces the granting of financial assistance from $ 609,862 to Cooperative Cooptel to support the deployment of a high-speed Internet (IHV) on the territory of the MRC de la Haute-Yamaska, particularly on the territory of the municipality of Saint-Joachim-de-Shefford, in the framework. Rural communities program connected. 
"It is with pride that I announce this important contribution from the Government of Quebec, which will stimulate the economic, social and cultural growth of the MRC while strengthening its attraction. In the long term, the realization of this project will allow citizens to connect to the world, "said Minister Lelièvre. 
The project involves installing fiber technology at home in some non-covered areas of the territory to connect 407 MRC households and businesses that still do not have access to an IHV connection. Funding for this project, estimated at more than $ 1.2 million, will be completed by Cooptel, which will remain the owner of the deployed telecommunications infrastructures. 
"Our government reiterates its willingness to offer all citizens and citizens of Quebec a high-speed Internet connection so that they can rely on this indispensable tool in today's world where knowledge and knowledge and Information and communications technologies play an increasingly strategic role in the socio-economic development of rural territories, "added the minister. 
Coming from the National Rural Policy 2007-2014, the Rural Communities Program are aimed at supporting environmental projects to provide rural communities with a similar and cost-comparable IHV service to that offered in urban areas.
