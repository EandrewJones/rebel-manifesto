Quebec- The member for René-Lévesque and head of the North Shore Region, Mr. Marjolain Dufour, on behalf of the Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and the Occupation of the Territory and Minister of Transport, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, and From Minister Delegate to Tourism, Mr. Pascal Bérubé, announces $ 76,757 financial assistance to the Forestville Tourism Development Corporation for the purchase of a groomer for the maintenance of cross-country ski trails. 
The Air Club is a cross-country skiing center in Forestville serving the territory of the MRC of Haute-Côte-Nord. He participates in the organization of the Boreal Loppet, an international competition that constitutes the lightning tourist attraction flagship of the entire region. 
The acquisition of a groomer can reduce the working time of volunteers and significantly improve the quality of the slopes. 
"It is necessary for the sustainability of the air club and the Boreal Loppet to have a maintenance vehicle designed specifically for cross-country skiing comparable to what is used in other ski centers. That's why my government financially supports this project-bearing project for the North Shore, "said the Deputy Dufour. 
Financial Assistance comes from the Cyprusural Development Fund of the Department of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Territorial Occupancy ($ 46,757) and the Manicouagan National Tourism Partnership Agreement of the Ministry of Tourism ( $ 30,000).
