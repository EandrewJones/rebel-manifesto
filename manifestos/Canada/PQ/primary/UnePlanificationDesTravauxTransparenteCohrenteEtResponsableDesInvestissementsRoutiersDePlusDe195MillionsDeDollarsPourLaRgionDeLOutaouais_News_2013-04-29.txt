Quebec- The Minister of Public Safety and Minister responsible for the Outaouais Region, Stéphane Bergeron, announces, on behalf of the Minister of Transport and Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and the Occupation of the Territory, Sylvain Gaudreault, investments of $ 195,256,000 for the 2013-2015 period in the territory of the Outaouais. 
"These investments for the next two years on the road network demonstrate the government's willingness to ensure rigorous prioritization for public infrastructure projects. In addition to the extension of Highway 5, projects like those of the repair of various bridges on Highway 50, Gatineau, and the correction of the road curve 148, near the Montée Saint-François, at pleasure , testify to the government's intention to ensure the realization of essential projects, "said Minister Bergeron.

Road investments for the entire Quebec for the 2013-2015 period are estimated at $ 5.6 billion, including a contribution from the partners of $ 435 million. In total, there are more than 2,000 projects that will start or continue, which will help create or maintain more than 42,600 jobs. The unveiling of the programming of the work covers a two-year horizon, which constitutes a first. This new way of improving better planning to determine road infrastructure priorities. 
The 2013-2015 road work programming demonstrates the government's desire to better manage infrastructure and pay the right cost for public investment. "The objectives of this approach are clear: to ensure better transparency in the choices and project planning, respect the capacity to pay from all Quebeckers, and resume the control of the costs of road works," added the Minister Gaudreault. 
To develop this programming, the Department of Transport conducted a thorough revaluation of projects by prioritizing road safety and the maintenance of the road network. The Minister recalled his concern for the effect that all the projects announced, in accordance with deadlines and budgets be completed. "It is finished when road projects were added and announced without being budgeted. From now on, the Leitmotiv in the government is the following: it is said what we do and we do what we say, "concluded Mr. Gaudreault.
