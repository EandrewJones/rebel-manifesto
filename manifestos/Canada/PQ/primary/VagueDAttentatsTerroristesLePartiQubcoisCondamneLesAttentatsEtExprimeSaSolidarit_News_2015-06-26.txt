Quebec- The hon. Member for Saint-Jérôme and Leader of the Official Opposition, Pierre Karl Péladeau, and the hon. Member for Hochelaga-Maisonneuve and spokesperson for international relations, Carole Poirier, strongly condemns terrorist attacks committed in different regions Of the world, today, and express, on behalf of the members of the Parti Québécois, their solidarity with all those affected closely or by far by these tragic events. 
"Several terrorist attacks took place in recent hours: in France, Tunisia, Kuwait, Somalia and Syria; We denounce them strongly and firmly. Like my colleagues from the Parti Québécois, I am shaken by such an injustice, in front of so many innocent lives mowed. I address, on their behalf, my deepest condolences to all those whose life is upset forever, whether they are here or abroad, "said Carole Poirier. 
"Nothing can ever justify such a degree of violence, barbarism and horror. I want to tell the affected peoples that we are by their side to condemn and combat terrorism in all its forms, and to make democracy triumph in all circumstances, "concluded the hon. Member.
