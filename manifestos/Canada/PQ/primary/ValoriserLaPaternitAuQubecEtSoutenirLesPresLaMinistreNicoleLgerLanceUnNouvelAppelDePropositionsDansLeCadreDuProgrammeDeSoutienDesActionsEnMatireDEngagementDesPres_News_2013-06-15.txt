The program concretizes one of the actions put forward by the Ministry of the Family in the Duplan of Governmental Action for Equality between Women and Men 2011-2015. With a budget of $ 1.45 million over three years, it is aimed at family organizations (OCF) and national groupings recognized by the Department. 
The program supports the realization of diversified local or regional initiatives promoting a greater commitment of fathers in their families and the adaptation of OCF practices to the realities of today's fathers. It may be, for example, workshops between fathers on their role, workshops on co-parenting or training for OCF staff and volunteers. 
As part of this program, the Department provides non-recurring financial support for annual or multi-year initiatives. Support is $ 5,000 per organization a year. It can reach $ 50,000 a year for an initiative of at least four OCF and $ 70,000 for the initiative of an eligible national grouping. 
For the year 2013-2014, the call for proposals takes place from June 15 to September 20, 2013. To submit a proposal, please visit the website of the Ministry of the family atwww.mfa.gouv.qc.ca/engagement.
