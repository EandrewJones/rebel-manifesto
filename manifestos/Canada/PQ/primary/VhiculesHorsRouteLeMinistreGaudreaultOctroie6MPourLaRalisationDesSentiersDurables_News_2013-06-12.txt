Qu√©bec- The Minister of Transport and Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Occupation of the Territory, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, announces the payment of an exceptional sum of $ 6 million that will enhance the financial assistance dedicated to vehicles Off Road (VHR), more specifically for the realization of durable trails of quad and snowmobiling. 
"This is a giant step for the whole industry. The granting of these amounts demonstrates the importance of the government to the VHR industry for Quebec's economic prosperity. These sums will ensure greater safety of the users of trails already in place and those to come. Our priority remains the establishment of durable trails. We continue to work closely with our partners to ensure that this project is completing this project, "said Minister Sylvain Gaudreault. 
The Federations will share a total of $ 2.4 million over three years for the remuneration of liaison officers who will be key resources for the implementation of sustainable trails. In addition, an amount of $ 3.6 million paid for the same period will finance infrastructure related to the realization of sustainable trails. 
A working committee to review funding formulas to enhance existing assistance programs will be created, so as to facilitate the implementation of the infrastructure needed to develop sustainable trails. 
Minister Gaudreault will soon challenge his federal counterpart, Denis Lebel, so that Ottawa is financially involved in the sustainable trails. "The federal government needs to significantly support this important industry for all regions of Quebec. I hear a strong leadership on this issue, "concluded Minister Gaudreault.
