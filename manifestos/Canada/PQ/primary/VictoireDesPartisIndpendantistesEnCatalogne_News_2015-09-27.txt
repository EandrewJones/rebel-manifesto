Quebec- In the light of the results of the regional elections held today in Catalonia, the member for Saint-Jérôme and Leader of the Official Opposition, Pierre Karl Péladeau, as well as the member for Hochelaga-Maisonneuve and spokesman of the party Quebec in international relations, Carole Poirier, wanted to highlight the victory of Catalan independence parties, who got a majority of seats. 
"It's stimulating to see all those people mobilized for their dream of freedom. Congratulations to the Catalan Independentists, including the Junts Pel Sí Coalition. Good luck to you all! Pierre Karl Péladeau said. 
"The movement for the independence of Catalonia is impressive. The victory of the Independentist Coalition demonstrates that the Union of Political and Citizens has borne fruit, "says Carole Poirier, who is in Barcelona as the official Parti Québécois representative.
