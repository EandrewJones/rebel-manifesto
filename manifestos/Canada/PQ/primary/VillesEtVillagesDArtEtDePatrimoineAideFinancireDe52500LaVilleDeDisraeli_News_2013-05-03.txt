Disraeli- On behalf of the Minister of Culture and Communications, Maka Kotto, the member for Sherbrooke, Serge Cardin, announces the signing of the Cities and Villages of Art and Heritage (VVAP) agreement with the city of Disraeli, matching financial assistance of $ 52,500, spread over three years. 
"Cities and villages of art and heritage has already proven itself in the cultural development of the region. I warmly salute the colossal work shot down by the elected representatives of the city of Disraeli, in order to complete the realization of this agreement. This stimulating project is part of a perspective of both artistic and heritage revitalization in favor of the population, "said Minister Maka Kotto. 
Financial aid of $ 52,500 from the Ministry of Culture and Communications will allow the hiring and training of a cultural development officer who will be mandated to structure the project, to support local stakeholders, to promote the consultation of the middle players and collaborate in the development of a cultural policy. The city of Disraeli, for its part, invests an amount of $ 67,500. 
"The hiring of a cultural development officer is a huge gain for Disraeli, a city that has several cultural organizations that are supported by many volunteers. I have no doubt that Accession to the VVAP network will have a favorable effect for the city's cultural influence and economic development, "said the member Serge Cardin. 
The mission of the VVAP network is to promote the cultural resources of the environment to ensure its growth. It relies on the support of 70 cultural development agents for the development of arts, culture and heritage in all regions of Quebec.
