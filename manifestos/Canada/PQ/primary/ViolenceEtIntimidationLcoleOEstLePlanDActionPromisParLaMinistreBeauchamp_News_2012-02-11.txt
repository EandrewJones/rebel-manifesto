Quebec- The member for Jonquière and official opposition spokesman in primary and secondary education, Sylvain Gaudreault, press the Minister of Education, Line Beauchamp, to present a new action plan to counter violence and intimidation at school. 
"Since last April, Minister Beauchamp promises a new plan of action to prevent and treat violence at school. Ten months later and still nothing. This presses, "said Sylvain Gaudreault, who recalled that last fall, the Parti Québécois made concrete proposals to counter intimidation in schools. 
The Parti Québécois is concerned about the lack of eagerness of the Liberal government to rewrite its plan of action, unveiled in 2008, and which has proved completely ineffective. "The fear of students suffer from violence or intimidation at school greatly harms their academic success," said Sylvain Gaudreault.
