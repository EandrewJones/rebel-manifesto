Saint-Lin-des-Laurentides - Minister of Transport and Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Occupation of the Territory, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, and the Minister of Finance and the Economy and Deputy of Rousseau, Mr. Nicolas Marceau, are pleased to announce that the project of the St. Lin-Laurentides bypass lane crosses a new step. 
"The steps relating to the opportunity study and the preliminary design are now completed, and the department is finalizing the environmental impact assessment of the project that will be filed very soon at the Ministry of Sustainable Development, the Environment, Wildlife and Parks (MDDEFP), "said Minister Gaudreault. 
Once the permissions received, the following steps can be crossed: preparation of plans and quotes, acquisition and release of right-of-way, tenders for construction work and completion of the work. 
"We are aware of the transport issues that affect Saint-Lin-Laurentides and the surrounding area. Also, the realization of a bypass is a major commitment from my colleague, the member for Rousseau. We are therefore gathered today to confirm the realization of this commitment, since the project is now enrolled in the government road planning, "Gaudreault continued. 
The project of circumvention of the city of Saint-Lin-Laurentides is to offer a displacement solution to the users who pass through the routes 158, 335 and 337. The project covers nearly 6 km, in the west part. from the city.  It became essential because of the 14,500 vehicles that circulate daily in the city center, the bypass will help reduce more than half of these movements. 
"This road safety project will contribute to the tranquility of the residents for whom local travel became increasingly difficult, among other things to the peak periods where the circulation flows identified reach the 1,500 vehicles per hour," said for his part the Minister Marceau.
