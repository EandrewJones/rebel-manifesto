Gaspé- On the occasion of the inauguration of the Gaspé Intermodal Carrefour, the Prime Minister of Quebec, Pauline Marois, accompanied by the Minister Delegate to the Regions and Minister responsible for the Gaspé-Îles-de-la-Madeleine Region, Gaétan Lelièvre, announced the grant of a $ 10 million grant to the Gaspésie Railway Society to carry out railway structures between Gaspé and Percé. 
This $ 10 million financial assistance primarily targets the rehabilitation of three railway bridges between Gaspé and Percé. The work provided for these infrastructures are a prerequisite for the commissioning of the admiral tourist train in this sector. This aid will also allow the realization of other works on the same section. 
"I am very pleased to announce an investment that will have positive spin-offs for Gaspésie by providing an enriched tourist services for the many international holidaymakers and cruises visiting the region. Today we ask a concrete gesture that reaffirms my government's desire to participate in the influence of our regions, "said the Prime Minister. 
"It's a very good news for the entire population of Gaspésie. This investment will accentuate the tourist attractiveness of our beautiful region. This project has long been hoped for a long time and I am pleased to see that it will materialize. Our region is a destination of choice for many tourists and, from now on, they will have another good reason to extend their stay, "concluded Gaétan Lelièvre.
