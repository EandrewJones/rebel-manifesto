Quebec - The Minister of Natural Resources, Ms. Martine Ouellet, is launching the 15th edition of Energy Efficiency Week, which takes place from November 24 to 30. On the theme “Energy efficiency matters! », The Week aims to make Quebecers aware of the importance of energy efficiency.
“In total, the government has invested more than $ 665 million in energy efficiency programs, unprecedented! The Week is a privileged moment to learn about its issues and its many challenges. By contributing to the reduction of greenhouse gas emissions, energy efficiency programs help develop our economy, ”said Martine Ouellet.
