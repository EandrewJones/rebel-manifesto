Amqui– The Minister for Tourism, Minister responsible for the Bas-Saint-Laurent region and Member of Parliament for Matane-Matapédia, Mr. Pascal Bérubé, on behalf of the Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Land Occupancy and Minister of Transports, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, announces the allocation of financial assistance of $ 100,000 to the City of Amqui for the construction of an agora in Pierre-et-Maurice-Gagné park.
With a capacity of 2,000 people, this vast outdoor stage, overlooked by a roof, will become the largest outdoor performance hall in the Matapedia Valley. It will host shows and events of all kinds, in particular as part of the festivities surrounding Amqui&#39;s 125th anniversary, which will be celebrated in 2014. The project, the cost of which is estimated at $ 624,000, will therefore allow to complete the range of services in this park, which already includes play areas for children, water games and an asphalt cycle ring.
&quot;It is with great pride that I announce this financial assistance from the Government of Quebec which will strengthen the attractiveness of this magnificent gathering place and will offer an invaluable springboard for the holding of cultural activities not only in Amqui, but also for the whole of the Matapedia Valley ”, declared Minister Pascal Bérubé.
Financial assistance comes from the Economic Development Fund of the Ministry of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Land Occupancy.
