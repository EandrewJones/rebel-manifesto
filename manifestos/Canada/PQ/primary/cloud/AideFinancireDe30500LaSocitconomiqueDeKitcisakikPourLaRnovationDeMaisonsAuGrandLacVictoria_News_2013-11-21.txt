Val-d&#39;Or– The Member of Parliament for Abitibi-Est and Minister for Native Affairs, Ms. Élizabeth Larouche, on behalf of the Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Land Occupancy and Minister of Transport, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, announces the granting of financial assistance of $ 30,500 to the Kitcisakik Economic Society for the renovation of houses on Grand lac Victoria.
More specifically, the project consists of restoring about twenty summer houses located on the peninsula of Grand Lac Victoria. This project will allow the inhabitants of the Kitcisakik community to continue their tradition of visiting these places each year for a period of approximately six months, from May to October.
&quot;The Government of Quebec is pleased to contribute to the well-being of the members of the Kitcisakik community as well as to the maintenance of their cultural richness by participating in the realization of this project which will allow them to continue the practice of traditions and rituals that are dear to them, ”declared Minister Larouche.
Financial assistance comes from the conjunctural development fund of the Ministry of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Land Occupancy.
It should also be remembered that the Secrétariat aux affaires autochtones recently granted a grant of $ 49,500 for the realization of this project.
