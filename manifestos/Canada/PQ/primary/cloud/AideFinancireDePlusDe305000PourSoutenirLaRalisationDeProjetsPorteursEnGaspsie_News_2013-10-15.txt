G aspé– The Minister for the Regions, Minister responsible for the Gaspésie-Îles-de-la-Madeleine region and Member of Parliament for the riding of Gaspé, Mr. Gaétan Lelièvre, announces the allocation of financial assistance of $ 305,657 within the framework of the Regional Initiatives Assistance Fund to support the implementation of seven structuring projects for the Gaspé region, the full list of which appears in the appendix.
“Our government has made the development of the Gaspé a priority, which is why I am proud to support these promising projects, which will promote the socio-economic development of the region. These various projects from the community and representing various sectors of the economy of the Gaspé region, including tourism, culture and wood processing, testify to the dynamism of the people of the region. These projects will have positive spinoffs for a large number of local businesses and organizations, but also on the quality of life of the citizens of Gaspé, ”said Minister Lelièvre.
The Regional Initiatives Assistance Fund is part of the support for the government intervention strategy for the development of the Gaspésie-Îles-de-la-Madeleine region and is a major lever to promote, in particular, the achievement of projects stemming from the region&#39;s development priorities. A maximum annual envelope of $ 6 million is provided for this fund for a period of five years. The program is in effect until March 31, 2018.
