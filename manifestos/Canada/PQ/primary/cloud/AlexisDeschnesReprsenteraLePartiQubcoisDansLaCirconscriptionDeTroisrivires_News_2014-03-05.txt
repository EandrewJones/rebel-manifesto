Trois-Rivières - Premier and leader of the Parti Québécois, Pauline Marois, is pleased to announce the candidacy of Alexis Deschênes in the riding of Trois-Rivières. “Alexis is a man of heart and conviction. This is an exceptional candidacy for the people of Trois-Rivières, who will recognize in him undeniable qualities of integrity and dedication. I ask them to support Alexis so that he represents them in the National Assembly, ”said Pauline Marois.
Father of three children, Alexis Deschênes is a former journalist and correspondent for the National Assembly. He has been a member of the Bar since 2013 and holds the position of litigator in the Litigation Department of the Quebec Ministry of Justice.
“I have always felt that my heart was anchored in the soil of the regions of Quebec. I want to play politics to be at the service of citizens. I will make a point of being very present on the ground to meet people and hear their concerns. I am committed to the social and economic development of the regions, particularly that of Trois-Rivières, ”said Alexis Deschênes.
“After taking courageous decisions in Mauricie, the government is committed to making the region more prosperous. We have delivered the goods: the unemployment rate has fallen and investments of more than 500 million have been made, which has made it possible to preserve or create more than 1,700 jobs. And that&#39;s only the beginning. We are determined to promote prosperity for the entire region, ”added Pauline Marois.
“Through the Economic Diversification Fund set up for Mauricie and Center-du-Québec, we are working with local stakeholders to attract investment and support businesses in their development. We have a plan and the team to be successful. Today, it is time to give us the means to go even further, ”concluded Mr. Deschênes.
