Montreal– Premier of Quebec, Pauline Marois, accompanied by Minister of Culture and Communications, Maka Kotto, and Minister of International Relations, La Francophonie and Foreign Trade and Minister responsible for the Montreal region, Jean-François Lisée, announces a first crucial step in the realization of a major project: the transformation of the Museum of Contemporary Art of Montreal.
“I am very proud to lead a government that deeply believes in the importance of a culture that is vibrant, up-to-date and accessible to all. Today, I am happy to announce that we are taking a further step towards access to this culture. It is therefore with pleasure and pride that I kick off the expansion project of the Musée d&#39;art contemporain de Montréal, ”declared the Premier.
