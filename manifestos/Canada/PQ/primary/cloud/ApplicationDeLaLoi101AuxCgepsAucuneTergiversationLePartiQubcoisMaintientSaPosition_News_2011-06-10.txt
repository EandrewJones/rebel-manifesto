Montreal - The leader of the Parti Québécois, Pauline Marois, is keen to rectify the facts concerning an article in La Presse this morning which suggests that the party &quot;would not commit to subjecting CEGEPs to Bill 101&quot;. This information conveyed by La Presse is completely false.
“At our last convention, the activists adopted a proposal aimed at extending the application of the Charter of the French language to CEGEPs. This proposal is part of our program and will be part of our electoral platform. Full stop, ”said Pauline Marois.
