Quebec - As Paris faces the horror of unprecedented terrorist attacks, the leader of the official opposition, Pierre Karl Péladeau, and the Parti Québécois spokesperson for international relations, Carole Poirier, wish to express , on behalf of all elected members of the Parti Québécois, their deep solidarity with France.
“We share this evening the sadness and the deep emotion of the French nation. Our hearts go out to the families of the victims, but also, more broadly, to all French people. We firmly denounce these attacks and offer France our support in the face of this abominable and despicable tragedy, ”said the leader of the official opposition.
“France is struck again this evening by unspeakable acts of terrorism. Faced with this barbarity, the French Republic must remain strong and united. She will know how to get up and defend herself. We are at his side, ”concluded the MNA for Hochelaga-Maisonneuve.
