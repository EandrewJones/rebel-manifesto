Quebec - The MNA for Chicoutimi and Leader of the Official Opposition, Stéphane Bédard, as well as the MNA for Hochelaga-Maisonneuve and spokesperson for international relations, Carole Poirier, express their solidarity with the relatives of the victims and the Tunisian people , following the attack on the Bardo National Museum in Tunis.
“Horror and violence have struck again, this time in the heart of a highly historic and touristic place. On behalf of the entire Parti Québécois team, I extend my sincere condolences to the relatives and families of the victims, as well as to all the Tunisian people; we are at your side in this event, ”said Stéphane Bédard.
“Regardless of the reasons that may have motivated these actions, they remain unacceptable and barbaric, and we must denounce them loud and clear each time. In the face of terror, solidarity and democracy must triumph, ”added Carole Poirier.
