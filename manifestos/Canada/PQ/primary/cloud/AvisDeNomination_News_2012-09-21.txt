Quebec - Quebec Premier Pauline Marois is pleased to announce the appointment of the Member of Parliament for Johnson and Chief Government Whip of Quebec, Yves-François Blanchet, as responsible for the Mauricie region and the region from Center-du-Québec to replace Bernard Drainville, Member of Parliament for Marie-Victorin and Minister responsible for Democratic Institutions and Citizen Participation, and of Pierre Duchesne, Member of Parliament for Borduas and Minister of Higher Education, Research, Science and Technology.
“I am very happy to entrust these new responsibilities to Yves-François Blanchet. He is a man of convictions and I am convinced that he will be able to brilliantly defend the interests of the citizens of Mauricie and Center-du-Québec, ”declared the Premier of Quebec, Pauline Marois.
