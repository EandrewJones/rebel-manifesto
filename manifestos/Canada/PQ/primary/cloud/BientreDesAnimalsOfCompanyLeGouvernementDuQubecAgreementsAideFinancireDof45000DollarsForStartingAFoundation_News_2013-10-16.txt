Quebec - The Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Agriculture, Fisheries and Food, Mr. François Gendron, is pleased to announce the allocation of financial assistance of $ 45,000 to the Fondation pour le bien -being pets in Quebec in order to support the start-up of this new organization.
“The health and well-being of companion animals are important issues that concern the Department and several partners. I am happy to support this new foundation which shares these concerns. This organization was born from the desire of stakeholders in the field to equip themselves with a common funding tool in order to intervene in a concerted and coherent manner on important issues, such as the sterilization of cats and dogs. We welcome this initiative, ”said François Gendron.
The Quebec Pet Welfare Foundation is the result of the commitment of the members of the Companion Animal Welfare Working Group, chaired by Ms. Marie Bouillé, Member of Parliament for Iberville. In carrying out its mandate, this working group, set up by the Ministry of Agriculture, Fisheries and Food, is called upon to find avenues for improvement in terms of health and well-being. for pets and leisure animals.
It should be remembered that the Animal Health Protection Act (RSQ, chap. P-42) includes a section (IV.1.1) which is expressly devoted to the safety and welfare of animals and which aims to strengthen the protection of animals. pets.
In this regard, more than 250 inspectors, at the service of the Ministry and 13 societies for the protection of animals and societies for the prevention of cruelty to animals, are deployed throughout Quebec to ensure compliance with the provisions of the Act. .
