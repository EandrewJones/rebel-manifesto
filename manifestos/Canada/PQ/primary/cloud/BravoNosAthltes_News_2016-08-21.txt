Quebec, August 21, 2016 - As the Olympic Games in Rio come to an end, the member for Jonquière and leader of the official opposition, Sylvain Gaudreault, as well as the member for Labelle and the Parti Québécois spokesperson for sports , leisure and healthy lifestyles, Sylvain Pagé, congratulated these formidable athletes who amazed the world with their remarkable performances and, more particularly, our Quebec athletes.
“At the top of their game, the athletes who took part in the Games made us experience intense emotions. All of them deserve our warmest congratulations, if only for having courageously persevered on the road leading to this prestigious competition. Participating in the Olympics is already an accomplishment in itself, the crowning of many years of work and preparation, mental and physical ”, declared Sylvain Gaudreault first.
“In total, 22 medals were collected by the athletes representing Canada at the Rio Games, and many Quebecers are behind these successes. This result is fantastic. We are very proud of these women and men who have shown us that dreams can come true, provided the necessary effort and conviction is put into them. I obviously hope that many young people will find in these Olympians a source of inspiration and motivation that will lead them to adopt healthy lifestyles, ”said Sylvain Pagé.
“Whether or not they have received top honors, whether they work alone or in a team, our athletes are among our noblest ambassadors; in addition to our congratulations, they deserve our unconditional support. Of course, I continue to wish to soon see athletes wearing the colors of Quebec at the Olympics, and to stand out there! », Concluded the leader of the official opposition.
