Toronto - The Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Land Occupancy, Minister of Transport and Minister responsible for the Société d&#39;habitation du Québec (SHQ), Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, takes part today, in Toronto, in the Meeting of Provincial and Territorial Ministers Responsible for Housing.
During the meeting, Minister Gaudreault will discuss with his counterparts various issues affecting housing in Canada. In particular, the conditions for renewing investment in affordable housing will be discussed.
“Quebec&#39;s participation in this meeting is essential. It is essential that the Government of Quebec communicate its vision, issues and challenges specific to Quebec in the area of housing, ”said Minister Gaudreault.
