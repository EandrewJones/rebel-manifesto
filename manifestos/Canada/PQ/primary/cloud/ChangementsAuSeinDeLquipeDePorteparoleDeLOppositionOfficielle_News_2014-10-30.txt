Québec, October 30, 2014 - The member for Chicoutimi and leader of the official opposition, Stéphane Bédard, announces changes to the team of spokespersons for the parliamentary wing of the Parti Québécois.
The MNA for Duplessis, Lorraine Richard, will henceforth be the official opposition spokesperson for northern development.
The member for Rimouski, Harold LeBel, becomes spokesperson for seniors, in addition to keeping his current role as spokesperson for the fight against poverty, solidarity and social economy.
