Montreal, March 12, 2020 - The Parti Québécois has decided to make important changes to its leadership race, in order to comply with the directives of the Direction de santé publique concerning the coronavirus (COVID-19).
The two debates, which were to be held in Montreal and Drummondville, will take place without an audience and will be webcast on social media and the Parti Québécois website. Details of these discussions will be communicated at a later date.
The vote to elect the next leader is maintained on the same dates and according to the same procedures, from June 15 to 19, by Internet or by telephone. Regarding the other events of the race and the territorial activities of the Parti Québécois, decisions will be made later.
In addition, until further notice, the national council which was to be held on April 18 in Montreal is canceled.
