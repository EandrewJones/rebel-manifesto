East Broughton - Parti Québécois leader Pauline Marois welcomes Kaven Mathieu&#39;s candidacy in the riding of Lotbinière-Frontenac.
A sovereignist activist for several years, Kaven Mathieu knows very well the constituency he intends to represent. He is currently the mayor of the Municipality of East Broughton.
“It is with a lot of pride, but also a lot of humility, that I am going to meet the citizens of Lotbinière-Frontenac. I will offer them my experience, my energy and all the strength of my convictions in order to earn their trust. The entire Parti Québécois team and I will work hard to ensure that the winds of change blow hard in Lotbinière-Frontenac, ”said Kaven Mathieu.
“The arrival of Kaven Mathieu in our team is excellent news for the Parti Québécois. Our candidate combines the energy of his youth with a solid political background. I am sure that the voters of Lotbinière-Frontenac will find in him a candidate who has everything it takes to represent them well in the National Assembly, he who already represents and serves the citizens of the municipality of which he is mayor ” , added the leader of the Parti Québécois.
“From now on, it is no longer for the Liberal Party donors to decide. It is up to the citizens of Lotbinière-Frontenac, ”concluded Pauline Marois.
