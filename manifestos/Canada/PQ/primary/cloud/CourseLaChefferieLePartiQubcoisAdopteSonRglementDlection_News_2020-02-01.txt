Montreal, February 1, 2020 - The Parti Québécois coordination conference, meeting in Montreal this weekend, has adopted the rules for the leadership race, which will lead to the election of a new or new leader . The results of the ballot will be unveiled on Friday, June 19, in the evening, in the Capitale-Nationale or Chaudière-Appalaches region.
“On the strength of our new statutes and a new declaration of principle, adopted last November, it is with enthusiasm and determination that we approach this race for leadership. Over the past year, we have carried out a serious and ambitious stimulus exercise to build a new Parti Québécois. This process will end on June 19, with the election of a new or a new leader, ”indicated Dieudonné Ella Oyono, president of the Parti Québécois.
Equipped with modernized rules, the Parti Québécois continues its process of openness, in particular by welcoming occasional members or sympathizers as voters, for a fee of $ 5.

In addition, the coordination conference appointed the former deputy and activist Agnès Maltais as returning officer. “During this leadership race, candidates will have the opportunity to voice their ideas to continue our progress towards the founding of the country of Quebec. From this democratic exercise - giving for the first time a voice not only to members, but also to supporters - the Parti Québécois will emerge ready to achieve all its ambitions, ”she declared.
