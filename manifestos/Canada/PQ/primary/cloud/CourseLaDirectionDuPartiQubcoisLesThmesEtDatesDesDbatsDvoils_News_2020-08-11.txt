Montreal, August 11, 2020 - The president of the Parti Québécois and president of the election for the leadership race, Dieudonné Ella Oyono, today announced the themes and dates of the three debates. “The issues facing Quebeckers will be at the heart of these meetings, as will the fundamental values of the Parti Québécois, defined in our declaration of principle,” he stressed.
Thus, the candidates for the leadership of the Parti Québécois will debate around the following four themes, divided into three meetings:
26 august
Theme 1 - Freedom and the Parti Québécois
September 8
Theme 2 - Equity and justice
September 22
Theme 3 - Nationalism
Theme 4 - Protection of the environment and territory
The virtual debates will be moderated by the former president of the Parti Québécois, Gabrielle Lemieux.
“The Parti Québécois will do everything in its power to ensure that as many people as possible can attend these exchanges between the candidates, via webcast, in accordance with public health instructions,” concluded Dieudonné Ella Oyono.
