Montreal - The Parti Québécois candidate in Saint-Jérôme, Pierre Karl Péladeau, specifies that he is already working fully with the ethics and professional conduct commissioner in order to define the necessary arrangements so that he can participate in democratic life , and this, in compliance with the highest ethical standards.
The National Assembly unanimously adopted a code of ethics to establish clear rules.
&quot;I will abide by the law, the Code of Ethics and Ethics for Members of the National Assembly, and all guidelines that will be set out by the Commissioner of Ethics and Ethics,&quot; said Pierre Karl Péladeau.
