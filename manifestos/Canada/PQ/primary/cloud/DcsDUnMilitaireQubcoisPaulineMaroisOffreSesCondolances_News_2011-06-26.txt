Montreal - The Leader of the Official Opposition and Member of Parliament for Charlevoix, Pauline Marois, and all the members of the Parti Québécois wish to offer their most sincere condolences to the family and loved ones of Francis Roy, Master Corporal, who d he was first an infantry soldier with the Royal 22e Régiment before joining the Canadian Special Operations Regiment in 2009.
“On behalf of the parliamentary wing of the Parti Québécois and on my behalf, I would like to offer my deepest condolences to the family and loved ones of Mr. Roy. I wish them all the peace, strength and comfort they need to get through this terrible ordeal, ”said the leader of the official opposition.
“My thoughts are also with the Quebec soldiers who have lost comrades in arms in recent years. I thank them for the will they have shown in defending their ideals in Afghanistan, ”concluded Pauline Marois.
