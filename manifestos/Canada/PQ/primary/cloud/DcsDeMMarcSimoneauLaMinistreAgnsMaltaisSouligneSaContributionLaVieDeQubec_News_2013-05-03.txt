Quebec- The Minister of Labor, Employment and Social Solidarity, Minister responsible for the Status of Women and Minister responsible for the Capitale-Nationale and Chaudière-Appalaches regions, Mrs. Agnès Maltais, learned with sadness of the death by Mr. Marc Simoneau.
“Deeply committed to his community, Mr. Simoneau has dedicated his life to serving the people of Quebec. Whether as a sports commentator or as a city councilor, a genuine and passionate man will be remembered. I salute the memory of a person who made a very personal contribution to the democratic life of Quebec and who was able to enthusiastically share his love of sport. He will remain a model for sports journalism in Quebec ”.
On behalf of the Government of Quebec, Minister Maltais offers her most sincere condolences to her family and loved ones.
