Quebec– The member for Laval-des-Rapides and parliamentary assistant to the premier of Quebec for youth issues, Léo Bureau-Blouin, is starting tomorrow on a tour of the regions of Quebec to meet Quebec youth. He will be accompanied by members of Parliament for Saint-Jean, Dave Turcotte, and from Terrebonne, Mathieu Traversy.
Here is the tour schedule from now until the summer:
May 24LavalMay 27EstrieJune 3MauriciaJune 8Chaudière-AppalachesJune 10Centre-du-QuébecJune 15Bas-Saint-LaurentJune 18Nord-du-QuébecJune 20Saguenay
The tours will be organized in collaboration with the youth forums in each of the regions and the Youth Secretariat.
