Chapais- The Minister of Natural Resources and responsible for northern development, Ms. Martine Ouellet, is pleased to announce that the City of Matagami has fulfilled all the conditions to benefit from an agreement with the Ministry of Natural Resources in accordance with the program relating to a cession of land in favor of northern municipalities.
“The public lands ceded to the City of Matagami will be entirely dedicated to the construction of single-family residences. Thirteen new houses could be erected in this new neighborhood, ”said Martine Ouellet.
&quot;With the economic development of the region, this allocation of land allows the City of Matagami to face a shortage of housing for workers. With the North for All, our government shows that northern development is done with the people of here ”, declared Luc Ferland, MP for Ungava.
The program relating to the free transfer of lands in the domain of the State to northern municipalities will allow the City of Matagami to correct the situation of housing shortage it is facing and which is detrimental to its ability to attract. and workforce retention. The ceded territory covers an area of 16,661 m2.
“This residential complex consolidates the urban core already served by municipal infrastructures. This project is an essential element in the demographic and economic vitality of the City of Matagami, ”concluded Martine Ouellet.
