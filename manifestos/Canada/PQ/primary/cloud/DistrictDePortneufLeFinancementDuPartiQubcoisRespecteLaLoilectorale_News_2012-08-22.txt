Donnacona– The Parti Québécois candidate in the constituency of Portneuf, René Perreault, is delighted with the results of the investigation by the Chief Electoral Officer of Quebec (DGEQ) on his fundraising campaign. &quot;The DGEQ concludes that I acted legally and that I did not commit any act going against the Election Act,&quot; he proudly declared.
The conclusions of the DGEQ are clear. &quot;The necessary verifications have been made with regard to all the contributions intended for you since January 1, 2012 and we have no reason to believe that corporate funding would exist within the company. Instance of the Parti Québécois in the constituency of Portneuf. &quot;
“The Liberals tried to smear my reputation and damage my election campaign. Today, I am proud of the DGEQ&#39;s conclusions, which totally whitewash my reputation. The contributions I have received comply with the law and the actions I have taken are beyond reproach. I would also like to thank all those who generously contributed to my fundraising campaign, ”added René Perreault, calling the complaint filed last week by the Liberals a wet firecracker.
René Perreault is continuing his electoral campaign for the September 4th poll. “The Parti Québécois is the best party to represent the interests of Quebecers. We have the most competent chef, the strongest team and the program that best matches the ambitions of Quebecers. It is now up to us to choose, ”he concluded, recalling that the Parti Québécois, with its 90,000 members, is the most important political force in Quebec.
