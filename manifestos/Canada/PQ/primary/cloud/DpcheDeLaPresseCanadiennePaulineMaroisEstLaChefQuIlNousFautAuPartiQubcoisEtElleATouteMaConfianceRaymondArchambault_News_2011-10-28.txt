Montreal - The president of the national executive council of the Parti Québécois wants to rectify the facts concerning a report from the Canadian Press published today. Indeed, the journalist Jocelyne Richer affirms that Raymond Archambault “refuses to comment on the future of Pauline Marois. Nothing is further from the truth. The president of the national executive council affirms that “Pauline Marois is the leader that the Parti Québécois needs and that she will lead us during the next election campaign. &quot;
