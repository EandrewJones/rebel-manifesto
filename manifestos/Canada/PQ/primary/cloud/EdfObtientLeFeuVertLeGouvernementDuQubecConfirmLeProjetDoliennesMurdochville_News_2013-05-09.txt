Quebec - The Premier of Quebec, Pauline Marois, accompanied by the Minister for the Regions and Minister responsible for the Gaspésie – Îles-de-la-Madeleine region, Gaétan Lelièvre, today announced the relocation on Mount Rothery in Murdochville, a 74 MW project from EDF Énergies Nouvelles. This project represents an investment of $ 175 million, and spinoffs of $ 185,000 per year, for 20 years, for the MRCs of Côte-de-Gaspé and Haute-Gaspésie.
“The Government of Quebec is pleased to continue its commitment to the wind energy sector and thus concretely support the maintenance of jobs in the regions that depend on this sector of activity. This announcement confirms the Gaspésie – Îles-de-la-Madeleine region as a pillar of the wind power industry in Quebec, ”said the Premier.
The site will generate significant regional economic benefits with the hiring of 150 workers during construction, while ensuring a supply of wind turbine components in the designated region of Gaspésie and the MRC of Matane.
“The government was keen to support the Municipality of Murdochville, which is struggling with significant difficulties. The announcement of this major project is a concrete gesture made by the government, ”concluded Gaétan Lelièvre.
