La Tuque - On the occasion of an exceptional tour of members of the Parti Québécois government in the Mauricie and Center-du-Québec regions, the Minister of Natural Resources, Ms. Martine Ouellet, announced an investment of $ 3 million. of dollars in the acquisition of knowledge on mineral resources in the Gouin reservoir area, northwest of La Tuque.
“The Mauricie territory covers nearly 40,000 km2 and conceals great natural wealth. This $ 3M investment will provide knowledge on the geology of the Mauricie region, ”said Martine Ouellet.
In 2012-2013, the Ministry of Natural Resources collaborated with the City of La Tuque to carry out a geological reconnaissance project in the Gouin reservoir area. Over the next six years, the Department will invest $ 3M in the pursuit of work to acquire knowledge on mineral resources in the territory. This work will serve to better document the mineral resources found in the region. Aeromagnetic surveys will be carried out in 2014 and 2015 in the Gouin reservoir area and mapping work will extend until 2020. This geological work will be done in collaboration with the Atikamekw communities in the region.
The members of the Parti Québécois and the government ministers will meet on February 5 and 6 in Shawinigan, on the occasion of the caucus of deputies and the Council of Ministers. They will take advantage of this meeting to tour the nine ridings of the Mauricie region and the Center-du-Québec region. In less than a week, they will make more than 80 announcements of different kinds. These announcements will contribute to the development and growth of the Mauricie and Center-du-Québec regions.
