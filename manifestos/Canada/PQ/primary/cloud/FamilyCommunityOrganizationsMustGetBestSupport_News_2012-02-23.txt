Quebec - “Every day, family community organizations work in support of families across Quebec. Whether for parental support, training workshops, homework help, community animation, cultural or family activities, these organizations help support parents in Quebec. In order to pursue their mission, family community organizations must obtain better financial support as of the next budget. &quot;
This is what the member for Iberville and Parti Québécois spokesperson for the family, Marie Bouillé, said in reaction to the request of community organizations Family who demand an improvement in their funding. The representatives of these Family community organizations were present this morning at the National Assembly to file a petition of nearly 10,000 signatories asking for an increase in their financial support.
“In anticipation of the preparation of the next budget, the Minister of Families must put forward the requests of community family organizations to the Minister of Finance. It is her responsibility and she must do it now, ”concluded Marie Bouillé.
