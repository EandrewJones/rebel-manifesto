Montreal - Premier of Quebec and leader of the Parti Québécois, Pauline Marois, announces that exceptional women are joining the Parti Québécois team, including Martine Desjardins, candidate in Groulx.
“I am happy to announce the candidacy of Martine Desjardins in the riding of Groulx. It is not only a woman of leadership that we welcome today, but above all a woman of heart. I know that when she commits to a cause, she devotes herself completely to it. And Quebec needs determined and convinced women. &quot;
Former president of the Quebec University Student Federation and student leader during the spring 2012 crisis, Martine Desjardins has since been a blogger and columnist for the Journal de Montréal as well as for the 98.5 FM station. Lately, she had participated in the documentary on Lise Payette, in which she had confided to him to be a convinced sovereignist.
“I am presenting myself with Ms. Marois&#39; team, because I deeply believe that political commitment is necessary to move Quebec forward. More than ever, we need young women to take their place in politics. Our generation must continue the work done by these remarkable women who came before us, ”said Martine Desjardins.
Pauline Marois said she was excited by the arrival of many candidates who come to strengthen the Parti Québécois team and believes that Quebecers will too. “Martine Desjardins joins a team of dynamic women who bring a breath of fresh air to our political life. The team that we are putting together is remarkable, not only for the quality of the people who make it up, but also for their unwavering attachment to Quebec. &quot;
We have a plan and the team to be successful. Today, it is time to give ourselves the means to go even further.
