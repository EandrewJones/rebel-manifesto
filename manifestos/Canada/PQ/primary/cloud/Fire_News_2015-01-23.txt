Quebec - The MNA for Chicoutimi and Leader of the Official Opposition, Stéphane Bédard, as well as the MNA for Matane-Matapédia and spokesperson for public safety, Pascal Bérubé, wish to once again pay tribute to the victims of the terrible tragedy that occurred a year ago at the Résidence du Havre, in L&#39;Isle-Verte, and which shocked all of Quebec.
“On January 23, 2014, an unimaginable tragedy struck all Quebecers with horror. Each of us has been touched right in the heart. As a government, we responded by providing all possible support to the community. On behalf of all my fellow members of the Parti Québécois, I would like to tell the families of the 32 victims, their loved ones and the populations of L&#39;Isle-Verte and Bas-Saint-Laurent that we remain united and that we share their pain. », Declared Stéphane Bédard.
“Those who have passed away will remain forever in our memories; to continue living in spite of their absence, we must remain united, in order to give each other support and comfort. The people of our region have shown great courage since that fateful night. I wish to pay tribute to them, and to express my gratitude to all the stakeholders who worked very hard, under extremely difficult conditions, to help the community to recover. I am thinking in particular of local elected officials, firefighters, police officers, artisans in the health network as well as volunteers; Thank you for showing such great humanity and for having worked with so much heart so that the people of L&#39;Isle-Verte can hope to find serenity, ”concluded Pascal Bérubé.
