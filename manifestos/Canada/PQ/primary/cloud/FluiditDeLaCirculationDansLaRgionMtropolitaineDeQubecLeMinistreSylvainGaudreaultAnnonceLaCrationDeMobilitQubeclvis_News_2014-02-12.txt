Quebec– The Minister of Transport and Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Land Occupancy, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, is pleased to announce the creation of the Quebec-Lévis Mobility Committee. This initiative aims to improve traffic in the Quebec City metropolitan area.
“We are aware of the traffic flow problems both on the north shore and on the south shore of the national capital. This committee will make it possible to better coordinate the actions of the various transport stakeholders and to work in concert, both at the administrative and political level, in order to better plan our interventions on the road network of the Department and the municipalities, ”declared Minister Gaudreault.
The Quebec-Lévis Mobility Committee will be made up of the Ministère des Transports, the cities of Quebec and Lévis, the Réseau de transport de la Capitale, the Société de transport de Lévis and the Association du camionnage du Québec.
“Better coordination between stakeholders will help mitigate the impacts on the road network, both for motorists, public transit users and truckers. The road user is at the heart of our actions, ”said Sylvain Gaudreault.
“The current challenges linked to the fluidity of the movement of people and goods require common solutions for the benefit of all; this committee is the essential tool to achieve this, ”concluded Sylvain Gaudreault.
