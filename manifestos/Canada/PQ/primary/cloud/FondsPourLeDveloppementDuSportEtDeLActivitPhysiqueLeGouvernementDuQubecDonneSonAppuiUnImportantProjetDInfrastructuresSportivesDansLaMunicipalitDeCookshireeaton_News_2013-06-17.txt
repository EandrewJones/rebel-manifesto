Cookshire-Eaton - The member for Sherbrooke, Mr. Serge Cardin, announced today, on behalf of the Minister of Education, Recreation and Sport, Ms. Marie Malavoy, that financial assistance of up to 138,606, $ 66 is granted to the Municipality of Cookshire-Eaton for the addition of a multipurpose room at the Johnville community center.
The work, the total cost of which is estimated at just over $ 280,000, consists mainly of the addition of a multipurpose room. The new facilities will, in particular, increase the sports activities offered at the community center.
“I am delighted with this project, which responds to an important need for the people of Cookshire-Eaton. We want to encourage them to move and stay in shape and this multifunctional room is without a doubt a great way to do that, ”said Mr. Cardin.
This project, in which the Municipality participates financially, was selected within the framework of the Support Program for Sports and Recreational Facilities - Phase II of the Fund for the Development of Sport and Physical Activity, which provides maximum financial assistance. equivalent to half of the eligible costs.
“Each of the projects that we financially support under this program is rigorously analyzed according to the eligibility criteria established in the rules and standards of the latter. The projects meet the needs of the population concerned and will allow greater accessibility to various clienteles, thus promoting the adoption of a physically active lifestyle by as many people as possible, ”said Minister Malavoy.
By creating the Fund for the Development of Sport and Physical Activity, the Government of Quebec wanted to ensure that all regions benefit from modern and safe sports and recreational facilities. For more details on this fund, see www.mels.gouv.qc.ca/sections/fondsDeveloppement/.
