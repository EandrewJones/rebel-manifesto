Quebec - The Minister of Sustainable Development, Environment, Wildlife and Parks, Mr. Yves-François Blanchet, in collaboration with the president of the Quebec Federation for Atlantic Salmon, Mr. Jean Boudreault, grants more than 50 $ 000 for the protection of salmon rivers in five regions of Quebec.
A total of 19 salmon river management organizations will share this support paid under the Assistance Fund for the Protection of Salmon Rivers in Quebec. &quot;This financial contribution, which clearly demonstrates the government&#39;s effort to protect wildlife resources, will promote, among other things, the purchase of equipment, the hiring of protection personnel or the maintenance of protection-related jobs. of fauna in the organizations that manage salmon rivers in Quebec benefiting from the Assistance Fund, ”indicated Minister Blanchet, who at the same time thanked the Fédération québécoise pour le salmon atlantique for its financial participation in the Assistance Fund. .
The purpose of this fund is to protect certain salmon rivers in difficulty located in five regions of Quebec where salmonids are found: Bas-Saint-Laurent, Saguenay-Lac-Saint-Jean, Capitale-Nationale, Gaspésie -Îles-de-la-Madeleine and the Côte-Nord.
In addition to being subject to certain conditions, each beneficiary organization is invited to consolidate its activities to ensure their sustainability and to work in partnership with local communities and salmon fishermen in order to find lasting solutions to the problems related to protection. of the resource. &quot;Quebec&#39;s salmon rivers bring significant economic benefits to these regions and it is our duty to ensure the sustainability of this resource,&quot; concludes Mr. Blanchet.
