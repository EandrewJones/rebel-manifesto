Hérouxville - The Minister of Education, Recreation and Sports, Ms. Marie Malavoy, today announced that financial assistance of up to $ 116,447.70 has been granted to the Municipality of Hérouxville for the renovation of the leisure Alexandre-Massicotte.
“Thanks to this project, the Municipality will be able to improve its offer of sports and recreational services and allow its residents to have access to safe facilities adapted to their needs. I firmly believe that it is thanks to projects like this that we will make everyone want to move! ”Said Minister Malavoy.
It should be noted that the planned work consists mainly of constructing a service building, installing play modules and renovating the tennis courts as well as the swimming pool filtration system.
The project announced today has been selected as part of the Sports and Recreation Facilities Support Program - Phase II of the Sports and Physical Activity Development Fund, which provides maximum financial assistance equivalent to half of the costs. eligible.
“Each of the projects that we financially support under this program is rigorously analyzed according to the eligibility criteria established in the rules and standards of the latter. The projects meet the needs of the population concerned and will allow greater accessibility to various clienteles, thus promoting the adoption of a physically active lifestyle by as many people as possible, ”added the Minister.
By creating the Fund for the Development of Sport and Physical Activity, the Government of Quebec wanted to ensure that all regions benefit from modern and safe sports and recreational facilities. For more details on this fund, see [www.mels.gouv.qc.ca/sections/fondsDeveloppement/(http://www.mels.gouv.qc.ca/sections/fondsDeveloppement/).
