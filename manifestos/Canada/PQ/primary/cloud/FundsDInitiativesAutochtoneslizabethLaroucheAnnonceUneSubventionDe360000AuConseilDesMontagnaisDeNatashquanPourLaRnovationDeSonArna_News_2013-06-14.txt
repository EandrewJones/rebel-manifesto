Quebec - The Minister for Native Affairs, Ms. Élizabeth Larouche, announced the granting of financial assistance of $ 360,000 for the repair of the dome of the skating rink in the municipality of Natashquan.
“The arena of a municipality is a place for family meetings and exchanges, where young and old alike hold recreational and sports activities. It is imperative that this type of building meets safety standards. The rehabilitation of the roof will reopen this place to its visitors, who will once again devote themselves to their leisure activities, ”declared Minister Larouche.
This project is structuring for the Innu community of Natashquan, since it promotes the consolidation of jobs in Aboriginal communities in addition to increasing the well-being of community members.
“I am delighted with this announcement for the entire community of Natashquan, and especially for its youth. It is important that young people be able to benefit from a place to play sports and have fun, ”said the MNA for Duplessis, Ms. Lorraine Richard.
The financial assistance granted to the Montagnais Council of Natashquan is equivalent to 50% of the project costs. It comes from the Native Initiatives Fund, community infrastructure envelope, from the Native Affairs Secretariat (SAA). The objective of this fund is to support projects that could have significant spinoffs on the economic, social or community development of the Aboriginal nations of Quebec.
Recall that in 2010, the Montagnais Council of Natashquan acquired the building serving as a shelter to renovate it into an arena. For this project, the Secrétariat aux affaires autochtones (SAA) had contributed financially to the tune of $ 569,967.
