Quebec, February 16, 2014 - Quebec Premier Pauline Marois and Minister of Education, Recreation and Sport Marie Malavoy are pleased to congratulate Dominique Maltais, who today won a medal of money in snowboarding (snowboard cross).
“Dominique Maltais is one of the brightest stars of snowboarding on the world stage. Its success is fully deserved. She can be proud of having achieved her goals and has made thousands of Quebecers dream, who have followed her journey with passion and who, today, are delighted with her performance. As this athlete comes from Petite-Rivière-Saint-François, it is not only as Premier, but also as Member of Parliament for Charlevoix – Côte-de-Beaupré that I say my sincere bravo! », Declared Pauline Marois.
“This medal from Dominique Maltais is the result of years of effort, and also of assiduous support and accompaniment from his parents and coaches. The values which are at the heart of our future national policy on sport, leisure and physical activity, Dominique Maltais embodies them all: the taste and the pleasure of moving, surpassing oneself, perseverance, discipline and confidence within our capabilities. Here is an athlete who represents what we want best for Quebec, ”declared Minister Malavoy.
We invite you to watch the speed skating and alpine skiing (giant slalom) competitions on Tuesday.
