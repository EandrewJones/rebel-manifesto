Quebec - “Minister Line Beauchamp herself admits that she does not have all the answers on the impacts of Intensive English on student success. She therefore admits that her government acts in the most total improvisation! Once again, we can see that, for lack of preparation and consultation, the Liberal government is seriously mortgaging the implementation of this program ”.
This is what Sylvain Gaudreault, the member for Jonquière and official opposition critic for primary and secondary education and literacy, declared the day after the withdrawal of the two main teachers&#39; unions in Quebec. , the Federation of Teaching Unions and the Autonomous Federation of Teaching, of the working committee ensuring the introduction of intensive English in grade 6.
The Parti Québécois says yes to learning a second or even a third language. “The main thing is academic success and perseverance. This is what matters most to us. The Ministry of Education must suspend the universal application of this measure. The ministry, community stakeholders and parents must work together to obtain a realistic and complete picture of the impacts of the implementation of intensive English in grade 6 on students, especially those experiencing the greatest academic difficulties ”, he added. supported the member for Jonquière.
“The successful implementation of such a program requires the support of everyone. To achieve this, Minister Beauchamp must commit to providing the human and material resources necessary for the implementation of intensive English in Quebec schools because, currently, we are heading straight into a wall, ”concluded Sylvain Gaudreault. .
