Montreal - By the end of the nomination period, six candidates had submitted valid nomination papers. The members of Marie-Victorin will have to express their choice among these six people at the investiture assembly on September 18.
The candidates are: Philippe CloutierNicolas DionneCatherine FournierCarl GilbertMathieu MarcotteSophie Stanké “The six candidates offer rich and diverse profiles. Their number attests to the vitality of the Parti Québécois and it is with great enthusiasm that I wish them all a good campaign ”declared the president of the Parti Québécois, Raymond Archambault.
