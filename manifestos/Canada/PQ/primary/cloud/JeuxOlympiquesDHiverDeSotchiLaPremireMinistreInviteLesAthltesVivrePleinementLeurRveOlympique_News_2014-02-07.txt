Quebec - The Premier of Quebec, Pauline Marois, and the Minister of Education, Recreation and Sport, Marie Malavoy, wished to send their best wishes for success to the Quebec athletes who took the road to Sochi to participate in the Winter Olympics.
“Our top athletes show us what we can aspire to when talent is driven by unwavering determination and properly supported. I sincerely hope that their efforts will be rewarded. For Quebec, they are all golden ambassadors, ”said the Premier.
In total, 48 Quebec athletes will take part in these games, in 10 disciplines. The majority of them are supported by the Government of Quebec, through the Team Quebec Program. This program financially supports athletes to enable them to reach the highest peaks of athletic performance. It also provides support to coaches who support Quebec&#39;s sporting elite, and promotes the professionalization of the work of coaching high-level athletes.
“Our athletes and their coaches have dedicated time and effort to achieve their Olympic dream. I wish them the success they hoped for. One thing is certain, our thoughts will be with them throughout their stay in Sochi and it is with enthusiasm that we will highlight each of their performances, ”added Ms. Malavoy.
It should be remembered that the support of the Government of Quebec is reflected in several programs and measures of the Ministry of Education, Recreation and Sport. Note, for example, the Financial Support Program for the Development of Sports Excellence, the Tax Credit for High-Level Athletes, Support for Sport-Studies Programs, the Support Program for Sporting Events, the Support Program for Quebec sports federations and the completion of the construction project for the future premises of the National Sports Institute of Quebec, currently underway at the Olympic Park. These initiatives also have the objective of developing a sporting culture in Quebec and encouraging the next generation.
