Quebec - Premier of Quebec, Pauline Marois, and Minister of Education, Recreation and Sports, Marie Malavoy, are pleased to congratulate Marie-Ève Drolet, Jessica Hewitt, Valérie Maltais and Marianne St-Gelais for the medal silver they won earlier today in the 3,000-meter relay event in short track speed skating.
“I want to offer my sincere congratulations to these four great athletes; they were able to combine their efforts to reach the podium. Together, they form a real shock team! What models of success for Quebec youth! », Declared the Prime Minister.
“It&#39;s a strong and experienced team that we saw at work today. Each of its members showed up on the rink with the firm determination to reach their potential. Thanks to their talent and the pooling of their efforts, their dream has come true, ”added Minister Malavoy.
We invite you to watch the snowboard and cross-country skiing competitions on Wednesday.
