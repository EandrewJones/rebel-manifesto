Quebec - The Minister of Transport and Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Land Occupancy, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, is proud of the fact that the Transport Association of Canada (ATC) recognized the excellence of Quebec during of its annual awards ceremony last Monday in Winnipeg.
In fact, the Ministère des Transports du Québec won the prize in three categories, out of a possible four:
Among these projects that have highlighted the innovation and excellence of the teams that contributed to it, we should also highlight the participation of several government partners in the photographic radar and red light surveillance project. As for the project for an innovative runoff water management system for the 73/175 road, it reflects the exceptional work accomplished in this file by Mr. Yves Bédard, in the area of environmental protection.
“Quebec&#39;s know-how in the field of transportation is already known. However, getting three of the four awards presented by the Transportation Association of Canada proves that the Department is a world-class leader in this area. I would like to highlight the exceptional work of the various teams who participated in the award-winning projects, as well as the exemplary collaboration of the Association québécoise des transports. This contribution ensures that Quebec&#39;s roads are safer and better integrated into their environment, ”said Minister Sylvain Gaudreault.
Next year, the ATC convention will take place from September 28 to October 1, 2014, at the Palais des congrès de Montréal. More than 1,400 delegates are expected as the organization, founded in Montreal, will celebrate its 100th anniversary.
