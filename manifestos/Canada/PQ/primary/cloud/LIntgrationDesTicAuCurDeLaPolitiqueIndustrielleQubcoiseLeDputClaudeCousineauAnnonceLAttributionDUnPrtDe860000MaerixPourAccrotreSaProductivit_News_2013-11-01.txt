Sainte-Adèle - The Member for Bertrand and Vice-President of the National Assembly, Mr. Claude Cousineau, announced, on behalf of the Minister for Industrial Policy and the Quebec Economic Development Bank, Ms. Élaine Zakaïb, the allocation, through Investissement Québec, of a loan of $ 860,000 to Maerix, a company that designs management software, mainly in the areas of health, safety and the environment, for businesses.
&quot;Maerix, through its capacity for innovation and its know-how, is a key player in the information and communications technology sector in Quebec,&quot; said MP Claude Cousineau. “The Government of Quebec is proud to support an investment project of more than $ 860,000, the economic spinoffs of which for the Laurentides region will be reflected in the creation of 5 new quality jobs. &quot;
The financing granted is intended for the acquisition of a new building of more than 4,588 square feet in Piedmont, where all of Maerix&#39;s activities will be repatriated. This financial contribution will make the company more efficient operationally and support its growth.
“Quebec&#39;s prosperity is linked to the growth of our SMEs and their competitiveness,” said Élaine Zakaïb. “Quebec&#39;s industrial policy 2013-2017 gives an important place to the introduction of information and communications technologies within companies in order to increase their productivity, crucial for their competitiveness. Maerix is an instrument of this integration. The company itself will be able to benefit from the improvement in the investment tax credit, a measure announced under this policy. &quot;
Founded in 1999, the Maerix company specializes in the design, development and sale of management software packages relating to advanced fields such as health, safety, the environment and human resources. Well established in Quebec, it counts among its clients major Quebec flagships, such as Bombardier, Kruger, Alcoa and Cirque du Soleil, as well as many well-known public institutions.
&quot;We are very grateful to be able to count on the participation of Investissement Québec to move forward with our growth project,&quot; said Maerix president, Éric Veilleux. “This significant financing will facilitate the acquisition of our new building, within which Maerix&#39;s activities will take place. Thanks to more spacious premises that are better suited to the reality of existing work teams, we will be able to increase our productivity and continue our development for the years to come. &quot;
Investissement Québec&#39;s mission is to promote the growth of investment in Québec, thereby contributing to economic development and job creation, in all regions. The Company provides businesses with a full range of financial solutions, including loans, loan guarantees and equity investments, to support them at all stages of their development. In addition, she is responsible for administering tax measures and prospecting for investments abroad.
