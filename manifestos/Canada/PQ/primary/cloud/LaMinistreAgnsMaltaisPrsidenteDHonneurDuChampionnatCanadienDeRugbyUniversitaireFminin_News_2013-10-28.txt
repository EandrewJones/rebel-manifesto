Quebec - The Minister responsible for the Capitale-Nationale and Minister responsible for the Status of Women, Ms. Agnès Maltais, is proud to act as Honorary President for the Canadian Women&#39;s University Rugby Championship, which will take place at the TELUS stadium. Laval University, from October 31 to November 3.
“Sporting events are one of the main attractions of the Capitale-Nationale region. It is therefore a pleasure for me to host, for the very first time, this major tournament and to be its honorary president. Reaffirming our expertise in the organization of high-level events, the presentation of this competition will demonstrate the modernity and quality of our sports infrastructure ”mentioned Minister Maltais.
This championship will host the six best women&#39;s rugby teams from across Canada, including two teams from Quebec. “I therefore invite you to come and encourage these determined players in large numbers, who will undoubtedly offer us a most exciting competition. Through their performance, both athletic and academic, these young women represent true sources of inspiration, ”added Minister Maltais.
The Secrétariat à la Capitale-Nationale is making a financial contribution to the 2013 Canadian University Women&#39;s Rugby Championship by allocating the sum of $ 7,500.
The Secretariat, attached to the Minister of the Executive Council, advises the government and the Minister responsible for the Capitale-Nationale, Ms. Agnès Maltais, on any question or measure having a significant impact on the region and on its economic, cultural, tourist and social development. .
The Secretariat acts in concert with its various governmental and regional partners, while financially supporting the realization of projects contributing to the growth of the Capitale-Nationale.
