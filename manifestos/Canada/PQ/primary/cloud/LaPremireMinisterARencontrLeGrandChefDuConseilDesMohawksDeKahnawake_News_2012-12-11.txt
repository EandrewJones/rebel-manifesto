Quebec - The Premier of Quebec, Ms. Pauline Marois, is pleased to announce that her meeting yesterday with the Grand Chief of the Mohawk Council of Kahnawake, Mr. Michael A. Delisle, made it possible to renew ties.
“We discussed issues of common interest, particularly transportation and taxation, and I reiterated Quebec&#39;s commitment to supporting the economic development of Kahnawake,” said Pauline Marois.
The Premier is also committed to ensuring greater efficiency of the Kahnawake-Quebec Central Table, responsible for maintaining communication, ensuring good relations and finding mutually satisfactory solutions. In addition, sectoral tables will have the mandate to deal more in depth with specific subjects.
“By relaunching the work of the central table, we want to seize an opportunity to strengthen our relationship with the Mohawk community of Kahnawake. In this regard, the meeting with Grand Chief Delisle marks a renewal in the establishment of a solid relationship between Quebec and Kahnawake, ”concluded the Premier.
