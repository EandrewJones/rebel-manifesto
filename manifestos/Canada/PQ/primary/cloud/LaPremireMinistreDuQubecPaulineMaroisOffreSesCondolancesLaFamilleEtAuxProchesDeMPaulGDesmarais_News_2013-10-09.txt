Quebec - On behalf of all Quebecers, the Premier of Quebec, Pauline Marois, offered her deepest condolences to the family members and friends of one of the great businessmen, Quebec philanthropists, patrons and builders, Paul G. Desmarais, who passed away last night.
“Quebec has lost a great builder. The remarkable success of Paul G. Desmarais and his many qualities have made him a recognized and eminently respected businessman around the world. His memory will forever be associated with one of the greatest personal successes of our time. I offer my most sincere condolences to his wife Jacqueline, his children, his family and his friends, ”said Pauline Marois.
The Prime Minister also wished to underline the departure of this great philanthropist who deeply loved culture and who believed that contact with art made people better. “His great generosity as well as that of his wife have enabled some of our greatest cultural institutions to develop and shine. Thanks to him, many Quebecers have been able to live exceptional experiences in contact with art, ”she added.
Finally, the Premier paid tribute to the memory of a citizen who deeply loved the Charlevoix region, who gave him back. “As the Member of Parliament for the riding of Charlevoix – Côte-de-Beaupré, I am the spokesperson for the people of Charlevoix to reiterate respect and affection for the whole family on this day of mourning,” concluded the Premier.
