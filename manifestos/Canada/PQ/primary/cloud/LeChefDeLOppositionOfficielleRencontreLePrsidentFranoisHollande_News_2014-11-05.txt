Québec, November 4, 2014 - The member for Chicoutimi and leader of the official opposition, Stéphane Bédard, met the President of the French Republic, François Hollande, at the end of the day. This interview was an opportunity to reaffirm the bonds of friendship that unite France and Quebec.
“I am very happy to have discussed with President Hollande. It is important to maintain the exceptional relationship that we have with France. In this rapidly changing world, where the challenges are becoming more complex, where benchmarks change without warning, in a world that is constantly redefining itself, the sustainability of the direct and privileged relationship between France and Quebec takes on its full value ” , said Stéphane Bédard.
