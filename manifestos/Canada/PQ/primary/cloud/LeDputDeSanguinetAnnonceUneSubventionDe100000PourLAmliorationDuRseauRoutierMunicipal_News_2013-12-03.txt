Quebec - The Member of Parliament for Sanguinet and Parliamentary Assistant to the Minister of Finance and the Economy, Mr. Alain Therrien, is pleased to announce, on behalf of the Minister of Transport and Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Occupation of territory, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, that a grant of $ 100,000 is granted to the municipality of Saint-Mathieu for the improvement of the Philie, Poissant, Saint-Édouard, Saint-François-Xavier and Montée de la Petite roads -Coast.
This subsidy is paid under the Municipal Road Network Improvement Assistance Program (PAARRM). This program helps finance improvement projects carried out by municipalities on their network. It aims to carry out improvement projects that will make it possible to achieve objectives such as improving the quality of the pavement, drainage and the safety of road users.
&quot;By participating in the financing of this project, our government is giving the municipality of Saint-Mathieu the means to carry out work aimed at contributing to the well-being and safety of its community,&quot; declared the Member for Sanguinet.
Mr. Therrien concluded by stating that “this investment demonstrates our government&#39;s desire to support municipalities in maintaining the integrity of their municipal network”.
