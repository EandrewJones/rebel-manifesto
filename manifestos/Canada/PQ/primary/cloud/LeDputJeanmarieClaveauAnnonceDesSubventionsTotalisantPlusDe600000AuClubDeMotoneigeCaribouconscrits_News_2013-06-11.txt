Quebec - The MP for Dubuc, Mr. Jean-Marie Claveau, is pleased to announce, on behalf of the Minister of Transport and Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Land Occupancy and MP for Jonquière, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault , the payment of three grants, totaling $ 618,514, to the Caribou-Conscrits snowmobile club in the Saguenay-Lac-Saint-Jean region, for the implementation of trail infrastructure projects.
These grants, stemming from Component I of the Financial Assistance Program for Trail Infrastructure and Wildlife Protection, will allow the club to ensure the sustainability of trails 328 and 93 by repairing bridges spanning the Valin and Saint-Laurent rivers. Louis. In addition, concerned with protecting the environment and wildlife habitats in the region, the club will be able to redevelop part of trail 328, which is heavily made up of wetlands.
“Such subsidies are critical to the sustainability of the off-road vehicle industry. With this assistance program, we thus wish to promote the permanence of the trails while limiting the inconveniences associated with the practice of this leisure activity, and this, in a context that is always more secure for enthusiasts ”, underlined Minister Sylvain Gaudreault.
“The practice of snowmobiling is an important economic lever for the Saguenay-Lac-Saint-Jean region. The development and improvement of trail infrastructures will allow our region to remain a must-see destination for all snowmobile enthusiasts, both in Quebec and abroad, ”added the MP for Dubuc, Mr. Jean-Marie Claveau.
For the 2012-2013 fiscal year, Component I of the Financial Assistance Program for Trail Infrastructure and Wildlife Protection will finance 38 off-road vehicle infrastructure projects for a total of nearly $ 4 million. dollars.
