Sherbrooke– The Minister of Health and Social Services, Minister responsible for Seniors and Minister responsible for the Estrie region, Mr. Réjean Hébert, on behalf of the Minister of Transport and Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Occupation of the territory, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, accompanied by the deputy for Sherbrooke, Mr. Serge Cardin, this morning confirmed the government&#39;s desire to complete the extension of Highway 410 south of Sherbrooke.
“This project remains a priority for my government. I am therefore happy to inform you that part 2 of the project has been included in the Quebec Infrastructure Plan, ”said Mr. Hébert. The Department can therefore proceed with the preparation of the project, which provides for the construction of a section between Highway 108/143 in Lennoxville to the eastern sector, opposite the federal experimental farm of Agriculture and Agri-Food Canada, in compliance with the Framework Policy on the Governance of Major Public Infrastructure Projects ”.
