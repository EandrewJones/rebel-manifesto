Quebec - Premier of Quebec, Pauline Marois, and Minister of Education, Recreation and Sport, Marie Malavoy, congratulate Alex Harvey on his performance at the World Cross-Country Skiing Championships currently taking place in Italy . The Quebec skier won the bronze medal in the classic style sprint event.
“Over the years, Alex Harvey has shown courage and determination to overcome the challenges that inevitably present themselves on the journey of every high performance athlete. He demonstrated impressive strength of character. Despite a shoulder injury shortly before the competition, he got up and gave everything he had. His efforts have paid off today. Quebecers can be proud of this inspiring young athlete from Saint-Ferréol-les-Neiges who is proof that hard work and surpassing oneself are the key to success. Congratulations! », Declared Pauline Marois.
“I would like to pass on my sincere congratulations to Alex Harvey. His performance today is a testament to tremendous talent, but also incredible determination. He is a true model of perseverance for young people who one day aspire to follow in his footsteps. The Government of Quebec is proud to support it in its quest for the highest peaks! », Concluded Minister Malavoy.
