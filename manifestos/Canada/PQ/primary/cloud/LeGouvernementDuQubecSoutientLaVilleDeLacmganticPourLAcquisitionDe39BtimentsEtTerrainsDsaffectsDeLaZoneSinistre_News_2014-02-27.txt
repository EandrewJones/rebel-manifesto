Lac-Mégantic– The Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Land Occupancy and Minister of Transport, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, is pleased to announce that the Government of Quebec will support the City of Lac-Mégantic in the acquisition of abandoned buildings and land in the disaster area.
“Since the tragic events that shook the City of Lac-Mégantic, the Government of Quebec has always been there to support the community. The commitment made today is another step towards rebuilding the City and will help homeowners who have been affected by the tragedy, ”said Sylvain Gaudreault.
The parameters and terms of this government assistance will be established in collaboration with the City of Lac-Mégantic. Preliminary evaluations indicate that the acquisition costs by the Municipality could reach around twenty million dollars.
