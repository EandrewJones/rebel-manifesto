Saint-Romuald– The Minister of Labor, Minister of Employment and Social Solidarity, Minister responsible for the Status of Women, and Minister responsible for the Capitale-Nationale and Chaudière-Appalaches regions, Ms. Agnès Maltais, announced today, on behalf of the Minister of Education, Recreation and Sports, Marie Malavoy, that the government would make investments of $ 17,843,142 to allow school boards in the region to carry out major renovations in 44 of their educational institutions.

“Our government is acting in an enlightened and responsible manner by investing in the educational heritage and by offering the population attractive places that are conducive to success. We are convinced that this choice opens the way to a quality future for the young people who will build the Quebec of tomorrow, ”declared Minister Maltais.
Thanks to these investments, various corrective works could be carried out, from the replacement of plumbing, through the repair of roofs, doors and windows, to the renovation of the exterior envelope and the structure of the buildings. It should be noted that government assistance is paid as part of the Réno-écoles program, one of the main measures stemming from the Economic policy: Priority employment, and more specifically, under the measures Adding spaces and Maintaining buildings 2013-2014 .
“For the past year, we have not stopped taking measures to ensure that our schools are attractive, healthy, safe living environments that are conducive to learning. We are committing ourselves even further on this path through the promising and strong actions that we are taking today, ”Minister Malavoy said.
