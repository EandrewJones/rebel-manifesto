Wendake- The Minister for Tourism, Mr. Pascal Bérubé is proud to announce financial assistance of $ 35,000 for the Wendake Pow Wow, which will take place from June 28 to 30.
The Wendake Pow Wow offers activities under the sign of tradition and authenticity but which are also part of modernity. This event offers spectators performances by Indigenous and non-Indigenous artists, craft workshops, various demonstrations of traditional skills, a family component and tastings of authentic First Nations dishes.
“The presence of the Huron-Wendat community constitutes a cultural wealth for the Capitale-Nationale region. The Wendake Pow Wow offers the opportunity to discover or rediscover this ancestral culture which is still very much alive. It is a friendly and warm meeting whose influence extends more and more beyond our borders, ”said Minister Bérubé.
It should be noted that financial assistance comes from Tourisme Québec&#39;s Development Program for the Tourism Industry of Quebec ($ 35,000) as part of the second year of a three-year agreement.
