Quebec - The Minister of Health and Social Services and Minister responsible for Seniors, Réjean Hébert, granted financial assistance of $ 10,000 to the Alzheimer Society of Gaspésie-Îles-de-la-Madeleine, a sum from his discretionary budget.
“Alzheimer&#39;s disease turns the lives of entire families upside down. People with Alzheimer&#39;s disease and their loved ones have a great need for support and accompaniment, and this is precisely what the Alzheimer Society of Gaspésie-Îles-de-la-Madeleine offers. I am therefore very happy to encourage its activities, especially since its role is set to gain in importance in the present context of an aging population, ”declared Minister Hébert.
The MNA for Bonaventure, Sylvain Roy, is very pleased with the contribution of Minister Réjean Hébert who aims to support the activities surrounding the 11 memorial walks that were held this year in the riding: “The funds raised on the occasion of the Memory Walk will allow the Alzheimer Society of Gaspésie-Îles-de-la-Madeleine to continue to offer quality services to people with the disease as well as to their loved ones, ”he said.
Remember that the Alzheimer Society of Gaspésie-Îles-de-la-Madeleine offers a very wide range of services including telephone listening, animation of support groups and various other information and awareness activities throughout the region. , thanks to its partnership with nine volunteer centers.
&quot;The activities of the Alzheimer Society are also in perfect complementarity with all of our actions aimed at supporting home support, facilitating access to adapted services and improving the range of services for loved ones. caregivers, ”Minister Hébert concluded.
