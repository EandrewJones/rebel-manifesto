Professor of philosophy, author and polemicist, Louise Mailloux has made an important contribution to the promotion of secularism, notably through her latest publications, interventions and columns during the debate that has taken place in recent months.
The award ceremony will take place on Sunday, November 23, 2014, at 1 p.m. at the Montreal Humanist Center 1225, boul. St-Joseph est, in Montreal (Laurier metro).
All MLQ members and friends are welcome. The general assembly of the MLQ will follow at 2 p.m.
