Montreal, October 18, 2019 - As part of the recovery process started last March, the national executive of the Parti Québécois has chosen to move the staff to new premises. This new location will better meet the needs of the party and offer more agility to both its operation and its organization.
“The Parti Québécois will soon adopt new statutes to modernize its operations and a declaration of principles to redefine its values and its mission. Our move to a brand new office is part of this process and will help give our political party all the tools to face the new political reality and advance our project of independence for Quebec. »Declared the general manager of the Parti Québécois, Carole Poirier.
