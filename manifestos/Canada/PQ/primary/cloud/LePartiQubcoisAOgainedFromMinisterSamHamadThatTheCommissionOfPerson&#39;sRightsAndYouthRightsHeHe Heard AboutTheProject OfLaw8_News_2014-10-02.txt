Quebec - The MNA for Berthier and Official Opposition critic for agriculture, fisheries and food, André Villeneuve, the MNA for Gaspé, Gaétan Lelièvre, and the MNA for Rimouski, Harold LeBel, obtained by the Minister of Labor, Sam Hamad, that the Commission des droits de la personne et des droits de la jeunesse be heard in a parliamentary committee before continuing to study Bill 8, An Act to amend the Labor Code with regard to certain farm workers.
“The Commission des droits de la personne et des droits de la jeunesse has expressed serious reservations about the bill tabled by the Liberal government. She believes that this bill, in its current form, violates the fundamental rights of workers, ”declared André Villeneuve.
Following the tabling of a motion by MP Harold LeBel, to which the Liberal MPs have rallied, the Committee on the Economy and Labor will hear the Committee on Human Rights and Youth Rights on the project. of law.
“It is essential to discuss with the Commission des droits de la personne et des droits de la jeunesse in order to deepen its point of view on the Liberal bill. The Commission, in the opinion submitted to the Minister, recommends the withdrawal of the bill, stating that &quot;if it were adopted as is, Bill 8 would infringe the equality rights of a category of workers guaranteed by section 10 of the Quebec Charter, ”added Harold LeBel.
