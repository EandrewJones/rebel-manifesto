Quebec– The MNA for Hochelaga-Maisonneuve and official opposition spokesperson for seniors, Carole Poirier, is proud to have participated yesterday in the march for quality care organized by caregivers of a little everywhere in Quebec.
This march, in connection with the petition &quot;Care provided to the elderly&quot; tabled in the National Assembly on December 1, 2011 by MP Carole Poirier, aimed to promote the improvement of the entire health system in Quebec by increasing its accessibility, quantity and quality of care for the elderly and all those in need.
“As the official opposition spokesperson for seniors, I had the honor to present this petition to the National Assembly and I continue to support the civic initiatives undertaken by these women and men who fight for the elderly to have better access to health care, ”said Carole Poirier.
