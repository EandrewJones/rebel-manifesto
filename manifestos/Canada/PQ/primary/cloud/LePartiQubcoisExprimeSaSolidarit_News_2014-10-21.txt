Quebec City, October 21, 2014 –The MNA for Chicoutimi and Leader of the Official Opposition, Stéphane Bédard, and the MNA for Saint-Jean, Dave Turcotte, offer their support to the families of the victims and to the entire community of Saint-Jean- sur-Richelieu.
“On behalf of my colleagues from the Parti Québécois, I offer my most sincere condolences to the family of Warrant Officer Patrice Vincent. Our hearts go out to his loved ones as well as to those of the soldier injured during these sad events, ”declared Stéphane Bédard.
“I offer my fellow citizens all my support; I follow the situation closely by staying in contact with the police forces of the city of Saint-Jean-sur-Richelieu and those of the Sûreté du Québec, as well as with the military authorities of the Saint-Jean garrison. Our entire community is sticking together in these difficult times, ”added Dave Turcotte.
“I also wish to underline the exceptional intervention work of the Saint-Jean-sur-Richelieu police officers and to congratulate them. It is reassuring to know that we have a police service that is able to react promptly in all situations in order to ensure public safety, ”concluded the member for Saint-Jean.
