Quebec - The member for Chicoutimi and leader of the official opposition, Stéphane Bédard, announces that members of the Parti Québécois will be responsible, on behalf of the official opposition, for regions where their political party is not represented. “The austerity policies of the Liberal government are hurting all of Quebec. All citizens must be heard; this is why we are appointing spokespersons for each of the regions where the Parti Québécois is not represented. Citizens, elected officials and groups in each of these regions can count on them to carry their messages and support them, ”said Stéphane Bédard.

“I am proud of the wonderful Parti Québécois team; more than ever, our MPs will listen and support the citizens of all regions of Quebec, ”concluded Stéphane Bédard.
