Quebec - Following the terrible earthquake that hit Nepal and claimed thousands of victims, the member for Chicoutimi and leader of the official opposition, Stéphane Bédard, and the member for Hochelaga-Maisonneuve and spokesperson for of international relations, Carole Poirier, send their most sincere condolences to the Nepalese people, to Quebecers of Nepalese origin and to their families.
“On behalf of myself and my fellow members of the official opposition, I wish to express my deep sympathy to all those affected, directly or indirectly, by these tragic events; we stand in solidarity with the Nepalese people and share the sorrow of its some 6,000 nationals living in Quebec ”, declared Stéphane Bédard.
“Such a catastrophe is an immense shock. The life of an entire nation is turned upside down, forever transformed. To recover, the Nepalese people will have to show great courage and will need international cooperation. We want help on the ground to be organized as quickly as possible, ”added Carole Poirier.
