Laval– The leader of the Parti Québécois, Pierre Karl Péladeau, is pleased to announce the candidacy of Jibril Akaaboune Le François in the by-election in the riding of Fabre.
“These by-elections are an opportunity for Quebeckers to send a strong message to the Liberal government of Philippe Couillard: we do not accept austerity. All over Quebec and here in Fabre, people are being hit hard by the Liberal cuts. Today, I want to tell the citizens of Fabre that there is a solution to liberal austerity, it is the Parti Québécois and Jibril Akaaboune Le François. Mr. Akaaboune Le François is a young person who has always been involved in Laval. He holds a bachelor&#39;s degree in communication and politics from the University of Montreal and has worked in municipal politics, ”said the leader of the Parti Québécois.
“It is with great enthusiasm that I begin this electoral campaign which promises to be exciting in the riding of Fabre. For some time now, I have been meeting the citizens here in the field. They are shocked by the attitude of the Couillard government and liberal austerity. On November 9, we will have our say to protect our education, our health system and our services. By electing a member of the Parti Québécois, the citizens of Fabre can clearly say no to the arrogance and cuts of the Liberal government, ”added Jibril Akaaboune Le François.
