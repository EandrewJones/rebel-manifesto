Québec, March 23, 2021 - Accompanied by the leader of the Parti Québécois, Paul St-Pierre Plamondon, the deputy for René-Lévesque, parliamentary leader and Parti Québécois spokesperson in matters of finance, Martin Ouellet, presented this morning the expectations of his political formation in view of the tabling, Thursday, of the 2021-2022 budget of the government of Quebec.
For a greener, fairer and more independent Quebec
&quot;After this year marked by the pandemic, a historic window appears for a revival that will make it possible to create a greener, more just and more independent Quebec,&quot; said Martin Ouellet.
