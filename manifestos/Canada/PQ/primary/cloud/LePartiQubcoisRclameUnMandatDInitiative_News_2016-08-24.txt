Québec, August 24, 2016 - Sylvain Rochon, the member for Richelieu and official opposition spokesperson for labor, has submitted a request to the members of the Committee on the Economy and Labor (CET) order of initiative concerning the increase of the minimum wage.
“Since its founding, the Parti Québécois has always been very concerned about the plight of low-wage workers. Of course, we support the idea of a gradual increase in the minimum wage, until we reach a target of $ 15 an hour, ”Sylvain Rochon first expressed.
“We therefore wish to bring together experts in order to allow elected officials to analyze this question in depth in the National Assembly. Although the members of Québec solidaire cannot table such a motion or vote in favor of it, not being members of the Committee on the Economy and Labor, we are delighted with their public support for this request ”, continued the Minister. spokesperson.
&quot;Now that the request for a mandate of initiative has been submitted to the CET, we are asking the members of the Liberal Party of Quebec and those of the Coalition Avenir Québec to support it, too,&quot; concluded the member for Richelieu.
