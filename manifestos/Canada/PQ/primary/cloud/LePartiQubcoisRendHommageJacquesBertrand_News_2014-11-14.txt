Québec– The member for Joliette and official opposition critic for culture and communications, Véronique Hivon, wishes to pay tribute to radio host Jacques Bertrand, who died on November 10.
“On behalf of my colleagues from the Parti Québécois, I extend my sincere condolences to the family, friends and ex-colleagues of Jacques Bertrand. He was an exceptional host and communicator who changed the world of radio in many ways. He was at the head of unforgettable programs on Radio-Canada&#39;s Première Chaîne, particularly the Macadam tribus program, from 1997 to 2009. In addition to his great professionalism, his daring, his humor and the love that he had left his mark on listeners of all ages for his audience during the three decades he spent at Radio-Canada, ”declared Véronique Hivon.
