Montreal - The leader of the Parti Québécois, Pierre Karl Péladeau, and the Member of Parliament for Taschereau, Agnès Maltais, wish to pay tribute, on behalf of the staff and members of the Parti Québécois, to Jacques Chabot, long-time political organizer and independence activist of the first hour, died suddenly in the late afternoon on April 27, 2016.
“Jacques has contributed his whole life to the realization of his vision of an independent, prosperous and united Quebec. It is with great sadness that we learned of the news of his passing. Today we are losing a tireless activist, a sensitive organizer and immense political expertise. Our entire political family is in mourning today. On behalf of the Parti Québécois team, the staff and the parliamentary wing, I offer my sincere condolences to the family, colleagues and friends of Jacques Chabot, ”said Pierre Karl Péladeau.
Jacques Chabot also worked as political advisor to the Member of Parliament for Taschereau, Agnès Maltais. “Driven by a real passion for political commitment and service to the population, Jacques deeply aspired to share his knowledge and train the next generation of politicians. He is therefore also a mentor for many whom we lost yesterday, ”added Ms. Maltais.
