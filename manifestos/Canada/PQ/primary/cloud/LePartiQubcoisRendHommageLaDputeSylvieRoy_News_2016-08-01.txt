Quebec– The MP for Jonquière and leader of the official opposition, Sylvain Gaudreault, learned with sadness of the death of the MP for Arthabaska, Sylvie Roy.
“On behalf of the entire Parti Québécois team, I extend my sincere condolences to Sylvie Roy&#39;s family, friends and staff. Sylvie was a combative MP, committed to the people of her constituency, close to her world. She defended her convictions with vigor and passion, first at the ADQ, then at the CAQ, and as an independent member, ”declared Sylvain Gaudreault.
“Sylvie Roy was a member of Parliament who placed justice and public service at the center of her priorities. In particular, she was the first to call for a commission of inquiry into corruption and collusion. All the deputies of the National Assembly feel a deep sadness today, while we are losing a colleague respected by all, ”added the leader of the official opposition.
