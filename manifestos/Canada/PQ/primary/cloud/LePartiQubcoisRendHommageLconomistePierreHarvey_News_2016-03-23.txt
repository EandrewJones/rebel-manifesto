Quebec - The leader of the Parti Québécois, Pierre Karl Péladeau, wishes to pay tribute to the economist Pierre Harvey, former director of HEC Montreal and economic advisor to the recently deceased Prime Minister René Lévesque.
“On behalf of the entire Parti Québécois team, I offer my sincere condolences to Pierre Harvey&#39;s wife and children, as well as to his relatives and former colleagues from the Parti Québécois. Mr. Harvey advised our political party and its leader, Premier René Lévesque, for several years. He has invested a lot in the sovereignty of our nation and its economic independence. Professor then director of HEC Montréal from 1982 to 1987, he left the mark of a generous, loyal man of great humility. Thank you, Mr. Harvey ”, declared Pierre Karl Péladeau.
