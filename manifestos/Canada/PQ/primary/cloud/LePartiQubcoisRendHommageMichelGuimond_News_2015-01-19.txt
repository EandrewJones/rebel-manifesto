Quebec - The member for Chicoutimi and leader of the official opposition, Stéphane Bédard, as well as the member for Taschereau and parliamentary leader, Agnès Maltais, pay tribute to the former member of the Bloc Québécois and former candidate of the Parti Québécois, Michel Guimond, died suddenly today.
“On behalf of my fellow members of the official opposition and the great family of the Parti Québécois, I extend my sincere condolences to the family and loved ones of Michel Guimond. Throughout his political career, this enthusiast has defended with conviction the place of the regions of Quebec and the importance of being close to his people. Michel was a particularly committed man, in particular for the advancement of Quebec and its independence, ”declared Stéphane Bédard.
“The greater Quebec City region is losing a formidable ambassador. Michel Guimond has always been very devoted to the citizens he represented. He was keen to defend them and to work for the development of the large regions of Quebec, Charlevoix and the Côte-Nord. We will all regret it, ”added Agnès Maltais.
