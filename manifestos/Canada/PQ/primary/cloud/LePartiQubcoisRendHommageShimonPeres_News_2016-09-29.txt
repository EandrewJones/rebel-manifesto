Quebec - The Leader of the Official Opposition and Member of Parliament for Jonquière, Sylvain Gaudreault, and the Member of Parliament for Hochelaga-Maisonneuve and Parti Québécois spokesperson for international relations, Carole Poirier, wish to salute the memory of the former Prime Minister and President of the State of Israel Shimon Peres.
“It is with great emotion that we learned of the passing of Shimon Peres. His long career is marked by his desire to find peaceful ways of resolving conflicts in the Middle East, obtaining the Nobel Peace Prize in 1994, alongside Yasser Arafat and Yitzhak Rabin, following the Oslo Accords, ”said Sylvain Gaudreault and Carole Poirier.
