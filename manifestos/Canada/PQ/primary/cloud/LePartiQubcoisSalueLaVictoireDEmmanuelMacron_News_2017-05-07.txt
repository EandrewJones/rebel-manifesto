Quebec City, May 7, 2017 - The leader of the official opposition, Jean-François Lisée, and the Parti Québécois spokesperson for international relations, Stéphane Bergeron, wish to salute the victory of Emmanuel Macron and his accession to the Presidency of the French Republic.
“The direct and privileged relationship that unites Quebec and France is an invaluable asset. With the election of Mr. Macron, I am confident that we will be able to maintain it, and I hope that we can even strengthen it. One thing is certain, the new President of the Republic will always be welcome in Quebec! », Affirmed Jean-François Lisée.
“In France as in Quebec, for several months - and more especially during the primaries -, we multiply the meetings with the actors of the French policy. We obviously intend to continue and enrich these exchanges, especially with the team of the new President of the French Republic, ”added Stéphane Bergeron.
