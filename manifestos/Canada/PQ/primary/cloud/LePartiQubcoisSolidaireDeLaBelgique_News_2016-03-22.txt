Quebec - Following the terrorist attacks in Brussels today, the leader of the official opposition, Pierre Karl Péladeau, and the Parti Québécois spokesperson for international relations, Carole Poirier, expressed their solidarity with Belgium and their friendship to the citizens of the capital of the European institutions.
“Hatred and violence hit Brussels this morning. We offer our sincere condolences to the families and loved ones of the victims of these barbaric attacks. These attacks on freedom and democracy are unacceptable, and we strongly condemn them, ”said Pierre Karl Péladeau.
“We share the sadness and the emotion of all Belgians. In these difficult times, we would like to reaffirm our friendship and support to them, ”added Carole Poirier.
