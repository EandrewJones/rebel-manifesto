Québec, July 15, 2016– The member for Jonquière and leader of the official opposition, Sylvain Gaudreault, as well as the member for Hochelaga-Maisonneuve and the Parti Québécois spokesperson for international relations, Carole Poirier, wish to condemn the attack on the night of July 14 in Nice. They express their full solidarity with the people of Nice and of France.
“Horror and barbarism have struck again. French people and tourists who came to celebrate the National Day were mowed down. We cannot remain silent in the face of terror. These acts attack our societies and our democracy. We strongly condemn this terrorist act. On behalf of the Parti Québécois, I extend my sincere condolences to the relatives and families of the victims, as well as to the entire population of Nice and France, ”declared Sylvain Gaudreault.
“The National Day is an opportunity for the French to celebrate what unites this great nation and its place in the world. She will rise again, strong and confident in the future. It can count on Quebec and the Parti Québécois to support it, with the same solidarity that has always marked our relations. Beyond the great sadness that pervades us today, we express our deep solidarity with the families of the victims and with France, ”concluded Carole Poirier.
