Quebec, September 9, 2016 - The member for Hochelaga-Maisonneuve and spokesperson for the Parti Québécois in international relations, Carole Poirier, wanted to speak about the events that have shaken Gabon since the announcement of the results of the &#39;presidential election.
“We are deeply concerned about the situation in Gabon. It seems essential to us that everyone works to restore calm as quickly as possible. For the good of the population, democratic institutions must be re-established in order to guarantee transparency as to the results of the ballot ”, declared Carole Poirier first.
The Member also spoke directly to Quebeckers from Gabon, as well as to those who have family or relatives there: “I know how affected each of you is by the events which disturb the peace in Gabon. Know that the Parti Québécois will remain by your side to defend the democratic values that we share, ”concluded Carole Poirier.
