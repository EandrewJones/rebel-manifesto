Quebec - The Quebec government welcomes the election of His Eminence Cardinal Jorge Mario Bergoglio, now François I, as the new Pope.
“The Catholic Church is intimately linked to the history of Quebec and remains present in the lives of many Quebecers. We hope that this new pontificate will be the bearer of a message of dialogue and reflection on the current challenges of our society ”, declared the Premier of Quebec, Pauline Marois.
