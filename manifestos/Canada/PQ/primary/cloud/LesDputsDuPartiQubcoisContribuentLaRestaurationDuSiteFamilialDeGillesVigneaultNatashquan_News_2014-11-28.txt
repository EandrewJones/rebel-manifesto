Quebec - At the invitation of the Natashquan Fund for the Protection of the Source, the members of the Parti Québécois contributed to the fundraising campaign to ensure the restoration, sustainability and operationalization of the museum component and the sharing of the Source of the site family run by Gilles Vigneault in Natashquan.
“An amount of $ 6,600 was raised by all of my colleagues from the parliamentary wing. I take this opportunity to thank them warmly. Highlighting the places where our great poet Gilles Vigneault spent his childhood and drew his inspiration is an important project for the local and regional heritage of Natashquan. I invite the entire population of Quebec to participate, ”said Lorraine Richard, Member of Parliament for Duplessis.
To contribute to the fundraising campaign, visit the website: www.avecnatashquan.com
