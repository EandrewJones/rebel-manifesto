Alma– Réjean Hébert, Minister of Health and Social Services and Minister responsible for Seniors, President of the Conseil du trésor, Minister responsible for the Saguenay-Lac-Saint-Jean region and Member of Parliament for Chicoutimi, Stéphane Bédard, as well as the Minister for Canadian Intergovernmental Affairs, the Canadian Francophonie and Sovereignty Governance and Member of Parliament for Lac-Saint-Jean, Alexandre Cloutier, today confirmed that the Center de santé et de services sociaux de Lac-Saint-Jean- East will be authorized to begin its expansion and redevelopment project for the emergency room and outpatient clinics at the Alma Hospital. Thus, work can begin next spring.
This project, which is estimated at approximately $ 40 million, or $ 24.5 million for fixed assets, was duly registered in the Quebec Infrastructure Plan (PQI), allowing the Ministry of Health and Social Services to ensure its funding. where applicable.
