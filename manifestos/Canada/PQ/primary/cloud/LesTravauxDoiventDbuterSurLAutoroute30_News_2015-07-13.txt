Quebec - The MP for Vachon and official opposition critic for transport, maritime strategy and transport electrification, Martine Ouellet, and the MP for Richelieu, Sylvain Rochon, ask the Minister of Transport to launch the work already scheduled on Autoroute 30 in the Sorel-Tracy region.
Yesterday evening, mysterious bumps appeared on Highway 30 eastbound, between Montée Saint-Roch and Chemin du Golf. Fortunately, the incident did not cause any accidents.
“This incident proves that the start of work is more than necessary on this portion of Highway 30. At present, the reasons for the appearance of these mysterious bumps are still unknown to the general public. This situation is worrying, ”said Martine Ouellet.
&quot;The incident which led, last night, to the closure of the 30 eastbound, between Montée Saint-Roch and Chemin du Golf, must convince the government to immediately go ahead with the work that is scheduled. in this sector. The running surface is no longer just unpleasant, it is dangerous. The closure of the section by the Ministry of Transport has confirmed this, ”concluded Sylvain Rochon.
