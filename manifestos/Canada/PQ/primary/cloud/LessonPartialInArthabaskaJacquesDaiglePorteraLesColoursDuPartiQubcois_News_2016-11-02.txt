Victoriaville - Jacques Daigle will be the Parti Québécois candidate in Arthabaska for the by-election of December 5, 2016. He intends to lead a campaign focused on the priorities of the constituency residents and propose pragmatic changes adapted to the needs of the population.
“The priorities in the riding must be the economy, health, education and prosperity. We need a deputy who will be present and listen to the people of Arthabaska. I will meet the citizens and I will listen. I will also formulate unifying proposals and I will invite people to join them, ”declared Jacques Daigle, Parti Quebec candidate in Arthabaska.
The leader of the Parti Québécois, Jean-François Lisée, chose Victoriaville to hold his first caucus following his election two weeks ago. It is a strong message that was sent to the population of Arthabaska by this gesture. The Parti Québécois is proud to present a solid, honest and effective candidacy to the voters of Arthabaska.
“Jacques is a well-known man in the Arthabaska community where he has been involved in school and municipal life for several years. It is with candidates who are involved and known in the community that the Parti Québécois demonstrates its ability to listen to the population and its local concerns. Having become a Member of Parliament, he will be a considerable asset for the people of Arthabaska and for all of Quebec as well, ”added Jean-François Lisée, leader of the Parti Québécois.
