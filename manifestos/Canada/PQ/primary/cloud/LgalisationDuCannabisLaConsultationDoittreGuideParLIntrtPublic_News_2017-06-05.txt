Quebec, June 5, 2017 - The Liberal government having finally heard the arguments of the official opposition about a consultation on the upcoming legalization of cannabis, the leader of the official opposition, Jean-François Lisée, and the public health spokesperson Sylvain Pagé nevertheless noted that the Liberals had wasted precious time. They also deplore their refusal to make the announced consultation a transpartisan exercise. However, the official opposition offers its cooperation and will participate in good faith, so that public health and prevention issues are at the heart of the process.
IN SHORT
- The government having complied with its arguments, the official opposition welcomes the announcement of the holding of a consultation.
