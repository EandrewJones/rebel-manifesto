Montreal - Premier and leader of the Parti Québécois, Pauline Marois, is proud to announce the candidacy of Lorraine Pintal in the riding of Verdun. “I am very happy to be able to count on the talent and creativity of this great Montrealer. Lorraine is a key figure in the cultural milieu of Montreal and Quebec. What she could bring, as a member for Verdun, is unique, ”said Pauline Marois.
Actress, director, director, host and author, Lorraine Pintal has been at the head of the Théâtre du Nouveau Monde since 1992. She currently sits as vice-president of the executive committee of the Quartier des Spectacles Partnership. Ms. Pintal also chairs the board of directors of the Vitrine culturelle de Montréal and has been a member of the board of Télé? Québec for five years.
“It is truly an honor for me to join the Parti Québécois team. I am enthusiastic about the idea of representing the citizens of Verdun. I will use the energy that I know to make the city of Montreal shine by supporting creators, organizations and businesses that export our know-how and our cultural vitality, ”said Lorraine Pintal.
Pauline Marois took the opportunity to recall the major investments made by her government to support cultural organizations in Montreal. We should only mention that the Montreal School of Contemporary Dance, Les Grands Ballets Canadiens and the Agora de la danse will benefit from the renovation and expansion of the Wilder building. In addition, $ 50 million will be invested in the Société de la Place des Arts.
“We have supported the entire Quebec cultural community. We have invested in our major institutions and we have taken action to promote access to culture. We are proud of our creators and we are determined to continue supporting them, to allow our culture to shine more, ”affirmed Pauline Marois.
We have a plan and the team to be successful. Today, it is time to give ourselves the means to go even further.
