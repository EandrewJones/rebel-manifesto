Québec, June 4, 2017 - The Leader of the Official Opposition, Jean-François Lisée, today presented the members and mandate of the Solidarity in Action team: MPs Catherine Fournier (Marie? Victorin), Harold LeBel (Rimouski) and Dave Turcotte (Saint-Jean).
IN SHORT
- The Solidarity in Action team has the mandate to go into the field, to meet organizations and groups, in order to develop a policy to fight poverty.
