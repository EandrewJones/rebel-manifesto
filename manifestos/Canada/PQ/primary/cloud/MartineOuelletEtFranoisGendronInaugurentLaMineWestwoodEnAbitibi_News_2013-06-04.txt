Quebec - The Minister of Natural Resources, Martine Ouellet, and the Deputy Premier and Minister responsible for the Abitibi-Témiscamingue region, François Gendron, are proud to participate in the official opening of the Westwood mine, a gold project of the Canadian mining company IAMGOLD, located between Val-d&#39;Or and Rouyn-Noranda.
“The opening of the Westwood mine confirms that mining development is continuing in Quebec. The announcement of 700 jobs for the next 19 years is excellent news in a region where the mining industry is at the center of economic activities, ”declared Martine Ouellet, Minister of Natural Resources.
IAMGOLD&#39;s investment reaches 700 million dollars, which includes the renovation of the processing plant, located two kilometers from the mine. Mine tonnage will be 800,000 tonnes of ore for an expected production of 180,000 ounces of gold per year.
