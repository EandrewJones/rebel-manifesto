Quebec - Premier of Quebec, Pauline Marois, and Minister of Education, Recreation and Sport, Marie Malavoy, are happy to congratulate the men&#39;s hockey team for this gold medal obtained today.
“I extend my sincere congratulations to the men&#39;s hockey team, which collected the gold medal at the Olympic Games in Sochi.
