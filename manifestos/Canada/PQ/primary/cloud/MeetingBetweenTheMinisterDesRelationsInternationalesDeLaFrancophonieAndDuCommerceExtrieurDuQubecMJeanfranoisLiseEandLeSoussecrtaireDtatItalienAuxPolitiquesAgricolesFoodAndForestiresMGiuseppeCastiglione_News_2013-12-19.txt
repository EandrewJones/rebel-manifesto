Quebec - The Italian Under-Secretary of State for Agricultural, Food and Forestry Policies Mr. Giuseppe Castiglione welcomed the Minister of International Relations, La Francophonie and Foreign Trade of Quebec Mr. Jean-François Lisée.
The meeting took place in an atmosphere of cordial collaboration, which made it possible to underline the desire for a new collaboration in the agrifood sector between Quebec and Italy.
Considering the expertise recognized on both sides, the cheese and organic products sectors offer interesting potential for collaboration, as does that of food safety and sustainable development.
They also welcomed the bilateral trade prospects that will result from the agreement in principle recently concluded between the European Union and Canada for the signing of a comprehensive economic and trade agreement.
Mr. Jean-François Lisée also indicated the intention of Quebec to participate in EXPO 2015 DE MILAN with a program of activities, despite the absence of a membership from Canada.
