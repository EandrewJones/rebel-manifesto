Quebec - This is the message that the Leader of the Official Opposition and Member of Parliament for Charlevoix, Pauline Marois, wishes to deliver to all Quebecers on the occasion of the National Day.
Pauline Marois will be present at the show that will take place tonight on the Plains of Abraham for Quebec&#39;s national holiday.
On June 24, Ms. Marois will be in Montreal where she will participate in the activities surrounding the National Day celebrations. In the morning, the Leader of the Opposition will attend the formal reception of the Mayor of Montreal, Gérald Tremblay, at City Hall.
In the afternoon, she will take part in the Défilé des Géants, which will kick off around 1:30 p.m. at the corner of Fullum and Sherbrooke streets. In the evening, Ms. Marois will be present at the national holiday show which will take place at Maisonneuve Park.
