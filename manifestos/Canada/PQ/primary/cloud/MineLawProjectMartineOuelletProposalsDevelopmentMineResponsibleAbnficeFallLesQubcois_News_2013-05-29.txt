Quebec - The Minister of Natural Resources, Martine Ouellet, today announced the tabling of the mining bill, which includes several important changes to modernize the Quebec mining regime.
“The new mining law will ensure economic, environmental and social stability for citizens and businesses alike. Worthy of today&#39;s economic development, this bill contains proposals concerning transparency, transformation as well as environmental protection and dialogue with communities. The new law will ensure responsible development of our mineral resources for the benefit of all Quebecers, ”said Martine Ouellet.
