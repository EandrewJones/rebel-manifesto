Chandler– The Minister for the Regions and Minister responsible for the Gaspésie-Îles-de-la-Madeleine region, Mr. Gaétan Lelièvre, and the Member for Bonaventure and Parliamentary Assistant to the Minister of Sustainable Development, the Environment, Wildlife and Parks for the wildlife and parks component, Mr. Sylvain Roy, are pleased to announce, on behalf of the Minister of Transport and Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Land Occupancy, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, additional investments in the order of $ 6 million for the 2013-2014 period, in the Gaspésie-Îles-de-la-Madeleine territory.
The 2013-2015 road works program, announced last spring, testifies to the government&#39;s desire to better manage infrastructure and pay the fair cost for public investments. “The objectives of this approach are bearing fruit. The amounts announced today reflect better project planning and respect for the ability to pay of all Quebecers. Controlling the costs of road works makes it possible to do more and even better meet the needs of the people of the Gaspé and the Magdalen Islands, ”said Minister Gaudreault.
