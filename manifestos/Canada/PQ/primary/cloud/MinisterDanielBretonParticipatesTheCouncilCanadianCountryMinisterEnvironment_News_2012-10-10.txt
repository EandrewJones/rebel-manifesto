Quebec - The Minister of Sustainable Development, Environment, Wildlife and Parks, Mr. Daniel Breton, will participate in the meeting of the Canadian Council of Ministers of the Environment (CCME) to be held on October 11 in Lake Louise , Alberta.
During this meeting, the environment ministers plan to discuss the implementation of a pan-Canadian air quality management system.
They also intend to speak out on the pan-Canadian approach to the management of biosolids from wastewater treatment and on industry commitments aimed at reducing the ecological footprint of packaging.
