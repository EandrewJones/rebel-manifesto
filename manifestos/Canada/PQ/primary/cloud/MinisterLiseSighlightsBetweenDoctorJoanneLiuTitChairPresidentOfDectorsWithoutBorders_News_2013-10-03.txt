Montreal - After taking office as president of the organization Médecins Sans Frontières (MSF), the Minister of International Relations, La Francophonie and Foreign Trade, Mr. Jean-François Lisée, congratulates Doctor Joanne Liu .
Doctor Liu, a pediatrician by training and practicing at Sainte-Justine Hospital, is the first Quebecer to be elected at the head of this international organization.
“I recently had the opportunity to discuss with Dr. Liu and I am convinced that her professionalism, her will and her great competence will give a positive and constructive tone to her presidency within the organization. I wish him the best of luck in his new role, ”said Minister Lisée.
