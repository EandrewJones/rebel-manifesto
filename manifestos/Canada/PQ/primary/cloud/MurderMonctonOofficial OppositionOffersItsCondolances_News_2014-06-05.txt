Quebec - The leader of the official opposition, Stéphane Bédard, and the member for Matane-Matapédia and public security spokesperson, Pascal Bérubé, offer, on behalf of the members of the parliamentary wing of the Parti Québécois, their most sincere condolences to the families and relatives of the three police officers killed Wednesday evening in the performance of their duties.
“We are wholeheartedly with the people of Moncton and the RCMP police, who are facing the tragic loss of three of their colleagues. This sad event reminds us that police officers risk their lives every day to ensure the safety and well-being of the population, ”declared Stéphane Bédard.
“I pay tribute to the three police officers who died and the two injured. Our thoughts are with the people of Moncton and New Brunswick. Of course, we all want the situation to be resolved as quickly as possible, ”added Pascal Bérubé.
