Montreal - The leader of the Parti Québécois, Jean-François Lisée, announces the appointment of Paul St-Pierre Plamondon as special advisor to the leader, renewal, within the Parti Québécois. Its mandate, over the coming months, will consist in particular of consulting the population on the renewal of the Parti Québécois and, at the end of this process, drafting a report and recommendations on the revival of the Parti Québécois. The theme of this consultation campaign will be Dare to rethink the PQ.
“The mandate that I entrusted to Paul embodies in a tangible way the will of the Parti Québécois to open its doors wide in order to start a real rally. The approach of the one who now becomes my special advisor will target the business community, in particular entrepreneurs and other professionals, members of diversity and, more generally, those under 40. As I have made a commitment to do, our political formation must be more in tune with all the voices that make up Quebec today, ”declared Jean-François Lisée.
&quot;My campaign slogan was&quot; Let us give back the PQ to Quebecers, to all Quebecers &quot;, and I have often said that this renewal would depend on the confidence we have in the intelligence and in the participation of people. I am therefore very enthusiastic about this mandate. I am very happy to see that our leader has confidence in me in this process to revive the Parti Québécois and open it up to all Quebeckers. For us, it starts with listening and dialogue in order to reconnect as many people as possible with our party, ”explained Paul St-Pierre Plamondon.
These consultations will be carried out in collaboration with the members of the party and its authorities. They will begin on November 1, 2016 and end on March 31, 2017, to culminate in a report that will be tabled in mid-April 2017. These recommendations will feed the party&#39;s debates in view of its congress in fall 2017 as well as general election in fall 2018.
