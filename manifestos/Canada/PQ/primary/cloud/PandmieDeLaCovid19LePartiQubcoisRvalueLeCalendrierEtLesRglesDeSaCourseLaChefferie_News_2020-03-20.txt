Montreal, March 20, 2020 - The coordination conference of the Parti Québécois made several changes to the way the leadership race works, in order to respect the instructions of the General Directorate of Public Health.
Here are the new dates to keep in mind for this leadership race: ▪ April 30: end of the nomination period and official start of the race▪ Between May 1 and August 20: holding of the debates▪ August 1: deadline to become member or sympathizer in order to be able to vote▪ August 24 to 28: ballot by telephone and Internet▪ August 28: election of the new leader or the new leader of the Parti Québécois
In addition, several changes have been made to the Rules of the Leadership Race, in order to adapt its operation to the context of the pandemic. It should also be noted that on Tuesday March 17, the returning officer, Ms. Agnès Maltais, decided to authorize the transmission by email of handwritten signatures.
“We are sensitive to what the population is going through in the current context. For the good, health and safety of all, we had to make compliance with Public Health directives a priority. Of course, we will remain attentive to developments in the pandemic and will act accordingly, ”said Agnès Maltais.
The MNA for Matane-Matapédia, Pascal Bérubé, will continue to act as parliamentary leader of the Parti Québécois until August 28.
