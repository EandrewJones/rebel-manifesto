Montreal - The Premier of Quebec, Pauline Marois, wishes to express her warm congratulations to Mgr Gérald Cyprien Lacroix, Archbishop of Quebec and Primate of the Canadian Church, on his appointment by Pope Francis to the rank of cardinal.
“This important appointment crowns Bishop Lacroix&#39;s deep commitment to serving the Catholic Church and its faithful, from his ordination until his arrival at the head of the Archdiocese of Quebec. On behalf of the government and all Quebecers, I would like to congratulate Bishop Lacroix and send him our best wishes for success in carrying out his new responsibilities, ”declared the Premier.
