Quebec - As the Games of the Thirtieth Olympiad will open today in London, the Leader of the Official Opposition and Member of Parliament for Charlevoix, Pauline Marois, offered her encouragement to the 66 Quebec athletes who will participate in the competitions during the course. the next two weeks.
“You have been preparing for years. At the cost of countless sacrifices, you have strived for excellence. With the audacity of your youth, you are an example for all Quebecers. For us, you are already champions, ”said Pauline Marois.
The London Olympics will take place from July 27 to August 12, 2012, when Quebec will likely be plunged into an election campaign. “I will certainly have a thought for the athletes. In politics, as in Olympism, a lifetime&#39;s work and four years&#39; preparation will be judged in a very short time. I stand in solidarity with these athletes who will lead an important battle in the coming days, ”she continued.
“One day, when the stadium lights have gone out and the crowd is silent, you will only remember those great moments. I wish they will know how to provide you with motivation that will last a lifetime. We, in any case, you already inspire us. Enjoy these games, live them fully! », Concluded the leader of the official opposition.
