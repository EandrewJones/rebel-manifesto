Quebec - The Leader of the Official Opposition and Member of Parliament for Saint-Jérôme, Pierre Karl Péladeau, announces changes to the parliamentary wing of the Parti Québécois team.
The newly elected member for the riding of Chicoutimi, Mireille Jean, becomes the Parti Québécois spokesperson for innovation and the digital economy. The MNA for René-Lévesque, Martin Ouellet, for his part, obtains the files of the social economy and SMEs. He retains his title of spokesperson for entrepreneurship and exports.
“Mireille is a seasoned businesswoman. She knows that a nation that wants to create wealth must be bold. For 25 years, she has been actively involved in ensuring the prosperity of the Saguenay – Lac-Saint-Jean region. As a spokesperson for innovation and the digital economy, his in-depth knowledge of the high-tech field will be put to good use, ”said Pierre Karl Péladeau.
“Martin is also very sensitive to the issue of regional economic development. He is well aware of the reality on the ground and has always supported local initiatives, which are often better suited to the context of a given region. I am happy to entrust him with new files directly related to his field of expertise, ”added the Leader of the Official Opposition.
