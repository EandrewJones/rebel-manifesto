Quebec - The member for Saint-Jérôme and leader of the official opposition, Pierre Karl Péladeau, announces changes to the team of spokespersons for the Parti Québécois.
The MNA for Joliette, Véronique Hivon, will henceforth be spokesperson for justice and professional laws. It retains its responsibilities for culture, communications and end-of-life care.
The member for Sanguinet, Alain Therrien, becomes spokesperson for energy and natural resources, while retaining his responsibilities in terms of the economy and exports.
The member for Beauharnois, Guy Leclair, will be the spokesperson for pension plans and information resources, while retaining his responsibilities in terms of aboriginal affairs.
Finally, the member for Gaspé, Gaétan Lelièvre, will be spokesperson for the Conseil du trésor, in addition to maintaining his responsibilities in terms of regional development and fisheries.
