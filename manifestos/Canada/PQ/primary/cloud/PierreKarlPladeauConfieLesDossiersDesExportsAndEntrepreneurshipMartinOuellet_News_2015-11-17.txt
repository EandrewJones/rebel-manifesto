Quebec - The leader of the official opposition, Pierre Karl Péladeau, announces that he is entrusting the export and entrepreneurship files to the new MNA for René-Lévesque, Martin Ouellet.
“Today we welcome a new colleague, a dynamic man committed to his community. Martin led a very good electoral campaign, listening to his fellow citizens and serving the development of his region. He will do the same as a Member of Parliament. He has great economic experience, which is why I entrusted him with files related to this area. He thus joins our economic team, dedicated to supporting job creation and the development of our regions, which have been totally abandoned by the Liberal government, ”declared the leader of the official opposition.
“I am very proud to make my official entry into the National Assembly today as part of the Parti Québécois team. I would like to thank Pierre Karl Péladeau for the confidence he has shown in me by entrusting me with the export and entrepreneurship files. The citizens of René-Lévesque can already count on me; my team is in place to support and accompany the people of the riding and the region, ”added Martin Ouellet.
Sylvain Gaudreault becomes responsible for the Saguenay – Lac-Saint-Jean region
In addition, Pierre Karl Péladeau announced that the member for Jonquière, Sylvain Gaudreault, is now responsible for the Saguenay – Lac-Saint-Jean region.
