Quebec - The member for Saint-Jérôme and leader of the official opposition, Pierre Karl Péladeau, wishes to warmly thank Stéphane Bédard for all the years he spent serving the people of Chicoutimi and the sovereignty of Quebec.
“Stéphane Bédard served the citizens of the Chicoutimi riding with dedication and determination. Since 1998, he has held several important parliamentary and government positions. He was notably parliamentary leader of the government and of the official opposition, President of the Conseil du trésor and leader of the official opposition. Stéphane is a colleague appreciated by his peers and respected by all members of Parliament, regardless of their political affiliation. I would like to thank him very sincerely for the very precious help he gave me when I returned to Parliament on May 19, following the choice of activists at the end of the leadership race ”, said Pierre Karl Péladeau.
“I would also like to pay tribute to Stéphane Bédard, the independence activist. He is first and foremost an activist of the Parti Québécois. From an early age, inspired by his father&#39;s action, he worked for the full freedom of the Quebec nation. Close to his world, he is also a tireless defender of the regions of Quebec. On behalf of the entire Parti Québécois team, I would like to thank Stéphane and wish him success in the future, ”concluded the leader of the official opposition.
