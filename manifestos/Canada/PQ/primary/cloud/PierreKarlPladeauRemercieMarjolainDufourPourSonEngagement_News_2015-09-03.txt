Quebec - It is with emotion that the Leader of the Official Opposition, Pierre Karl Péladeau, warmly thanks the MNA for René-Lévesque and Official Opposition Whip, Marjolain Dufour, who is forced to retire from political life to health reasons.
“In my name and that of the Parti Québécois, I would like to salute the exceptional work that Marjolain Dufour has accomplished over the past twelve years. Obviously I would have preferred to keep him with us, but I understand and respect his decision. When the body tells us to slow down, we have to know how to listen to it and review our priorities, ”said Pierre Karl Péladeau.
Dedicated Member of Parliament and respected colleague, Marjolain Dufour has held various positions within the parliamentary wing of the Parti Québécois in the National Assembly, since his election as Member of Parliament for René-Lévesque, on the North Shore, on April 14, 2003 He was notably the spokesperson for land, mining, forestry and work; he has also served as Deputy Official Opposition Whip, Chair of the Government Caucus, Chief Government Whip and, until today, Chief Official Opposition Whip.
“Marjolain&#39;s commitment to his colleagues, the Parti Québécois, his fellow citizens in the riding of René-Lévesque and to Quebec as a whole was flawless. I wish him a well-deserved rest and to take full advantage of the presence of his family and his grandchildren, ”concluded Pierre Karl Péladeau.
