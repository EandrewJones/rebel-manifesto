Stoneham-et-Tewkesbury - The Minister of Education, Recreation and Sport, Mrs. Marie Malavoy, took part today, in Stoneham-et-Tewkesbury, in the inauguration of the second building of the Harfang school. of snow.
“It is at school that many dreams and projects take shape, not only for thousands of children, but also for all of Quebec society. It is therefore essential to offer students quality educational services in a stimulating environment adapted to their learning, as the Government of Quebec has done here in Stoneham-et-Tewkesbury. I wish the school staff and students good hours here in their new spaces, ”said the Minister.
This building, which opened its doors last August, has two classes reserved for preschool education and twelve for primary education. The project was carried out with the allocation of government assistance of $ 6.37 million. Its construction meets a need of the Première-Seigneuries School Board, which foresaw a significant shortage of premises over the next few years, due to the decrease in the number of students per class in elementary school and the growth in enrollment. school in this rapidly developing sector.
“The Quebec government will continue its efforts to meet the significant need for premises in the area. I also announced last spring that a sum of more than $ 17 million was granted to the Commission scolaire des Premières-Seigneuries for the construction of two elementary schools, in Sainte-Brigitte-de-Laval and in Lac. -Beauport. This shows that education is at the heart of our priorities, ”concluded the Minister.
