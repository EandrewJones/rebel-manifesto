Montreal– The Parti Québécois electoral platform has three components: asserting oneself, enriching oneself and helping each other. “We are proud to be Quebeckers and pride is also used to enrich ourselves. Pride is the engine of overtaking. We were told that we could not run a hydroelectric business. We were told that we could not transport electricity from the north to the south. It was impossible, so we did it. We were called water carriers. Today we carry people on our trains and on our planes. The Plan Nord is already an open bar. We no longer have any control over Hydro-Québec, in which we are, however, all shareholders. The ownership of our agricultural land is subject to speculation. How did we get there? I answer you that that can change. It&#39;s up to us to choose, ”said Pauline Marois.
