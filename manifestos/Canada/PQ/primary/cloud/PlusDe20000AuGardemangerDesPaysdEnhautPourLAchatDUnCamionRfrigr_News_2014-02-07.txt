Sainte-Agathe-des-Monts– The Vice-President of the National Assembly and Member of Parliament for Bertrand, Mr. Claude Cousineau, on behalf of the Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Land Occupancy and Minister of Transport, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, announces the allocation of financial assistance of $ 20,520 to the Garde-Manger des Pays-d&#39;en-Haut for the purchase of a refrigerated truck used for the delivery of food.
Created in 1995, the Garde-Manger des Pays-d&#39;en-Haut is a non-profit organization that offers food and clothing support services to underprivileged people in the Regional County Municipality (MRC) of the Pays-d&#39;en-Haut. -High. Each week, some 500 households benefit from its services in the food counters of the seven municipalities and the four community organizations in the territory where it delivers food. However, due to the obsolescence of its current truck, purchased 10 years ago, the organization is forced to acquire a new refrigerated truck in order to continue its mission.
“Providing support services to the most vulnerable people in our society is a constant issue that particularly affects our government. This is why we are proud to support this project, which will strengthen solidarity on the territory of the MRC des Pays-d&#39;en-Haut, ”said MP Cousineau.
Financial assistance comes from the conjunctural development fund of the Ministry of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Land Occupancy.
