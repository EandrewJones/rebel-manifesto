Lac-au-Saumon- The MNA for Matane-Matapédia, Minister responsible for Tourism and Minister responsible for the Bas-Saint-Laurent region, Mr. Pascal Bérubé, on behalf of the Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Occupation of territory and Minister of Transport, Sylvain Gaudreault, announces the allocation of financial assistance of $ 35,450 to the Mont-Climont cross-country ski club in Lac-au-Saumon for the acquisition or adaptation of technical equipment , improvement of cross-country ski trails and renovation of the main building.
Since its founding, the Mont-Climont cross-country ski club has played an important role in the La Matapedia community by being the only organization to offer cross-country ski lessons given by qualified instructors, in addition to offer the possibility of renting cross-country ski equipment for the whole family. The population can therefore learn to practice cross-country skiing and take part in sliding and snowshoeing activities on the trails. During the summer season, this ski center also serves as a day camp for young people from the region. Thus, the acquisition and renovation of the main building are necessary for the organization to be able to maintain its service offering while promoting the growth of its clientele.
“Our government is committed to providing adequate services to Quebeckers. It is therefore with great pride that we award this financial assistance which will make it possible, in particular, to make the main chalet safe and attractive, ”declared Minister Pascal Bérubé.
Financial assistance comes from the Economic Development Fund and the Support Fund for Territories in Difficulty of the Ministry of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Land Occupancy.
