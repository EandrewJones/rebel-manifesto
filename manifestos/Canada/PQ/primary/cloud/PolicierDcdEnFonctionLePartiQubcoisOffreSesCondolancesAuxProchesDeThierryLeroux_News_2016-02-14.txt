Quebec - The leader of the official opposition, Pierre Karl Péladeau, and the Parti Québécois spokesperson for public security, Pascal Bérubé, wish to offer their condolences to the family, relatives and colleagues of police officer Thierry Leroux, died last night during an operation in Lac-Simon, in Abitibi-Témiscamingue.
“All our thoughts are with the family and relatives of Agent Thierry Leroux, who died in the line of duty. This reminds us that members of the police forces everywhere in Quebec risk their lives every day, putting it at the service of their fellow citizens. The loss of one of theirs is always tragic, and we express our solidarity with them, ”said Pierre Karl Péladeau.
“Thierry Leroux was appreciated and involved in his community. I would like to express our gratitude to this young police officer as well as to his colleague present at the scene of the tragedy, just as we thank all the women and men who ensure the safety of Quebecers every day in all regions. of Quebec, ”added Pascal Bérubé.
