Quebec - As part of the Priorité emploi economic policy presented by the Premier, Ms. Pauline Marois, on October 7, the Minister for Tourism, Mr. Pascal Bérubé announces an additional investment of $ 225 million to promote the development of tourism in Quebec and the creation of thousands of jobs.
Intended to develop several priority sectors, including the St.Lawrence enhancement strategy and the winter tourism enhancement strategy, this unprecedented financial effort by the Government of Quebec to support the tourism industry will further develop the many attractions of Quebec in order to promote our destination on the international market.
“In a context of strong international competition, the challenge for our destination is to stand out through the renewal of our tourism offer and the quality of our marketing. To achieve this quickly, we must accelerate investments and focus on targeted priorities aimed at Québec&#39;s competitive advantages, the competitiveness of businesses and the destination&#39;s influence, ”said the Minister.
