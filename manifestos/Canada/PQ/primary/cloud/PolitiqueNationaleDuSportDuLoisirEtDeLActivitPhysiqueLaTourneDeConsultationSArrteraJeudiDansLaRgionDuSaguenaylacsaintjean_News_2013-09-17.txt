Quebec - The Minister of Education, Recreation and Sports, Ms. Marie Malavoy, and her parliamentary assistant and chair of the government caucus, Mr. Sylvain Pagé, announce that the regional consultation tour, led by the latter, is taking place. will stop in the Saguenay-Lac-Saint-Jean region on Thursday, September 19.
“These consultations allow us to meet many partners in the fields of sport, recreation and physical activity throughout Quebec. I am very happy to use their expertise so that together, we can realize our desire to improve the physical condition of Quebecers, to promote the practice of physical, recreational and sports activities as well as to ensure our sports elite the conditions necessary for its development, ”said Mr. Pagé.
The regional consultations follow the launch, on June 10, of the green paper entitled The taste and pleasure of moving: towards a national policy on sport, leisure and physical activity. The Green Paper is essentially a consultation tool which proposes a vision, issues and orientations on which the discussions are based.
“The points of view that will be shared during these consultations will shed valuable light on the development of our future national policy on sport, recreation and physical activity. Let us together set the objective that the policy is the reflection of this society that we want to build, a society in action, which takes itself in hand and which goes to the end of its potential ”, indicated Minister Malavoy.
To consult the green paper, visit www.mels.gouv.qc.ca/livrevert.
