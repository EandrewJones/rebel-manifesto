The parliamentary leader of the Parti Québécois and member for Matane-Matapédia, Pascal Bérubé, as well as the member for Jonquière, Sylvain Gaudreault today presented at a press conference the candidate of the Parti Québécois in the riding of Roberval, Thomas Gaudreault, already a candidate in the general election of October 1 for the Parti Québécois, in view of the by-election of December 10, 2018.
The local executive is preparing for the upcoming campaign alongside teams from other counties in the region. “I invite members and activists of the Parti Québécois from the region to come to Roberval. We are going to work to win, ”said Sylvain Gaudreault.
“I have next to me a young man who will be the youngest member of the National Assembly on December 10, and an heir to Bernard Landry. A young man from Albanel, competent, combative and courageous. We are here to win, and we will give all our efforts. &quot;Continued Pascal Bérubé.
“The important thing is to make sure we keep our hospitals up and running. The same goes for schools, each municipality must have its own to make education more accessible. The same goes for agriculture: we have to keep it local, with people from here for people from here. The people of Roberval want a strong voice that will represent them in the National Assembly and that will defend the independence project, it is with pride that I will do so. »Concluded Thomas Gaudreault.
