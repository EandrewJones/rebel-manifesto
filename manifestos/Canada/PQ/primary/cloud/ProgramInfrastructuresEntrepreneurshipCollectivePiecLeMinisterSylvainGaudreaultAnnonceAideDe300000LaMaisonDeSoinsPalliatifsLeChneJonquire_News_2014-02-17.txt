Jonquière - The Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Land Occupancy, Minister of Transport and Member of Parliament for Jonquière, Sylvain Gaudreault, is pleased to announce the granting of government assistance of $ 300,000 to La Maison Le Chêne for the construction of a residence specializing in palliative care on the territory of the Jonquière Health and Social Services Center (CSSS). This assistance comes from the Collective Entrepreneurship Infrastructure Program (PIEC).
A social economy company committed to people requiring palliative care, La Maison Le Chêne&#39;s mission is to offer end-of-life beneficiaries and their loved ones an environment and a range of diversified services allowing them to experience this stage in the respect, integrity and serenity.
“I am very proud of the assistance granted by our government to the La Maison Le Chêne project, which is a way of recognizing the important contribution of this organization to the community. Thus, the construction of a new residence specializing in palliative care will help provide care and support services to beneficiaries until the end of their life, in dignity. Carrying out this project will enable this establishment to provide itself with adequate spaces for the benefit of people and their families who will have access to a range of diversified services in a warm living environment, ”declared Minister Gaudreault.
