QUébec - The MNA for Mirabel, Ms. Denise Beaudoin, on behalf of the Minister of Public Security, Mr. Stéphane Bergeron, presented financial assistance of $ 13,273.39 to the Municipality of Pointe-Calumet under the Joint Program of civil protection (PCPC) 2011-2012.
“The Municipality of Pointe-Calumet is benefiting from this financial assistance for the project, allowing it to acquire a boat for emergency measures, and thus to deal with possible disasters more efficiently. I make a major stake in the quality of life of our citizens and even more so of their safety, ”declared Ms. Beaudoin.
This project will improve the speed of intervention and evacuation of citizens.
“We are proud to support the municipalities that put in place measures to prevent a disaster from occurring or, where applicable, to facilitate the return to normal life of victims affected by such circumstances. I would like to underline the efforts made by the Municipality of Pointe-Calumet to ensure the safety of the citizens of the region, ”said Minister Bergeron.
Remember that the PCPC is a federal program aimed at municipalities, regional county municipalities, metropolitan communities as well as departments and agencies of the Government of Quebec. This program, under the responsibility of Public Safety Canada, is administered in Quebec by the Ministère de la Sécurité publique. It aims to encourage efforts to improve and increase emergency response capacity through civil protection projects.
