Quebec - The MNA for Rouyn-Noranda-Témiscamingue, Mr. Gilles Chapadeau, on behalf of the Minister of Public Security, Mr. Stéphane Bergeron, submitted financial assistance of $ 15,161.93 to the MRC de Témiscamingue under the Joint Emergency Preparedness Program (JEPP) 2011-2012.
&quot;The MRC de Témiscamingue is benefiting from this financial assistance for the project allowing it to install a generator for the emergency coordination center, and thus to deal with possible disasters more efficiently&quot;, declared M Hat.
This project will allow the emergency measures coordination center to be operational at all times and the municipalities of the MRC to benefit from the facilities in the event of an emergency.
“We are proud to support the municipalities that put in place measures to prevent a disaster from occurring or, where applicable, to facilitate the return to normal life of victims affected by such circumstances. I would like to underline the efforts made by the MRC de Témiscamingue to ensure the safety of the citizens of the region, ”said Minister Bergeron.
Remember that the PCPC is a federal program aimed at municipalities, regional county municipalities, metropolitan communities as well as departments and agencies of the Government of Quebec. This program, under the responsibility of Public Safety Canada, is administered in Quebec by the Ministère de la Sécurité publique. It aims to encourage efforts to improve and increase emergency response capacity through civil protection projects.
