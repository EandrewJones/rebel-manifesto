Quebec - The Minister of Public Security, Mr. Stéphane Bergeron, together with the Minister responsible for the Estrie region, Mr. Réjean Hébert, announces financial assistance of $ 10,314.33 to the City of Valcourt under the Joint Emergency Preparedness Program (JEPP) 2011-2012.
&quot;The City of Valcourt is benefiting from this financial assistance for the project, allowing it to develop a training program, and thus to deal with possible disasters more effectively,&quot; declared Mr. Hébert.
This training will help improve knowledge of civil security and the ability to respond to an emergency situation.
“We are proud to support the municipalities that put in place measures to prevent a disaster from occurring or, where applicable, to facilitate the return to normal life of victims affected by such circumstances. I would like to underline the efforts made by the City of Valcourt to ensure the safety of the citizens of the region, ”said Minister Bergeron.
Remember that the PCPC is a federal program aimed at municipalities, regional county municipalities, metropolitan communities as well as departments and agencies of the Government of Quebec. This program, under the responsibility of Public Safety Canada, is administered in Quebec by the Ministère de la Sécurité publique. It aims to encourage efforts to improve and increase emergency response capacity through civil protection projects.
