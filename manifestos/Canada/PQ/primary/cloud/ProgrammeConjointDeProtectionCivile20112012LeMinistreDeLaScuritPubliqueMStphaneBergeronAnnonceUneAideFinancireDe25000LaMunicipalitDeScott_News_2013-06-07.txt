Quebec - The Minister of Public Security, Mr. Stéphane Bergeron, joins the Minister responsible for the Capitale-Nationale region and the Chaudière-Appalaches region, Ms. Agnès Maltais, to announce financial assistance of 25,000 $ to the Municipality of Scott under the 2011-2012 Joint Emergency Preparedness Program (JEPP).
“The Municipality of Scott is benefiting from this financial assistance for the project allowing it to acquire a vehicle, a boat and equipment for water rescue, and to offer training in this area, in order to deal with possible claims with greater efficiency, ”said Ms. Maltais.
This project will make it possible to cover the entire territory for the safety of personnel, to intervene quickly in situations of rapid flooding of water throughout the year, and also to intervene in the event of accidents or sporting incidents. .
“We are proud to support the municipalities that put in place measures to prevent a disaster from occurring or, where applicable, to facilitate the return to normal life of victims affected by such circumstances. I would like to underline the efforts made by the Municipality of Scott to ensure the safety of the citizens of the region, ”said Minister Bergeron.
Remember that the PCPC is a federal program aimed at municipalities, regional county municipalities, metropolitan communities as well as departments and agencies of the Government of Quebec. This program, under the responsibility of Public Safety Canada, is administered in Quebec by the Ministère de la Sécurité publique. It aims to encourage efforts to improve and increase emergency response capacity through civil protection projects.
