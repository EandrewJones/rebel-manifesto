Quebec - The Minister of Public Security, Mr. Stéphane Bergeron, joins the Minister responsible for the Capitale-Nationale region and the Chaudière-Appalaches region, Ms. Agnès Maltais, to announce financial assistance of 3,317 , $ 72 to the Municipality of Kinnear&#39;s Mills under the 2011-2012 Joint Emergency Preparedness Program (JEPP).
&quot;The Municipality of Kinnear&#39;s Mills is benefiting from this financial assistance for the project, allowing it to set up a municipal coordination center, and thus to deal with possible disasters more effectively,&quot; said Ms. Maltais.
This project will improve the municipality&#39;s response capacity in terms of civil security.
“We are proud to support the municipalities that put in place measures to prevent a disaster from occurring or, where applicable, to facilitate the return to normal life of victims affected by such circumstances. I would like to underline the efforts made by the Municipality of Kinnear&#39;s Mills to ensure the safety of the citizens of the region, ”said Minister Bergeron.
Remember that the PCPC is a federal program aimed at municipalities, regional county municipalities, metropolitan communities as well as departments and agencies of the Government of Quebec. This program, under the responsibility of Public Safety Canada, is administered in Quebec by the Ministère de la Sécurité publique. It aims to encourage efforts to improve and increase emergency response capacity through civil protection projects.
