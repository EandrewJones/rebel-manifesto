Quebec - The Minister of Public Security, Mr. Stéphane Bergeron, joins the Minister responsible for the Capitale-Nationale region and the Chaudière-Appalaches region, Ms. Agnès Maltais, to announce financial assistance of 6,544 , $ 82 to the Municipality of Lac-Beauport under the Joint Emergency Preparedness Program (PCPC) 2011-2012.
&quot;The Municipality of Lac-Beauport is benefiting from this financial assistance for the project allowing it to acquire water rescue equipment, and thus to deal with possible disasters more efficiently&quot;, declared Ms. Maltais. .
This project will greatly improve the quality of most interventions related to civil security, while ensuring the safety of those involved.
“We are proud to support the municipalities that put in place measures to prevent a disaster from occurring or, where applicable, to facilitate the return to normal life of victims affected by such circumstances. I would like to underline the efforts made by the Municipality of Lac-Beauport to ensure the safety of the citizens of the region, ”said Minister Bergeron.
Remember that the PCPC is a federal program aimed at municipalities, regional county municipalities, metropolitan communities as well as departments and agencies of the Government of Quebec. This program, under the responsibility of Public Safety Canada, is administered in Quebec by the Ministère de la Sécurité publique. It aims to encourage efforts to improve and increase emergency response capacity through civil protection projects.
