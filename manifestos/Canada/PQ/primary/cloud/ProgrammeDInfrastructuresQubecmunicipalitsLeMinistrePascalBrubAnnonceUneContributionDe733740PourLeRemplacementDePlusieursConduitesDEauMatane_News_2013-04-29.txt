Matane– The Minister for Tourism, Minister responsible for the Bas-Saint-Laurent region and Member of Parliament for Matane-Matapédia, Mr. Pascal Bérubé, is pleased to announce, on behalf of the Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Occupation of the territory, Minister of Transport and Member of Parliament for Jonquière, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, that the City of Matane will benefit from a maximum government financial assistance of $ 733,740 for the replacement of several water pipes in the city. This assistance comes from the Québec-Municipalities Infrastructure Program (PIQM).
More specifically, the project targets work to replace drinking water, wastewater and stormwater pipes in several segments of Saint-Jérôme Avenue. There will also be a sewer separation under some of these segments. At the end of this work, water pipes on nearly half a kilometer of streets will be renewed in Matane.
“I am very happy with the financial support granted today to the City of Matane. Our government recognizes the need to renew and replace drinking water, wastewater and storm sewer infrastructure in communities across Quebec. This is why, in partnership with the municipalities, we want to ensure that citizens benefit from sustainable and quality public services for current and future generations, ”said Minister Pascal Bérubé.
Completion of this project requires maximum government financial assistance of $ 733,740, under section 1.5 of the PIQM. With a total envelope of $ 462 million, this new component of the PIQM program not only ensures the sustainability of infrastructure, but also improves the quality of life of the population and creates many jobs overall. regions of Quebec.
This government assistance is part of the Quebec Infrastructure Plan.
