Rouyn-Noranda– The MNA for Rouyn-Noranda-Témiscamingue, Mr. Gilles Chapadeau, on behalf of the Deputy Premier, Minister of Agriculture, Fisheries and Food and Minister responsible for Abitibi-Témiscamingue, Mr. François Gendron is pleased to announce financial support of $ 997,000 to 49 companies in Abitibi-Témiscamingue.
Financial assistance, granted under the Abitibi-Témiscamingue and Nord-du-Québec Land Drainage Support Program, will increase the areas drained underground and, consequently, improve land productivity. cultivated. In the long term, it is the dynamism of agriculture in this territory that will be enhanced.
“It is always a pleasure for me to support businesses in my region. Last April, we launched a tailor-made program to support agricultural producers in the region in their efforts to harness the full potential of their land. I am happy that they answered the call and that projects are undertaken thanks to this program ”, underlined the Minister.
“I am proud that nearly 50 agricultural businesses in Abitibi-Témiscamingue have benefited from the Program. Such enthusiasm means that our region has not lost its dynamism and that agricultural producers feel stimulated by the policies and measures that we have put in place specifically for them, ”added Mr. Chapadeau.
“This program and the initiatives it sparks off are perfectly in line with the Food Sovereignty Policy tabled last May. Indeed, the projects implemented greatly promote the accessibility of agricultural land and contribute to a dynamic and optimal use of the territory and the wealth of Abitibi-Témiscamingue and Nord-du-Québec ”, added Mr. François Gendron.
