Témiscamingue - The Minister of Finance and the Economy, Mr. Nicolas Marceau, accompanied by the Deputy Prime Minister, Minister of Agriculture, Fisheries and Food and Minister responsible for the Abitibi-Témiscamingue region , Mr. François Gendron, and the Member of Parliament for Rouyn-Noranda-Témiscamingue, Mr. Gilles Chapadeau, today announced the allocation of a loan of $ 17.8 million to the Tembec company for the continuation of the work modernization of the Témiscaming plant.
This project, valued at $ 355 million, requires a modernization of the plant&#39;s facilities in order to increase the production capacity and the quality of the specialty cellulose produced and thus increase the company&#39;s profitability. The plant&#39;s modernization project provides for the installation of high-performance equipment dedicated to the manufacture of cellulose.
