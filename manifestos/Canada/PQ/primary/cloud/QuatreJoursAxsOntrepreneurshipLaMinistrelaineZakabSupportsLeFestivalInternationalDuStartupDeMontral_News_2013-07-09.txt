Montreal - The Minister for Industrial Policy and the Banque de développement économique du Québec, Ms. Élaine Zakaïb, announced the allocation of financial assistance of $ 10,000 for the holding of the third Montreal International Startup Festival, which will take place from July 10 to 13.
“The Montreal International Startup Festival is the ideal opportunity to increase Quebec entrepreneurship by promoting networking and sharing of expertise. For our entrepreneurs, it is a privileged moment to create fruitful partnerships with investors and establish an effective network. This festival will bring together more than 1,000 entrepreneurs and investors from both North America and Europe, ”declared Élaine Zakaïb. “Ensuring the next generation of entrepreneurship is one of our government&#39;s major concerns and that is why we are proud to be associated with this event. &quot;
The Montreal International Startup Festival is an annual event that brings together entrepreneurs, investors and key players in the technology sector. Exhibitions, conferences and workshops dedicated to entrepreneurship and information and communications technology start-ups take place there. Start-ups are young start-ups with high growth potential.
&quot;We are pleased with the support from the Quebec government, which will provide our companies with significant investment opportunities,&quot; said the founder of the Montreal International Startup Festival, Mr. Philippe Telio. “Thanks to this festival, we are promoting the growth of dynamic and promising companies, in Quebec and abroad. Our entrepreneurs must be prominent internationally. They must draw inspiration from and network with other communities around the world in order to ensure their prosperity. &quot;
