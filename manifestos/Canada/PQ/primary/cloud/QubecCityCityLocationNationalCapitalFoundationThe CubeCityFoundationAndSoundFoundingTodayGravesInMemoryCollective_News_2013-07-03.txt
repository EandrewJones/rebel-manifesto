Quebec - On the occasion of the anniversary of the founding of the national capital, the Minister of Culture and Communications, Maka Kotto, proceeded to the designation of Samuel de Champlain as a historical person, under the Act respecting cultural heritage, which entered into force on October 19. The Minister also designated the founding of Quebec City as a historic event.
“These gestures that we are taking today are remarkable, because they allow us to highlight the first signs of a French presence in America. This highly symbolic exercise aims in particular to make our fellow citizens aware of this major aspect of our national history. This recognition is all the more relevant as it reminds us of an important part of the history of Quebec for the generations of today and those of tomorrow, ”declared Minister Kotto.
