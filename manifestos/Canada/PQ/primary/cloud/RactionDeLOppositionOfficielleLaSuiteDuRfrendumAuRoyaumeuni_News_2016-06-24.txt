Quebec - The Leader of the Official Opposition and Member of Parliament for Jonquière, Sylvain Gaudreault, and the Member of Parliament for Hochelaga-Maisonneuve and Parti Québécois spokesperson for international relations, Carole Poirier, reacted to the result of the referendum which took place. is held in the UK this Thursday.
“Yesterday, as a sovereign state, the United Kingdom spoke out on the future of its relationship with the European Union. We welcome this democratic exercise in which the British people have embarked. The Parti Québécois takes note of the result of the vote of the people, who have chosen to leave the European Union. It is obvious that we will continue to follow closely the debates which will take place and the decisions which will be taken in the near future, in the United Kingdom and in Europe, following this referendum ”, declared the Leader of the Official Opposition and the Member of Parliament. from Hochelaga-Maisonneuve.
