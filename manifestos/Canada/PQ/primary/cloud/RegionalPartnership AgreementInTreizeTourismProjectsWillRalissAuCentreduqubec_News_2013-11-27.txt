Quebec - The Minister for Tourism, Mr. Pascal Bérubé, in partnership with the president of the management committee of the Regional Tourism Partnership Agreement (EPRT) of Center-du-Quebec, Mr. Yves Zahra, is proud to unveil the thirteen projects that will receive financial assistance totaling $ 485,360 under this agreement. The projects selected will generate tourism investments of nearly $ 12 million in Center-du-Québec.
“Thanks to the concerted action of all the partners linked to the agreement, the projects supported will make the region shine and enrich it, both in terms of the diversity and the quality of its tourist offer. The Center-du-Québec stands out for its dynamism and its entrepreneurial rate among the highest in Québec. I therefore congratulate the promoters who, through their work and the renewal of their product, contribute to the economic development of their region, ”said the Minister for Tourism, Pascal Bérubé.
“Center-du-Québec is characterized by typical agricultural landscapes, distinctive local products, thriving business tourism and numerous festivals and events. The projects supported through the agreement will promote the maintenance and creation of several jobs and, I am convinced, will make the region more competitive on the tourist scene in Quebec, ”said the minister responsible for the region. , Mr. Yves-François Blanchet.
“This agreement has aroused the interest of new promoters for the implementation of different projects. These help to shape the diversification of the tourist offer in the Center. We are happy to see the promoters come to us in order to bet on an active and strong industry ”, indicated the chairman of the Management Committee of the Center-du-Québec EPRT and General Manager of Tourisme Center-du-Québec. Quebec, Mr. Yves Zahra.
