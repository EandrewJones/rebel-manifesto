Saguenay– The Minister of Health and Social Services and Minister responsible for Seniors, Réjean Hébert, and the Minister responsible for the Saguenay-Lac-Saint-Jean region, Stéphane Bédard, are pleased to announce new annual budgets of 3 $ 879,800 to improve home care and services for seniors in the region. To this amount is added an injection of additional appropriations of $ 2,181,400, granted for the development of services in the territory and thus better meet the needs of the population.
“The additional funding announced today is one more step towards improving home care. Our seniors want to stay at home as long as possible, and we are taking the necessary measures to make this wish a reality, as confirmed by our autonomy insurance project unveiled recently. These sums will therefore make it possible to serve more seniors, to help even more judiciously those who already receive services and to better support families, ”said Minister Hébert.
