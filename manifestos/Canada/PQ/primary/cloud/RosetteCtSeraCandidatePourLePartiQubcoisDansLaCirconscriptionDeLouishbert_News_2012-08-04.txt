Quebec - The leader of the Parti Québécois, Pauline Marois, is pleased to announce the candidacy of Rosette Côté in the riding of Louis-Hébert. “President of the Pay Equity Commission, member of the Bélanger-Campeau commission and first vice-president of the CEQ, Rosette&#39;s political, professional and union career is extraordinary. She is an exceptional, progressive and committed woman, ”said Pauline Marois.
During her career, Rosette Côté has actively participated in the creation of the Pay Equity Act and the regulation of its application for the Quebec public service. She also worked on the reform of social assistance and the establishment of the network of childcare services.
“The Parti Québécois presents an exceptional team of women and men who have absolutely admirable public service experience. I am honored to be part of this extraordinary team which bears witness to the power of attraction and leadership of Pauline Marois, the most competent person to lead Quebec, ”said Rosette Côté.
&quot;Now, it is our task to make our program known and to inspire Quebecers to choose us,&quot; concluded Rosette Côté.
Remember that the Parti Québécois is the largest political formation in Quebec with its 90,000 members.
