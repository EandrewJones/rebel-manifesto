Mont-Saint-Hilaire - The Minister for Industrial Policy and the Quebec Economic Development Bank, Ms. Élaine Zakaïb, wished to mark the 5th anniversary of the Angels Quebec network.
“Thanks to the Anges Québec network, we are seeing innovative and wealth-creating projects come to fruition for all of Quebec,” said Élaine Zakaïb. “To ensure our economic growth, we must rely more on private investment and the capacity to innovate of our companies. By supporting investors and encouraging the start-up and growth of young local businesses, the Anges Québec network is showing that it shares our vision for the future of a flourishing Quebec. &quot;
Created in 2008, Anges Québec, a network made up of more than 130 active members, targets, finances and supports innovative companies with high potential. Since its creation, Anges Québec has made 27 investments totaling $ 12.3 million. Its mission is to facilitate financing by enabling its members, experienced entrepreneurs who are leaders in their field, to make profitable investments and generate profitable business opportunities.
&quot;With Anges Québec, our companies benefit from an important forum to promote their projects for the future to a group of investors from all regions of Quebec,&quot; said the President and CEO of Anges Québec, Mr. François Gilbert. “We thank the Government of Quebec for supporting our mission to promote and promote the private investment and entrepreneurship sectors in Quebec. &quot;
