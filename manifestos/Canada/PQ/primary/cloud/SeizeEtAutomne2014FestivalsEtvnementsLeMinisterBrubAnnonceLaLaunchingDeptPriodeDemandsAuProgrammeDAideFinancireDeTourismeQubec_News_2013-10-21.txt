Quebec - The Minister for Tourism, Mr. Pascal Bérubé, announces that the registration period for the financial assistance program for festivals and tourist events for the summer / fall 2014 period is now open. From today until November 29, promoters whose events will be held between May 1 and October 31, 2014 will be able to submit a request via the Tourisme Québec website.
Tourisme Québec&#39;s financial assistance program for festivals and tourist events supports events that demonstrate significant tourism performance. These events generate significant spinoffs throughout Quebec and allow us to enrich and diversify our tourism offer.
Business or organization representatives who wish to apply must consult the eligibility criteria, register on the Tourisme Québec institutional website and provide the required documents by November 29, 2013.
