Quebec– The MP for Jonquière and leader of the official opposition, Sylvain Gaudreault, as well as his colleague MP for Hochelaga-Maisonneuve and Parti Québécois spokesperson for international relations, Carole Poirier, learned with sadness of the natural disaster which took place in Italy today.
“A magnitude 6.2 earthquake struck central Italy on Wednesday at 3:36 am local time. Dozens of aftershocks followed. At least 120 people have lost their lives, according to a provisional toll. Many more are missing. This is terrible news, and our thoughts are with the victims and their families, ”said Sylvain Gaudreault.
“All our solidarity goes to the Italian people. We are also at the side of the many Quebecers of Italian origin, as well as those who have relatives or family in this region of the world. We are wholeheartedly with them and remain convinced that the Italian people will recover from this ordeal with strength and dignity, ”added Carole Poirier.
