Quebec - The member for Chicoutimi and leader of the official opposition, Stéphane Bédard, announces changes to the team of spokespersons for the parliamentary wing of the Parti Québécois.
The MNA for Bonaventure, Sylvain Roy, becomes spokesperson for forests, wildlife and parks.
Beauharnois MP Guy Leclair becomes labor spokesperson; he retains his responsibilities as spokesperson for aboriginal affairs.
The member for Sanguinet, Alain Therrien, becomes spokesperson for information resources; he retains his responsibilities as spokesperson for the Conseil du trésor.
