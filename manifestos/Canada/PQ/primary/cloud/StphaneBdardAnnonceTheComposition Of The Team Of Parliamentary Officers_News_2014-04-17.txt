Quebec - On the occasion of the caucus of the parliamentary wing of the Parti Québécois, the new interim leader of the official opposition and member for Chicoutimi, Stéphane Bédard, announced the team of parliamentary officers of the official opposition.
“The parliamentary team of the Parti Québécois will be able to take up the task of carrying out constructive and rigorous work, in the interest of Quebecers. Through her experience and her strength, she will know how to act and work to ensure her essential and democratic role in the National Assembly, ”concluded Mr. Bédard.
