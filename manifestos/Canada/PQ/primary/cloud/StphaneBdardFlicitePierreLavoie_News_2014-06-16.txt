Quebec - The member for Chicoutimi and leader of the official opposition, Stéphane Bédard, warmly congratulates Pierre Lavoie for the 6th edition of the 1000 km of the Grand défi Pierre Lavoie, as well as all the cyclists who took part.
“I congratulate Pierre Lavoie and thank him for his exceptional contribution to the health of our young people. He is proof that it is possible for a citizen to change the world when he shows leadership and tenacity. May the movement he started harvest ever more success and may he continue to make us move! », Indicated the leader of the official opposition.
Stéphane Bédard also congratulated the deputies, all parties combined, who cycled 1000 km in 60 hours. The team of Sylvain Pagé (Labelle), Sylvain Gaudreault (Jonquière), François Bonnardel (Granby), Sébastien Schneeberger (Drummond – Bois-Francs) and Stéphane Billette (Huntingdon) rose to the challenge with flying colors, and this, despite the difficult weather of the last few days.
The two members of the Parti Québécois participating in the challenge for the team of the National Assembly of Quebec, Sylvain Gaudreault and Sylvain Pagé, raised more than $ 5,000 from their colleagues from the Parti Québécois. This amount will be donated to two primary schools, namely the International Mosaic School, located in Jonquière, and the Friendship School, located in Notre-Dame-du-Laus, in the district of Labelle.
“This money will be used to finance projects promoting healthy lifestyles in these schools, to which sport and physical activity greatly contribute. We are happy to participate in this great initiative which encourages our young people to move, ”concluded Stéphane Bédard.
