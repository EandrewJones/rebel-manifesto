Québec, July 2, 2014 - The MNA for Chicoutimi and Leader of the Official Opposition, Stéphane Bédard, confirms that the MNA for Jonquière, Sylvain Gaudreault, was the victim, yesterday, of a bicycle accident while riding on Chemin Saint-Damien, in his riding, in Saguenay.
“On behalf of all my colleagues on the parliamentary wing, I wish Sylvain Gaudreault a speedy recovery. We hope to see him return to his activities quickly, although he will have to take the time to regain his strength, ”said Stéphane Bédard.
The MP is currently hospitalized at the Chicoutimi Hospital, where he was operated on last night for a hip fracture. No date of leave is planned at the moment. He will devote his energies to his convalescence and will therefore not give any interviews.
