Montreal - The Minister of Education, Recreation and Sports, Ms. Marie Malavoy, accompanied by the Member of Parliament for Hochelaga-Maisonneuve, Ms. Carole Poirier, today announced the allocation of financial assistance of $ 100,000 to the organization L&#39;Avenue inc.
L&#39;Avenue inc. is a non-profit organization dedicated to the democratization of information technologies among community groups and underprivileged populations of Montreal. Ministerial assistance is paid to him under the Support for education partners program. It will allow the organization to pursue its educational mission.
“These days, it is absolutely essential to acquire basic computer skills to be able to function well in society. The work carried out by L&#39;Avenue is similar to that of literacy organizations, except that it is digitally focused. Learning to use the computer and to browse the Internet is an important guarantee of independence. By granting this assistance, the Ministère de l&#39;Éducation, du Loisir et du Sport thus recognizes the quality and relevance of the services offered by the organization, particularly in terms of integration into the labor market, ”declared Minister Malavoy. .
“An organization like L&#39;Avenue inc. is a major asset when it comes to training to promote social reintegration. With this announcement, our government is demonstrating the importance of local education services. The riding of Hochelaga-Maisonneuve needs organizations like this one. My support is theirs, ”concluded Ms. Poirier.
