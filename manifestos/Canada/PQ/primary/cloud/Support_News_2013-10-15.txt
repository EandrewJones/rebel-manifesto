Quebec - The Minister of Sustainable Development, Environment, Wildlife and Parks, Mr. Yves-François Blanchet, makes public the 35 projects that will be put forward by 32 wildlife organizations by the end of the year. 2013-2014 financial year. Thus, grants totaling $ 250,000 were granted by the Ministry of Sustainable Development, Environment, Wildlife and Parks, as part of the Relève et enhancement component - regional envelope of Reinvestment in the field of wildlife.
“The support we are offering for the completion of these 35 projects clearly underscores our government&#39;s commitment to Quebec&#39;s wildlife community. By reinvesting public funds from the sale of hunting, fishing and trapping licenses, our government intends to strengthen the development of sustainable wildlife initiatives and activities, with the aim of making them an important driver of the dynamism of communities. regions, ”said Minister Blanchet.
The projects selected (see appendix) are proposed by 32 organizations working to develop hunting, fishing and trapping activities in 15 regions of Quebec.
“These projects relate mainly to the renewal of products and clientele of wildlife activities. They are particularly aimed at the transmission of part of our heritage to future generations and thus contribute to the perpetuation of Quebec culture while maintaining the economic spinoffs linked to these wildlife activities, ”concluded the Minister.
