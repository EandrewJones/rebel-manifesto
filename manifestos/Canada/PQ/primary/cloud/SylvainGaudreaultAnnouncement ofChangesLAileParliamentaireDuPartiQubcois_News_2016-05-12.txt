Quebec– The member for Jonquière and leader of the official opposition, Sylvain Gaudreault, announces changes in the parliamentary team of the Parti Québécois.
“The members of the Parti Québécois will continue their work, in the National Assembly and in the field, to defend the interests and promote the aspirations of Quebeckers. Our team is diverse and competent; it will work with rigor to continue to reflect the concerns of the population towards this government, in all fields and in all regions of Quebec, ”declared Sylvain Gaudreault.
