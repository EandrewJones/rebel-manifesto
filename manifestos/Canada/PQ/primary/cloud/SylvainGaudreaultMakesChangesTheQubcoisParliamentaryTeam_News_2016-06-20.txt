Quebec - The Leader of the Official Opposition and Member of Parliament for Jonquière, Sylvain Gaudreault, is pleased to announce that the Member for Rousseau and the Parti Québécois Finance and Revenue Critic, Nicolas Marceau, has been appointed House Leader of the official opposition. The member for Taschereau, Agnès Maltais, for her part, is entrusted with new files.
“Nicolas is a seasoned MP who is respected by all of his colleagues. He joins our team of parliamentary officers and will help coordinate the work of the members of the Parti Québécois in the National Assembly. We will be able to count on his experience and rigor in the day-to-day management of our parliamentary work, an additional asset for the official opposition team, ”declared Sylvain Gaudreault.
The leader of the official opposition also indicated that the Member of Parliament for Taschereau, Agnès Maltais, was entrusted with the files concerning ethics, integrity, the reform of democratic institutions, the electoral law and access to information. , in addition to remaining spokesperson for the Parti Québécois in matters of secularism, and Member of Parliament responsible for the Capitale-Nationale region. &quot;Agnès is a rigorous parliamentarian, an essential quality for taking charge of these files and confronting the Liberal government on important questions of ethics and integrity,&quot; he added.
“We are determined to build on the momentum of the last session, during which we not only highlighted the flaws of this government, but also proposed important bills for Quebec. We have worked with all the rigor that has always characterized the Parti Québécois, and we will continue to do so, as a team, ”concluded Sylvain Gaudreault.
