Quebec– The MNA for Jonquière and Leader of the Official Opposition, Sylvain Gaudreault, is pleased to announce that the MNA for Verchères, Stéphane Bergeron, will now serve as Chief Whip of the Official Opposition. This appointment follows the decision of the Member of Parliament for Rimouski, Harold LeBel, to step down from this post.
“On behalf of my colleagues, I would like to thank Harold LeBel for his efforts and commitment to our entire parliamentary team. He will of course continue his work as a parliamentarian and as a representative of the people of Rimouski, ”declared the leader of the official opposition.
“I am also very happy to see Stéphane Bergeron joining our team of officers; he is an experienced parliamentarian, seasoned and concerned about the rigor and efficiency of the official opposition. He has already exercised many parliamentary functions, including Chief Whip in the House of Commons and Deputy Leader of our team here in the National Assembly, ”continued Sylvain Gaudreault.
Note in closing that Stéphane Bergeron retains his functions as spokesperson for the Parti Québécois in matters of Quebec-Canada relations and the Francophonie in America, and responsible for the Outaouais region.
