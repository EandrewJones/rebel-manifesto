Quebec - The Leader of the Official Opposition and Member of Parliament for Jonquière, Sylvain Gaudreault, hailed the contribution to the political and public life of Quebec by the Member of Parliament for Marie-Victorin, Bernard Drainville, who announced this morning that he was leaving its functions.
“Bernard served with dedication and passion the electors of the riding of Marie-Victorin. As an activist, member of Parliament, government minister of the Parti Québécois and parliamentary leader of the official opposition, he showed unparalleled ardor and combativeness. He was a colleague appreciated by all. On behalf of the Parti Québécois team, thank you, Bernard, and good luck in the future! », Declared Sylvain Gaudreault.
The leader of the official opposition recalls that the source of Bernard Drainville&#39;s political commitment is to make Quebec an independent country. &quot;Parti Québécois activists will always remember his contribution to the advancement of this cause and his enthusiasm to promote it to as many Quebeckers as possible,&quot; he added.
Sylvain Gaudreault also wished to recall the contribution of Bernard Drainville as Minister. “He contributed to the reorganization of political mores, by tabling important laws concerning the financing of parties, fixed-date elections, voting on campus. These are essential laws for the exercise of a healthy democracy. Bernard also supported the bill abolishing severance pay. All parliamentarians and our society owe him a lot, ”he concluded.
