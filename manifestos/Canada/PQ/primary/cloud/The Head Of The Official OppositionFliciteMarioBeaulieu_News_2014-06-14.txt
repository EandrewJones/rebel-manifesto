Quebec– The member for Chicoutimi and leader of the official opposition, Stéphane Bédard, congratulates Mario Beaulieu on his election as leader of the Bloc Québécois.
“On behalf of the entire parliamentary wing of the Parti Québécois, I offer my most sincere congratulations to Mario Beaulieu on his election. When it comes to defending Quebec&#39;s interests in Ottawa, he knows he can count on our support. I wish him the best of luck in his mandate leading up to the 2015 federal election.
Congratulations also to the MNA for Richmond – Arthabaska, André Bellavance, for the wonderful campaign he waged, ”declared Stéphane Bédard.
