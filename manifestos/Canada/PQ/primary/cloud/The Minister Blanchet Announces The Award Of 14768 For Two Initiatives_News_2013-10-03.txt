Quebec - While in Rimouski, the Minister of Sustainable Development, Environment, Wildlife and Parks, Mr. Yves-François Blanchet, announced the projects that have been selected for the Bas-Saint-Laurent region in the under the Recovery and enhancement component - regional envelope, of the Reinvestment in the field of wildlife.
&quot;Through this reinvestment of public funds, the government intends to strengthen the implementation of sustainable wildlife initiatives and activities to make them one of the driving forces behind the dynamism of the regions,&quot; said Minister Blanchet.
