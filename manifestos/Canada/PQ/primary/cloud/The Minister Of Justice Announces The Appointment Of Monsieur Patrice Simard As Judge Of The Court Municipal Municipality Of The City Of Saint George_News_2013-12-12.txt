Quebec - The Minister of Justice of Quebec, Mr. Bertrand St-Arnaud, announces the appointment of Mr. Patrice Simard as judge of the common municipal court of the City of Saint-Georges.
Mr. Patrice Simard holds a bachelor&#39;s degree in law from Laval University. Since his admission to the Bar in 1994, he has practiced in private practice in various fields including municipal law and criminal law. Since October 10, 2013, he has also been a judge of the common municipal court of the MRC de La Côte-de-Beaupré.
