Laval– The Minister of Families and Minister responsible for the Laval region, Ms. Nicole Léger, on behalf of the Minister of Transport and Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Land Occupancy, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, is pleased to confirm the financial participation of the Ministère des Transports for the construction of noise barriers in Laval.
This project involves installing three noise barriers totaling 2.1 km along Autoroute 440 westbound, between Autoroute 19 and rue de Lublin, in the Vimont district of Laval. This project is funded in accordance with the Highway Noise Policy of the Ministère des Transports. The City of Laval will participate in this project up to 50% of the costs and, as provided for in the agreement, it will act as project manager.
“By participating in the financing of these noise barriers, the Government of Quebec is showing the citizens of Laval that their demand has been heard. We work daily on projects that meet the needs of the region, and this is the response to a demand dating back more than a decade, ”said Ms. Léger.
&quot;These noise barriers will undoubtedly improve the tranquility of Laval residents who live on the outskirts of Highway 440,&quot; said the member for Laval-des-Rapides, Mr. Léo Bureau-Blouin. .
