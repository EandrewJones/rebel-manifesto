Quebec - The Deputy Premier, Minister of Agriculture, Fisheries and Food and Minister responsible for the Abitibi-Témiscamingue region, Mr. François Gendron, wishes to extend his condolences to the family and loved ones of Mr. Réjean Comeau, a pillar of the agricultural sector of Abitibi-Témiscamingue, who passed away on January 22.
“Réjean Comeau has undoubtedly left its mark on the agricultural world of our region through his dynamism and his great ability to rally the strengths of the sector around the major issues we were facing. I know that he was greatly appreciated by all those who had the privilege of working with him over the course of his 30 years of involvement in agricultural unionism, ”said the Minister.
President of the Federation of the UPA of Abitibi-Témiscamingue from 2008 to 2011, Mr. Réjean Comeau also chaired the Union de base of the UPA of Macamic from 1999 to 2008.
“To his family and loved ones, I offer my most sincere condolences,” added François Gendron.
