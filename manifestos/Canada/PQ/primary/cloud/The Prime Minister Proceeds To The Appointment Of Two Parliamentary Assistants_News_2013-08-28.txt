Quebec - The Premier of Quebec, Pauline Marois, announces the appointment of two deputies as parliamentary assistants within the Ministry of Sustainable Development, Environment, Wildlife and Parks.
Scott McKay, Member of Parliament for Repentigny, is appointed Parliamentary Assistant to the Minister for the national water policy component. Mr. McKay was previously responsible for the wildlife and parks component of the same department.
Sylvain Roy, Member of Parliament for Bonaventure, becomes parliamentary assistant to the Minister for the wildlife and parks component.
“I am happy to make this announcement, MM. McKay and Roy are recognized for their commitment and for the high quality of their work, ”said the Premier.
