Quebec - The Minister for Tourism, Mr. Pascal Bérubé, is pleased to announce the allocation of financial assistance of $ 17,500 to support the holding of the Quebec City Jazz Festival, which will take place from October 15 to 27. .
This year, the Quebec City Jazz Festival is celebrating its 7th edition with a rich program that features great musicians from Quebec and Canada, but also from abroad. The Festival helps people appreciate jazz and the talented musicians who devote themselves to it. It aims to create an unmissable event that will make Quebec City a recognized jazz destination in North America.
“The Quebec City Jazz Festival will undoubtedly appeal to music lovers, whether they are from the region or visiting. I am happy to support this musical event which contributes to making the reputation of Quebec here and abroad. I would like to underline the work of the organizing committee, the artists and the volunteers who, through their efforts, ensure the success of this great event, ”said Minister Bérubé.
The financial assistance granted by Tourisme Québec comes from the Tourism Industry Development Program - financial assistance for festivals and tourist events.
