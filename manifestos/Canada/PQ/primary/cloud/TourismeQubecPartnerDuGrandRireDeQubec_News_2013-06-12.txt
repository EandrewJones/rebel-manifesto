Québec - The Minister for Tourism, Mr. Pascal Bérubé is pleased to announce financial assistance of $ 350,000 for the Grand Rire de Québec, which will take place from June 12 to 23, 2013.
Each year, the Grand Rire de Québec is a highly anticipated event. By offering an always very attractive program, this event mobilizes the crowds who have access to several quality shows. During the 12 days of festivities, humor lovers will be delighted, having access to a multitude of events taking place all over the heart of Quebec City.
“Over the years, the Grand Rire de Québec has become an attractive event on the Québec tourist scene. The performances of internationally renowned artists and those of the next generation have allowed Le Grand rire to stand out in the tourist offer in Quebec. I am proud to support this major event, which illustrates the festive and dynamic nature of our destination, ”declared Minister Bérubé.
The financial assistance granted by Tourisme Québec comes from the Tourism Industry Development Program - financial assistance for festivals and tourist events.
By funding festivals and events, Tourisme Québec supports the implementation of initiatives that contribute to the economic and tourism development of the regions of Quebec. It thus promotes the renewal of the tourist offer with a view to continuous and sustainable improvement.
