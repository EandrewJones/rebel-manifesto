Montreal - The Minister for Tourism, Mr. Pascal Bérubé is pleased to announce financial assistance of $ 425,000 for the International des Fires Loto-Québec, which begins today and will continue until August 3, 2013.
Once again this year, the best fireworks in the world will compete in originality and creativity to win this competition. During nine exceptional evenings, the La Ronde site becomes the scene of unique pyromusical shows by competing teams from Australia, Spain, Hong Kong (China), England, Croatia, the United States. , Italy and Canada. For this 29th edition, the International des Fires Loto-Québec innovates by offering daring shows such as The Magic of Cinema, The Power of Rock, Artificial Intelligence as well as a tribute to U2.
“The International des Fires Loto-Québec is an example of an event that allows Montreal to stand out among the most festive and dynamic destinations. I am proud to support this organization, which has been able to renew itself over the years and thus become a key player in the Quebec festival circuit, ”said Minister Bérubé.
The financial assistance granted by Tourisme Québec comes from the Tourism Industry Development Program - financial assistance for festivals and tourist events.
By funding festivals and events, Tourisme Québec supports the implementation of initiatives that contribute to the economic and tourism development of the regions of Quebec. It thus promotes the renewal of the tourist offer with a view to continuous and sustainable improvement.
