Saint-Alexis-des-Monts– The Minister for Tourism, Mr. Pascal Bérubé, announces the allocation of financial assistance of $ 10,000 to the Speckled Trout Festival of Saint-Alexis-des-Monts, which is held from June 22 to July 1.
For this new edition, the pleasures of fishing are once again in the spotlight with this unique festival. The organizers offer a variety of activities, including a fishing tournament, shows by popular artists, entertainment and fireworks, all in a family-friendly atmosphere.
“It is with pride that I am associated with the Speckled Trout Festival, one of the most important fishing tournaments in Quebec. The exceptional work of the organizers and volunteers made this event a real success. I am convinced that festival-goers will be there to take advantage of the many festivities as well as the attractions of the beautiful Mauricie region, ”said Minister Bérubé.
The financial assistance granted by Tourisme Québec comes from the Tourism Industry Development Program - financial assistance for festivals and tourist events.
By funding festivals and events, the Government of Quebec supports the implementation of initiatives that contribute to the economic and cultural development of the regions of Quebec. It thus promotes the renewal of the Mauricie tourist offer with a view to continuous and sustainable improvement.
