Rivière-du-Loup - On the occasion of an unprecedented tour of members of the Government of Quebec in the Bas-Saint-Laurent and Gaspésie region, the Minister of Transport and Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Land Occupancy, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, in the presence of the Minister of Natural Resources, Ms. Martine Ouellet, and the Member of Parliament for Repentigny and Parliamentary Assistant to the Minister of Sustainable Development, Environment, Wildlife and Parks, Mr. Scott McKay, on behalf of the Minister, Yves-François Blanchet, is pleased to announce additional investments in the order of $ 1.7 million for the period 2013-2014, in the Bas-Saint-Laurent territory .
The 2013-2015 road works program, announced last spring, testifies to the government&#39;s desire to better manage infrastructure and pay the fair cost for public investments. “The objectives of this approach are bearing fruit. The amounts announced today reflect better project planning and respect for the ability to pay of all Quebecers. Controlling the costs of road works makes it possible to do more and respond even better to the needs of the people of Bas-Saint-Laurent, ”said Minister Gaudreault.
