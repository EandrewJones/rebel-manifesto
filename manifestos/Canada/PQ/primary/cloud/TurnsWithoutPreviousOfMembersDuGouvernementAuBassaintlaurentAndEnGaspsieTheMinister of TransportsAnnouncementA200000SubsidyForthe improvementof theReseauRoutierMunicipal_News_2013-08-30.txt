News - On the occasion of an unprecedented tour of members of the Government of Quebec in the Bas-Saint-Laurent and Gaspésie region, the Minister of Transport and Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Occupation of territory, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, and the MP for Bonaventure and parliamentary assistant to the Minister of Sustainable Development, Environment, Wildlife and Parks for the wildlife and parks component, Mr. Sylvain Roy, are pleased to announce that he grant of $ 200,000 is granted to the municipality of Nouvelle for the redevelopment of rue Maguire and route 132 giving access to the industrial park.
This subsidy is paid under the Municipal Road Network Improvement Assistance Program. This program helps finance improvement projects carried out by municipalities on their network. It aims to carry out work to improve the quality of the local road network and the safety of users.
“By participating as a partner in the financing of this project, our government is supporting the municipality of Nouvelle to improve its road network, by giving it the means to carry out work and contribute to the well-being of its community. Today&#39;s announcement will allow the municipality to ensure safe access and traffic flow for heavy vehicles entering the industrial park, ”said Minister Gaudreault.
The projects targeted by this program concern improving the safety and quality of the roadway through asphalting or road reconstruction work, the correction of dangerous curves or the installation of guardrails.
For his part, the member for Bonaventure is pleased with the completion of this work. &quot;This new access will improve both travel safety, by taking a road better suited to heavy vehicles, and the quality of life of the population in this sector,&quot; said Sylvain Roy.
