Quebec - The Minister for Industrial Policy and the Quebec Economic Development Bank, Ms. Élaine Zakaïb, announced the allocation of a non-repayable financial contribution of $ 246,350 to Quattriuum, a company specializing in the analysis of human gesture, for the development of a technology used to evaluate the slapshots of hockey players.
The company&#39;s investment plan, totaling nearly $ 630,000, is to design and market a product designed to measure the slapping performance of expert and novice hockey players. The product includes a sensor and a mobile software application. It is linked to a social web platform where players can view and compare their statistics.
“By putting technological innovation at the service of the favorite sport of Quebecers, hockey, the Quattriuum company shows that creativity has its place in all fields. This is an eloquent demonstration of our inventive potential, ”declared Élaine Zakaïb. “It is with great pride that our government supports this project, which will contribute to our reputation for excellence in a forward-thinking industry and which will allow amateur and professional athletes to push their limits and surpass themselves. &quot;
