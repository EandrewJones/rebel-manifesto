Quebec - The Member of Parliament for Saint-Jérôme and Leader of the Official Opposition, Pierre Karl Péladeau, and the Parti Québécois Culture Spokesperson, Véronique Hivon, paid tribute to Mr. René Angélil, a great Quebecer, a man of culture and song.
“It is with sadness that I learn of the death of my friend René Angélil. On behalf of the entire Parti Québécois team, I would like to express my sincere condolences to Céline as well as to the children, Patrick, Jean-Pierre, Anne-Marie, René-Charles, Eddy and Nelson. On a personal note, our family is losing a friend today. The links we have forged over the past several years will forever be etched in our memories. Thank you for everything, René ”, declared Pierre Karl Péladeau.
“All of Quebec will remember René Angélil with gratitude and emotion. Throughout his career as a singer and impresario, he brought his very particular touch, with humanity and respect. Quebec culture and local songs owe him a great deal, ”added Véronique Hivon.
“René was not only a talented businessman and impresario, he was also a man deeply attached to Quebec and its culture. He has done a lot for artists from here and elsewhere, from France in particular. My thoughts are with Céline, René&#39;s children and relatives, ”concluded the leader of the official opposition.
