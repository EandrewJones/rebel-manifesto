Presented by the Leader of the Official Opposition, Jean-François Lisée and Dave Turcotte, Member of Parliament for Saint-Jean and Official Opposition Critic for Social Services and Youth Protection.
Three axes:
Axis I: Actions to support people
1.Reduce the waiting time
2.Invest in Intensive Behavioral Interventions (IBI);
3. Ensure continuity of services between early childhood and the school level as well as between the different regions of Quebec;
4.Develop and improve the offer of meaningful and rewarding daytime activities, in order to promote the maintenance of skills for people aged 21 and over;
5.Promote the maintenance and creation of workplaces in order to offer employment prospects to adult clients;
6.Support technological initiatives promoting the autonomy of people living with an intellectual disability or an autism spectrum disorder;
7.Implement awareness campaigns to reduce prejudices against people living with an intellectual disability or an autism spectrum disorder;
