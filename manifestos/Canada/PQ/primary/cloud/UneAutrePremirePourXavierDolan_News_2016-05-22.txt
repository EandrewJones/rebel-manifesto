Quebec - The Leader of the Official Opposition and Member of Parliament for Jonquière, Sylvain Gaudreault, as well as the Member for Joliette and Parti Québécois spokesperson for culture and communications, Véronique Hivon, warmly congratulated Xavier Dolan for obtaining the Grand Prix and the Ecumenical Jury Prize at the Cannes Film Festival.
“On behalf of the entire Parti Québécois team, I extend my sincere congratulations to Xavier Dolan. He has taken a new step in his already busy career. His sixth feature film, Juste la fin du monde, won the second best film award at the Festival, a first for a Quebec director. The recognized talent of this exceptional artist and his accomplishments are a source of pride for all Quebecers. Well done, Xavier! », Declared the leader of the official opposition.
“Xavier Dolan&#39;s work is a source of inspiration. It is the bearer of dreams and hope for Quebec. The Grand Prix is a remarkable recognition for this local creator, and it undoubtedly places him among the best directors in the world. To see so much talent, daring and ingenuity in this Quebecer is in itself stupendous; seeing its qualities recognized once again internationally is further proof that Quebec is achieving great things and that it has nothing to envy in the world in terms of creativity. This is further proof that Quebec is making the world a better place, ”concluded Véronique Hivon.
