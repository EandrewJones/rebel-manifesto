Sainte-Anne-des-Monts– The Minister for the Regions, Minister responsible for the Gaspésie-Îles-de-la-Madeleine region and Member of Parliament for the riding of Gaspé, Mr. Gaétan Lelièvre, announces the allocation of aid funding of $ 70,000 under the Regional Initiatives Assistance Fund to support the Haute-Gaspésie Avalanche Center.
“The government intervention strategy for the development of the Gaspésie-Îles-de-la-Madeleine region provides for support for the development of winter tourism in Gaspésie. The Avalanche Center supports the development of winter recreational tourism activities, particularly in Gaspésie Park. The development of in-depth expertise in recent years has also enabled the Avalanche Center to train professionals throughout the province. I am happy to support this organization, which is an important element in the safe development of winter tourism activities in the Gaspé ”, declared Minister Lelièvre.
The Regional Initiatives Assistance Fund is part of the government&#39;s intervention strategy for the development of the Gaspésie-Îles-de-la-Madeleine region and is a major lever in the implementation of projects resulting from the priorities. development of the region. A maximum annual envelope of $ 6 million is provided for this fund for a period of five years. The program is in effect until March 31, 2018.
