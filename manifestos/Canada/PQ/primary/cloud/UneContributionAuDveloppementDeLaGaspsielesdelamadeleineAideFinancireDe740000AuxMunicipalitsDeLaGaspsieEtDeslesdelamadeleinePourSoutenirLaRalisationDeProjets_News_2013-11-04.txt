Sainte-Anne-des-Monts– The Minister for the Regions, Minister responsible for the Gaspésie-Îles-de-la-Madeleine region and Member of Parliament for the riding of Gaspé, Mr. Gaétan Lelièvre, announces the allocation of assistance financial support of $ 740,000 within the framework of the Regional Initiatives Assistance Fund to support the municipalities of all the RCMs of the Gaspé and of the Magdalen Islands agglomeration in the implementation of improvement projects of their recreational, cultural and leisure infrastructures. Details of the financial assistance are given in the appendix.
“Our government has made the development of the Gaspé one of its priorities. This is why I am proud to support these municipal projects which will promote the socioeconomic development of the region. I am strongly convinced that these projects will have positive spinoffs for the municipalities of the region, ”said Minister Lelièvre.
The Regional Initiatives Assistance Fund is part of the government&#39;s intervention strategy for the development of the Gaspésie-Îles-de-la-Madeleine region and is a major lever in the implementation of projects resulting from the priorities. development of the region. A maximum annual envelope of $ 6 million is provided for this fund for a period of five years. The program is in effect until March 31, 2018.
