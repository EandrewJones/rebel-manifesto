G aspé– The Minister for the Regions, Minister responsible for the Gaspésie-Îles-de-la-Madeleine region and Member of Parliament for the riding of Gaspé, Mr. Gaétan Lelièvre, announces the granting of financial assistance of $ 187,785 as part of the Regional Initiatives Assistance Fund to support the implementation of structuring projects in the regional county municipality of La Haute-Gaspésie, the complete list of which is appended.
“It is with pride that I announce this contribution, which will support the financing of salary measures and promising projects stemming from the Government Intervention Strategy of our beautiful region. These initiatives will ensure the economic and tourism development of the Gaspésie-Îles-de-la-Madeleine region with the aim of promoting tourism and job creation, ”declared the Minister.
The Regional Initiatives Assistance Fund aims to support the government intervention strategy for the development of the Gaspésie-Îles-de-la-Madeleine region and is a major lever intended to promote, in particular, the implementation of projects resulting from development priorities of the region. A maximum annual envelope of $ 6 million is provided for this Fund for a period of five years. The program is in effect until March 31, 2018.
