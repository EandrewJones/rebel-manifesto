Les Îles-de-la-Madeleine– The Member for Îles-de-la-Madeleine, Ms. Jeannine Richard, on behalf of the Minister for the Regions, Minister responsible for the Gaspésie-Îles-de-la-Madeleine region and Member of Parliament from the riding of Gaspé, Mr. Gaétan Lelièvre, announces the granting of financial assistance of $ 237,602 within the framework of the Regional Initiatives Assistance Fund to support the implementation of five structuring projects for the Îles-de- la-Madeleine, the complete list of which is appended.
&quot;Taken as a whole, these five projects, as different as they are, all have a common denominator, namely that of ensuring the maintenance and creation of jobs, of diversifying economic activities and of consolidating the tourism industry of the Îles-de-la-Madeleine. -Madeleine. It is therefore with pride that I announce this contribution, which will support the financing of salary measures and promising projects stemming from the Government Intervention Strategy, ”declared Ms. Richard.
The Regional Initiatives Assistance Fund is part of the government intervention strategy for the development of the Gaspésie-Îles-de-la-Madeleine region and is a major lever to promote the implementation of projects stemming from development priorities of the region. A maximum annual envelope of $ 6 million is provided for this fund for a period of five years. The program is in effect until March 31, 2018.
