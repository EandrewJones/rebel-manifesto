Bonaventure– The MNA for Bonaventure, Mr. Sylvain Roy, on behalf of the Minister for the Regions and Minister responsible for the Gaspésie-Îles-de-la-Madeleine region, Mr. Gaétan Lelièvre, and the Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and of Land Occupancy and Minister of Transport, Sylvain Gaudreault, announces the allocation of financial assistance of $ 313,633 to support the implementation of three structuring projects for the development and promotion of nautical activities in the Baie des Chaleurs , listed in the appendix.
“There is no doubt that the development of nautical activities in marinas in the Baie des Chaleurs will play a major role in the region&#39;s recreational tourism development. It is therefore with great pride that our government supports these three projects which will contribute to the enhancement of the Baie des Chaleurs, one of the most beautiful bays in the world, and will encourage water sports enthusiasts to come and visit our region ” said MP Roy.
Financial assistance is provided by two sources. The first sum of $ 168,559 comes from the Regional Initiatives Assistance Fund, which supports the government intervention strategy for the development of the Gaspésie-Îles-de-la-Madeleine region. This fund constitutes a major lever for the realization of projects responding to the development priorities of the region. A maximum annual envelope of $ 6 million is foreseen for a period of five years. The program is in effect until March 31, 2018.
A second sum of $ 145,074 comes from the Collective Entrepreneurship Infrastructure Program. This program is used to support social economy enterprises in the implementation of repair, expansion, construction or acquisition projects of commercial or industrial infrastructure in order to promote the socio-economic vitality of the territories as well. as the preservation and enhancement of the environment. Remember that this program has a budget of $ 10 million.
