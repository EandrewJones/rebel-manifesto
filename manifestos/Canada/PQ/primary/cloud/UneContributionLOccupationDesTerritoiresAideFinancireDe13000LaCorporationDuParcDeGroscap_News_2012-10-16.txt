L&#39;Étang-du-Nord - The Minister for the Regions, Minister responsible for the Gaspésie-Îles-de-la-Madeleine region and Member of Parliament for the riding of Gaspé, Mr. Gaétan Lelièvre, is pleased to announce the award $ 13,000 in financial assistance to the Corporation du Parc de Gros-Cap for the renovation of its reception kiosk.
The improvements that will be made to the building will make it possible to offer a quality service that meets the needs of the clientele, in particular by the addition of access for disabled people, and to obtain a three-star classification from the Industry Corporation. tourism in Quebec. In addition to having a positive impact on the increase in the overall tourism offer of the Magdalen Islands, the completion of this project will help consolidate 23 seasonal jobs.
&quot;Our government is proud to support this social economy enterprise which is working to develop new products and services for young people in the region and which thus contributes to improving the tourist offer,&quot; said Mr. Lelièvre.
The announced financial assistance comes from the Regional Strategic Intervention Fund.
