New Richmond– The Member for Bonaventure and Parliamentary Assistant to the Minister of Sustainable Development, Environment, Wildlife and Parks (wildlife and parks component), Mr. Sylvain Roy, on behalf of the Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Land Occupancy and Minister of Transport, Sylvain Gaudreault, announces the granting of financial assistance of $ 150,000 to the Petite-Cascapedia Regional Park to upgrade the sports equipment of the Pin rouge tourist resort.
More specifically, the project submitted consists, among other things, in enabling the regional park to acquire a snow groomer, install a new snow line, add lighting on the tube sliding track. and to install an air conditioning system in the reception pavilion of the station.
&quot;It is with great pride that the Government of Quebec is contributing to the realization of this project, which will improve the service offer and better meet the needs of the tourist clientele of the Gaspésie-Îles-de- la-Madeleine ”, indicated Mr. Roy.
Financial assistance comes from the conjunctural development fund of the Ministry of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Land Occupancy.
