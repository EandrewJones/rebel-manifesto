Beloeil– The Minister of Higher Education, Research, Science and Technology and Member of Parliament for Borduas, Mr. Pierre Duchesne, on behalf of the Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Land Occupancy and Minister of Transports, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, announces the granting of financial assistance of $ 35,000 to the Corporation de développement communautaire de la Vallée-du-Richelieu to support the establishment of a multi-sector and multi-network stowage structure in of social development.
More concretely, this sum will be used to finance two human resources assigned to the creation of the stowage structure which is in line with the aims of the R.É.V.É. (Reflection, Evaluation, Vision, Evolution of the consultation). Launched in 2011, this approach aims to establish synergy between the various social development stakeholders on the territory of the MRC in order to avoid duplication of interventions, to ensure consistency in actions and to optimize available resources. Ultimately, this project should allow the community to develop a common vision for social and community development.
“By contributing to the pursuit of this important process of social and community mobilization, our government is choosing to support the creation of a strong community that has the means to act according to its own needs. I am also convinced that the relevance and quality of this innovative project will inspire similar initiatives in Quebec, for the benefit of all Quebecers, ”said Mr. Duchesne.
Financial assistance comes from the conjunctural development fund of the Ministry of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Land Occupancy.
