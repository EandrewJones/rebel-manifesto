Baie-James - The Member of Parliament for Ungava and Parliamentary Assistant to the Minister of Natural Resources (Northern Affairs section), Mr. Luc Ferland, on behalf of the Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Land Occupancy and Minister of Transport, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, announces the granting of financial assistance of $ 60,000 to AGLA Médias to support the production and distribution of the Focus Baie-James project.
More specifically, this sum will be used to finance the production of a documentary series on James Bay. This project, which will present the odyssey of two photographers on the roads of this vast country, will highlight the cultural, natural and historical riches of this region unknown to Quebecers.
&quot;It is with great pride that the Government of Quebec contributes to the realization of this project which will make James Bay shine throughout Quebec while allowing Jamesians to see their region from a new perspective and to discover it. facets still unknown. A whole new image of James Bay will be presented, a unique and positive image, ”said Mr. Ferland.
Financial assistance comes from the conjunctural development fund of the Ministry of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Land Occupancy.
