Sainte-Anne-des-Monts– The Minister for the Regions, Minister responsible for the Gaspésie-Îles-de-la-Madeleine region and Member for the riding of Gaspé, Mr. Gaétan Lelièvre, is pleased to announce the award financial assistance of $ 7,250 to the company X.Pert&#39;Son for the acquisition of an LED screen wall.
Specialist in sound, lighting and multimedia, X.Pert&#39;Son has been offering event and conference production services in the Gaspé region and northern New Brunswick since 1996. Financial assistance granted will allow X.Pert&#39;Son to diversify its market while providing the region with a new service currently offered by outside companies. This acquisition will consolidate the six current jobs in addition to helping to create a new job as a specialized video technician.
“Our government is proud to support this company which wishes to diversify its activities and, at the same time, contribute to the development of the events sector. This support testifies to our desire to promote economic activity in the region, ”indicated Mr. Lelièvre.
The announced financial assistance comes from the Regional Strategic Intervention Fund.
