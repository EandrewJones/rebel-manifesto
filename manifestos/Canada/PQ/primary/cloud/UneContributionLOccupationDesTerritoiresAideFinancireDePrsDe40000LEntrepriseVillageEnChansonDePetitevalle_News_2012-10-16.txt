Petite-Vallée– The Minister for the Regions, Minister responsible for the Gaspésie-Îles-de-la-Madeleine region and Member of Parliament for the riding of Gaspé, Mr. Gaétan Lelièvre, is pleased to announce the granting of assistance funding of $ 38,631 to the company Village en chanson de Petite-Vallée to modernize the facilities of the Théâtre de la Vieille Forge.
Non-profit organization, Village en chanson de Petite-Vallée has been working in the cultural, tourist and social circles of the region since 1998. Thanks to the financial assistance granted, it will be able to carry out the interior and exterior design of the theater, the upgrading of theater and small school equipment as well as the acquisition of a parking lot. It is important to stress that this upgrade will be based on sustainable development principles, including the purchase of equipment for the management of recyclable waste and composting.
“Our government is proud to support this organization which sets itself apart nationally through its very strong artistic direction and original activities. The support granted will make it possible to maintain the presence of the places, to encourage cultural activities and, by the same token, the recreational tourism offer in the region ”, indicated Mr. Lelièvre.
The announced financial assistance comes from the Regional Strategic Intervention Fund.
