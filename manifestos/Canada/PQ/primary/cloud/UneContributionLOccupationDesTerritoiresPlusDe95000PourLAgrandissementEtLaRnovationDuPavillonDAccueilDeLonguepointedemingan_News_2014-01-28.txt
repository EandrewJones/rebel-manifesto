Quebec - The parliamentary assistant to the Minister of Transport (maritime transport component) and Member of Parliament for Duplessis, Ms. Lorraine Richard, on behalf of the Minister of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Land Occupancy, Mr. Sylvain Gaudreault, announces financial assistance of $ 96,160 for the expansion and renovation of the Longue-Pointe-de-Mingan reception pavilion.
The project responds to a need for increased traffic to cultural, leisure and tourist activities. Its realization will consolidate the tourist vocation of the municipality and improve the regional offer, in conjunction with the Mingan Islands research station, the Parks Canada reception and interpretation center and the Island. -aux-Parroquets with its lighthouse and interpretation activities.
“This is a project that immediately fits in with government guidelines to ensure the occupation and vitality of the territories. The expansion work will promote the integration of the reception pavilion with other leisure facilities and thereby create a gateway to Île-aux-Perroquets, ”said MP Lorraine Richard.
Financial assistance comes from the conjunctural development fund of the Ministry of Municipal Affairs, Regions and Land Occupancy.
