Quebec - The MP for Saint-Jérôme and leader of the official opposition, Pierre Karl Péladeau, and the MP for Hochelaga-Maisonneuve and spokesperson for international relations, Carole Poirier, strongly condemn the terrorist attacks committed in various regions of the world today, and express, on behalf of the members of the Parti Québécois, their solidarity with all those affected directly or indirectly by these tragic events.
“Several terrorist attacks have taken place in the past few hours: in France, Tunisia, Kuwait, Somalia and Syria; we strongly and firmly denounce them. Like my colleagues from the Parti Québécois, I am shaken by such injustice, in front of so many innocent lives cut short. On their behalf, I extend my most sincere condolences to all those whose lives are forever turned upside down, whether they are here or abroad, ”said Carole Poirier.
“Nothing can ever justify such a degree of violence, barbarity and horror. I would like to tell the affected peoples that we are at their side to condemn and fight terrorism in all its forms, and to make democracy triumph in all circumstances, ”concluded the deputy.
