Quebec - The member for Masson, Guillaume Tremblay, met with investigators from the Sûreté du Québec following an altercation in Mascouche. Mr. Tremblay officially filed a complaint.
Mr. Tremblay came to a restaurant in Mascouche on the evening of May 12th. A few minutes after his arrival, in the presence of members of his family and a friend, Mr. Tremblay was intimidated and threatened by two individuals who are said to be linked to the construction industry.
Since an investigation is underway, there will be no further comments.
