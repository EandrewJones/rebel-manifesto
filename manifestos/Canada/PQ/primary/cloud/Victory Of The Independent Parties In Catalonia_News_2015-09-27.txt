Quebec - In light of the results of the regional elections held today in Catalonia, the member for Saint-Jérôme and leader of the official opposition, Pierre Karl Péladeau, as well as the member for Hochelaga-Maisonneuve and spokesperson for the Party Quebecer in international relations, Carole Poirier, wished to underline the victory of the Catalan separatist parties, which obtained a majority of seats.
“It&#39;s stimulating to see all these people mobilized for their dream of freedom. Congratulations to the Catalan separatists, including the Junts pel Sí coalition. Good luck to you all! », Declared Pierre Karl Péladeau.
“The movement for the independence of Catalonia is impressive. The victory of the independence coalition shows that the union of political and citizen forces has borne fruit, ”said Carole Poirier, who is in Barcelona as official representative of the Parti Québécois.
