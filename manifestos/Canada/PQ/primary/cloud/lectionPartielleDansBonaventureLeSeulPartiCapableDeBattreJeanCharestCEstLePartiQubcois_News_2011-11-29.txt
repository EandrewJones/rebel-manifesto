Montreal - The member for Matane and responsible for the Gaspé region for the Parti Québécois, Pascal Bérubé, is delighted that the candidate of the Green Party of Quebec in Bonaventure, Jean Cloutier, has asked the sovereignists to vote for Sylvain Roy and the Party Quebecois on December 5.
“By inviting the voters of Bonaventure to vote for the Parti Québécois candidate, Sylvain Roy, Jean Cloutier is showing courage and realism. Its message is clear, the only party capable of defeating the Liberal Party and Jean Charest is the Parti Québécois, ”declared Pascal Bérubé.
Pascal Bérubé invites Quebec solidaire supporters to do like Jean Cloutier and join the Parti Québécois to beat Jean Charest in Bonaventure.
