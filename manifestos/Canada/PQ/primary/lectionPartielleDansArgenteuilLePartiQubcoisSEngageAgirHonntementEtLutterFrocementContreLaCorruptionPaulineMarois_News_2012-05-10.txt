Lachute - The leader of the Parti Québécois, Pauline Marois, today inaugurated the electional of the Québécois candidate, Roland Richer, with a view to the partial election of June 11. 
Pauline Marois took advantage of the event to remind you that the Parti Québécois is resolved to clean up political manners and proposes a series of measures in this direction. "At the Québécois party, we will act honestly and we will be intractable in our fight against corruption. In order to achieve this, we propose to lower contributions to political parties at $ 100 per person, to establish fixed date elections and limit the number of mandates of mayors and the Prime Minister. These changes go through the election of the Parti Québécois, "said Pauline Marois. 
"Whether in the field of economy, social solidarity, culture, health and the environment, the candidate present by the Parti Québécois is clearly the best choice for the people of Argenteuil. Roland Richer is an honest and honest man who is there to serve the citizens, "added Pauline Marois, recalling that this partial election is made necessary because of the resignation of David Whissel who left under conflicts of interest. . 
"Quebeckers need elected listening, ready to discuss, flexible when it comes to getting along with citizens and inflexible to corruption, patronage, federal attacks against our nation. What the Parti Québécois offers is clear: asserting, enriching and helping each other, unlike the Liberal Party and the CAQ, who prefer to confront the citizens and defend the interests of the privileged ", has concluded the candidate of the Quebecois party, Roland Richer. 
Remember that the Parti Québécois is the greatest political force in Quebec with its 90,000 members.
