Longueuil- The Assembly to choose the candidate or the candidate for the Partial Party for the partial election in the constituency of Marie-Victorin will take place on September 18th. All the electors and voters of Marie-Victorin can take a membership card from the Parti Québécois by August 18 to be members in good standing and thus participate in the investiture assembly. 
Partician members will therefore have the opportunity to choose who will bring the colors of political training at this meeting. The members wishing to be a candidate have until 8 September to file their application form. 
"The enthusiasm for our political training is at the rendezvous. With this investiture assembly, the word will be to the members of the Parti Québécois. This is the first step in this partial election which, with all the determination and work we are capable, will solve by a victory of the Parti Québécois in Marie-Victorin, "said Quebec's president, Raymond Archambault. 
The Investiture Assembly will be held at the Square Desaulniers, at 1023 Boulevard Taschereau in Longueuil.
