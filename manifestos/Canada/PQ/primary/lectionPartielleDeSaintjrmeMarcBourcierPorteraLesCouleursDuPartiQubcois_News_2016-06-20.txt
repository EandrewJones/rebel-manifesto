Saint-Jérôme- At the end of an activist evening, the Parti Québécois announced that Marc Bourcier will be the candidate for political training during the partial election of Saint-Jérôme. "For me, tomorrow, the campaign begins. I will meet the voters of Saint-Jérôme to share my vision and my pride of being Jéromian. I will be listening to their concerns and I will show that Saint-Jérôme has everything to gain to continue the work started by Pierre Karl Péladeau with the Parti Québécois in the constituency, "said Mr. Bourcier. 
Teacher for nearly 35 years in Saint-Jérôme, Mr. Bourcier has also been a municipal councilor of Saint-Jérôme since 2013. During his career, Marc Bourcier has undertaken several community activities to help the people of St. Jerome. "It's a great pride for me and the Parti Québécois to welcome as a candidate Marc Bourcier, a man working and dedicated for Saint-Jérôme. With the partial election, constituency voters will be able to send a clear message by choosing an integrity deputy, anchored in his community and the Jéômians' service, "said Sylvain Gaudreault, leader of the Parti Québécois. 
From here the triggering of the campaign, the Parti Québécois of Saint-Jérôme and the candidate Marc Bourcier will leave no effort to meet the citizens. Whether by the door-to-door or by the presence at the public events of Saint-Jérôme, the whole team of the Parti Québécois will be on the ground attentive to the concerns of the voters.
