In the Achuntza community, Parroquia Macuma, subsidiary of the Shuar nation of Ecuador Nashe, the presidents of Conaie, Jorge Herrera and Conféniae, Franco Viteri, on October 10 attended. 
The members of the community support the management of the CONAIE and CONFENIATE. 
Between one of the resolutions taken in the Community is that the bases supported the work that is carried out at the national level, such as mobilization, unemployment and lifting. 
They pointed out that they agree with the posture of the indigenous movement of Ecuador. 
Other pronouncements was that they support Franco Viteri as president of Conféniae and affirmed his unit for the fighting platform and mobilizations at the national level.
