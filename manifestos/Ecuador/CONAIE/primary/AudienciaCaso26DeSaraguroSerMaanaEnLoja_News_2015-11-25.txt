According to the report of the coordinator of social organizations of Loja, the fighters of the Saraguro people denounced irregularities in the judicial processes that are followed. 
This prior to the evaluation and preparatory audience of the trial that will be developed on November 26, 2015 from 11:000 in the Auditorium Hall of the Council of the Judiciary. 
On August 17, 2015, 26 members of the Saraguro people were arrested among men, women and young people. 
After a month, approximately, they were released under precautionary measure. 
The indigenous, social organizations of the canton Saraguro and De Loja have pointed out that they demand justice for the Luchores of the People.
