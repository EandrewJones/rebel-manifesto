Quito, April 20, 2017  The Confederation of Indigenous Nationalities of Ecuador-Conanea categorically rejects the criminal demand that it weighs against the nine members of the National Anti-Corruption Commission (CNA) on the alleged crime of slander against the State Comptroller Carlos Polit. 
The CNA was constituted by the Unitary National Collective of Workers, Indigenous and Social Sectors with the aim of controlping the acts of corruption that has been fame in the ten years of Rafael Correa, Country Alliance. 
The members have not said or made more than submitted allegations of acts of corruption that have been found after a meticulous observation to some public procurement carried out by the current government. 
We urge Ecuadorian justice to act with efficiency and rigor before acts of corruption and other facts or crimes that have affected the image of the country's public administration with cases such as: tax havens; Odebrecht and others. 
The CONAIE ratifies its total support to the National Anti-Corruption Commission and is sympathetic with the nine members facing harassment, a process that ammented - vulnerating human rights, which for fighting and denouncing acts of corruption resist the handworthy in the Administration of Justice by the Executive. 
By the Governing Council  Jorge Herrera.  President Conaie.  Bulletin in PDF.  .
