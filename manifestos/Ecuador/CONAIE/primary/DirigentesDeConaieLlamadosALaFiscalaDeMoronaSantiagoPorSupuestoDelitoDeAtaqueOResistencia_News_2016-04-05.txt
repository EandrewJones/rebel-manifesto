Jorge Herrera, president of the Confederation of the Indigenous Nationalities of Ecuador, Conaie, and Rómulo Akachu, Vice President, dated March 18, 2016, are called before the prosecution of Morona Santiago, City of Macas, to surrender Assumption crime of attack or resistance for the actions of August 2015. 
The President of the Conaie, Jorge Herrera, is cited for Friday, April 8, 2016, at 9:00 a.m. in the offices located in the streets Juan de la Cruz and May 24 of the city of Macas, who according to the part , You must attend accompanied by your defense attorney. 
Herrera confirmed that he will attend such a judicial call because "this does not frighten us," he said. 
In addition, he believes that it is a government strategy attempting to generate fear of leaders. 
Akachu, Vice President of Conaie, must be presented before the Prosecutor's Office on Thursday, April 7, 2016, at 11h00 in the same direction. 
In the first citation they were called, also leaders of the Amazon. 
Among those who were the president of the Conféniae, Franco Viteri; The president of the Interprovincial Federation of Shuar Centers - FICSH, Agustín Wachapá; and other representations of the area. 
It is not confirmed even if they are again cited.
