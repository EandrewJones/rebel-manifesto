The Kichwa Confederation of Ecuador, ECUARUNARI, took the Constitutional Court an amicus Curiae today, signed by the Legal Center for Human Rights and Environment Defenders, International Body based in the US, on the unconstitutionality of Decree 16. 
Jorge Herrera, president, Severino Sharupi and Rumy Yumbay leaders of Conaie, and delegation of the provinces accompanied to make the delivery. 
With this action once again the leaders demand that the date and time for the hearing is indicated within this process, because the order-process - was carried out last May 2014 without obtaining any result to the present. 
"It is worrisome for Conaie and Ecuarunari the situation of organizations, communities that can not be legalized," said Jorge Herrera.
