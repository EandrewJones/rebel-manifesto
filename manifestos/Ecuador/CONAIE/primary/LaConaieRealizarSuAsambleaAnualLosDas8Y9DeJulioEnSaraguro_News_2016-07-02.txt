The Confederation of Indigenous Nationalities of Ecuador - Conaie, will develop its ordinary annual assembly on July 8 and 9, 2016 in the Saraguro, Province of Loja. 
The points to be discussed in the Assembly are:  Annual Report of the Governing Council. 
Criminalization of the Social Protest and Defense of the Territories. 
Political proposal for the elections of 2017. 
Agrarian Summit. 
New actions to be done. 
.
