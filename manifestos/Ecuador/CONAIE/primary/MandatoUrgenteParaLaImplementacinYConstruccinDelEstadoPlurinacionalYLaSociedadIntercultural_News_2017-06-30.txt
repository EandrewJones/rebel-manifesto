The Confederation of Indigenous Nationalities of Ecuador-CONAIE socialized the urgent mandate for the implementation and construction of the plurinational State and the Intercultural Society "on Tuesday, June 28, 2017 to civil society and media in the city of Quito. 
The mandate consists of the following main axes: 1) for the construction of the plurinational state; 2) towards the change of the economic model; 3) democracy and human rights; and 4) Combat corruption and impunity. 
On Tuesday, July 4, said mandate was presented before the Ecuadorian Government, in which more than 3 thousand representatives of the peoples and nationalities of the country participated. 
Mandate of the peoples and nationalities delivered to the Government of Ecuador  .
