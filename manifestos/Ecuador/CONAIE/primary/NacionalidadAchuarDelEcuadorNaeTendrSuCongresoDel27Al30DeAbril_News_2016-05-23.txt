By unitary decision of the Achuar nationality from May 27 to 30, Morona Santiago will be held in Wampuik, Morona Santiago his Congress for the Unity of Nationality after the organizational difficulties that have been going through in recent months. 
After several working meetings, the 16 presidents of the NAE-Base Associations resolved to carry out this Unity Congress with which differences are left aside and working on the consolidation and strengthening of basic communities in Pastaza and Morona Santiago. 
The Congress will discuss the organizational situation of the NAE and will elect the new Government Council once the two leaders formed during this time decided to advance for the benefit of its bases and jointly convene to Congress. 
We will keep informed of the incidents of this important consolidation event of one of the historical and strong bases of Conféniae.
