The nationalities of Pastaza Achuar, Shiwiar, Sapara and Kichwa provided this morning a press conference in which they announced their position before the announcement of the central government of the signing of exploration and exploitation contracts for the oil blocks 79 and 83 and a New round of bidding in the south of the Amazon. 
They also rejected police and military intrusion by the government within the territories of Shuar Peoples and Achuar de Morona Santiago at the Makuma Parish, Canton Taisha.
