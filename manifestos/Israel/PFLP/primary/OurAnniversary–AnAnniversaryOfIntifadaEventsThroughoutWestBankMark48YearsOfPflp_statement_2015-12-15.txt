
Following a series of mass arrests, particularly focused in the refugee camps, and raids on offices of the PFLP and mass Palestinian institutions by the occupation forces in Tulkarem, Jenin, Qalqilya and elsewhere, the PFLP and its cadres, members and supporters in the occupied West Bank of Palestine held a series of rallies, marches and actions commemorating the 48th anniversary of the Front’s founding.
In the village of Asira near Nablus, masked supporters of the Front marched through the village, carrying Palestinian flags and PFLP banners and pictures of the banners, pledging to continue the uprising. The marches burned a flag of the Zionist occupier and played the songs of the Front through the village.

In Dheisheh refugee camp near Bethlehem, the home of two of the Front’s recent martyrs, shot down by occupation forces as they marched for freedom, Comrades Malek Shaheen and Muataz Zawahreh, comrades of the Front took part in a masked march throughout the camp commemorating the 48th anniversary.
The marchers carried Palestinian flags, PFLP banners and posters of the prisoners and martyrs, chanting for the rising intifada and the liberation of Palestine and demanding an end to security coordination between the Palestinian Authority and the occupation.
They pledged to continue on the path of intifada and resistance until liberation.

The Front in the city of Nablus held a mass rally and march through the streets of the city to the monument for the Front’s martyrs, carrying Palestinian flags, PFLP banners and posters of imprisoned General Secretary Comrade Ahmad Sa’adat and the martyrs of the Front.
The marchers chanted to escalate the intifada and resistance against the Zionist occupation and demanded the abolition of security coordination between the Palestinian Authority and the occupier.
The comrades participating in the march emphasized the anniversary of the Intifada of 1987 and the anniversary of the Front as critically important in this current era of national struggle, at a time when the escalation of the intifada against the occupier is on the rise. They noted that the Palestinian people are determined to win their freedom through struggle and resistance not by begging and negotiations that have won nothing for our people.
