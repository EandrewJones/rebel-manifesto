
The Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine called for demonstrations on Sunday, July 28 against the resumption of negotiations. Demonstrations will take place on Sunday afternoon in both Ramallah and Gaza City.
To reject the futile negotiations and their path and to honour the martyrs and prisoners of our great people…
The PFLP is calling on you to express your rejection to the negotiations and their catastrophic path by participating in a mass rally amd march, to honour the sacrifices of our martyrs, prisoners, and our people in the refugee camps and to oppose the negotiations that are violating popular and national consensus.
Place: occupied Ramallah, assemble in the Orthodox Club, and to rally in the streets of Ramallah; and Gaza City, Palestine Square
Date and time: Sunday, July 28 – 1 pm
