Justice and Equality Movement – Sudan ( JEM )
Press Statement
A high-level delegation led by the chairman Dr. Gebriel Ibrahim Mohamed , will visit the republic of Chad from 2 to 5 November 2019, in response to an invitation from president Idriss Deby Etno , to discuss the ways of advancing the peace process and the role of N’Djamena in achieving peace and stability in Sudan.
Motasim A. AdamSpokespersonNovember 1, 2019
