Darfur Union in the UK: The GoS Attack on 40 St. Hospital in Omdurman is Not an Isolated Incident, It is Part of Their Ethos
Only yesterday, the GoS launched an attack on 40 Street Hospital in Omdurman, in the evening of Jan. 9th, 2019. This by no mean is a new tactic. Bashir and his army have been terrorizing the people of Sudan by using war crimes tactics in Darfur, Nuba Mountains and Blue Nile State. Two related examples are below
May 2014 & Jan. 2016 onwards GoS bombed Mercy Hospital in Nuba Mount. (Ref. 1) & used chemical weapons in West Darfur (ref. 2), respectively. More atrocious attacks over the years have lead to the indictment of the sitting president, Omer El-Bashir, by the international Criminal Court on 4th March 2009 for genocide, war crimes and crimes against the humanity (ref. 3).
May the revolt of the people continues until the ICC indictee Bashir and his government are removed they are facing justice.
