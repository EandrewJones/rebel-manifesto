[email protected]GoS Loses A Major Battle in North Darfur
A Military Statement
JEM branch of the SRF scored another defeat against SAF at Daunky Baasheem in North Darfur, near Al-Fahir city.  The battle took place yesterday, February 6th, at 4pm local time. Our gallant troops overran a large SAF and their allied militia forces and which were armed with 138 vehicles and eight military tanks.  Enemy forces were completely neutralised and most of their military hardware were either destroyed or taken by our forces.  Long live our strugglefor justice General Badawi Musa El SakinMilitary Spokesperson for JEMNorth Darfur07/03/2012
