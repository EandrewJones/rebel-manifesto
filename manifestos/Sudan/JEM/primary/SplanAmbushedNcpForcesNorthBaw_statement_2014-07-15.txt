Yesterday 13th of July 014, SPLA/N forces of SRF ambushed National Congress Party (NCP) forces in Fadmia-Doom area north of Baw County in Blue Nile state, the enemy inflicted heavy casualties, 4 dead bodies left on the ground and 8 others wounded, and the following are destroyed completely 4 tractors,2 excavators and 2 big trucks loaded with different logistic supply including ammunition, from our side we lost 2 martyrs, our forces are monitoring NCP forces and militias movements closely.
Arnu Ngutulu Loddi
SPLM/A-N official spokesman
