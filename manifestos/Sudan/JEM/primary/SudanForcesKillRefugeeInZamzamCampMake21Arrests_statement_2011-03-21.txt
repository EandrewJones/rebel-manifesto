Sudan forces kill refugee in Zamzam Camp, make 21 arrestsZAMZAM CAMPOne internally displaced person (IDP) was killed Sunday in the market of Zamzam Camp and 21 people were arrested. The camp hosts at least 200.000 people.
Witnesses said government forces made three arrests the day before yesterday, including Omda Ismail Ahmed Rabah. A larger force entered the camp late Sunday afternoon, killing a man in the market and arrestesting 15 others. Witnesses said that the entry of government troops caused panic and fear in the hearts of the camp residents.Source: radiodabangahttp://www.radiodabanga.org/node/12584
