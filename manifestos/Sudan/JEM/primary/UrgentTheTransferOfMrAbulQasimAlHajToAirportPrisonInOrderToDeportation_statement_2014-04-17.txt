URGENT: the transfer of Mr. Abul Qasim Al -Haj to airport prison in order to deportation
An urgent breaking news said that, the Egyptian security authorities will transfer this evening the Sudanese opposition activist Mr. Abu al-Qasim Ibrahim Al haj from Al Qanatir prison to the Cairo International Airport prison , and that in preparation for his extradition to the Sudanese authorities on tomorrow Thursday 17 April 2014 p.The deportation Of Mr. Al haj is a flagrant violation of the principles of human rights ; in the fact that Mr. Abu al-Qasim Alhaj registered as a refugee recognized by the Office of the UNHCR in Cairo, and he is an activist and human rights defender ;,so it is definitely will exposed sooner or later to die if he been handing to the Khartoum authorities.Mr. Alhaj was been put in jail by the Egyptian authorities without trial for four years and four months , and this top violation of human rights and it is against the justice and freedom values that Egyptian revolution has been called to it.SCCsdMonitoring Section16 .
